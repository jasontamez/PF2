Sickle-sword - Weapons - Equipment Sickle-sword Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 9 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type S; Special distracting, see text Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy Description Favored by those residents of Irrisen with no talent for witchcraft, this exotic blade curves multiple times along its 3-1/2 feet. The sickle-sword's hilt is long and curved, and its blade bears a small secondary grip partway up its length. These allow a wielder with a free hand to rapidly twist the sword in unpredictable ways. When wielding a sickle-sword in one hand and nothing in the other, you can spend a swift action to gain a +2 bonus on your next attack roll before the end of your turn.
