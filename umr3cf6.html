Universal Monster Rules Amazing Initiative (Ex) [Mythic] Source Bestiary 4 pg. 290 The creature has a bonus on initiative checks equal to its mythic rank. As a free action on its turn, it can expend one use of mythic power to take an additional standard action during that turn. This additional standard action can't be used to cast a spell. It can't gain an extra action in this way more than once per round. Format: amazing initiative Location: Special Attacks.
