Pantheistic Clasp - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pantheistic Clasp Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 30 Aura faint abjuration; CL 3rd Slot neck; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description A simple clasp dangles from this fine brass chain. As a standard action, the wearer can attach a single holy symbol to the clasp. The wearer gains a +2 sacred bonus on saving throws against spells and effects with an alignment descriptor that matches the alignment of the deity represented by the attached holy symbol. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, corruption resistance; Cost 1,500 gp
