Werewhistle - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Werewhistle Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 325 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 9th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight — Description This whistle is shaped like a wolf's head and is crafted out of silver. Once per day the bearer may blow the whistle to cause one lycanthrope within 30 feet to make a DC 17 Will save or be forced back into its humanoid form for the next 24 hours. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, baleful polymorph; Cost 4,000 gp
