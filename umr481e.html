Universal Monster Rules Light Blindness (Ex) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 295, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 301, Bestiary 2 pg. 298, Bestiary 3 pg. 296, Bestiary 4 pg. 296, Bestiary 5 pg. 295 Creatures with light blindness are blinded for 1 round if exposed to bright light, such as sunlight or the daylight spell.
