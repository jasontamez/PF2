Trick Shooter - Feats Trick Shooter (Combat) Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 9 Your uncanny aim allows you to disable your opponents without harming them. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Deadly Aim, base attack bonus +1. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on ranged combat maneuvers. When you attempt to perform a ranged combat maneuver, you can refrain from damaging the target at no penalty.
