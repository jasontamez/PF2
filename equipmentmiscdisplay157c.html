Tanner's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tanner's kit Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 244 Price 10 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Description This kit contains a fleshing knife, a bottle of tanning solution, a small tanning board, and a leather apron. If you use this kit to harvest and tan an animal's hide, you receive a +5 circumstance bonus on Craft checks to create leather items such as belts, boots, cloaks, clothing, gloves, or hide or leather armor. A kit contains enough tanning solution to create a total of 150 pounds of leather items.
