Everburning torch - Miscellaneous - Equipment Everburning torch Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 110 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This otherwise normal torch has a continual flame spell cast on it. This causes it to shed light like an ordinary torch, but it does not emit heat or deal fire damage if used as a weapon.
