Mammoth goad - Miscellaneous - Equipment Mammoth goad Source People of the North pg. 28 Price 5 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description Used in the Realm of the Mammoth Lords to train and direct domesticated megafauna, this goad is a sturdy wooden pole 10 to 15 feet long with a prod of polished bone, ivory, or soapstone on one end. The goad grants a +2 bonus on all Handle Animal checks made against mammoths and other megafauna.
