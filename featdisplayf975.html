Titan Strike - Feats Titan Strike Source Mythic Adventures pg. 75 Your fists can fell titanic foes. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike (mythic). Benefit: Your unarmed strike deals damage as if you were one size category larger. You also gain a +1 bonus for each size category that your target is larger than you on the following: bull rush, drag, grapple, overrun, sunder, and trip combat maneuver checks and the DC of your Stunning Fist.
