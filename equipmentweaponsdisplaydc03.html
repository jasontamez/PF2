Kyoketsu shoge - Weapons - Equipment Kyoketsu shoge Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 21, Ultimate Combat pg. 132 Statistics Cost 6 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type S or P; Special disarm, grapple, monk, reach Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Flails; Monk Description This weapon consists of a foot-long double-edged blade, with another smaller, curved hook-blade sticking out the side like a rooster's spur. The blade is strung to a large iron hoop with a 10-foot length of rope. The blade can be used as an off-handed melee weapon or thrown like a dagger, while the rope and circlet can be whipped around and swung at opponents as a bludgeoning reach weapon.
