Improved Feinting Flurry - Feats Improved Feinting Flurry (Combat) Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 25 Your first melee attack throws your opponent completely off guard. Prerequisites: Dex 17, flurry of blows class feature, Combat Expertise, Feinting Flurry, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: While using Feinting Flurry, if you successfully feint, your opponent is denied its Dexterity bonus to AC until the end of your turn. Normal: A successful feint causes your opponent to be denied its Dexterity bonus to AC against your next attack.
