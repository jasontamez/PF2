Charging Hurler - Feats Charging Hurler (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 92 You know how to use your momentum to enhance your thrown weapon attacks. Prerequisites: Point-Blank Shot. Benefit: You can use the charge rules to make a thrown weapon attack. All the parameters of a charge apply, except that you must only move closer to your opponent, and you must end your movement within 30 feet of that opponent. If you do, you can make a single thrown weapon attack against that opponent, gaining the +2 bonus on the attack roll and taking a -2 penalty to your AC until the start of your next turn. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 115 When using this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to end your movement within 50 feet of your target instead of 30 feet.
