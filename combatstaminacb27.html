Quarterstaff Master - Combat Stamina - Feats Quarterstaff Master Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 129 Related Feat Quarterstaff Master When you use this feat to wield a quarterstaff as a one-handed weapon, you can spend 5 stamina points to retain the ability to use it as a double weapon and still keep a hand free. This effect lasts until the beginning of your next turn.
