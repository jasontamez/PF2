Hyena Shawl - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Hyena Shawl Source Monster Codex pg. 93 Aura moderate abjuration and necromancy; CL 8th Slot head; Price 11,000 gp; Weight — Description When wrapped around the neck and jaw, this swath of black cloth lets its wearer see normally in a sandstorm (her vision isn't reduced, nor does she take the -4 penalty on Perception checks) and avoid taking nonlethal damage from the storm. Once per day on command, the shawl's wearer can emit a strange cackling. Enemies within 30 feet of the shawl's wearer who can hear the cackling must succeed at a DC 16 Will saving throw or become frightened for 1 round. Those who succeed at the saving throw are shaken for 1d4 rounds instead. Both of the cackle's effects are sonic mind-affecting fear effects. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fear; Cost 5,500 gp
