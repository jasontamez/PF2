Get Hunches Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 61 You can make a gut assessment of a social situation with a successful DC 20 Perception check. You get a feeling that indicates whether something is wrong or if a person is generally untrustworthy, though you don't receive specific information. To attempt to determine whether a particular statement is a lie, use the sense motive function. Action: 1 minute.
