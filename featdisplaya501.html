Extra Shadow Jump - Feats Extra Shadow Jump Source Blood of Shadows pg. 25 You have an improved ability to leap through shadow. Prerequisites: Shadow jump shadowdancer class feature. Benefit: Add 10 feet to the total distance you can jump each day using the shadow jump ability. The extra distance provided by this feat doubles whenever the distance that you can jump doubles (20 feet at 6th, 40 feet at 8th, and 80 feet at 10th level). Special: This feat can be selected up to four times. Its effects stack.
