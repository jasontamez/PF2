Archon's Aura - Spells Archon's Aura Source Ultimate Magic pg. 206 School evocation [good, lawful]; Level cleric 3, oracle 3, paladin 3, warpriest 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 20 ft. Area 20-ft. radius centered on you Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You gain a powerful aura, similar to an archon's aura of menace. Any hostile creature within a 20-foot radius of you must make a Will save to resist the effects of this aura. If the creature fails, it takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls and saving throws and to Armor Class for the duration of this spell, or until it successfully hits you with an attack. A creature that has resisted or broken the effect cannot be affected again by this particular casting of archon's aura.
