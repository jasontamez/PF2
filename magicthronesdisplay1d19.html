Throne of the Ardent Defense - Thrones - Magic Equipment Throne of the Ardent Defense Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 31 Aura moderate abjuration and enchantment CL 10th Slot none; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 350 lbs. Description This heavy throne is made of elaborate wrought iron and decorated with shields bearing the ruler's heraldry. A ruler seated upon a throne of ardent defense gains the throne's continuous heroism effect. Acknowledging a ruler sitting upon the throne grants the ability to cast shield other as a spell-like ability once in the next 24 hours. The spell ends if the subject of the spell moves more than 50 feet from either the caster or the throne. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, heroism, shield other; Cost 8,000 gp
