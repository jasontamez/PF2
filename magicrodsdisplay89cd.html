Rod of Giant Summoning (normal) - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Giant Summoning Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 20 Aura strong conjuration CL 17th Slot none; Price 3,000 gp (lesser), 11,000 gp (normal), 24,500 gp (greater); Weight 5 lbs. Description This iron rod has long metal flanges, like a mace, which widen as they expand from the handle. The wielder can use the rod up to three times per day to augment her casting of a conjuration (summoning) spell, causing all of her summoned creatures to gain the giant simple template. The lesser rod can be used with spells of 3rd level or lower, the standard rod can be used with spells of 6th level or lower, and the greater rod can be used with spells of 9th level or lower. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, Augment Summoning, summon monster III or summon nature's ally III; Cost 1,500 gp (lesser), 5,500 gp (normal), 12,250 gp (greater)
