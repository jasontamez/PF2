Litany of Weakness - Spells Litany of Weakness Source Ultimate Combat pg. 236 School necromancy [language-dependent]; Level antipaladin 1, inquisitor 1 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 round Saving Throw no; Spell Resistance yes Description Your litany proclaims your target weak, sapping its strength. The target is fatigued for 1 round. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.
