Knuckle axe - Weapons - Equipment Knuckle axe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 144 Statistics Cost 9 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special monk, performance Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Axes Description Knuckle axes, usually used in pairs, resemble axe blades mounted on brass knuckles. You may hold, but not wield, a weapon or other object in a hand wearing a knuckle axe. Unlike when wearing brass knuckles, you cannot use a hand wearing a knuckle axe for spellcasting.
