Archetypes Oceanrider Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 53 Oceanriders are underwater cavaliers who ride dolphins, orcas, or seahorses. Armor Proficiency: An oceanrider is proficient with light and medium armor, and with shields (but not tower shields) . This alters armor proficiency. Aquatic Mount: A Medium oceanrider can ride either a seahorse or an orca (unlike most orcas, this one starts at size Large with none of the other level 7 abilities until level 7) instead of the normal options, while a Small oceanrider can ride a dolphin. This alters mount and replaces expert trainer.
