Photokinetic Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Photokinetic Infusion Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 24 Element wood; Type substance infusion; Level 1; Burn 1 Prerequisite positive blast Associated Blasts positive Saving Throw none Your blast damages living creatures with light. This deals normal damage to undead creatures but also deals minimum damage (1 point of damage per d6, can't be increased by the 9th-level improvement to metakinesis) to living creatures. Undead creatures that are particularly vulnerable to sunlight, such as vampires, take 1 extra point of damage per die from the infused blast.
