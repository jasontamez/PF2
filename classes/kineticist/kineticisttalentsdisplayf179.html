Many Throw - Wild Talents - Kineticist Many Throw Source Occult Adventures pg. 21 Element aether; Type form infusion; Level 8; Burn 4 Prerequisite extended range Associated Blasts telekinesis Saving Throw none You grab a large number of objects and throw them all at once.
