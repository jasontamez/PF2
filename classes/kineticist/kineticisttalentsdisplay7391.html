Tengu Blade Lore - Wild Talents - Kineticist Tengu Blade Lore Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 26 Element universal; Type utility (Su); Level 1; Burn 0 Prerequisite tengu Whenever you use kinetic blade or an infusion that lists kinetic blade as a prerequisite, the weapon you create counts as having one of the following weapon special features of your choice: blocking, brace, deadly, disarm, distracting, sunder, or trip.
