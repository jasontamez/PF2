Unfolding Wind Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Unfolding Wind Infusion Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 30 Element air; Type substance infusion; Level 5; Burn 3 Prerequisite kinetic fist, member of the Monastery of Unfolding Wind Associated Blasts air, thunderstorm Saving Throw none Unfolding winds carry you along. Before your first attack and again after each unarmed strike with the infused blast, you can move 5 feet without provoking attacks of opportunity. You can use this infusion only while also using the kinetic fist form infusion.
