Slick Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Slick Infusion Source Psychic Anthology pg. 21 Element water; Type substance infusion; Level 2; Burn 2 Prerequisite slick Associated Blasts blizzard, charged water, cold, ice, water Saving Throw Reflex negates Your blast leaves behind slippery water or ice in its area for as long as the blast is in that area and for 1 round thereafter, making the area difficult terrain and increasing the DC of Acrobatics checks attempted in the area by 5. You can use this infusion only if you also use a form infusion that has an area of effect.
