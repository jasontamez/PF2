Telekinetic Deflection - Wild Talents - Kineticist Telekinetic Deflection Source Occult Adventures pg. 27 Element aether; Type utility (Sp); Level 8; Burn 0 You can deflect your foes' attacks, as the spell deflection with a duration of 1 round. You can accept 1 point of burn to increase the duration to 1 round per kineticist level you possess.
