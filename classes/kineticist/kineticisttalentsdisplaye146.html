Bolt - Wild Talents - Kineticist Bolt Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 26 Element air; Type form infusion; Level 3; Burn 2 Associated Blasts electric blast, thunderstorm blast Saving Throw Reflex half You unleash a bolt of lightning above an enemy within 30 feet and send it spiraling down with tremendous force. Each bolt is a 5-foot-wide, 30-foot-long, vertical bolt, dealing your full blast damage to creatures within the line. If all squares in the bolt's area are outdoors and in a storm, the blast deals an additional 1 point of damage per die. The saving throw is Dexterity-based.
