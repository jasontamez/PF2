Bowling Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Bowling Infusion Source Occult Adventures pg. 17 Element aether, earth; Type substance infusion; Level 2; Burn 2 Associated Blasts earth, magma, metal, mud, telekinetic Saving Throw none You bowl your foes over with the sheer mass of your kinetic blast. Attempt a trip combat maneuver check against each target damaged by your infused blast, using your Constitution modifier instead of your Strength modifier to determine your Combat Maneuver Bonus.
