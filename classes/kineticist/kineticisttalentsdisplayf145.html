Veil of Mists - Wild Talents - Kineticist Veil of Mists Source Occult Adventures pg. 28 Element water; Type utility (Su); Level 2; Burn 0 You create a misty veil over your features, disguising yourself as disguise self for a number of minutes equal to your Constitution modifier. If you accept 1 point of burn, until the next time your burn is removed, each use of this wild talent lasts until you dismiss it.
