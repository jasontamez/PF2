Synaptic Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Synaptic Infusion Source Psychic Anthology pg. 21 Element air; Type substance infusion; Level 3; Burn 2 Associated Blasts electric, charged water, thunderstorm Saving Throw Will negates Your blast sends strange electric signals through the target's body, scrambling its synapses and causing it to become staggered for 1 round. The target is aware it can choose to take a move action to remove the staggered condition inflicted by this infusion. This infusion is a mind-affecting effect.
