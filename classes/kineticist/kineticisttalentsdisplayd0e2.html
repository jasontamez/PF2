Flame Jet - Wild Talents - Kineticist Flame Jet Source Occult Adventures pg. 25 Element fire; Type utility (Sp); Level 3; Burn - You shoot a burst of flame behind you as a standard action, propelling you up to 60 feet in a straight line, including into the air; any movement upward costs double.
