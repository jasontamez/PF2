Telekinetic Globe - Wild Talents - Kineticist Telekinetic Globe Source Occult Adventures pg. 28 Element aether; Type utility (Sp); Level 8; Burn 0 Prerequisite force barrier, force ward You can create a globe of force, as the telekinetic sphere spell, that lasts as long as you concentrate as a standard action, and you can move the globe as a move action. You can accept 1 point of burn to increase the duration to 1 minute per level and eliminate the need to concentrate to maintain the globe.
