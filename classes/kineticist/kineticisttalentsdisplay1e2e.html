Unnerving Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Unnerving Infusion Source Elemental Master's Handbook pg. 22 Element void; Type substance infusion; Level 3; Burn 2 Associated Blasts negative, void Saving Throw Will negates Your kinetic blast sends the fear of oblivion into your foes. Whenever an infused blast deals negative energy damage to a living foe, it is shaken for 1 round. This shaken condition doesn't stack with itself.
