Air's Leap - Wild Talents - Kineticist Air's Leap Source Occult Adventures pg. 23 Element air; Type utility (Su); Level 1; Burn 0 You are always considered to have a running start when jumping, you add your kineticist level as a bonus on all Acrobatics checks to jump, you jump twice as far or high as the results of your check indicate, and you can accept 1 point of burn when jumping to double the distance you jump again (to a total of four times as far).
