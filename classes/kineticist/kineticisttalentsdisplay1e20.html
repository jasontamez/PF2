Snake - Wild Talents - Kineticist Snake Source Occult Adventures pg. 22 Element universal; Type form infusion; Level 3; Burn 2 Prerequisite extended range Associated Blasts any Saving Throw none You have fine control over your kinetic blast, allowing you to alter its path to avoid obstacles. You can trace out any path no more than 120 feet long for your blast, potentially allowing you to avoid cover (even total cover). You can choose a path that leads into squares you cannot see.
