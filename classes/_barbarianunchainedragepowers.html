Rage Powers - Barbarian (Unchained) Barbarian (Unchained) Rage Powers Description Source: Pathfinder Unchained As a barbarian gains levels, she learns to use her rage in new ways. At 2nd level and every 2 levels thereafter, the barbarian gains a rage power. A barbarian gains the benefits of rage powers only while raging. Some of these powers are always active during a rage, and others require the barbarian to take an action to use them.
