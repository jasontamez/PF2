Enchanting Words - Tricks - Mesmerist Enchanting Words(Blood of the Beast pg. 18): The mesmerist can trigger this trick whenever the subject attempts a Diplomacy or Intimidate check to adjust a creature's attitude. The subject can use the mesmerist's Charisma modifier instead of her own Charisma modifier on the check. If the subject uses Intimidate, the adjustment in attitude lasts an additional number of minutes equal to 10 ?? the mesmerist's Charisma modifier (minimum of 10 additional minutes).
