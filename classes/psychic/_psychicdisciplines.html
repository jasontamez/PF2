Disciplines - Psychic Psychic Disciplines Description Source: Occult Adventures The following disciplines represent those most commonly chosen by psychics. Followers of a discipline might come to it in a variety of different ways, and though some might join together in orders, others follow personal, singular interpretations of the discipline.
