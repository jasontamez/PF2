Liberation - Blessings - Warpriest Liberation Blessing Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 66 Deities: Desna. Liberation (minor): At 1st level, for 1 round as a swift action, you can ignore impediments to your mobility and effects that cause paralysis (as freedom of movement). You can activate this blessing even if you're otherwise unable to take actions, but not if you're unconscious. Freedom's Shout (major): At 10th level, as a swift action you can emit a 30-foot aura that affects all allies with the liberation blessing described above. This effect lasts for 1 round. Freedom Source Healer's Handbook pg. 13 Associated Blessing: Liberation Replacement Blessing: The following major blessing replaces the freedom's shout ability of the Liberation blessing. Greater Liberation (Major): At 10th level, when you use the liberation ability of the Liberation blessing, its effects last for 1 minute.
