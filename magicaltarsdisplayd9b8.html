Altar of Urgathoa - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Urgathoa Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 249 Aura moderate abjuration and necromancy [evil] CL 10th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 500 lbs. Description This altar is a large sculpted table or slab for ceremonial feasts. Praying at the altar grants a +2 circumstance bonus on saving throws against death effects, disease, energy drain, and negative energy effects; an undead worshiper instead gains 5 temporary hit points. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, death ward, false life, magic circle against good, creator must worship Urgathoa; Cost 4,000 gp
