Maple syrup (jar) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Maple syrup (jar) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 95 Price 1 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Food/Drink Description This sweet liquid comes from tapping, and partially draining, the sap of maple trees during the early spring. The sap is then boiled down into a syrup, though it is sometimes thickened further and then poured over snow to create a taffylike candy, known as snow candy.
