Shotel - Weapons - Equipment Shotel Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Adventurer's Armory pg. 5, Ultimate Combat pg. 144 Statistics Cost 30 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special performance Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy Description The shotel is a downward-curving sword designed to reach over or around an opponent's shield. You gain a +1 bonus on attack rolls with a shotel against opponents using bucklers, light shields, or heavy shields. The curve of a shotel's blade varies from sword to sword, and some models are double-edged, while others are single-edged, with the back edge able to be braced against a shield for additional power. Shotels are particularly useful for hooking mounted combatants and ripping them from their steeds.
