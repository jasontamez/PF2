Pauldrons of the Serpent - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pauldrons of the Serpent Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 267 Aura faint abjuration; CL 3rd Slot shoulders; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description Constructed of mithral, each of these pauldrons bears an embossed device of a viper coiled defensively. These pauldrons grant their wearer a +2 dodge bonus to AC against attacks of opportunity, and increase the wearer's CMD by 2 against bull rush, grapple, reposition, and trip combat maneuvers. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cat's grace; Cost 1,500 gp
