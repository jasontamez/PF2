Umbral Scion - Feats Umbral Scion Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 106, Pathfinder #15: The Armageddon Echo pg. 59, Pathfinder #18: Descent into Midnight pg. 14 New spell-like abilities are unlocked for you as you rise to ascendency among the drow people. Prerequisites: Cha 13, Wis 13, Drow Nobility, Greater Drow Nobility, Improved Drow Nobility, able to use drow spell-like abilities, drow. Benefit: Select one of the following: dispel magic, divine favor, or suggestion. You may use this spell once per day as a spell-like ability. Your caster level is equal to your character level.
