Yenchaburian hybrid - Miscellaneous - Equipment Yenchaburian hybrid Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 27 Price 1 gp; Weight — Category Food/Drink Description This bitter coffee cultivar increases the drinker's alertness to nearby threats. For 1 hour after drinking two cups of Yenchaburian hybrid coffee, you gain a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks to notice a creature that is using Stealth. You can benefit from yenchaburian hybrid coffee only once per day.
