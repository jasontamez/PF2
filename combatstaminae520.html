Vast Hatred - Combat Stamina - Feats Vast Hatred Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 135 Related Feat Vast Hatred You can spend 5 stamina points to designate a creature type (and subtype, if appropriate) from the Ranger Favored Enemies table (Core Rulebook 64). Your attack bonus from the hatred racial trait also applies to creatures of the selected type (and subtype, if appropriate) until the start of your next turn.
