Spells Ray of Exhaustion Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 330 School necromancy; Level arcanist 3, bloodrager 3, magus 3, mesmerist 3, sorcerer 3, spiritualist 3, witch 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a drop of sweat) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect ray Duration 1 min./level Saving Throw Fortitude partial; see text; Spell Resistance yes Description A black ray projects from your pointing finger. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to strike a target. The subject is immediately exhausted for the spell's duration. A successful Fortitude save means the creature is only fatigued. A character that is already fatigued instead becomes exhausted. This spell has no effect on a creature that is already exhausted. Unlike normal exhaustion or fatigue, the effect ends as soon as the spell's duration expires.
