Kitsune Star Gem (2nd) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Kitsune Star Gem Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 193 Aura faint transmutation; CL 1st (1st), 3rd (2nd), or 5th (3rd) Slot none; Price 750 gp (1st), 3,000 gp (2nd), 6,750 gp (3rd); Weight — Description This magical jewel glows like faerie fire, shedding light as a candle. It acts as pearl of power, except it only allows a kitsune to recover a spent use of a spell-like ability of the appropriate spell level.
