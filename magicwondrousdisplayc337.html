Boots of Earth and Wind - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of Earth and Wind Source Mythic Adventures pg. 147 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 9th Slot feet; Price 20,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description These strange, heavy, leather-and-steel boots feature a pair of ever-fluttering wings on each side. These boots function like winged boots, but also grant a +5 bonus to CMD against bull rush, overrun, and reposition combat maneuvers as long as the wearer is standing on solid ground. A mythic creature can expend one use of mythic power when commanding the boots to fly to gain the bonus to CMD for as long as she uses the boots to fly. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Mythic Crafter, entangle, fly; Cost 10,000 gp
