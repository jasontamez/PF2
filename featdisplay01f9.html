Horn Rider - Feats Horn Rider (Combat) Source Monster Codex pg. 164 You can defend yourself using the horn or tusk of a creature you're lashed to. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1. Benefit: You gain a +1 shield bonus to AC when lashed into a horn harness. Special: A creature with this feat can take the Mounted Archery feat without possessing 1 rank in the Ride skill or the Mounted Combat feat.
