Plants - Magic Equipment Sheltershrub Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 250 Aura faint abjuration CL 1st Slot none; Price 12,000 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description This leafy red or blue shrub grows to a height of 6 feet. It grows small, blue, overly sweet berries. The berries provide an effect identical to endure elements when consumed. If a berry is consumed before the effect of a previously consumed berry has worn off, it has no effect. A sheltershrub produces six berries per day during its two growing seasons, which are 13 weeks each in the summer and winter. A shrub has no more than 50 berries at a time. Construction Requirements Cultivate Magic Plant, endure elements, cultivator must have 1 rank in Knowledge (nature); Cost 6,000 gp
