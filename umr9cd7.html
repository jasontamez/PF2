Universal Monster Rules Rake (Ex) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 297, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 303, Bestiary 2 pg. 300, Bestiary 3 pg. 298, Bestiary 4 pg. 298, Bestiary 5 pg. 298 A creature with this special attack gains extra natural attacks under certain conditions, typically when it grapples its foe. In addition to the options available to all grapplers, a monster with the rake ability gains two free claw attacks that it can use only against a grappled foe. The bonus and damage caused by these attacks are included in the creature's description. A monster with the rake ability must begin its turn already grappling to use its rake-it can't begin a grapple and rake in the same turn. Format: rake (2 claws +8, 1d4+2) Location: Special Attacks.
