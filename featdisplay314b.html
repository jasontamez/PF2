Gruesome Slaughter - Feats Gruesome Slaughter (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 150 You can inspire extreme revulsion when you slaughter your enemies. Prerequisites: Intimidating Prowess, Killing Flourish, Intimidate 11 ranks, slayer level 11th. Benefit: Creatures you demoralize by using the Killing Flourish feat must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC = 10 + 1/2 your character level + the higher of your Strength or Dexterity modif ier) or become sickened for 1 minute. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 When using this feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the DC of the effect by an amount equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
