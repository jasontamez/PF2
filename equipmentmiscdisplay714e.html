False-bottomed chest - Miscellaneous - Equipment False-bottomed chest Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 52 gp; Weight 25 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description These chests are typically used by smugglers transporting contraband or those with treasures they would prefer to keep hidden. The secret compartment in this chest is approximately 1 inch deep. Different styles open from the inside, from the underside, or through the back. Detecting the compartment requires a DC 20 Perception check.
