Greatclub - Weapons - Equipment Greatclub Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 8 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special — Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Hammers; Tribal Description This larger, bulkier version of the common club is heavy enough that you can't wield it with one hand. It may be ornate and carved, reinforced with metal, or a simple branch from a tree. Like simple clubs, greatclubs have many names, such as cudgels, bludgeons, shillelaghs, and more.
