Seaborne - Weapons - Magic Equipment Seaborne Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 147 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. A seaborne weapon functions easily in watery environments. With the weapon in hand, the wielder gains a bonus on Swim checks equal to twice the weapon's enhancement bonus. In addition, the wielder does not take the normal penalties to attack and damage rolls imposed by being underwater, as if he were subject to the spell freedom of movement.
