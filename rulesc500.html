Elusive Haunt (CR +1 or +2) Source Horror Adventures pg. 172 A typical haunt can be harmed within the area of its manifestation, but an elusive haunt's source is in a separate location. An elusive haunt can be damaged only at its source, and can manifest far away from that source, up to 100 feet per point of CR. The elusive element typically increases a haunt's CR by 1. If the haunt is also persistent, the elusive property increases the haunt's CR by 2.
