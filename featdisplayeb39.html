Splintering Weapon - Feats Splintering Weapon Source Ultimate Combat pg. 120 Your fragile weapon works to your advantage, breaking off fragments in wounds you inflict. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, proficient with weapon, weapon made of primitive material. Benefit: Whenever you use a melee or thrown weapon with the fragile weapon feature (page 146) or similar quality and hit an opponent, you can give your weapon the broken condition to deal that opponent 1d4 points of bleed damage.
