Boots of Fiendish Escape (Tiefling) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of Fiendish Escape (Tiefling) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 229 Aura strong conjuration; CL 15th Slot feet; Price 45,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description The following wondrous item is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity. This pair of supple, leather-heeled boots can be worn comfortably whether the wearer has feet or hooves. The wearer can use fog cloud once per day by clicking the boot's heels together as a swift action. If the wearer is a tiefling, he can simultaneously teleport up to 1,000 feet as per dimension door. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Quicken Spell, dimension door, fog cloud, creator must be a tiefling; Cost 22,500 gp
