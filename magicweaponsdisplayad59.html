Spear of the Honored Ancestors - Weapons - Magic Equipment Spear of the Honored Ancestors Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 31 Aura moderate conjuration CL 9th Slot none; Price 15,302 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description When the wielder of this glimmering +1 ghost touch spear enters a rage, she can drum the spear against a shield or the ground as a swift action. If she does, colorful lights dance around the spear and the wielder can choose to gain the benefit of guidance, grant the touched shield the ghost touch special ability, or grant the spear the returning special ability. The chosen benefit lasts for the duration of the rage. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, guidance, faerie fire, plane shift; Price 7,802 gp
