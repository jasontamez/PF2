Emei piercer - Weapons - Equipment Emei piercer Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 130 Statistics Cost 3 gp Weight — Damage 1d2 (small), 1d3 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type P; Special see text Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Close; Monk Description This weapon is used to augment unarmed martial techniques. It consists of an 8- to 10-inch-long, dual-pointed steel spike set on swivels and mounted on a ring, so that it can be spun around at high speeds when slipped over the wielder's ring finger. The ring prevents the wielder from being disarmed and turns unarmed strikes into piercing attacks.
