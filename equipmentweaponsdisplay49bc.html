Bill - Weapons - Equipment Bill Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, Adventurer's Armory pg. 2, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176 Statistics Cost 11 gp Weight 11 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special brace, disarm, reach, see text Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Polearms Description The blade of this polearm is a curved or hooked chopping blade with a spiked projection on the back of the blade. The spike is commonly used to block and parry. When fighting defensively or with full defense, this weapon gives you a +1 shield bonus to AC. A mounted opponent hit by a bill takes a -1 penalty on his Ride check to stay mounted.
