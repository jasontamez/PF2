Shieldsplitter Lance - Weapons - Magic Equipment Shieldsplitter Lance Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 159, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 290 Aura moderate evocation CL 10th Slot none; Price 18,310 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This +1 keen lance has the ability to penetrate and destroy shields. Whenever its wielder uses it to successfully strike an opponent who carries a shield, the opponent's shield takes the same damage as the opponent. If the wielder uses the lance to make a sunder attack against a shield, it bypasses the shield's hardness. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, keen edge, shatter; Price 9,310 gp
