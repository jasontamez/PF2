Dragonbreath Bow - Weapons - Magic Equipment Dragonbreath Bow Source Mythic Adventures pg. 144 Aura strong evocation CL 14th Slot none; Price 50,375 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This +2 flaming burst longbow's arrow rest is shaped like a dragon's head with its mouth agape-as if about to unleash its breath weapon. A mythic wielder can expend one use of mythic power when drawing back the bow to create an arrow of pure flame. This flame arrow acts as a ranged touch attack that can target only creatures within the first range increment of the bow. When it hits its target, this flame arrow deals 3d10+2 points of fire damage. This damage stacks with the bow's flaming burst special ability. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Mythic Crafter, flame strike, scorching ray; Price 25,375 gp
