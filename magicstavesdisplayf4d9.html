Staff of Cackling Wrath - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Cackling Wrath Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 195, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 296 Aura moderate varies CL 9th Slot none; Price 56,700 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description The thorns embedded in this gnarled length of wood make it rather tricky for the unwary to grasp. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Blindness/deafness (1 charge)
  • Charm person (1 charge)
  • Hold person (1 charge)
  • Inflict moderate wounds (1 charge)
  • Baleful polymorph (2 charges)
  • Bestow curse (2 charges)
  • Vampiric touch (2 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, baleful polymorph, bestow curse, blindness/deafness, charm person, hold person, inflict moderate wounds, vampiric touch; Cost 28,450 gp
