Scorching Weapons - Combat Stamina - Feats Scorching Weapons Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130 Related Feat Scorching Weapons When you use this feat to make one or more metallic weapons red hot for 1 round, you can spend 2 stamina points to increase the additional fire damage to 2 points, and have that damage stack with other effects that add fire damage to a weapon (such as the flaming weapon special ability).
