Shatter Defenses - Combat Stamina - Feats Shatter Defenses Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 131 Related Feat Shatter Defenses When you hit an opponent that has been made flat-footed to your attacks by this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to make that opponent flat-footed to the attacks of one ally who is within 30 feet of that creature until the start of your next turn.
