Boarding axe - Weapons - Equipment Boarding axe Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 8, Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 19, Skull and Shackles Player's Guide pg. 6 Statistics Cost 6 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P or S; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Axes Description A boarding axe is similar to a handaxe, but features a spike that allows the weapon to do either slashing or piercing damage. When in hand, a boarding axe grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Climb checks to scale wooden or other penetrable surfaces, such as ice.
