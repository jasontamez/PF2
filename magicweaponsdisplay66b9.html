Fighter's Fork - Weapons - Magic Equipment Fighter's Fork Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 155 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot none; Price 7,315 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This steel-tined +1 trident can be commanded to lengthen or shorten its haft (or return to its normal length) as a swift action. It is a two-handed reach weapon when extended but cannot be thrown. If shortened, it can be wielded as a light weapon or hurled as a javelin. Feats or class abilities that apply to tridents apply to a fighter's fork in any form. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shrink item; Price 3,815 gp
