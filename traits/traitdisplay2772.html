Strength of Body - Traits Strength of Body Source Blood of the Moon pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Wereboar-Kin You took longer than most to learn control over your transforming abilities, but in that time you learned a trick to make the most of your strength. Once per day, you can use your Strength modifier in place of your Constitution modifier on a single Fortitude saving throw. You must announce that you are using this ability before you roll the saving throw.
