Shackles Seafarer - Traits Shackles Seafarer Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) The Shackles You're familiar with the treacherous coastlines, currents, and tides of the Shackles, and can use that knowledge anywhere. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (nature) and Knowledge (geography) checks while on the ocean. You also gain a +1 trait bonus on Swim checks, and Swim is always a class skill for you.
