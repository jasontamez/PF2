Scarred by Space Pirates - Traits Scarred by Space Pirates Source People of the Stars pg. 15 Category Basic (Combat) You were attacked by Vercite space pirates in your youth, inspiring you to find ways to deal with the alien interlopers should they return. You gain a +1 trait bonus on attack rolls against humanoids from beyond Golarion, including androids, kasathas, Triaxians, and Vercites, among others.
