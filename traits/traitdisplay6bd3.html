Unidentifiable Appeal - Traits Unidentifiable Appeal Source Blood of the Night pg. 21 Category Race Requirement(s) Dhampir-Ajibachana The unnatural symmetry of your face gives you an attractiveness that is at once captivating but hard to quantify. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Disguise checks and a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks made to influence those who would be attracted to you.
