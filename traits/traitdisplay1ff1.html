Humble Beginnings - Traits Humble Beginnings Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 27 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia You didn't have any intention of becoming an adventurer, but a single, unexpected confrontation changed everything. Choose one of the following: boot, bucket, frying pan, mug, rolling pin, spade, or stool. You are treated as having the Catch Off- Guard feat when wielding the chosen item. (These items should all be considered improvised melee weapons that deal 1d4 points of bludgeoning damage.)
