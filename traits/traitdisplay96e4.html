Following Breeze - Traits Following Breeze Source People of the Sands pg. 15 Category Race Requirement(s) Sylph You always seem to be chased by drafts of moving air, and know exactly the right moment and method to lift yourself upon the breezes. As long as you get a running start, you gain a +2 trait bonus on Acrobatics checks made to jump. Your speed increases by 5 feet when you use the run action.
