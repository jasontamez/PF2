Giant-Harried - Traits Giant-Harried Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Wastelands-Ash, Hill, River, Shadow You were robbed by giants or nearly killed by a giant's prank, and the experience gave you a keen instinct for outmaneuvering bigger creatures. You gain a +1 trait bonus on combat maneuver checks against creatures larger than you and a +1 trait bonus to CMD against such creatures. Likely Regions: Geb, Mana Wastes, Nex, Nidal, Numeria.
