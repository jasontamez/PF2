Blackjacket Weapon Flair - Traits Blackjacket Weapon Flair Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 4 Category Basic (Combat) You trained with the Blackjackets of Druma to make optimal use of magic weapons. Whenever you roll a natural 20 (the die shows a -20-) on a combat maneuver check with a magic weapon, you gain a +5 trait bonus on the check.
