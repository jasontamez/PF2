Vexing Defender - Traits Vexing Defender Source Giantslayer Player's Guide pg. 7, Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 31 Category Campaign You're trained in fighting opponents that are larger than you, and you are skilled at keeping them on their toes, pestering them from all directions. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Acrobatics checks, and Acrobatics becomes a class skill for you. In addition, you gain a +4 trait bonus on Acrobatics checks to move through an enemy's space without provoking an attack of opportunity, provided that enemy is larger than you.
