Jinx Eater - Traits Jinx Eater Source Blood of the Beast pg. 3 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Tengu native to the Shackles Your presence dampens curses and other ill effects. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saves against curses, including the hex class ability of shamans and witches. Once per day, you can grant this bonus to all allies within 30 feet for 1 round as an immediate action.
