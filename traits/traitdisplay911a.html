Folgrit's Mercy - Traits Folgrit's Mercy Source Divine Anthology pg. 17 Category Religion Requirement(s) Folgrit You were called upon to defend your home or family at a vulnerable time and led with a clarity of mind you did not know you had. Once per day when you use lay on hands, you also cure the target of 1d4 points of Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma damage. You choose which ability score gains the healing effect.
