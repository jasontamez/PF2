Merchant's Child - Traits Merchant's Child Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 10, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Katapesh One of your close relatives was a gifted merchant in the bustling bazaars of Katapesh, and taught you early in life how to see the innate value in any object. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise checks, and Appraise is always a class skill for you.
