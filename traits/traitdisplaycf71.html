Charau-Ka Slayer - Traits Charau-Ka Slayer Source Demon Hunter's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Mwangi Expanse As a native of the Mwangi Jungle, you've had frequent encounters with the charau-ka and learned how to fight against these coldly calculating demon worshipers. You gain a +1 trait bonus to AC against attacks made with thrown weapons.
