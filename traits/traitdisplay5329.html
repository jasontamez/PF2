Ancient Historian - Traits Ancient Historian Source PFS Guide to Organized Play 6.0 pg. 16 Category Faction You are well acquainted with the lore of fallen empires. Choose either Knowledge (history) or Linguistics. That skill becomes a class skill for you, and you begin play able to speak and read one of the following languages: Ancient Osiriani, Azlanti, Cyclops, Jistkan, Tekritanin, or Thassilonian.
