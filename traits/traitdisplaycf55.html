Dog-Sniff-Hate - Traits Dog-Sniff-Hate Source Goblins of Golarion pg. 16 Category Race Requirement(s) Goblin You gain the scent special quality (Bestiary 304) but only against canine creatures, like dogs or wolves, including monstrous hounds such as hell hounds, yeth hounds, and even werewolves. You gain a +2 trait bonus on attack rolls against dogs you can smell.
