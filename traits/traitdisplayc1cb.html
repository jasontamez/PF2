Animal Friend - Traits Animal Friend Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 7, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Gnome You've long been a friend to animals, and feel safer when animals are nearby. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Will saving throws as long as an animal (Tiny or larger, must be at least indifferent toward you) is within 30 feet, and Handle Animal is always a class skill for you.
