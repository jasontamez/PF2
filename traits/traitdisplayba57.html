Crusader Road Acolyte - Traits Crusader Road Acolyte Source People of the River pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Thornkeep or Echo Wood You trained with the Hellknights at Fort Inevitable or the paladins of Iomedae at Fort Riverwatch. If you trained with the Hellknights, you gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against chaotic spells and the spell-like abilities of chaotic outsiders. If you trained with the Iomedaean Paladins, you gain this bonus against evil spells and evil outsiders instead.
