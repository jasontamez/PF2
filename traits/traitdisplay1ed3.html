Eldritch Delver - Traits Eldritch Delver Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 57 Category Basic (Magic) You have an unquenchable thirst for knowledge about the world and desire to obtain it firsthand. You gain a +1 trait bonus on all Knowledge (dungeoneering) and Knowledge (history) checks, and you may treat your caster level as 1 level higher for all conjuration spells of the teleportation subschool.
