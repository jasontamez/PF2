Witch-Tongued - Traits Witch-Tongued Source Blood of the Coven pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Irrisen Hailing from frozen Irrisen, where witches rule the land, you deal with others in a cool, calculated way. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks, and you gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks against non-witches.
