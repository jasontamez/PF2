Sargavan Guard - Traits Sargavan Guard Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Sargava You served in the Sargavan Guard, either as a colonial sub-praetor or as a native Mwangi regular, and have grown accustomed to marching in hot temperatures while wearing armor. When you wear armor of any sort, reduce the armor check penalty by 1, to a minimum penalty of 0.
