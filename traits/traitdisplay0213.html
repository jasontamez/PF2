Erastil's Speaker - Traits Erastil's Speaker Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 219, Faiths of Purity pg. 9 Category Religion Requirement(s) Erastil You understand the importance of keeping the peace in your community, and you have learned how to speak to the faithful in ways that they understand. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks, and Diplomacy is a class skill for you.
