Boarded in the Shackles - Traits Boarded in the Shackles Source Serpent's Skull Player's Guide pg. 11 Category Campaign Your last home was in the Shackles, a dangerous land of pirates and treachery, but also of fantastic opportunity. A life of weal and woe on the sea and amid the pirate controlled lands has toughened you to a variety of hardships. Pick one of the three categories of saving throws-you gain a +1 trait bonus on all saving throws of that type.
