Cavern Survivor - Traits Cavern Survivor Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Nar-Voth You've regularly traversed Nar-Voth and know how to subsist in that lightless realm better than most surface dwellers. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Survival checks to survive in a subterranean wilderness, and Survival is a class skill for you.
