Agent of Chance - Traits Agent of Chance Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 218, Faiths of Purity pg. 20 Category Religion Requirement(s) Nivi Rhombodazzle You can change the fortunes of others, typically for the better. Once per day, you can allow an adjacent ally to reroll a skill check as an immediate action before the result is revealed. The ally must take the second roll, even if it is worse.
