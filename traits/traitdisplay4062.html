Vermin Wrangler - Traits Vermin Wrangler Source People of the Sands pg. 14 Category Race Requirement(s) Gnome In gathering ingredients necessary for mind-altering drugs and other concoctions, you have become intimately familiar with the desert's many-legged pests. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks to identify vermin and on saving throws against vermin's extraordinary abilities.
