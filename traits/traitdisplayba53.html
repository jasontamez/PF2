Shadow Dealer - Traits Shadow Dealer Source Blood of the Coven pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Ustalav You have survived and thrived in the dark forests of Ustalav by knowing how to make deals with terrible things. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against fear effects and a +1 trait bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy skills checks against evil fey, monstrous humanoids, and undead.
