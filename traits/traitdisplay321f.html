Chelish Guerrilla - Traits Chelish Guerrilla Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) Cheliax In overthrowing House Thrune, you've spent many years training to face the diabolist nobles and the devils they bind. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge checks to identify devils (and their special powers and weaknesses) and a +1 trait bonus on weapon damage rolls against devils.
