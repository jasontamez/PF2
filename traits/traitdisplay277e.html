Thoughtful Wish-Maker - Traits Thoughtful Wish-Maker Source Blood of the Elements pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) Plane of Fire You are well acquainted with the many ways words can be twisted. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks. Furthermore, if you succeed at a DC 25 Sense Motive check prior to making any wish granted by an outsider, you become aware of your wish's potential pitfalls. If you succeed at this check by 5 or more, you figure out how to word your wish in such a way that your words are not twisted.
