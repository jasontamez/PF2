Bountiful Herb-Lore - Traits Bountiful Herb-Lore Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 3 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Cassomir Once per day after 10 minutes of foraging, you can sprinkle nutritious herbs into a magical consumable item (such as a potion of cure light wounds). If the item is consumed within 1 minute, it provides nourishment as if it were a normal meal to the creature that partakes of it.
