Merchant of Katheer - Traits Merchant of Katheer Source Qadira, Gateway to the East pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Qadira You grew up among the market stalls of the greatest trading post in all the world, and can almost smell the value of an item before you touch it. You gain a +1 trait bonus to Appraise checks, and Appraise is always a class skill for you.
