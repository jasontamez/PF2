Unwelcome Business - Traits Unwelcome Business Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 2 Category Basic (Social) You specialize in delivering or procuring shipments where those commodities are not allowed or where your identity would elicit negative bias. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Disguise or Sleight of Hand checks, and that skill becomes a class skill for you. In addition, choose one humanoid species; you ignore the -2 penalty on Disguise checks to disguise yourself as a member of that species.
