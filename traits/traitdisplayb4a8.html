Sun Orchid Harvester - Traits Sun Orchid Harvester Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 12 Category Region Requirement(s) Thuvia You were employed brief ly by Deyrubrujuan's alchemist cabal to harvest sun orchids deep in the Thuvian desert. Though you never worked your way deeper into the cabal, you retain the knowledge gained during your employment. You gain a +1 bonus on Knowledge (nature) and Survival checks made in desert environments.
