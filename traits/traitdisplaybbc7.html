Disciplined Body - Traits Disciplined Body Source Inner Sea Races pg. 196 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Vudrani You respect your mind's mastery over your body's full potential. Once per day, you can use your Wisdom modifier instead of either your Strength or Dexterity modifier on an attack roll, a combat maneuver check, a Strength or Dexterity-based skill check, a Strength check, a Dexterity check, or a Reflex save. You must choose to use this trait before rolling.
