Triaxian Tradition - Traits Triaxian Tradition Source People of the Stars pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) Triaxus Your experience with one of the long Triaxian seasons has taught you much about the dangers of a long summer or winter. Choose disease or cold. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saves against spells and spell-like abilities with the chosen descriptor. If you choose disease, you gain the bonus on Fortitude saves against nonmagical diseases as well.
