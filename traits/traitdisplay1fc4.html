Jadwiga Scion - Traits Jadwiga Scion Source People of the North pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Jadwiga You come from a family that commands respect. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks, and Intimidate is always a class skill for you. In addition, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (nobility) checks related to the Irrisen aristocracy.
