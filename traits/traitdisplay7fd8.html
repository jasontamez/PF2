Brute - Traits Brute Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 14, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Half-Orc You have worked for a crime lord, either as a lowlevel enforcer or as a guard, and are adept at frightening away people. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks, and Intimidate is always a class skill for you.
