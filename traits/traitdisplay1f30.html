Egorian School Apprentice - Traits Egorian School Apprentice Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Egorian You studied under one of the masters of the Egorian School style of architecture. You gain a +3 trait bonus on Knowledge (engineering) checks and a +2 trait bonus on Perception checks to find secret doors or traps in buildings built in the Egorian School style.
