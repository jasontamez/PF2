Friends in Low Places - Traits Friends in Low Places Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 25 Category Basic (Social) Outcasts know their own, and that knowledge opens doors and loosens tongues. Gathering information in lower quarters, such as vice dens and poorer districts, takes you 1d2 hours (instead of 1d4 hours). In addition, the attitudes of any destitute or impoverished NPCs you interact with begin one step closer to helpful.
