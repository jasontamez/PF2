Evident Supporter - Traits Evident Supporter Source Champions of Balance pg. 12 Category Region Requirement(s) Galt You know how best to appear supportive of the current regime despite the vagaries of a shifting government. The Sense Motive DC needed for a city guard, a politician, or other governmental functionary in a large town or larger settlement to gain a hunch about your trustworthiness is 25 (instead of 20). On a failed check, you're seen as trustworthy regardless of the situation.
