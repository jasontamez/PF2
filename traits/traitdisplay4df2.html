St. Clydwell's Ward - Traits St. Clydwell's Ward Source Faction Guide pg. 62 Category Basic (Faith) Requirement(s) Mendev Crusaders You have learned the secrets of an ancient prayer that wards off the attacks of demons. As a standard action, you can recite a holy chant that affects demons as if you were under the effect of a sanctuary spell (caster level equal to 1/2 your Hit Dice + your Charisma modifier).
