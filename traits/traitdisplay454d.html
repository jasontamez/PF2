Spirit of the Law - Traits Spirit of the Law Source Antihero's Handbook pg. 7 Category Basic (Combat) You have seen the rules of society endlessly twisted in their implementation, and as such, you are especially wary of those who exploit the law to impose their own will. You gain a +1 trait bonus on attacks against lawful-aligned humanoids and a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against effects with the lawful descriptor.
