Numerological Gift - Traits Numerological Gift Source Blood of the Night pg. 20 Category Race Requirement(s) Dhampir-Ru-Shi Since birth you have had an intimate connection with a certain number. When you select this trait, roll 3d6. The resulting number becomes your numerological totem and can never be changed. Once per day, when you roll your totem number on a d20 (such as an attack roll, save, or skill check), you may treat that roll as if you had rolled a natural 20 on the die.
