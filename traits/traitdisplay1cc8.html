Accustomed to Trickery - Traits Accustomed to Trickery Source Legacy of the First World pg. 2 Category Region Requirement(s) Darkmoon Wood You have been exposed to fey magic and know tricks to avoid it. Once per day, when you fail a saving throw against a fey's spell-like or supernatural ability, you may reroll the save. You must take the second result, even if it is worse.
