Questing Hellknight - Traits Questing Hellknight Source Path of the Hellknight pg. 56 Category Basic (Social) Requirement(s) Lawful alignment You are a quick study when it comes to local laws. Once per day, you can attempt a Knowledge (local) check untrained to recall information about local laws, rulers, and popular locations. Additionally, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (local) checks, and Knowledge (local) is a class skill for you. You must be of a lawful alignment to take this trait.
