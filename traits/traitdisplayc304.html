Valashmai Veteran - Traits Valashmai Veteran Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Valashmai Jungle You have traveled to the Valashmai Jungle on numerous occasions, and your prowess in traversing the jungle wilderness makes you a formidable guide and explorer. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Perception checks, and a +1 trait bonus on Survival checks in jungle terrain. One of these skills becomes a class skill for you.
