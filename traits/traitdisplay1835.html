Brastlewark Businessman - Traits Brastlewark Businessman Source Gnomes of Golarion pg. 15, Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 19 Category Basic (Social) Requirement(s) Gnome Your time among the gnome elite of Brastlewark in Cheliax has led to an extensive knowledge of alchemy and ready connections in the business. You gain a +2 trait bonus on all Craft (alchemy) checks.
