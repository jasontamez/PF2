Iron Control - Traits Iron Control Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 10 Category Race Requirement(s) Half-Orc-Human-Raised You learned the hard way that expressing the rage you inherited from your orc blood exacerbates humans' fearful and hostile reactions to you; after years of effort, you've mastered self-control and buried your anger deep. Whenever you're confused and roll to determine behavior in a given round, subtract 10 from the result.
