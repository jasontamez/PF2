Transitional - Traits Transitional Source People of the Stars pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Triaxian As a Transitional Triaxian, you are attuned to the weather no matter what world you stand upon. Once per day when you attempt a Survival check to predict the weather, you can add a trait bonus equal to 3 + 1/2 your character level. In addition, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Fortitude saves against the effects of cold and hot weather.
