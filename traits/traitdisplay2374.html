Osirionologist - Traits Osirionologist Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 16 Category Region Requirement(s) Osirion You have studied the history of ancient Osirion, and may have even ventured inside one of the great pyramids. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (engineering) and Knowledge (history) checks, and one of them is always a class skill for you. In addition, you may choose Ancient Osiriani as one of your bonus languages.
