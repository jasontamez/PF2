Hill Fighter - Traits Hill Fighter Source Sargava, the Lost Colony pg. 23 Category Basic (Combat) Requirement(s) Sargavan You are used to fighting in hilly environments (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 427-428). You may run or charge downhill on a steep slope (moving up to your base speed) without making an Acrobatics check to avoid stumbling-but if you travel farther than this distance, the normal rules for steep slopes apply.
