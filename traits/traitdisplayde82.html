Fixer of Odds - Traits Fixer of Odds Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 29 Category Campaign You're going to win the Golden Goblin's upcoming "Cheat the Devil and Take His Gold" tournament one way or the other. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Profession (gambling) checks, and creatures searching you for objects hidden on your body don't gain the usual +4 bonus on their Perception checks. Additionally, you begin play with a "fixed" six-sided die (it has two -6s' and no -1-) and a dagger.
