House of Green Mothers Pupil - Traits House of Green Mothers Pupil Source Distant Shores pg. 23 Category Basic (Magic) You studied at Anuli's center for druidic magic long enough to begin bonding with a familiar. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Handle Animal checks. You may substitute this trait for Iron Will as the prerequisite for the Familiar Bond feat (Pathfinder Player Companion: Familiar Folio).
