Starlit Hexer - Traits Starlit Hexer Source People of the Stars pg. 22 Category Religion Requirement(s) any Outer God Your spellcasting powers are infused with the powers of the occult Outer Gods. Whenever you cast a spell or spell-like ability with the curse descriptor (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 251), increase the DC of the spell's saving throw by 1. If you cast such a spell or spell-like ability at night, as long as the sky is clear and the stars are visible, treat your caster level as 1 higher when determining the spell's duration.
