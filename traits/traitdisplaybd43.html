Furious Vengeance - Traits Furious Vengeance Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 220 Category Religion Requirement(s) Dranngvit Once wronged, your vengeance can be swift and brutal. Once per day as an immediate action, you gain a +1 trait bonus on a single attack roll. In addition, if the target has damaged you within the last round, you deal 1 additional point of damage on a successful hit. You must choose to use this ability before making the attack roll.
