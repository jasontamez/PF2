Expert Duelist - Traits Expert Duelist Source PFS Guide to Organized Play 6.0 pg. 17 Category Faction In your youth, you spent countless hours perfecting the art of the duel, focusing your efforts on defeating a single foe. You gain a +1 trait bonus to your Armor Class so long as you are adjacent to a single foe. This trait bonus is not applied to your Armor Class for touch attacks or when you are denied your Dexterity bonus.
