Strong Arm, Supple Wrist - Traits Strong Arm, Supple Wrist Source Qadira, Jewel of the East pg. 15, Qadira, Gateway to the East pg. 9 Category Basic (Combat) You have been trained in the javelin and the spear, and you know how to make the best use of your momentum. When you move at least 10 feet before making an attack with a thrown weapon, you can add 10 feet to the range increment of the weapon thrown. You can gain this benefit only once per round.
