Call of the Longships - Traits Call of the Longships Source People of the North pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Lands of the Linnorm Kings Your blood sings with longing to relive the adventures of one of your ancestors, and you are inexorably drawn to the sea. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Profession (sailor) checks and a +1 trait bonus on attack rolls made onboard ships.
