Lycanthropic Bloodlust - Traits Lycanthropic Bloodlust Source Blood of the Moon pg. 25 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Werewolf-Kin When your lycanthropic heritage awakened, you thrilled at any excuse for violence. Although you might have learned to focus or suppress this urge, it still gives you energy in a fight. Once per day, when you deal damage to a foe with a natural attack, you may gain 1d6 temporary hit points as an immediate action. These temporary hit points last for 1 minute.
