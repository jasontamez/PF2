Anxious - Traits Anxious Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 22 Category Drawback After suffering terribly for not being tightlipped enough as a child, such as when you accidentally exposed your family to enemy inquisitors, you developed a habit of being overly cautious with your words. You take a -2 penalty on Diplomacy checks and must speak slowly due to the concentration required. Unless stated otherwise, you are assumed to not be speaking at a volume above a whisper.
