Ustalavic Noble - Traits Ustalavic Noble Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 22 Category Region Requirement(s) Ustalav You come from an aristocratic family, though your family has long since lost any power or prestige it once had. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Knowledge (nobility) checks. In addition, your starting money is increased by 100 gp- your "birthright," such as it is.
