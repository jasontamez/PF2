Swamp Survivor - Traits Swamp Survivor Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Ustalav Leaving Graidmere Swamp is forbidden, and to flee the mire is to court Seryzilian's wrath. However, you are one of the few to successfully make it out of the swamp alive. Now, you're resolved to liberate your kinfolk. You gain a +2 bonus on Survival checks to find fresh water, and magical liquids in your possession gain a +2 bonus on Will saves to avoid being corrupted or otherwise spoiled.
