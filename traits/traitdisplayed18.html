Szaezan Crags Miner - Traits Szaezan Crags Miner Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 8 Category Region Requirement(s) Bachuan You were a miner of the nowscarce ores of the Szaezan Crags, and have become adept at identifying minerals and gems. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise checks, and Appraise becomes a class skill for you.
