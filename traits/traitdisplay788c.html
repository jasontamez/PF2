Muscle of the Society - Traits Muscle of the Society Source Pathfinder Society Primer pg. 15 Category Basic (Combat) Your intense training with the Swords has taught you how to infiltrate otherwise inaccessible ruins and extract their treasures without too much exertion. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Strength checks made to break doors and lift portcullises, and you treat your Strength score as 2 higher for the purpose of determining your carrying capacity.
