Industrious - Traits Industrious Source People of the River pg. 21 Category Race Requirement(s) Gnome You are helpful and hardworking. When you successfully use the aid another action to aid an ally's Craft or Profession check, that character gains an additional +1 trait bonus on the check (in addition to the +2 bonus normally granted for successfully aiding another). If you have a racial bonus on that skill, your ally gains a +2 trait bonus instead.
