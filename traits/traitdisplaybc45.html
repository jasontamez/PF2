Shared Ancestors - Traits Shared Ancestors Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 11 Category Religion Requirement(s) Myr Through your detailed study of your own lineage, you can better understand and honor even your most distant relatives. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy, Knowledge (history), Knowledge (local), Knowledge (nobility), and Sense Motive checks associated with any creature who shares your type and at least one of your subtypes. One of these skills becomes a class skill.
