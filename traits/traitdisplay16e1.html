Debt to a Witch - Traits Debt to a Witch Source Healer's Handbook pg. 16 Category Region Requirement(s) Must hail from Irrisen or have lived there for at least 1 year. Your life was spared in youth by the magic of a White Witch healer who was using your survival to perpetuate her agenda. Her magic was painful, but it made your body stronger. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against spells with the cold descriptor and against the dangers of cold weather.
