Hermean Paragon - Traits Hermean Paragon Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 20 Category Region Requirement(s) The Steaming Sea You are a product of Hermea's breeding programs-either your parents were chosen to be citizens, or you were, but later failed to live up to the island's high standards. Whatever the case, you are quicker than normal members of your race, and gain a +2 trait bonus on Initiative checks.
