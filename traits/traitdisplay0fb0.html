Unintentional Linguist - Traits Unintentional Linguist Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 61 Category Basic (Social) You can speak with outsiders. You gain a +1 trait bonus on all Linguistics checks, and you begin play knowing one of the following languages (in addition to those granted by your race and Intelligence modifier): Abyssal, Aquan, Celestial, Ignan, Infernal, Protean, or Terran.
