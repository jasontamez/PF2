Precision Hearing - Traits Precision Hearing Source Blood of the Moon pg. 9 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Werebat-Kin You have long relied on your enhanced hearing to warn you of those who would persecute you for your nature. Whenever you succeed at a Perception check to hear a creature you can't see and your check exceeds the DC by 10 or more, you can pinpoint the exact square where the sound originated.
