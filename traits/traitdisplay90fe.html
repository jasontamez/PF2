Affinity for the Elements - Traits Affinity for the Elements Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 218, Faiths of Corruption pg. 19 Category Religion Requirement(s) any elemental lord Add 1 to the DC of saving throws made to resist the effects of spells cast by you that have either the air, earth, fire, or water descriptor, depending on which elemental lord you worship. You must choose a single elemental lord when you take this trait; once chosen, it cannot be changed.
