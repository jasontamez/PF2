Roof Racer - Traits Roof Racer Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia-Korvosa You've spent more than your fair share of time among the strange sloping slums that cover the rooftops of much of Old Korvosa. When using the Acrobatics skill to make a long jump, you treat the DC of the skill check as if the distance being jumped were 5 feet shorter than it actually is.
