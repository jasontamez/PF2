Havoc of the Society - Traits Havoc of the Society Source Shattered Star Player's Guide pg. 5, Faction Guide pg. 63 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Sorcerer, Pathfinder Society Through countless hours studying vast libraries of arcane power, you have learned how to cause greater damage to your foes. Whenever you cast a spell that deals damage, you gain a +1 trait bonus to the total damage dealt. This damage is considered force damage regardless of the type of damage dealt by the spell.
