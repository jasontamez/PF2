Poverty-Stricken - Traits Poverty-Stricken Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 61, Second Darkness Player's Guide pg. 14, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Basic (Social) Your childhood was tough, and your parents always had to make every copper piece count. Hunger was your constant companion, and you often had to live off the land or sleep in the wild. You gain a +1 bonus on Survival checks, and Survival is always a class skill for you.
