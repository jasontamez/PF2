Calistrian Courtesan - Traits Calistrian Courtesan Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 63, Elves of Golarion pg. 15, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Calistria You worked in one of Calistria's temples as a sacred courtesan, and you know how to flatter, please, and (most of all) listen. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks and Diplomacy checks to gather information, and one of these skills (your choice) is always a class skill for you.
