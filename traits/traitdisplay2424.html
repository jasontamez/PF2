Elemental Ties - Traits Elemental Ties Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) Osirion Interactions with elementals are commonplace for you. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks when dealing with creatures of the elemental type, and you begin play knowing one of the following languages as an additional bonus language: Aquan, Auran, Ignan, or Terran.
