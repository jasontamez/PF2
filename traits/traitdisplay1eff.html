Eldritch Smith - Traits Eldritch Smith Source Dwarves of Golarion pg. 11 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Dwarf You are learned in the secret lore of the forge, rituals handed down for generations that some say come from Torag himself. Whenever you use the Craft skill or a crafting feat to make a stone or metal item, you reduce the cost of making the item by 5%. This includes metal-headed weapons with nonmetal parts, such as axes and spears.
