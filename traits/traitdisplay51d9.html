Mystery Initiate Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 19 Category Basic (Faith) You were initiated into a mystery cult in your youth, and learned secrets that turned your understanding of your faith and the world on its head. This experience has given you a knack for lateral thinking. Once per day, you may reroll any Knowledge skill check. You must decide to use this ability after the first roll is made but before the results are revealed. You must take the second result, even if it is worse. Suggested Characters: Mystery cultists, Nethys worshipers, Norgorber worshipers.
