Caravan Drover - Traits Caravan Drover Source People of the Sands pg. 14 Category Race Requirement(s) Half-Orc With your hand on the lash, your caravans always arrive on time. You treat whips as martial weapons, and up to 10 animals and humanoids traveling with you can hustle or make a forced march for 1 hour longer than normal before taking nonlethal damage and becoming fatigued.
