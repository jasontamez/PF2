Carpenden Lobber - Traits Carpenden Lobber Source Andoran, Spirit of Liberty pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Carpenden [in Andoran] Your regular participation in the moonmelon festival has made you wickedly effective at hurling harmless objects. You gain a +2 trait bonus on attack rolls when throwing items that do not deal normal hit point damage (such as weapons that deal nonlethal damage or items such as tanglefoot bags and thunderstones).
