Rebuke the Curse - Traits Rebuke the Curse Source Blood of the Coven pg. 3 Category Basic (Faith) Once per day, you can attempt to suppress the effects of a curse on a single creature with a touch. This functions as a remove curse spell, treating your character level as your caster level. If you succeed at your caster level check against the curse, the curse's effects are negated for 1 minute. This trait does not allow a person burdened with a cursed item to remove or discard it. Use of this trait is a supernatural ability.
