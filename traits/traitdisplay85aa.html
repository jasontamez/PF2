Forlorn - Traits Forlorn Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 8, Elves of Golarion pg. 15, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Elf Having lived outside of traditional elf society for much or all of your life, you know the world can be cruel, dangerous, and unforgiving of the weak. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Fortitude saving throws.
