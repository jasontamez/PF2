Aballonian Resilience - Traits Aballonian Resilience Source People of the Stars pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Aballon You spent at least part of your youth in Horsethroat and are acclimated to the frigid cold of Aballon's Ice Wells. You gain a +4 trait bonus on saves to avoid nonlethal damage from cold environments. In addition, you begin play with a cold-weather outfit at no cost.
