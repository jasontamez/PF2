Educated Druggist - Traits Educated Druggist Source Healer's Handbook pg. 10 Category Region Requirement(s) Alchemist healers who hail from Osibu, Thuvia, Tian Xia, or Utstalav, or who have lived there for at least 1 year Any alchemical remedies you administer that provide an alchemical bonus have that bonus increased by 1. These remedies also restore 1 hit point to the target in addition to their usual effects.
