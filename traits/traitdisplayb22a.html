Thuvian Merchant - Traits Thuvian Merchant Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Thuvia You have traveled the great circular trade route around Thuvia, following the sale of the sun orchid elixir from city to city, and are used to haggling with foreigners. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise, Bluff, and Sense Motive checks made for business deals and transactions.
