Market Rat - Traits Market Rat Source Humans of Golarion pg. 30 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Keleshite You spent your youth amid the varied market stalls and mercantile leagues across Golarion. Perhaps you came from a family of merchants, or you dwelt amid the many booths of a market, begging to survive. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise and Knowledge (local) checks.
