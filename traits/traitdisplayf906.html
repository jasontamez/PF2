Screaming Leap - Traits Screaming Leap Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Realm of the Mammoth Lords The rocky foothills of your homeland are ideal for staging leaping ambushes that leave your victims battered and routed. You gain a +1 trait bonus on melee damage rolls when you make a charge attack from higher ground. Once per day, when you make a charge attack from higher ground, you can use the Intimidate skill to demoralize your target as a swift action.
