Earthbound - Traits Earthbound Source Dwarves of Golarion pg. 11 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Dwarf Your druid spells use a handful of earth or sand as a divine focus in addition to the normal components. While you are touching the ground, you add a +2 trait bonus to saving throw DCs and on caster level checks to overcome spell resistance for all spells you cast against creatures with the air subtype.
