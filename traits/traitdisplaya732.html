Varisian Tattoo - Traits Varisian Tattoo Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 8 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Varisian You bear the elaborate tattoos of your people, marking you as a free son or daughter of the road. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against charm and compulsion effects. Additionally, you are proficient with bladed scarves and starknives.
