Lessons of Chaldira - Traits Lessons of Chaldira Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 220, Faiths of Purity pg. 20 Category Religion Requirement(s) Chaldira Zuzaristan Your studies of Chaldira Zuzaristan's exploits have given you a knack for avoiding trouble. Once per day before the result of a saving throw is known, you can reroll that saving throw. You must take the second result even if it is worse.
