Magic Is Life - Traits Magic Is Life Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 9, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Nethys Your faith in magic allows you to ref lexively use the energy of any spell effect currently on you to save you from death. As long as you are under the effects of a spell, you gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against death effects. If you are reduced to negative hit points while you are under the effects of any spell, you automatically confirm stabilization checks to stop bleeding.
