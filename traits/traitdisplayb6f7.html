Foul Belch - Traits Foul Belch Source Goblins of Golarion pg. 16 Category Race Requirement(s) Goblin Once per day as a standard action, you can force a particularly odious belch at a single opponent within 5 feet. The character affected must make a DC 12 Fortitude save or be sickened for 1d6 rounds. The save DC is Constitution-based.
