Legalistic Liberation - Traits Legalistic Liberation Source Halflings of Golarion pg. 30 Category Region Requirement(s) [Halfling] Molthune, Osirion You followed-or some might say exploited-the rules and earned your freedom by finding the loopholes within your homeland's labyrinthine codes regulating slavery and indentured servitude. You gain a +1 bonus on all Knowledge checks to find a legitimate way to avoid, negate, or escape an existing obligation.
