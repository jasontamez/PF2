Adherent of Ancient Osirion - Traits Adherent of Ancient Osirion Source Blood of the Beast pg. 2 Category Religion Requirement(s) Catfolk native to Osirion Although Bastet has been all but forgotten by the native Osirians, you either sojourned with or were raised in a cult dedicated to the cat-headed goddess of Ancient Osirion. You treat the tekko-kagi as a martial weapon instead of an exotic weapon. You also treat the tekko-kagi as if it were a set of claw blades when determining how it interacts with other effects.
