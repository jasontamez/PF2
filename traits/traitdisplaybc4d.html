Darkblight Survivor - Traits Darkblight Survivor Source People of the Wastes pg. 16 Category Region Requirement(s) Nirmathas Your woodland home in Nirmathas struggles against the tenacious Darkblight, forcing you to steel yourself against its poisonous influence. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Fortitude saving throws against diseases and poisons inflicted by fey, plants, and forest-related hazards.
