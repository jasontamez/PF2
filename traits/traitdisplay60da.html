Trench Navigator - Traits Trench Navigator Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Xidao You've sailed the treacherous waters of the Xidao Gulf in your past, and you can navigate their dangers adroitly. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (geography) checks, and a +1 trait bonus on Profession (sailor) checks. One of these skills becomes a class skill for you.
