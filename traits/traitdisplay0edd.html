Free People - Traits Free People Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Kellid, Realm of the Mammoth Lords native The independent tribes from the Realm of the Mammoth Lords hold little regard for the control others wield over free people. Their willful, independent streak makes them suspicious of any attempt to guide their behavior. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks and on Will saves against mind-affecting effects.
