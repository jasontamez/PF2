Telekinetic Dependence - Traits Telekinetic Dependence Source Blood of the Night pg. 11 Category Race Requirement(s) Vampire-Nosferatu Your body is so ancient you fear damaging it with exertion. You gain a +1 trait bonus to your caster level when casting (or using as a spell-like ability) fly, levitate, mage hand, telekinesis, and similar spells that move objects through sheer will.
