Eyes and Ears of the City - Traits Eyes and Ears of the City Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 9, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Abadar Your religious training involved serving in the city watch of a large city, the primary duty of which was standing sentinel on a city wall. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Perception checks, and Perception is always a class skill for you.
