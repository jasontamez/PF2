Maestro of the Society - Traits Maestro of the Society Source Shattered Star Player's Guide pg. 5, Faction Guide pg. 63 Category Basic (Social) Requirement(s) Bard, Pathfinder Society The skills of Golarion's greatest musicians are at your fingertips, thanks to the vast treasure trove of musical knowledge in the vaults beneath the Grand Lodge in Absalom. Studying this knowledge gives you the ability to use your bardic performance an additional 3 rounds per day.
