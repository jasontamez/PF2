Greenskin Stalker - Traits Greenskin Stalker Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Isger, River Kingdoms, or Varisia You're a member of the Greenskin Stalkers. Due to your affiliation with this brotherhood of goblin slayers, you've learned to think and act like a goblin, as disturbing as that sounds. You receive a +1 trait bonus on Bluff, Knowledge, Perception, Sense Motive, and Survival checks against or concerning goblinoids. You can also attempt Knowledge checks concerning goblinoids untrained.
