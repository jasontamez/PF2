Chivalrous - Traits Chivalrous Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 21, Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Taldor You were raised on tales of heroic knights and benevolent wizards from Taldor's Golden Age, and try to emulate their great deeds. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Knowledge (history) checks.
