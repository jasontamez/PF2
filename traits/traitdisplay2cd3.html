Keeper of the Secret Shame - Traits Keeper of the Secret Shame Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 7 Category Race Requirement(s) Elf You are a member of the Lantern Bearers or an agent of the Winter Council, tasked with ensuring that knowledge of the elves' secret shame-the drow-never reaches the surface. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge checks pertaining to drow and drow settlements.
