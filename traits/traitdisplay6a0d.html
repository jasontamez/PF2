Rahadoumi Disbeliever - Traits Rahadoumi Disbeliever Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 17 Category Region Requirement(s) Rahadoum As a Rahadoumi who rejects covenants with gods, your belief is strong enough to repel divine spells. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against divine spells, but you must make a saving throw even when that magic is beneficial to you.
