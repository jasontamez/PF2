Kobold Faith - Traits Kobold Faith Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold You are one of the diehard kobold faithful, respected in your kobold community. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks when dealing with other kobolds. Additionally, if your deity grants the Artifice, Earth, or Trickery domain, then your kobold faith unlocks a corresponding subdomain: Trap, Radiation, or Ambush.
