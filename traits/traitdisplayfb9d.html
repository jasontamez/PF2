Haunting Regret - Traits Haunting Regret Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 22 Category Drawback When you were young, a relative with whom you had frequently quarreled passed away where his or her soul could not rest. Now, the unquiet spirit appears around you at inconvenient times, distracting you with regret for being unable to help. You take a -2 penalty on saving throws against the distraction ability of swarms and mind-affecting effects and on concentration checks.
