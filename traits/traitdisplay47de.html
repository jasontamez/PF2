Prestigious Healer - Traits Prestigious Healer Source Healer's Handbook pg. 2 Category Region Requirement(s) Cheliax Once per day as an immediate action when you cast a spell of the healing subschool, you can attempt a Diplomacy check to improve the attitude of your target or anyone within 30 feet who can see you before the end of your next turn. You gain a +2 trait bonus on this check.
