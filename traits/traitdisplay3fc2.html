Fleshwarp Exposure - Traits Fleshwarp Exposure Source Inner Sea Races pg. 197 Category Race Requirement(s) Drow Your body responds strongly to transmutations because you were exposed to fleshwarping magic. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against transmutation spells and effects. Transmutations you willingly accept affect you as though they were cast at 1 caster level higher for the purpose of determining duration.
