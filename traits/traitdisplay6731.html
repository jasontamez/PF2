Emberkin Imposter - Traits Emberkin Imposter Source Qadira, Jewel of the East pg. 29 Category Region Requirement(s) Qadira You have found it is easier to be mistaken for someone with celestial blood than to reveal your true nature. You gain a +5 trait bonus on Disguise checks when pretending to be an aasimar, and a +2 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks against those you have convinced of your supposed celestial nature.
