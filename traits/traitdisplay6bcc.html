Snap Ignition - Traits Snap Ignition Source People of the Sands pg. 15 Category Race Requirement(s) Ifrit You are a pyromaniac by nature and love nothing more than setting fires. As a full-round action, you can produce a small flame at the end of your finger that ignites combustible materials as a tindertwig. Alternatively, you can maintain a flame equivalent to a candle by spending a standard action each round to concentrate on it.
