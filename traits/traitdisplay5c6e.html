Alchemical Intuition - Traits Alchemical Intuition Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 56 Category Basic (Magic) You are often struck with epiphanies about alchemical processes and substances. Once per day as a free action, you may gain a trait bonus equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 0) on a Craft (alchemy) check you make. You can apply this bonus after you roll the check, possibly turning a failure into a success.
