Unflinching Faith - Traits Unflinching Faith Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Nidal Numerous expeditions to accursed regions like Nidal's Finder's Gulch have reinforced your already steadfast faith. Once per day, you may reroll a Will saving throw made against an attack or effect that originates from an undead creature. You must decide to use this trait after the first saving throw is rolled, but before the results are revealed. You must take the result of the second roll, even if it is lower.
