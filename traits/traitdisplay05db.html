Jadwiga Medicine - Traits Jadwiga Medicine Source Healer's Handbook pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Irrisen Once per day as an immediate action when you cast a spell of the healing subschool or a hex that produces the effect of such a spell, you can attempt an Intimidate check to demoralize your target or anyone who can see you within 30 feet.
