Bekyar Slave Maestro - Traits Bekyar Slave Maestro Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Bekyar Tribe or Shoanti Clan You spent years using your magic to bend the will of captured slaves to suit your whims. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks against anyone you have held captive for at least 24 hours.
