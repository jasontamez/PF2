Self-Actualization - Traits Self-Actualization Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 33 Category Basic (Faith) Inspired by stories of mortals ascending to godhood, you're certain that you can do the same, and do all you can to hone your talents and reach your utmost potential. Once per day, when you take 20 on a roll or check, you can complete the action in only five times the normal amount of time for that action, rather than 20 times the normal amount.
