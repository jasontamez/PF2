Firebug - Traits Firebug Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 18 Category Basic (Combat) You were the child of a gunsmith or alchemist and always enjoyed experimenting with fiery alchemical items and minor explosives. You gain a +1 trait bonus on attack rolls made with thrown splash weapons and alchemist bombs. Suggested Characters: Alkenstar natives, Katapeshi, Thuvians.
