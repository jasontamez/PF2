Savannah Child - Traits Savannah Child Source Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Katapesh You were born and raised in central Katapesh, among the rolling savannahs that dominate the nation's interior. You spent many of your youth exploring these vast reaches, and know much of the savannah's secrets. Pick one of the following skills: Handle Animal, Knowledge (nature), or Ride. You gain a +1 trait bonus on that skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
