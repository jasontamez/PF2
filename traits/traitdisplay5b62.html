The Stranger - Traits The Stranger Source Occult Mysteries pg. 37 Category Cosmic Requirement(s) 21 Neth-29 Neth Like the lone traveler that walks alongside the caravan for but a brief while, you have a way with strangers. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Knowledge (local) checks to gather information. Additionally, either Diplomacy or Knowledge (local) becomes a class skill for you.
