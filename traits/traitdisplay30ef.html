Insider Knowledge - Traits Insider Knowledge Source PFS Guide to Organized Play 6.0 pg. 15 Category Faction Venture-Captain Valsin likes to keep abreast of situations within the Pathfinder Society, and you do your best to emulate him. Choose either Diplomacy or Knowledge (local). You gain a +1 trait bonus on all checks for the chosen skill, and the chosen skill becomes a class skill for you.
