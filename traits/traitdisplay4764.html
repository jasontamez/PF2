Disillusioned - Traits Disillusioned Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 18 Category Basic (Combat) Your childhood was ended by a great disaster or atrocity that you witnessed. To cope with the horror, you have learned to reflexively suppress all emotion. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Will saving throws against emotion and fear effects. Suggested Characters: Galtans, Hellknights, Nidalese.
