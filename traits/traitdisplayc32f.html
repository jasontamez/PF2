Nature Worshiper - Traits Nature Worshiper Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 0 Category Basic (Faith) When you were young, you saw a dead creature's body fertilize a field. Witnessing the connection between life and death moved you to recognize your own connection with the world, and ever since you have revered nature and feared death less. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against death effects and effects that use negative energy, such as energy drain and inflict spells.
