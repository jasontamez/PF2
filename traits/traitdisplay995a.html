Unearth Secrets - Traits Unearth Secrets Source Blood of Fiends pg. 21 Category Race Requirement(s) Tiefling-Hellspawn You have a gift for recognizing secret desires. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks when trying to get a hunch that might reveal a target's hidden vice, dark impulse, or any other craving it would not want publicly revealed.
