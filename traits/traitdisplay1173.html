Urban Acolyte - Traits Urban Acolyte Source Heroes of the Streets pg. 18 Category Basic (Faith) Growing up in a city has broadened your philosophy, and helped you interpret your patron's divine will in a novel way. You gain Knowledge (local) as a class skill. Additionally, you can select one of the following subdomains using the normal rules for subdomains (Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player Guide 86) so long as your deity grants access to its associated domain: Competition, Duels, Espionage, Fortifications, Industry, Legislation, Plague, or Riot.
