Lover of the Law - Traits Lover of the Law Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 221, Faiths of Balance pg. 5 Category Religion Requirement(s) Abadar You have a strong belief in the righteousness of law and justice. You receive a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against charm and compulsion effects, and may attempt a new saving throw with an additional +1 bonus (for a total of +2) if you are directed to break the law.
