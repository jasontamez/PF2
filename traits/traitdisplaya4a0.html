Sense of Order - Traits Sense of Order Source Halflings of Golarion pg. 31 Category Religion Requirement(s) [Halfling] Abadar Your ability to notice even the slightest change, and the discomfort it causes, made service to Abadar a logical choice. You gain a +3 trait bonus on any Perception check made to identify any changes to a location that have occurred since you last visited it.
