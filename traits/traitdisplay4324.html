Freed Slave (Andoran) - Traits Freed Slave (Andoran) Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 4 Category Region Requirement(s) Andoran You were either born or sold into slavery, but were freed by Andoren abolitionists. Your strong will helped you persevere in captivity, and gave you strength to start again from nothing in your new life in Andoran. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Will saves.
