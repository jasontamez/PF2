Deep Cover - Traits Deep Cover Source Spymaster's Handbook pg. 6 Category Basic (Social) You have lived a double life since your youth, perhaps to avoid persecution for your true identity or in service to an enemy of the people or group associated with your cover identity. You can always take 10 on Bluff and Disguise checks to assume and maintain your cover identity. Bluff or Disguise (your choice) is a class skill for you.
