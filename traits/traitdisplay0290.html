Witness to Nature's Cruelty - Traits Witness to Nature's Cruelty Source Inner Sea Races pg. 195 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Kellid You saw firsthand the terrible toll of starvation and the elements, and refuse to allow even your worst enemy to suffer that way. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Heal and Survival checks made on behalf of others. One of these skills (your choice) is always a class skill for you.
