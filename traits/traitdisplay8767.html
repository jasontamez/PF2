Akitonian Ferocity - Traits Akitonian Ferocity Source People of the Stars pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Akiton You were raised on Akiton, where you were hardened against the ravages of battle. Once per day, the first time you are brought below 0 hit points but not killed, you can fight on for 1 more round as if disabled. At the end of your next turn, unless brought to above 0 hit points, you immediately fall unconscious and begin dying.
