Liquid Healing - Traits Liquid Healing Source Potions and Poisons pg. 2 Category Region Requirement(s) Rahadoum Growing up in Rahadoum, you did not have access to clerics and other divine magic for healing, so you relied heavily on potions to cure your wounds. As a result, potions heal you more effectively. Whenever you consume a potion that heals any number of hit points, you heal 2 additional hit points.
