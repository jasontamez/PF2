Oregent Timing - Traits Oregent Timing Source Andoran, Spirit of Liberty pg. 20 Category Region Requirement(s) Oregent [in Andoran] After years of soulnumbing obedience to the schedules of others, you find synchronizing your actions with an ally second nature. Once per day when you take a readied action in combat, you gain a +3 trait bonus on the d20 roll for that action if it was triggered by an ally's action.
