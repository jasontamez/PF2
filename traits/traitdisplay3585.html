Successful Shirker - Traits Successful Shirker Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Halfling Born and raised a slave with no hope of advancement, you are adept at avoiding your overseer's ire and attention, the better to spend time on your own interests. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Stealth checks and a +3 trait bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy checks to avoid punishment by lawful authority.
