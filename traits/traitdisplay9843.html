Oathkeeper's Vow - Traits Oathkeeper's Vow Source Divine Anthology pg. 18 Category Religion Requirement(s) Kol The clergy of Kols trained you to make unbreakable vows. You add the following spells to the paladin spell list at the indicated levels: command (1st), forbid action (1st), suggestion (to fulfill a prior promise only; 2nd), lesser geas (3rd), geas/quest (4th).
