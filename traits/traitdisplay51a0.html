Blood Algorithm - Traits Blood Algorithm Source People of the River pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Android Your inhumanity extends to your very core. Pain receptors were omitted during your construction, so you gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against spells with the pain descriptor (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 138). Additionally, once per day when you are healed by a spell that targets constructs (such as a make whole spell), you heal 1 extra hit point per level of the spell.
