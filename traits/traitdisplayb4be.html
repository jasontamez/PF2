Spell Duel Prodigy - Traits Spell Duel Prodigy Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Erages You've participated in many spell duels during your time at Erages Academy, and You've developed a keen talent for identifying magical arts. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Spellcraft checks to identify a spell as it is being cast and on dispel checks to counterspell a spell.
