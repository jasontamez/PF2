Planar Negotiator - Traits Planar Negotiator Source Demon Hunter's Handbook pg. 25, Blood of Angels pg. 30 Category Region Requirement(s) The Abyss You are used to dealing with outsiders that would fluster a less experienced negotiator. When dealing with an outsider within one step of your alignment, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Sense Motive checks. Increase these bonuses to +2 if you and the outsider worship the same deity.
