Balancer's Banishing - Traits Balancer's Banishing Source Champions of Balance pg. 9 Category Basic (Magic) The interference of the Outer Planes in mortal affairs angers you to no end and invigorates the potency of your magic. As long as you remain neutral, whenever you cast a spell that would send an outsider with the chaotic, evil, good, or lawful subtype back to its home plane, the spell's saving throw DC increases by 1.
