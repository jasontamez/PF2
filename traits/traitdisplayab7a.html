Ear for Music - Traits Ear for Music Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Shelyn You spent countless hours of your youth in one of Shelyn's temples, listening to wonderful musicians and singers. You gain a +1 trait bonus on one category of Perform checks and a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge (local) checks that deal with the local art or music scene.
