Sacred Orienteer - Traits Sacred Orienteer Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 11 Category Religion Requirement(s) Elion You have traveled far with only your compass and faith as your guides. You begin play with a compass, and any compass or wayfinder in your possession also functions as a holy symbol of Elion. Either Knowledge (geography) or Survival becomes a class skill for you. Once per day you can consult your compass or wayfinder as a fullround action to gain a +2 trait bonus on one Knowledge (geography) or Survival check.
