Soldier of the Faith - Traits Soldier of the Faith Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 18 Category Region Requirement(s) Razmiran You have served Razmir as a faithful enforcer, or upon one of the "faith barges" plying the waters of Lake Encarthan, and have learned the enforcement techniques of Razmiran. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks, and Intimidate is always a class skill for you.
