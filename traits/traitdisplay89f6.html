The Flexing Arm - Traits The Flexing Arm Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 220, Faiths of Purity pg. 18 Category Religion Requirement(s) Kurgess You are practiced at using physical might to escape your bonds. You can use Strength instead of Dexterity as your base ability for Escape Artist skill checks to free yourself from bondage. Additionally, you gain a +1 trait bonus on such Escape Artist checks.
