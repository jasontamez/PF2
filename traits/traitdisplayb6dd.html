Captain's Blade - Traits Captain's Blade Source PFS Guide to Organized Play 6.0 pg. 15 Category Faction You were born on board a ship and learned to fight beside the sailing men and women of the Andoran fleet. While on board any vessel afloat on water, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Acrobatics and Climb checks. One of these skills (your choice) becomes a class skill for you.
