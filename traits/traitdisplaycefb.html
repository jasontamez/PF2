Self-Sustaining - Traits Self-Sustaining Source Disciple's Doctrine pg. 3 Category Basic (Faith) As a follower of Razmir, the Prophecies of Kalistrade, or some other tradition that preaches self-reliance, you make a point of not seeking divine aid for minor matters. When you gain this trait, choose one of the following 0-level spells: create water, mending, purify food and drink, or stabilize. You can cast this spell once per day as a spell-like ability, using your character level as your caster level. Once chosen, this selection can't change.
