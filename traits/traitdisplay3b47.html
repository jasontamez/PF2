Shadowsight - Traits Shadowsight Source Champions of Corruption pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Nidal Your people's ancient compact with Zon-Kuthon manifests strongly in you. While the darkness remains fearsome, you can at least see what lurks there. As a swift action once per day, your eyes can pierce the surrounding darkness-you gain the benefit of low-light vision for a number of rounds equal to your level.
