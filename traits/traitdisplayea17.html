Called Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 18 Category Basic (Faith) Your deity or a powerful spirit spoke to you when you were young, dropping cryptic hints about the important destiny that lay before you and how you must win your fights if you are to achieve your predetermined greatness. Once per day upon rolling a natural 1 on an attack roll, you may reroll the die and take the second result instead. Suggested Characters: Oracles, paladins, Ulfen.
