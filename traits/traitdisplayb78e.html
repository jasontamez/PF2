Left Behind - Traits Left Behind Source Elves of Golarion pg. 15 Category Region Requirement(s) [Elf] Mwangi Expanse or Varisia Having been born to those who chose to remain on Golarion during the Age of Darkness in the Mwangi Expanse, you understand the people of this world better than most elves. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Gather Information checks when interacting with Forlorn elves and non-elf races.
