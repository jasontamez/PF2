Ever Wary - Traits Ever Wary Source Blood of Fiends pg. 30 Category Race Requirement(s) Tiefling Constant fear that your fiendish nature might provoke a sudden attack ensures that you never completely let down your guard. During the surprise round and before your first action in combat, you can apply half your Dexterity bonus (if any) to your AC. You still count as flat-footed for the purposes of attacks and effects.
