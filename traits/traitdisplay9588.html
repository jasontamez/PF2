Guardian Smite - Traits Guardian Smite Source Divine Anthology pg. 19 Category Religion Requirement(s) Trudd Paladins of Trudd taught you to be a blessed, living barrier against enemies who threaten those you hold dear. Once per day, when you strike a foe with smite evil, you can halve the total damage dealt to the foe to reduce that foe's speed to 5 feet for 1 round.
