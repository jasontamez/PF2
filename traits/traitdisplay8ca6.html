Tempter's Tongue - Traits Tempter's Tongue Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 29 Category Race Requirement(s) Tiefling The blood of crafty, bargain-making fiends runs in your veins, and you have a talent for brokering deals of your own. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks and on opposed Charisma checks to influence creatures you have charmed (such as with charm person).
