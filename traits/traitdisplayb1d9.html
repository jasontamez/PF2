Stalwart of the Society - Traits Stalwart of the Society Source Shattered Star Player's Guide pg. 5, Faction Guide pg. 63 Category Basic (Faith) Requirement(s) Paladin, Pathfinder Society The courageous powers of Golarion's ancient paladin orders are open to you thanks to the Society's records of heroic deeds. As a result, your aura of courage class ability grants an additional +1 trait bonus on saving throws against fear effects.
