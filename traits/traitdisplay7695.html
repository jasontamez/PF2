Pathfinder's Focus Source Pathfinder Society Primer pg. 13 Category Basic (Magic) You can select a magnetic compass for your arcane bond class feature; this compass is nonmagical, but you can later spend 250 gp to upgrade it into a wayfinder. Choosing this trait allows you to upgrade this wayfinder to a variant or unique wayfinder per the normal rules for improving magic items on page 553 of the Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook. A bonded wayfinder must be held in your hand to grant the benefits of being a bonded object.
