Dispelled Battler Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 52 Category Basic (Combat) When deprived of magic, you fight harder. You gain a +1 trait bonus on weapon attack and damage rolls while within an area of antimagic such as that created by an antimagic field. You also gain this bonus for 1 round after you are subject to an area or targeted dispel magic spell, regardless of the effect's success or failure in dispelling your spells and magical effects.
