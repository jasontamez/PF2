Azlanti Inheritor - Traits Azlanti Inheritor Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 29, Humans of Golarion pg. 30 Category Race Requirement(s) Gillman You have prominent Azlanti features, which you can use to inspire confidence in your allies. Whenever you use the aid another action to aid an adjacent ally, increase the bonus you grant your ally by 1. This is a trait bonus.
