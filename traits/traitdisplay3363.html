Demonbane Summoner - Traits Demonbane Summoner Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) The Worldwound Your line is derived directly from the god callers of Sarkoris. You adamantly oppose the demonic forces of the Worldwound in hopes of reclaiming your lost lands. The attacks of creatures you summon are treated as cold iron for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction.
