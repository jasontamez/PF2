Lamasaran Performer - Traits Lamasaran Performer Source People of the Sands pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) Thuvia You are one of the famed performers of Lamasara, and your skill is known throughout Thuvia. Once per day when you start a bardic performance, you can increase the DC of the saving throw to resist your performance's effects by 1. This effect lasts until you switch performances or end your performance.
