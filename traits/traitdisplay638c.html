Jungle Guide - Traits Jungle Guide Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Sargava You've made your living outfitting and guiding expeditions deep into the Mwangi interior in search of ancient ruins and lost cities. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Handle Animal checks, and a +1 trait bonus on Survival checks in jungle terrain. One of these skills is a class skill for you.
