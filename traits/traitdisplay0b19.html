Snake Bleeder - Traits Snake Bleeder Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 15 Category Region Requirement(s) Nagajor You were a nagaji vassal of knowledgeable nagas, and they taught you much regarding how they concoct their various poisons. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Craft (alchemy) checks when brewing poisons, and Craft (alchemy) becomes a class skill for you.
