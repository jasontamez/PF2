Freedom Fighter (Shokuro) - Traits Freedom Fighter (Shokuro) Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 18 Category Region Requirement(s) Shokuro You come from a family of farmers, but are determined to fight for your rights. You gain a +1 trait bonus on attacks made with improvised weapons. If you do not possess the Catch Off-Guard or Throw Anything feats, you still take penalties on these attack rolls as normal.
