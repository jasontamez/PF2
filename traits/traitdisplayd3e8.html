Anatomist - Traits Anatomist Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 51, Second Darkness Player's Guide pg. 11, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Basic (Combat) You have studied the workings of anatomy, either as a student at university or as an apprentice mortician or necromancer. You know where to aim your blows to strike vital organs. You gain a +1 trait bonus on all rolls made to confirm critical hits.
