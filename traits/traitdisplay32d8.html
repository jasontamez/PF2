Kobold Herbalist Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold-Green-Scaled You are familiar with the flora of your native forest and have rudimentary skill as an herbalist. Once per day, you can attempt a DC 20 Survival check instead of a Craft (alchemy) check to create a single dose of scent cloak (Ultimate Equipment 105) without paying any cost for raw materials. Any scent cloak created using this method must be used within 24 hours, or it becomes inert.
