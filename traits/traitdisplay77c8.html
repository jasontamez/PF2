Elaborate Trapper - Traits Elaborate Trapper Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 18 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold-Blue-Scaled You're blessed with a devious mind, and the more elaborate your traps are, the more effective they become. When creating a trap, if your Craft (trap) roll exceeds the DC of the check by 5 or more, that trap's Disable Device DC increases by 2.
