River Rat - Traits River Rat Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 63, Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 13, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Region Requirement(s) Marsh or River You learned to swim right after you learned to walk. When you were a youth, a gang of river pirates put you to work swimming in nighttime rivers and canals with a dagger between your teeth so you could sever the anchor ropes of merchant vessels. You gain a +1 trait bonus on damage rolls with a dagger and a +1 trait bonus on Swim checks. Swim is always a class skill for you.
