Loner - Traits Loner Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 22 Category Drawback Raised by pirates, bandits, or other troublemakers unwilling to stick out their necks for you, you are accustomed to operating on your own. The presence of allies can easily become a distraction to you, and you do your best to keep them out of your space. You take a -1 penalty to AC and on attack rolls while adjacent to allies or when taking the aid another action.
