Azlanti Scholar - Traits Azlanti Scholar Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 2, Ruins of Azlant Player's Guide pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Ironbound Archipelago, non-elf Your interactions with the elves of the Mordant Spire have taught you much about Azlanti artifacts, and you now share the Mordant Spire elves' fascination with these relics. You gain Azlanti as a bonus language, and you gain a +2 trait bonus on Appraise and Use Magic Device checks when attempting to identify or use authentic Azlanti artifacts.
