Unflappable Arrogance - Traits Unflappable Arrogance Source Blood of the Elements pg. 7 Category Race Requirement(s) Ifrit You laugh in the face of your aggressors. The DC to demoralize you with the Intimidate skill increases by 5. If an opponent's Intimidate check would be high enough to cause you to become demoralized for more than 1 round, you are instead demoralized for only 1 round.
