Sovyrian Intellectual - Traits Sovyrian Intellectual Source People of the Stars pg. 25 Category Race Requirement(s) Elf, 1st-level only Having grown up in one of the intellectual centers of Castrovel, you speak precisely and persuasively, especially among elves. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks. This bonus increases to +3 against creatures with the elf subtype.
