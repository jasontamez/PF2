Div Hunter - Traits Div Hunter Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) Thuvia Divs have tormented you and your loved ones for as long as you can remember. You've improved your skills to better oppose the creatures. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge checks to identify divs (and their special powers and weaknesses) and a +1 trait bonus on weapon damage rolls against divs.
