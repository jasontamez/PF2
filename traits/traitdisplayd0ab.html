Firebug (Kobold) Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold-Red-Scaled Like the red dragons that inspire your tribe, you're blessed with a small spark of incendiary magic. You are able to cast spark three times per day as a spell-like ability. The caster level is equal to your character level. This spell-like ability's save DC is Intelligence-based.
