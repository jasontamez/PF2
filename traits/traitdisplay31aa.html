Sevenarches Seeker - Traits Sevenarches Seeker Source People of the River pg. 14 Category Region Requirement(s) Sevenarches You've long heard rumors about the mysterious archways of Sevenarches and have spent much of your life trying to unravel the mysteries behind the portals. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (history) checks and Perception checks to notice secret doors, and Knowledge (history) becomes a class skill for you.
