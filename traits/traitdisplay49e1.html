Passionate Inertia - Traits Passionate Inertia Source Champions of Corruption pg. 11 Category Basic (Social) Your passions drive your thinking so irresistibly that even magic struggles to change your mind. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Will saves to resist having your mind changed about something, but you take a -1 penalty on Will saves to resist temptation and compulsions you might reasonably be already inclined toward.
