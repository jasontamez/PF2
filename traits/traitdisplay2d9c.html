The Follower - Traits The Follower Source Occult Mysteries pg. 37 Category Cosmic Requirement(s) 30 Neth-17 Kuthona You are born under the sign of the dark figure that trails the Caravan-the specter of death itself. You receive a +2 bonus on saving throws against death effects. All allies within 10 feet of you take a -1 penalty on saving throws against death effects.
