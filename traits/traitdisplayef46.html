Dilettante Artist - Traits Dilettante Artist Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 14 Category Race Requirement(s) Elf Art for you is a social gateway and you use it to influence and penetrate high society. You gain a +1 trait bonus to one category of Perform checks and a +1 trait bonus to Diplomacy checks. One of these skills (your choice) is always a class skill for you.
