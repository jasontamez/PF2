Stabbing Spells - Traits Stabbing Spells Source Arcane Anthology pg. 10 Category Basic (Magic) Aroden wrote much about ways to use weapons to enhance magic's effectiveness, and you have taken to heart the insights gleaned from his words. Whenever you strike a foe with a weapon, you gain a +2 trait bonus on caster level checks to overcome that foe's spell resistance until the end of your next turn.
