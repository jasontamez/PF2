Underbridge Dweller - Traits Underbridge Dweller Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia-Magnimar You were raised in the Shadow district of Magnimar beneath the Irespan, a veiled place the light of the sun touches for only 3 hours per day. Your well-founded suspicions grant you a +2 trait bonus on Perception checks in dim light, and Perception is always a class skill for you.
