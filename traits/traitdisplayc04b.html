Mammoth Master - Traits Mammoth Master Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 12, Orcs of Golarion pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) Realm of the Mammoth Lords You have experience trapping and training large creatures such as mammoths, mastodons, woolly rhinoceroses, and other megafauna. You gain a +4 trait bonus on Handle Animal checks related to these creatures.
