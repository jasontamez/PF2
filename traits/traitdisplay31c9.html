Tunnel Fighter - Traits Tunnel Fighter Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Dwarves of Golarion pg. 10, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Dwarf Caves and tunnels are a second home to you. While underground, you receive a +2 trait bonus on initiative checks and a +1 trait bonus on weapon damage rolls for critical hits (this damage is multiplied on a critical hit).
