Boar Resilience - Traits Boar Resilience Source Blood of the Moon pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Wereboar-Kin You were victimized by bullies or even abusive adults. Although the harassment got worse when your wereboar features became more obvious, those features also gave you fortitude that helped you resist it.
