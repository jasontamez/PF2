Blade Bravado - Traits Blade Bravado Source Humans of Golarion pg. 30 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Taldan You grew up fighting in one of the many Taldan dueling academies and have honed your skills with one-handed weapons. When wielding a one-handed weapon and nothing in your off hand, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Acrobatics and Bluff checks.
