Cliff Strider - Traits Cliff Strider Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Arcadia native You grew up along the Grinding Coast of Arcadia, scaling perilous cliffs and dealing with the dangerous creatures that live on these rocky surfaces. As a move action while climbing, you can climb 5 feet without provoking an attack of opportunity, even if you don't have a climb speed.
