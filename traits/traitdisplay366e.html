Divine Confidante - Traits Divine Confidante Source Spymaster's Handbook pg. 6 Category Basic (Faith) You were inspired with the reverence offered by worshipers to priests and other spiritual leaders, so you assisted them. You gain a +3 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks to get hunches about those who discuss matters of faith, mythology, morality, religion, or the Outer Planes with you. Sense Motive is a class skill for you.
