Planetar's Visions - Traits Planetar's Visions Source Blood of Angels pg. 21 Category Race Requirement(s) Aasimar-Angelkin Ever since you were a child, you've experienced vivid dreams of cutting down hordes of shrieking, twisted demons. Whenever you succeed at a critical hit with a melee weapon against an evil outsider, you ignore an amount of the outsider's damage reduction (if any) equal to the critical multiplier of your weapon (this cannot reduce a creature's damage reduction to below 0).
