Honeyed Tongue - Traits Honeyed Tongue Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 8 Category Region Requirement(s) Amanandar Having matured in the melting pot of New Oppara, you know the customs of the Tian- Shus as well as those of the Taldans, and you utilize this knowledge to create peace between rival groups. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks, and Diplomacy becomes a class skill for you.
