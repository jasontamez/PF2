Acadamae Neophyte - Traits Acadamae Neophyte Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 10 Category Basic (Magic) You have shown a significant talent for the magical arts, gaining the attention of the famed Acadamae of Korvosa. Whenever you succeed at a Knowledge (planes) check to identify a creature and its special powers or vulnerabilities, you gain one additional piece of useful information (as if your skill check result had been 5 higher).
