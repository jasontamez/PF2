Centaur Vengeance - Traits Centaur Vengeance Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Iobaria Your nomadic tribe wanders the Ice Steppes of Iobaria. They live in constant fear of the great white wyrm Sjohvornor and feel helpless to stop the dragon's aggression. Your tribe is filled with wise sages and great warriors, and they resent being cowed by any foe. You vowed that one day you would not have to be afraid of Sjohvornor or any other wyrm. You gain a +2 bonus on saving throws against any dragon's frightful presence.
