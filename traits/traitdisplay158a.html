Liquid-Tongued - Traits Liquid-Tongued Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 18 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold-Blue-Scaled The magic-users in your tribe pride themselves on their ability to control the forces of water. Integral to this practice is the ability to study and communicate with visitors from the Elemental Plane of Water. In addition to speaking Draconic, you begin play with the ability to speak Aquan.
