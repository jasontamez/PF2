Trailblazer - Traits Trailblazer Source Humans of Golarion pg. 31 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Mwangi Your ability to endure in the darkest jungles has earned you a reputation as a novice guide. While traversing any wilderness, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Survival checks, and gain a +1 trait bonus on Fortitude saving throws against diseases.
