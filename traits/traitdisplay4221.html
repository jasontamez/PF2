Spooked - Traits Spooked Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 23 Category Drawback You had a traumatic experience with a spirit at a young age that colors your reactions to such creatures even to this day. Whenever you perceive a fey, outsider, or undead from within 60 feet, you become shaken for 1d4 rounds unless you succeed at a Will save with a DC equal to 15 + the creature's Charisma modif ier. Immunity to fear effects does not allow you to forgo this saving throw; even if you are immune to fear, you must roll the Will saving throw for this drawback when applicable, albeit with a +4 bonus.
