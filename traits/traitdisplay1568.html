Fight with the Flock - Traits Fight with the Flock Source Blood of the Moon pg. 9 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Werebat-Kin You have always been most comfortable in a group. Since awakening to your werebat powers, this feeling has only grown. As long as you are within 10 feet of at least two allies, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks and attack rolls to make attack of opportunity.
