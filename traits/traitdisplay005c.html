Canter - Traits Canter Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 59, Second Darkness Player's Guide pg. 13, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Basic (Social) You grew up among thieves and scoundrels, and their unusual speech patterns and turns of phrase don't faze you in the slightest. Anyone who attempts to use Bluff to deliver a secret message to you gains a +5 bonus on her Bluff check. When you attempt to intercept a secret message using Sense Motive, you gain a +5 trait bonus on the attempt.
