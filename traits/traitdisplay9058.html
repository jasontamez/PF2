Loner of the Rocks - Traits Loner of the Rocks Source Inner Sea Races pg. 197 Category Race Requirement(s) Oread Feeling harried by flighty non-oreads, you chose to embrace the life of a hermit with few contacts beyond your rocky home. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Heal and Survival checks. Your bonus on Survival checks increases by 1 in underground or mountain environments.
