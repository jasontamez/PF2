Battlefield Caster - Traits Battlefield Caster Source Dwarves of Golarion pg. 10 Category Religion Requirement(s) Angradd, Gorum, Torag, Trudd Your faith protects you even as you unleash spells in the thick of battle. You receive a +1 trait bonus on concentration checks to cast defensively and a +1 trait bonus to AC against attacks provoked by or readied against spellcasting.
