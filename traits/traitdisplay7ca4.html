Crocodile Swim - Traits Crocodile Swim Source Blood of the Moon pg. 15 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker-Werecrocodile-Kin You grew up near crocodiles, learning to move as they do. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Swim and Stealth checks while moving at half speed or less and mostly or completely underwater. One of these skills (your choice) becomes a class skill for you.
