Lichblood - Traits Lichblood Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Hold of Belkzen You are among the fabled lichblood orcs, and so you command respect among the orcs of Belkzen. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Diplomacy and Intimidate checks made against orcs of Belkzen. You must have the undead bloodrager bloodline to select this trait.
