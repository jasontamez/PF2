Community-Minded - Traits Community-Minded Source People of the Sands pg. 21 Category Region Requirement(s) Rahadoum While some pray to the gods for mercy or prosperity, you follow a different tack-you believe in improving the lives of those around you through earnest labor and the efforts of you and your community. Your hard-earned discipline and the candor of your words affect all who bear witness. Any morale bonuses you confer upon your allies through your own abilities or spells last 2 additional rounds.
