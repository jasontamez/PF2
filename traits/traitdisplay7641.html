Isgeri Orphan - Traits Isgeri Orphan Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Isger You grew up in one of the many Asmodean orphanages that dot the Isgeri countryside. Whether or not you took to their teachings, your strict indoctrination at the hands of the "devil nuns" has toughened your body to punishment. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Fortitude saves.
