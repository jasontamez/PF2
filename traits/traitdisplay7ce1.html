Radiant Healing - Traits Radiant Healing Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 7 Category Basic (Magic) You've learned how to infuse your healing magic with lingering traces of light. Whenever you cast a spell from the conjuration (healing) school that has a casting time of a standard action, you can extend its casting time to a full-round action to imbue the target with light, as per light, with a duration of 1 round per caster level.
