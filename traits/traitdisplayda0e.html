Secret-Keeper (Svirfneblin) - Traits Secret-Keeper (Svirfneblin) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 199 Category Race Requirement(s) Svirfneblin You trained from birth to never reveal secrets and never betray anyone's trust. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against divinations or enchantments that would compel you to reveal a secret or allow anyone to read your mind.
