Ancestral Grounds - Traits Ancestral Grounds Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 7 Category Race Requirement(s) Dwarf You recall the dangers the dwarves faced during the Quest for Sky. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks and a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge (history) checks pertaining to the Quest for Sky. Select one of these skills to be a class skill for you.
