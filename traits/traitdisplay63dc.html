Giant Dodger - Traits Giant Dodger Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 12 Category Region Requirement(s) Desert-Cliff, Desert, River In your youth, you were chased by giants for trespassing-or for sport-and you have learned to evade the reach of your opponents. You gain a +3 bonus on Acrobatics checks to avoid provoking an attack of opportunity when moving out of a threatened space. Likely Regions: Katapesh, Osirion, Qadira, Rahadoum, Thuvia.
