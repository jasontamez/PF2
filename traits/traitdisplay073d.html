Child of the Streets - Traits Child of the Streets Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 59, Second Darkness Player's Guide pg. 13, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Basic (Social) You grew up on the streets of a large city, and as a result you have developed a knack for picking pockets and hiding small objects on your person. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sleight of Hand checks, and Sleight of Hand is always a class skill for you.
