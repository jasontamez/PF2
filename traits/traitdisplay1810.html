Ecumenical - Traits Ecumenical Source Disciple's Doctrine pg. 3 Category Basic (Social) Your childhood in Magnimar (or in a similar cosmopolitan city) taught you to recognize signs of worship of demigods such as empyreal lords. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge (religion) checks involving entities other than the most influential gods. You also gain a +2 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks to influence worshipers of such entities.
