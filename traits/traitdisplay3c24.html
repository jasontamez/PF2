Jungle Opportunist - Traits Jungle Opportunist Source Inner Sea Races pg. 198 Category Race Requirement(s) Catfolk You trained with your clan to hunt large prey such as the dinosaurs of the Valashmai Jungle. When you grant an ally a bonus on an attack roll against a foe of Large or larger size due to flanking or the aid another action, that ally gains a +1 trait bonus on weapon damage rolls against that creature as well.
