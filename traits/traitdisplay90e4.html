Scourge of the Seas - Traits Scourge of the Seas Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) The Shackles You have long appreciated the flamboyance and versatility of the whip, both as a weapon and a tool for enforcing discipline. When you wield a whip or scourge, you gain a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks. Additionally, once per day when you use a whip or scourge to perform a successful disarm or trip combat maneuver against an opponent, you regain 1 point of grit or panache.
