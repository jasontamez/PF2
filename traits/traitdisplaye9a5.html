Wise Teacher - Traits Wise Teacher Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 223, Faiths of Purity pg. 9 Category Religion Requirement(s) Erastil You are skilled in passing along knowledge of rural matters. If you have 4 or more ranks in Knowledge (Nature) or Survival, when you aid another with these skills, you grant a +4 bonus on the check you are aiding instead of the normal +2.
