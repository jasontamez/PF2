Draconic Lineage - Traits Draconic Lineage Source Legacy of Dragons pg. 3 Category Race Somewhere in your family line, draconic power entered your bloodline. As a result of this lineage, you gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against fear and against any effect created by a creature of the dragon type. If you gain a bloodrager or sorcerer bloodline, you must choose draconic.
