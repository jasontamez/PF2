Amiable Briber - Traits Amiable Briber Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 3 Category Basic (Social) Requirement(s) Absalom Your attempts to bribe others usually come off as playful or as a gag. The first time someone refuses a bribe you offer, that person's attitude toward you doesn't worsen, even if the offer would normally offend the person (as though you had failed a Diplomacy check by 5 or more).
