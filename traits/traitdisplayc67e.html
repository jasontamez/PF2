Bellis Honey Harvester - Traits Bellis Honey Harvester Source Andoran, Spirit of Liberty pg. 18 Category Region Requirement(s) Bellis [in Andoran] Your frequent exposure to bees-and their stings-makes all such creatures seem more like nuisances than actual threats. You gain DR 3/- against damage dealt by swarms and a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against distraction and poison from swarms.
