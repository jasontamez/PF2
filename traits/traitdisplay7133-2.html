Inciter (Hobgoblin) - Traits Inciter (Hobgoblin) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 199 Category Race Requirement(s) Hobgoblin You witnessed or were part of a feud that turned close friends into hateful enemies. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Bluff checks to convince others that their allies have wronged them and on Diplomacy checks to persuade others to betray their allies' trust.
