Deathkeeper - Traits Deathkeeper Source People of the Sands pg. 13 Category Race Requirement(s) Dwarf-Pahmet You have lived for a time deep inside the necropolises of the pharaohs. Your exposure to undead creatures and their necromantic energies has strengthened your ability to resist the powers of the unliving. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against the supernatural abilities of undead creatures.
