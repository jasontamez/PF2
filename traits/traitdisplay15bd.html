Carnation Scales - Traits Carnation Scales Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold Though your light pink scales made you the subject of ridicule by your tribe, you can adapt to more environments than they can. You gain a +1 bonus on Survival checks, and Survival is always a class skill for you. Most kobolds with carnation scales also have the day raider alternate racial trait (see page 7).
