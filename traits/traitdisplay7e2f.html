Antiquities Smuggler - Traits Antiquities Smuggler Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 16 Category Region Requirement(s) Osirion You have moved contraband relics from Osirion's elder days for years, and have learned some tricks. Choose one of the following skills: Appraise, Bluff, or Sleight of Hand. You gain a +1 trait bonus on that skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
