Merabian Mentorship - Traits Merabian Mentorship Source Blood of the Elements pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) Thuvia [Suli] You were apprenticed to a renowned alchemist of Merab, and she taught you how to recognize elemental reagents by taste. Upon imbibing a potion containing a spell on your formula list of a spell level you are capable of casting, you can attempt a Spellcraft check (DC 15 + extract level). On a success, you can immediately add the spell to your formula book.
