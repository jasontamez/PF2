Fortified Drinker - Traits Fortified Drinker Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Cayden Cailean Cayden Cailean's holy brews invigorate your mind, making you less susceptible to mental attacks. Whenever you imbibe any alcoholic beverage, you gain a +2 trait bonus on saves against mindaffecting effects for 1 hour.
