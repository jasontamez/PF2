Mutabi-qi Explorer - Traits Mutabi-qi Explorer Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 17 Category Region Requirement(s) Shanguang You belong to one of the nomadic Mutabi-qi tribes that wander the wastes of Shaguang, and you are adept at navigating the seemingly endless desert wastes. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (geography) checks and Survival checks made in the desert.
