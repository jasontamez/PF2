Andoletta's Consolation - Traits Andoletta's Consolation Source Divine Anthology pg. 16 Category Religion Requirement(s) Andoletta You had a close encounter with a servant or worshiper of Andoletta who provided you with a memorable, if unconventional, consolation after a loss. As a result, you gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against emotion and fear effects. If you are immune to fear, you instead gain a +4 trait bonus on saves against emotion effects.
