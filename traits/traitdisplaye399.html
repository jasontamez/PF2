Wealthy Dabbler - Traits Wealthy Dabbler Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 21, Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Taldor You study magic at one of Taldor's many social clubs, wowing your friends with your expertise in the simplest of magical exploits. Select two non-harmful arcane cantrips. You can cast these two cantrips once per day each (caster level 1st). If you have levels in a class that can cast these cantrips, your caster level for these cantrips is equal to that class level.
