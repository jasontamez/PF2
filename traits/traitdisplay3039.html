Marked by Nature's Magic - Traits Marked by Nature's Magic Source Inner Sea Races pg. 199 Category Race Requirement(s) Skinwalker You hail from a community-perhaps in Arcadia or the Lands of the Linnorm Kings-that encouraged you to interact with fey. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (nature) checks involving fey and on Diplomacy checks to influence fey. Knowledge (nature) is always a class skill for you.
