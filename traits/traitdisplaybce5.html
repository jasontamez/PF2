Ingratiating - Traits Ingratiating Source Inner Sea Races pg. 195 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Chelaxian Someone close to you was removed from her position and replaced after losing the government's favor, and you had to quickly prove your loyalty to her replacement or face your own disgrace. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy checks when interacting with people in positions of authority over you.
