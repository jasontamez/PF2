Open-Minded Explorer - Traits Open-Minded Explorer Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 15 Category Region Requirement(s) Averaka You grew up constantly interacting with people who speak unfamiliar languages and have diverse customs; your manners may not be polished, but your acceptance of others is genuine. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks. This bonus increases to +2 when you're speaking the racial or ethnic language of the target creature.
