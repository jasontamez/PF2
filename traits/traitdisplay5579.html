Boarded in Mwangi Expanse - Traits Boarded in Mwangi Expanse Source Serpent's Skull Player's Guide pg. 11 Category Campaign You boarded the Jenivere in the Mwangi Expanse, where you've lived or recently traveled through. You harbor no illusions regarding the deadliness of the jungles, the creatures that inhabit it, or the peoples that make their home therein. You gain Polyglot as a bonus language and receive a +1 trait bonus on all Knowledge (nature) checks regarding the jungle.
