Storied Scars - Traits Storied Scars Source People of the North pg. 6 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Kellid You proudly bear the numerous scars of your life on your body, and each one tells a tale of your experiences. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks made to interact with other Kellids, and a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks when dealing with non-Kellid peoples.
