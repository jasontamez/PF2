Mwangi Herbal Tradition - Traits Mwangi Herbal Tradition Source Healer's Handbook pg. 22 Category Region Requirement(s) Must hail from the Mwangi Expanse or have lived there for at least 1 year You are trained in a Mwangi tradition of herbalism dating back to the Age of Darkness and therefore have great insight into how to use the natural ingredients at your disposal to nurse an ailing body. You gain a +4 trait bonus on Heal checks when providing long-term care, and Heal is a class skill for you.
