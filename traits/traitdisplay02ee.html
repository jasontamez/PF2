Greater Link - Traits Greater Link Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 57 Category Basic (Magic) The link between you and your eidolon is stronger than most. Your eidolon's current and maximum hit point totals are not reduced by 50% until you are separated by 110 feet or more. Your eidolon's current and maximum hit point totals are not reduced by 75% until you are separated by 1,100 feet or more. All other aspects of your life link class feature are unaffected.
