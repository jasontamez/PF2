Prehensile Whip - Traits Prehensile Whip Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 31 Category Equipment In your hand a whip has a life of its own, wrapping around pillars and beams with ease. You can use a whip as if it were a rope with a grappling hook at the end. Attaching your whip is a standard action, but detaching it is a full-round action.
