Talented Organizer - Traits Talented Organizer Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 223, Faiths of Purity pg. 19 Category Religion Requirement(s) Milani You are both skilled and knowledgeable when at putting together strong resistance organizations, thanks to your keen sense of how to motivate people. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sense Motive skill checks, and Sense Motive is a class skill for you.
