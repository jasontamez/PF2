Cypher Resistance - Traits Cypher Resistance Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 23 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia-Riddleport You have grown up near the Cyphergate, passing under its arch countless times in your lifetime. You can recount the runes from memory, and repeated exposure has imbued you with a faint resistance to runic magic. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against spells with the word "glyph" or "symbol" in their name.
