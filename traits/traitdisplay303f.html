Curse Queller - Traits Curse Queller Source Healer's Handbook pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Osirion Whenever a caster level check is required to treat an affliction with magic (such as when casting remove curse on a cursed creature or object), you gain a +2 trait bonus on your caster level check. Additionally, you gain a +1 trait bonus on your saving throws against curses.
