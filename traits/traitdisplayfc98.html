Authoritarian Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 18 Category Basic (Faith) You spent much of your youth helping the clergy of your chosen faith perform their duties, and learned from them respect for authority. As long as you are acting in the service of a liege or leader you recognize as legitimate, you gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws against fear and compulsion effects. Suggested Characters: Abadarans, Hellknights, Hermeans.
