Domineering Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 19 Category Basic (Magic) When you were young, you attracted a group of devoted companions thanks to your impressive talent, and the experience inspired in you a powerful selfassurance and air of superiority. Your confidence is so strong that others feel naturally compelled to follow your commands. Choose one 1st-level enchantment spell you are capable of casting. The DC of that spell increases by 1. Suggested Characters: Chelaxians, Irriseni, Mwangi.
