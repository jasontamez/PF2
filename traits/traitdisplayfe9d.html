Desperate Speed - Traits Desperate Speed Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 56 Category Basic (Magic) Your eidolon excels at a movement type you do not possess. Your eidolon receives a foot enhancement bonus to one type of speed it has that you do not, such as a climb or swim speed. If your eidolon doesn't already possess such a mode of movement, you may apply this trait bonus later if it gains an evolution that grants it such a mode. Once this bonus is applied to a mode of movement, it can't be switched to another mode.
