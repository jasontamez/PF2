Martyr's Blood - Traits Martyr's Blood Source Bastards of Golarion pg. 28 Category Race Requirement(s) Aasimar You carry the blood of a self-sacrificing celestial, and strive to live up to your potential for heroism. As long as your current hit point total is less than half of your maximum hit points possible, you gain a +1 trait bonus on attack rolls against evil foes.
