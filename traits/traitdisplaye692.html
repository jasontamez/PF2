Business Venturer - Traits Business Venturer Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 23 Category Race Requirement(s) Ratfolk Your mercantile pursuits have sent you across Golarion, making you better traveled than most ratfolk. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise, Knowledge (local), and Profession (merchant) checks, and one of these skills (your choice) is a class skill for you.
