Slippery Step - Traits Slippery Step Source People of the Sands pg. 15 Category Race Requirement(s) Undine You are used to walking on wet and slippery surfaces. You are always considered to have firm footing when fighting underwater (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 433), and you gain a +2 trait bonus on Acrobatics checks and Climb checks on wet surfaces or in wet terrain, including bogs, standing or running water, or a storm-tossed ship.
