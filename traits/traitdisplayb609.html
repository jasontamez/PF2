Devotee of Kalistrade - Traits Devotee of Kalistrade Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 6 Category Region Requirement(s) Druma As an adherent of the Prophecies of Kalistrade, you understand the importance of a good deal. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise checks, and a +1 trait bonus on Bluff checks when trying to make a deal with nonbelievers.
