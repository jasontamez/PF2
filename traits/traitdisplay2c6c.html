Dinosaur Whisperer - Traits Dinosaur Whisperer Source Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Mediogalti After spending a month in the Mediogaltian jungle, you developed a knack for dealing with dinosaurs. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (nature) and Survival checks concerning dinosaurs. You also gain a +1 trait bonus on wild empathy checks made to affect dinosaurs.
