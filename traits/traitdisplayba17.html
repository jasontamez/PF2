Frost Spitter - Traits Frost Spitter Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 19 Category Race Requirement(s) Kobold-White-Scaled The icy breath of a white dragon fills your lungs. Once per day, you can quickly exhale to produce a frigid cloud of dense fog that quickly sinks to the ground. This freezing mist covers a 10-foot-square area with slippery ice that's as difficult to traverse as an ice sheet (Core Rulebook 430).
