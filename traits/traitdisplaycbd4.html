Callous - Traits Callous Source Antihero's Handbook pg. 7 Category Basic (Social) You have endured many hardships throughout your life, and this has hardened you to the suffering of others to the point that you are not easily swayed by a sad story. You gain a +4 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks that oppose the Bluff checks of creatures that attempt to deceive you by winning your sympathy.
