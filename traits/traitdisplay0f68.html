Shadow-Scarred - Traits Shadow-Scarred Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 23 Category Drawback You were touched by terrible horrors that live in the darkness just outside the human sphere, such as shadow-beasts or aberrations linked to the Dark Tapestry, and feel your life-force ebb away ever so slightly whenever you return to the shadows. Whenever you are in an area of dim light or darkness, you take a -1 penalty on saving throws.
