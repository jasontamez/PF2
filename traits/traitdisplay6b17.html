Shenmen Prospector - Traits Shenmen Prospector Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 17 Category Region Requirement(s) Shenmen You were a talented laborer in the silver mines of Shenmen, and have seen many wonders in the haunted caverns beneath the surface of the cursed nation. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Appraise and Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks.
