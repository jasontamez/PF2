Suspicious - Traits Suspicious Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 61, Second Darkness Player's Guide pg. 14, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Basic (Social) You discovered at an early age that someone you trusted, perhaps an older sibling or a parent, had lied to you, and lied often, about something you had taken for granted, leaving you quick to question the claims of others. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks, and Sense Motive is always a class skill for you.
