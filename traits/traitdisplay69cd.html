Veteran of Battle - Traits Veteran of Battle Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Gorum You have fought in several battles, and each time felt the presence of Gorum guiding your sword-arm, making you ready to act at a moment's notice. You gain a +1 trait bonus on initiative checks, and if you are able to act during a surprise round, you may draw a weapon (but not a potion or magic item) as a free action during that round.
