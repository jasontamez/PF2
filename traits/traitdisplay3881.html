Dueling Cloak Adept - Traits Dueling Cloak Adept Source Knights of the Inner Sea pg. 0 Category Basic (Combat) You are trained in using your cloak to disguise your body and deceive your enemies. While wearing a cloak and using an Aldori dueling sword, you gain a +2 trait bonus on Bluff checks made to feint. Additionally, every time you successfully feint while wearing a cloak, you gain a +1 dodge bonus to your Armor Class until your next turn.
