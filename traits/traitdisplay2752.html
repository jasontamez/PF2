Warrior of Old - Traits Warrior of Old Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Elves of Golarion pg. 15, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Elf As a child, you put in long hours on combat drills, and though time has made this training a dim memory, you still have a knack for quickly responding to trouble. You gain a +2 trait bonus on initiative checks.
