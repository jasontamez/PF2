Demon Smiter - Traits Demon Smiter Source Champions of Purity pg. 13 Category Basic (Combat) You grew up around those who fight the demons streaming out of the Worldwound, and from their stories you learned about the demons' weaknesses. You are likely from Mendev or have joined that nation's cause as a crusader. Once per day when fighting demons, you gain a +4 trait bonus on a single attack roll.
