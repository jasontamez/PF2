Operatic - Traits Operatic Source Humans of Golarion pg. 31 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Chelaxian You grew up in wonder of Chelish theater, and spent countless hours in the opera houses across Cheliax and even more time perfecting your voice. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Perform (sing) checks. Furthermore you have memorized the librettos of many works sung in the ancient Azlanti tongue, granting you a +2 trait bonus on Linguistic checks when trying to decipher the ancient Azlanti language.
