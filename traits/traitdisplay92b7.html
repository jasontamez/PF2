Buried Anxiety - Traits Buried Anxiety Source Blood of Fiends pg. 21 Category Race Requirement(s) Tiefling'Spitespawn You laugh at what makes others tremble, but are secretly disturbed by a particular sort of mundane item. Pick a specific sort of object, color, sound, or similar relatively common phenomena. You gain a +2 trait bonus on saving throws made to resist fear effects except when you can see, hear, smell, or taste that which unnerves you.
