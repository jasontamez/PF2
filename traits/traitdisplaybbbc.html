Patient Calm Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 19 Category Basic (Faith) As a child, you struggled to suppress a sharp temper, but the care and patient respect of a trusted community leader rubbed off on you. As you grew older, you developed a remarkable ability to keep calm and collected when performing a task you are familiar with. Choose one Craft or Profession skill. Whenever you take 10 with that skill, determine the result as if you had rolled a 12 instead of a 10. Suggested Characters: Asmodeans, Drumans, Irorans.
