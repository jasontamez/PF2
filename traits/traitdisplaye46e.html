Venom Resistance - Traits Venom Resistance Source People of the Wastes pg. 23 Category Basic (Combat) You have spent considerable time in arid wastelands where girtablilus and other venomous creatures are common, and you have built up a resistance to poison. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Fortitude saving throws against poison, and once per day when attempting a saving throw to resist girtablilu poison, you can roll twice and take the higher result.
