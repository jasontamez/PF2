Varisian Wanderer - Traits Varisian Wanderer Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 22 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia You were raised among or have spent time with a group of Varisian nomads, whether travelers, Sczarni criminals, or entertainers, and have learned their ways. Choose one of the following skills: Perform (choose one type), Profession (fortuneteller), or Sleight of Hand. You gain a +1 trait bonus on this skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
