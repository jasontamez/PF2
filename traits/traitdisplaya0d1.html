Vagabond Child - Traits Vagabond Child Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 63, Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 13, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Region Requirement(s) Urban You grew up among the outcasts and outlaws of your society, learning to forage and survive in an urban environment. Select one of the following skills: Disable Device, Escape Artist, or Sleight of Hand. You gain a +1 trait bonus on checks with that skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
