Dragon-Hunted - Traits Dragon-Hunted Source Legacy of Dragons pg. 7 Category Basic (Social) You've survived being hunted by the draconic nightmares on the Shackles islands of Dahak's Fang, Dahak's Horn, or Dahak's Tooth. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Will saves against fear effects, and against any saving throw from a spell or effect created by a dragon.
