Savanna Child - Traits Savanna Child Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 63, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Region Requirement(s) Plains You were born and raised on the rolling plains or savannas. You spent much of your youth exploring these vast reaches and know many of the region's secrets. Pick one of the following skills: Handle Animal, Knowledge (nature), or Ride. You gain a +1 trait bonus on checks with that skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
