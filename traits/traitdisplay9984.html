Asmodean Demon Hunter - Traits Asmodean Demon Hunter Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 63, Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 19 Category Religion Requirement(s) Asmodeus Raised in the church of Asmodeus (whether or not you are currently a follower), you've focused your indoctrinated fervor primarily on the elimination of demons. You gain a +3 trait bonus on Knowledge (planes) checks about demons and a +2 trait bonus on Will saves against mind-affecting spells and effects from demons.
