Hwan Artist - Traits Hwan Artist Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 12 Category Region Requirement(s) Hwanggot You grew up in Hwanggot, and your artistic talents were cherished and honed from an early age. You have been nurtured on the artistic spirit of Hwanggot. Choose one Perform skill. You gain a +1 trait bonus on checks with that skill, and it becomes a class skill for you.
