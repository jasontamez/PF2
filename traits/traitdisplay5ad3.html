Student of Nantambu - Traits Student of Nantambu Source Humans of Golarion pg. 31 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Mwangi You have spent time at Magaambya, the magical academy of Nantambu, absorbing the ancient knowledge taught there. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (arcana) and Spellcraft checks, and you may make these checks untrained.
