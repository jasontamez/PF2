Ghoulish Affinity - Traits Ghoulish Affinity Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 11 Category Region Requirement(s) The Darklands Ever since you recovered from ghoul fever, the scars on your arms and hands itch whenever one of these vile creatures is nearby. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Perception checks to notice undead disguised as living creatures, and a +2 trait bonus on Fortitude saves against diseases and poisons inflicted by undead.
