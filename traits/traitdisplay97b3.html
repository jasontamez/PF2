Starchild - Traits Starchild Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Elves of Golarion pg. 15, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Desna Desna sensed your love of travel and promised you would always be able to find your way home. You can automatically determine where true north is. You gain a +4 trait bonus on Survival checks to avoid becoming lost.
