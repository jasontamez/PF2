Tar-Baphon's Whisper - Traits Tar-Baphon's Whisper Source Faction Guide pg. 63 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Whispering Way You have learned the secrets of sotto voce, the eerie, dry whispering voice used to pass on cult secrets. You may cast sotto voce (page 61) as a spell-like ability three times per day as a spell-like ability. This spell-like ability is cast at your highest caster level; if you have no caster level, it functions at CL 1st.
