Scion of the Shory - Traits Scion of the Shory Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Mwangi Expanse The blood of a Shory aeromancer flows through your veins. You gain a +2 bonus on concentration checks when casting flight spells such as levitate, fly, or overland flight. Once per day, you can use message as a spell-like ability.
