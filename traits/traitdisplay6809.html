Acolyte of Razmir - Traits Acolyte of Razmir Source Inner Sea Primer pg. 18 Category Region Requirement(s) Razmiran You are an adherent of the Razmiri faith, or once were and have since renounced it. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (local) and Knowledge (religion) checks, and one of these skills is always a class skill for you. This bonus increases to +2 when dealing with specifics of the Razmiri faith.
