Indentured Blacksmith - Traits Indentured Blacksmith Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Kaoling As a former slave of the hobgoblins, you were forced to make many of the weapons and shields that your captors used in war. Choose either Craft (armor) or Craft (weapons). You gain a +1 trait bonus on the chosen skill, and it becomes a class skill for you.
