Canopy Prowler - Traits Canopy Prowler Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 22 Category Race Requirement(s) Catfolk Growing up in the Mwangi Expanse, you learned to hunt from above, where your prey seldom searches for danger. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Climb checks, and Climb is always a class skill for you. If you possess the climber racial trait, you also gain a +1 trait bonus on Stealth checks while climbing.
