Beast of the Society - Traits Beast of the Society Source Shattered Star Player's Guide pg. 5, Faction Guide pg. 63 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Druid, Pathfinder Society A druid at the Grand Lodge revealed to you secrets changing your form into that of an animal. Whenever you use your wild shape class ability to turn yourself into a Small or Medium animal, the effect lasts for 2 hours per druid level instead of 1 hour per druid level.
