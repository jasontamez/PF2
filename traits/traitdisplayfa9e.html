Charlatan - Traits Charlatan Source Gnomes of Golarion pg. 14 Category Basic (Magic) Requirement(s) Gnome Your talent for illusion goes beyond a mere knack for figments and phantasms. You can actually channel spell energy into simple illusions that augment your attempts at deception. As a swift action, you may expend one prepared spell or spell slot to gain a bonus on the next Bluff check you make, provided the check takes place before the beginning of your next turn. The bonus on this check is equal to the level of the spell consumed. You may use this ability 3 times per day. Only gnomes of nonlawful alignment may take this trait.
