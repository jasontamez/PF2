Honeyed Words - Traits Honeyed Words Source Dwarves of Golarion pg. 10 Category Religion Requirement(s) Grundinnar Your tact soothes bristling tempers and cools heated blood. You receive a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks. In addition, you receive a +1 trait bonus to the DC of any charm or compulsion that does not provide ongoing control and results in peaceful acts, such as calm emotions, sleep, or a suggestion to lay down arms.
