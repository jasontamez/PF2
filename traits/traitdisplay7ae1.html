Pirate Duelist - Traits Pirate Duelist Source Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 7 Category Region Requirement(s) Mediogalti You've rubbed elbows with pirates of all stripes in Ilizmagorti's taverns and convinced a few to show off their swordfighting skills. You gain a +1 trait bonus on attacks of opportunity made with daggers, short swords, cutlasses, rapiers, scimitars, or hook hands.
