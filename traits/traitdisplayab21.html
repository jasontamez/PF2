Flame of the Dawnflower - Traits Flame of the Dawnflower Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Qadira, Gateway to the East pg. 23, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Sarenrae You have been raised to view yourself as a blade in Sarenrae's service, or you have taken on that duty for yourself. Whenever you score a critical hit with a scimitar, you deal 2 additional points of fire damage to your target.
