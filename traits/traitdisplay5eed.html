Goblin Pirate - Traits Goblin Pirate Source Goblins of Golarion pg. 16 Category Region Requirement(s) Goblin, Shackles Although you might prefer life on board a ship, you often end up in the water for some odd reason. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Profession (sailor) and Swim checks, and Swim is always a class skill for you. You might also have a peg leg or a hook for a hand, but that's not so helpful.
