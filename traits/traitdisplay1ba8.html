Marid's Fury - Traits Marid's Fury Source Blood of the Elements pg. 15 Category Race Requirement(s) Undine Your marid ancestry is particularly strong, and magic runs thick through your veins. When you use your hydraulic push spell-like ability, it can extinguish magical fires as well as nonmagical fires as long as you succeed at an opposed caster level check (the DC is equal to 11 + the magical fire's caster level).
