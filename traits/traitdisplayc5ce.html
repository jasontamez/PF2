Magaambyan Arcana - Traits Magaambyan Arcana Source Inner Sea Races pg. 195 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Mwangi Your study of Magaambyan magic traditions allows you to add one spell with the good descriptor from the druid or cleric spell list to the spell list of an arcane spellcasting class of your choice, at the same spell level as it appears on the cleric or druid list. Pick the spell when you choose this trait.
