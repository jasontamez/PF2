Gemstone Collector - Traits Gemstone Collector Source Divine Anthology pg. 20 Category Religion Requirement(s) Yulreal You have long found focus and inspiration in the natural beauty of rare gemstones. As long as you carry bejeweled objects worth a total combined value of 200 gp x your character level, you gain a +1 trait bonus on all Will saving throws and a +2 trait bonus on a single Charisma-based skill check of your choice.
