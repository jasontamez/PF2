Darklands Delver - Traits Darklands Delver Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Tian Xia Darklands You are familiar with the byways and passages of Tian Xia's underworld. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks, and a +1 trait bonus on Survival checks made in the Darklands. One of these skills becomes class skill for you.
