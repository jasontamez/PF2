Demon Hunter - Traits Demon Hunter Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Asmodeus Raised in the church (whether or not you are currently a follower), you've focused your indoctrinated fervor primarily on the elimination of demons. You gain a +3 trait bonus on Knowledge (planes) checks about demons and a +2 trait bonus on Will saves against mind-affecting spells and effects from demons.
