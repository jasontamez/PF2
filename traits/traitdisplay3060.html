Glint-Tongued - Traits Glint-Tongued Source People of the North pg. 10 Category Race Requirement(s) Human-Ulfen You have a gift for poetic turns of phrase and vicious streams of insults. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Charisma-based skill checks made to impress or persuade other Ulfen (or, at the GM's option, other poetically-minded people), and a +2 trait bonus on Intimidate checks.
