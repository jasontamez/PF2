Shaer - Traits Shaer Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Qadira native, native Kelish speaker Qadira is a land of poetry, and you have grown up immersed in the words and language of that culture. Your facility with the language is evident to all when you speak. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy checks when speaking with anyone fluent in Kelish.
