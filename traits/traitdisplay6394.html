Enchanted by Giants - Traits Enchanted by Giants Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 12 Category Region Requirement(s) Forests-Jungle, Taiga, Wood You are familiar with the primeval magic of forestdwelling giants and have witnessed their close attention to the natural world around them, whether because your tribe engaged in trade with those giants or because you stumbled into their territory. The experience opened your eyes to the workings of nature and magic. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Survival checks and Spellcraft checks. You can always take 10 on Spellcraft checks to identify spell-like abilities. Likely Regions: Kyonin, Lands of the Linnorm Kings, Mwangi Expanse, Nirmathas, River Kingdoms, Taldor.
