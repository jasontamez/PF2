Undergrowth Specialist - Traits Undergrowth Specialist Source People of the Wastes pg. 3 Category Basic (Combat) You've trained yourself to move in and out of undergrowth with ease. The DCs of Acrobatics checks don't increase for you when you're moving through light undergrowth, and the DCs of Acrobatics checks you attempt while you're moving through heavy undergrowth increase only by 2.
