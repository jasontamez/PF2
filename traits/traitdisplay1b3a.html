World Traveler - Traits World Traveler Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 62, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 8, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Race Requirement(s) Human Your family has taken the love of travel to an extreme, roaming the world extensively. You've seen dozens of cultures and have learned to appreciate the diversity of what the world has to offer. Select one of the following skills: Diplomacy, Knowledge (local), or Sense Motive. You gain a +1 trait bonus on checks with that skill, and it is always a class skill for you.
