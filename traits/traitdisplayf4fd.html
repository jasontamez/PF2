Savage Breaker - Traits Savage Breaker Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Varisia-Korvosa You openly despise the crude Shoanti and their backward ways. You gain a +2 trait bonus on combat maneuver checks made to sunder clubs, earth breakers, klars, hide armor, primitive armor and weapons (see Ultimate Combat), and any other equipment the GM deems "barbarian-made."
