Spell Intuition Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 19 Category Basic (Faith) Blessed by a priest of a deity of magic at birth, you demonstrated a remarkable knack for guessing the nature of spells without the schooling of your instructors. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Spellcraft checks, and Spellcraft becomes a class skill for you. Suggested Characters: Gebbites, Nethys worshipers, Nexians.
