Foreign-Tongued - Traits Foreign-Tongued Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 29 Category Campaign Intrigued by the travels of one of your long-time acquaintances, you've convinced her to teach you one of the exotic languages she picked up in her journeys. Choose one of the following characters. You may select one of that character's languages as a bonus language. Additionally, you gain a +2 trait bonus on Bluff checks made to pass secret messages to the chosen character (see the description of the Bluff skill). NPC Choices: Ameiko, Koya, Sandru, Shalelu. Ameiko: Tien, Varisian Koya or Sandru: Varisian Shalelu: Elven, Goblin
