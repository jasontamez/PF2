Outcast's Intuition - Traits Outcast's Intuition Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 58 Category Basic (Magic) You are able to sense the motives of others and use that sense to bolster your magic against dispelling. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Sense Motive checks, and Sense Motive is always a class skill for you. In addition, your caster level is treated as 1 level higher for the purposes of any attempts to dispel your magic.
