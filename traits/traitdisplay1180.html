Devotee of a Dead God - Traits Devotee of a Dead God Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 14 Category Religion Requirement(s) Aroden You simply cannot and will not accept that Aroden is dead. Your faith in his teachings and religion are stronger than ever. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Diplomacy checks and Knowledge (religion) checks. One of these skills (your choice) is always a class skill for you.
