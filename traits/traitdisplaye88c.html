Scourge of the Darkness - Traits Scourge of the Darkness Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 7 Category Basic (Faith) Stories of the depredations present in the Darklands drive you to explore its shadowy depths and purge the corruption from within. You gain a +1 trait bonus on saving throws against fear while in the Darklands and a +1 trait bonus on Intimidate checks against creatures native to the Darklands.
