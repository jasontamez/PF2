Accelerated Drinker - Traits Accelerated Drinker Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 18 Category Basic (Combat) You know how to drink a potion efficiently, such as by not using your hands, tossing it in the air and catching it in your mouth, or opening it with your teeth. You may drink a potion as a move action instead of a standard as long as you start your turn with the potion in your hand.
