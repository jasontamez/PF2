Godclaw Disciple - Traits Godclaw Disciple Source Path of the Hellknight pg. 56 Category Basic (Faith) Requirement(s) Lawful alignment You worship the amalgamation of lawful deities known as the Godclaw. You gain a +2 trait bonus on Knowledge (religion) checks related to lawful deities, their clergy, their mythology, and their tenets. Additionally, Knowledge (religion) is a class skill for you. You must be of a lawful alignment to take this trait.
