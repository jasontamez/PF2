Foul Brand - Traits Foul Brand Source Champions of Corruption pg. 29 Category Drawback You have the symbol of an evil deity burned into your flesh. If the symbol is on your hand, you take a -1 penalty on Disable Device, Disguise, and Sleight of Hand checks. If the symbol is on your face, you take a -2 penalty on Bluff, Diplomacy, and Disguise checks. This does not count as a holy symbol for the purposes of a divine focus for spellcasting.
