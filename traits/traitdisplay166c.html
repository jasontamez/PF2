Stream Nomad - Traits Stream Nomad Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 9 Category Region Requirement(s) Darklands, usually under rivers Digging tunnels near a body of rushing water has taught you how to use water as a means of travel and escape. Others whisper that you must have been born with webbed feet. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Swim checks, and Swim is always a class skill for you. You increase the speed of any raft, barge, keelboat, or rowboat you pilot by 4 miles per day (1/2 mile per hour).
