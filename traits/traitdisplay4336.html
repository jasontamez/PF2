Undead Slayer (Pharasma) - Traits Undead Slayer (Pharasma) Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 64, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 1 Category Religion Requirement(s) Pharasma Instructed at a young age in the tenets of the faith of Pharasma, you view the undead as abominations that must be destroyed so their souls can journey beyond to be judged. You gain a +1 trait bonus on weapon damage rolls against undead.
