Wharf Rat - Traits Wharf Rat Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 19 Category Region Requirement(s) Ostenso You may never have been on a boat larger than a garbage scow, but you've been helping them dock all your life. You gain a +2 trait bonus on skill checks involving climbing and rope use, and a +4 trait bonus on Appraise checks to determine the value of large quantities of bulk goods without opening their containers.
