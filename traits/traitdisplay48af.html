Weed Resistance - Traits Weed Resistance Source Potions and Poisons pg. 3 Category Region Requirement(s) Kyonin You have learned how to keep your flesh out of the way of the toxic thorns and vines that are often prevalent in verdant lands. You gain a +3 trait bonus on saving throws against poison and disease abilities and effects inflicted by creatures of the plant type (such as a basidirond's spores).
