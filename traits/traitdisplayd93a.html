Belem Pirate - Traits Belem Pirate Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 14 Category Region Requirement(s) Minata The hidden coves and bays of the island of Belem have been your home throughout most of your career as a pirate, and the winding waterways of Minata have trained you well in the art of sailing. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (geography) checks and Profession (sailor) checks, and one of these skills becomes a class skill for you.
