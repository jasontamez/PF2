Inner Beauty - Traits Inner Beauty Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 220, Faiths of Purity pg. 15 Category Religion Requirement(s) Shelyn Once per day when you manifest your faith in your goddess, you gain a +4 trait bonus on a single Bluff, Craft, Diplomacy, or Perform skill check. You must choose to use this trait immediately after rolling the skill check but before the result is revealed by the GM.
