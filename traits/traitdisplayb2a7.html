Planar Wayfarer - Traits Planar Wayfarer Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 11 Category Religion Requirement(s) Onos The countless natural hazards of the planes cannot deter you from witnessing their grandeur. Once per day you can meditate on the nature of the planes for 1 hour to gain energy resistance 2 against acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic damage. This resistance lasts 24 hours or until you perform this meditation again and choose a new resistance.
