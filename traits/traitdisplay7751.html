Well Bred - Traits Well Bred Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 13 Category Region Requirement(s) Hermea You are the product of generations of selective breeding, organized by the gold dragon Mengkare. Your education is top-notch, and you have a broad base of knowledge. Unfortunately, the isolation of Hermea has also made you slightly naive. You gain a +1 trait bonus on Knowledge (history) and Knowledge (religion) checks but take a -1 penalty on all Sense Motive checks.
