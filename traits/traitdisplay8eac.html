Tracker of the Society - Traits Tracker of the Society Source Shattered Star Player's Guide pg. 5, Faction Guide pg. 62 Category Basic (Combat) Requirement(s) Ranger, Pathfinder Society Thanks to your time as a Pathfinder, you've mastered the art of tracking. You gain a +1 trait bonus on initiative checks and a +2 trait bonus on Survival checks made while traveling through any of your favored terrains. This is in addition to any bonuses you receive from your favored terrain class ability.
