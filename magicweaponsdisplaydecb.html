Reaver's Scythe - Weapons - Magic Equipment Reaver's Scythe Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 325 Aura moderate necromancy CL 12th Slot none; Price 80,318 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description The black blade of this +2 keen unholy scythe reflects faint images of the souls of those it has claimed. Whenever the scythe is used to drop a creature to -1 hit points or less, the creature must immediately make a DC 14 Will save or be instantly slain. If the reaver's scythe kills a creature, either through damage or because the creature failed its Will save, the wielder immediately receives 1 hero point. This hero point must be used within 1 hour or it is lost. The wielder cannot gain another hero point from the scythe until the first is used or lost. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, keen edge, malediction, unholy blight; Price 40,318 gp
