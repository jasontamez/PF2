Spyglass (masterwork) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spyglass (masterwork) Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 21 Price 4,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description Objects viewed through a masterwork spyglass are magnified to four times their size. Characters using a masterwork spyglass take a -1 penalty on Perception checks per 40 feet of distance to the target (instead of per 20 feet as with a normal spyglass).
