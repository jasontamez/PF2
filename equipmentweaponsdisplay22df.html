Fighting fan - Weapons - Equipment Fighting fan Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 130 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight — Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special distracting, monk Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Close; Monk Description Made to resemble a courtier's fan, this device consists of silk paper stretched and folded over a folding frame of hardened steel. Folding fans are typically wielded in pairs using a technique that distracts the opponent. The fans sport sharp tips, which can be poisoned. For a list of appropriate poisons, see Poisons on page 110.
