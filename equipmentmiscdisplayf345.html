Mastodon - Miscellaneous - Equipment Mastodon Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81 Price 2,000 gp; Weight 8 tons Category Mounts/Pets Description These cousins to elephants are larger and more powerfully built. There is a nearly hairless version that dwells in southern climates and a woolly variety adapted to cold. More temperamental than elephants, they are used primarily as mounts.
