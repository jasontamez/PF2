Greater Meditation Master - Feats Greater Meditation Master (Meditation) Source Blood of the Beast pg. 28 You are able to glean insight from your momentary meditation longer than most. Prerequisites: Wis 15, Combat Meditation, Meditation Master, character level 11th. Benefit: When you meditate, you gain a +2 insight bonus instead of a +1 insight bonus. You can split this bonus between any two d20 rolls made over the duration of the feat that you used to meditate, adding +1 to each d20 roll, or using the full bonus on any one d20 roll, adding +2 to that single roll.
