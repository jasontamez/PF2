Basic Chaokinesis - Wild Talents - Kineticist Basic Chaokinesis Source Occult Origins pg. 6 Element void; Type utility (Sp); Level 1; Burn 0 You can create a shadow that protects a target from bright light. You can also change gravity to increase a creature's carrying capacity by half or grant a creature a +4 bonus on Acrobatics checks to jump. Each benefit lasts 1 hour or until you use basic chaokinesis again.
