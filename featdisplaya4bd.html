Endure Pain (Zon-Kuthon's Kiss) - Feats Endure Pain (Zon-Kuthon's Kiss) Source Cities of Golarion pg. 42 Through violent and torturous conditioning, you have built up a resistance to pain. Prerequisites: Survived being tortured 10 or more times. Benefit: If you take nonlethal damage, you may attempt a Fortitude saving throw (DC equal to 10 + the nonlethal damage dealt) to reduce that damage by half. The Endurance feat grants a +4 bonus on this saving throw.
