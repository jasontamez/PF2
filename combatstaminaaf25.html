Devastating Strike - Combat Stamina - Feats Devastating Strike Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 117 Related Feat Devastating Strike Whenever you use Vital Strike, Improved Vital Strike, or Greater Vital Strike, you can spend 5 stamina points to increase the bonus damage to +4 on each extra weapon damage die. For weapons with more than one damage die, count all of the weapon's base damage dice together as one set.
