Blackfingers Apron - Armor - Magic Equipment Blackfingers Apron Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 250 Aura faint conjuration and transmutation CL 5th Slot armor; Price 5,240 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 bitter leather armor looks much like a blacksmith's apron or an alchemist's smock, and has many small pockets suitable for holding extracts, potions, or poisons. Once per day, the wearer can use negate aroma (lasting 1 hour). This effect suspends the armor's bitter special ability if used on the wearer. Once per day, the wearer can pull a tanglefoot bag from one of the apron's pockets, although the bag crumbles into dust if it is not used within 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, negate aroma, stinking cloud, web; Price 2,700 gp
