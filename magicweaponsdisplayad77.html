Peacekeeping Pistol - Weapons - Magic Equipment Peacekeeping Pistol Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 55 Aura faint enchantment CL 5th Slot none; Price 11,700 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description Crafted in the forges of Alkenstar, this +1 merciful pistol is ornamented with filigree of angelic creatures along the barrel, and houses intricate brass gears. Whenever the wielder scores a critical hit while using this weapon to deal nonlethal damage, the target must succeed at a DC 12 Will saving throw or be dazed for 1 round. As a swift action, a wielder with the grit class feature can spend 1 grit point to increase the DC of this save by 4. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cure light wounds, daze monster; Price 6,500 gp
