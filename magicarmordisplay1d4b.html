Elven Vengeance - Armor - Magic Equipment Elven Vengeance Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 115 Aura strong abjuration CL 12th Slot armor; Price 16,150 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Description This set of +2 elven chain grants its wearer a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws against spells, spell-like abilities, and other effects created by worshipers and minions of a specific demon lord, chosen when the armor is created. Against such foes, the armor's enhancement bonus increases to +4. The first suit of this armor was created quite recently by Perelir for her close friend Malindil as part of the ranger's vengeful crusade against Abraxas; since then, many other suits focused against other powerful demons (particularly the nascent demon lord Treerazer) have been crafted. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, detect evil, resistance; Price 10,650 gp
