Gloomstick - Miscellaneous - Equipment Gloomstick Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 24 Price 25 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This variant sunrod was developed by fetchling alchemists. When twisted as a standard action, a gloomstick begins drawing in light from the surrounding area, reducing the light level by one step (to a minimum of dim light) in a 20-foot radius. A gloomstick remains active for 1 minute before crumbling to dust. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
