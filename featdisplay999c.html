Point-Blank Shot - Feats Point-Blank Shot (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 131 You are especially accurate when making ranged attacks against close targets. Benefit: You get a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with ranged weapons at ranges of up to 30 feet. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 129 You can spend up to 6 stamina points to increase this feat's range by 5 feet for each stamina point you spent. Mythic Point-Blank Shot Source Mythic Adventures pg. 71 Your accuracy with ranged attacks is unmatched at pointblank range. Prerequisites: Point-Blank Shot. Benefit: The bonus on attack and damage rolls granted by Point-Blank Shot increases to +2. As a swift action, you can expend one use of mythic power to gain an additional bonus on these rolls equal to half your tier.
