Robe of Blending Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 216, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 527 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot body; Price 8,400 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Once per day this simple woolen robe allows you to assume the form of another humanoid creature, as if using alter self. This change lasts for 1 hour, although you can end it prematurely as a free action. While in this form, you also gain the ability to speak and understand the basic racial languages of your chosen form. For example, if you take the form of an orc, you can speak and understand Orc. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, alter self, tongues; Cost 4,200 gp
