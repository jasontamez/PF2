Cudgeler Sweep - Feats Cudgeler Sweep (Combat) Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 13 Your powerful blows can knock down your opponents. Prerequisites: Bludgeoner, Cudgeler Style, base attack bonus +5 or monk level 5th. Benefit: When you hit with a charge attack using the Cudgeler Style feat, you can immediately attempt a single trip combat maneuver check against the target with a +2 competence bonus as a free action. This provokes an attack of opportunity as normal for a trip combat maneuver.
