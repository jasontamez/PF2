Inner Light (BoA) - Feats Inner Light (BoA) Source Blood of Angels pg. 25 Even when your innate powers over light fail you, you and your allies prove vigilant against dark forces, fighting until the end. Prerequisites: Aasimar, daylight spell-like ability. Benefit: Whenever you cast your daylight spell-like ability, you do so as if your caster level were one higher. Whenever an enemy successfully counters or dispels your daylight spell-like ability, you and any allies within a 20-foot radius gain darkvision 60 feet for a number of rounds equal to your character level. This is a supernatural effect.
