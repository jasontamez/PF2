Falcon - Miscellaneous - Equipment Falcon Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 40 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Mounts/Pets Description This Tiny, long-winged hawk is a popular hunting bird among many aristocracies. Falcons are technically a subset of hawks but are considered more "noble" in many cultures. Ownership of these birds is denied to commoners in many nations with hierarchical monarchies.
