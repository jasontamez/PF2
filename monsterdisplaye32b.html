Jellyfish, Giant Jellyfish The bell of this enormous amber jellyfish is as large as a carriage. A sinister bloom of tentacles dangles and writhes below. The save DC is Constitution based. Description Unlike its smaller cousins, the giant jellyfish is an active predator that seeks out prey. Capable of slithering through narrow cracks, a giant jellyfish is a horrifying beast to encounter lurking in the hold of a flooded or sunken ship.
