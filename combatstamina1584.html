Quick Bull Rush - Combat Stamina - Feats Quick Bull Rush Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 129 Related Feat Quick Bull Rush You can spend 2 stamina points to change an attack that does not have your highest base attack bonus into a bull rush combat maneuver. You can use this combat trick only once per round, but you can use it even if you already used your attack at the highest base attack bonus to bull rush.
