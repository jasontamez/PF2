Gusting Infusion - Wild Talents - Kineticist Gusting Infusion Source Occult Adventures pg. 20 Element air; Type substance infusion; Level 1; Burn 1 Associated Blasts air, blizzard, sandstorm, thunderstorm Saving Throw Fortitude negates The wind from your infusion causes your blast to act as an instantaneous gust of wind. If your blast has a clear path, you can accept 2 additional points of burn or reduce the damage to 0 in order to cause the gust of wind effect to persist for 1 round along that path.
