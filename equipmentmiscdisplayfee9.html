Keelboat - Miscellaneous - Equipment Keelboat Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 3,000 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 1 sp Category Transport, Sea Description This 50- to 75-foot-long ship is 15 to 20 feet wide and has a dozen oars to supplement its single mast with a square sail. It requires a crew of 8 to 15 to sail and can carry 40 to 50 tons of cargo or 100 soldiers. It can make sea voyages, as well as sail down rivers (thanks to its flat bottom). It moves about 1 mile per hour.
