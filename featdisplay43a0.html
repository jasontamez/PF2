Armor Adept - Feats Armor Adept (Combat) Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 11 You are skilled at using customized armor. Benefit: Choose two armor modifications (such as deflecting or jarring). You no longer suffer the drawbacks of wearing armor with those modifications. Normal: Each type of armor modification imparts a drawback on a wearer. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times, choosing two different armor modifications each time.
