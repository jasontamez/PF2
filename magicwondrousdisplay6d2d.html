Cloak of the Jungle - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Cloak of the Jungle Source Sargava, the Lost Colony pg. 28 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot shoulders; Price 2,400 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description When this plain green cloak is worn with the hood drawn up around the head, the wearer can assume the form of a Large tree or shrub, as per the tree shape spell. The cloak is usable once per day, and the wearer may remain in tree form for up to 1 hour. The wearer can return to her normal form as a free action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, tree shape; Cost 1,200 gp
