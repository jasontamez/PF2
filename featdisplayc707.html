Terrifying Mask - Feats Terrifying Mask Source Blood of Fiends pg. 25 Your fiendish visage has made you a truly terrifying spectacle to behold, and you can force others to betray their feelings with a single look. Prerequisites: Cha 13, Monstrous Mask, tiefling. Benefit: Anytime you can make a Sense Motive check to get a hunch or detect whether someone is trustworthy or not, you can choose to instead make an Intimidate check at a -2 penalty. This ability only works on creatures of the humanoid type.
