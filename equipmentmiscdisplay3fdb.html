Darklight lantern - Miscellaneous - Equipment Darklight lantern Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 111 Price 20 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Alchemical Tools Description This lantern does not burn oil, but instead burns shadowcloy (see below). When shadowcloy is used as its fuel, this lantern creates a strange, hazy darkness that decreases the light level for 30 feet around it by one step . Unlike when shadowcloy is thrown at a single target, this haze does not decrease natural darkness to supernatural darkness. One flask of shadowcloy fuels a darklight lantern for 1 minute.
