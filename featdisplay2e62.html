Crusader's Fist - Feats Crusader's Fist (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 94 You pour divine energy into the enemy you strike. Prerequisites: Lay on hands class feature or touch of corruption class feature, Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: When you attack with an unarmed strike and hit a creature that you can harm with your lay on hands or touch of corruption feature, you can use a swift action to expend a daily use of that feature to deal its normal damage as if you had hit with the feature's normal touch attack. This extra damage is not multiplied if you scored a critical hit. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 116 When you use Crusader's Fist and confirm a critical hit, you can spend 5 stamina points to multiply the extra damage for that critical hit.
