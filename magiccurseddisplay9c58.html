Drums of Lethargy Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 345 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Weight 5 lbs. Description This set of kettle drums appears to be drums of haste. When a creature plays the drums, it affects all creatures (including the creature playing the drums) as though subject to a slow spell. A DC 14 Will save negates the effect.
