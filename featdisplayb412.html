Unfolding Wind Rush - Feats Unfolding Wind Rush (Combat) Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 106, Inner Sea Combat pg. 13 Thrown weapons fly from your hands as if blown by a gale, and you leave behind a wake of wind as you move. Prerequisites: Dex 13; Wis 13; Dodge; Mobility; Perfect Style; Quick Draw; Unfolding Wind Strike; base attack bonus +13 or monk level 13th. Benefit: When using Perfect Style and wielding thrown weapons, you can combine a full-attack action with a single move action. You must forgo the attack at your highest bonus but can take the remaining attacks as normal at any point during your movement. You can spend 1 ki point as a swift action to create a path of wind along your path of movement that functions as wind wall for 1 round; this effect does not affect your ranged attacks. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25 You can spend 2 stamina points instead of 1 ki point when using Unfolding Wind Rush.
