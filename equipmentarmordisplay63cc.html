Studded leather - Armor - Equipment Studded leather Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight 20 lbs. Armor Bonus +3; Max Dex Bonus +5; Armor Check Penalty -1 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 15%; Speed 30 ft./20 ft. Description An improved form of leather armor, studded leather armor is covered with dozens of metal protuberances. While these rounded studs offer little defense individually, in the numbers they are arrayed in upon such armor, they help catch lethal edges and channel them away from vital spots. The rigidity caused by the additional metal does, however, result in less mobility than is afforded by a suit of normal leather armor.
