Rope of Entanglement Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 318, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 529 Aura strong transmutation; CL 12th Slot none; Price 21,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description A rope of entanglement looks just like any other hempen rope about 30 feet long. Upon command, the rope lashes forward 20 feet or upward 10 feet to entangle a victim. An entangled creature can break free with a DC 20 Strength check or a DC 20 Escape Artist check. A rope of entanglement has AC 22, 12 hit points, hardness 10, and damage reduction 5/slashing. The rope repairs damage to itself at a rate of 1 point per 5 minutes, but if a rope of entanglement is severed (all 12 hit points lost to damage), it is destroyed. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, animate objects, animate rope or entangle; Cost 10,500 gp
