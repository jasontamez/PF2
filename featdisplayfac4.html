Glorious Blaze - Feats Glorious Blaze Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 9 The goddess Sarenrae welcomes all who seek good in the world into her fold, and some goblins are drawn to worship her, in part due to her incarnation as a giant ball of fire. Prerequisites: Goblin, worshipper of Sarenrae. Benefit: If the sun is visible, you can spend 1 round performing a wild dance in worship of the Dawnflower. Starting at the beginning of your next turn, allies who can see you gain a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, and saving throws. This effect lasts for a number of rounds equal to half your character level + your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1 round). At 9th level, this bonus increases to +2.
