Psychofeedback - Phrenic Amplifications - Psychic Psychofeedback (Su) (Occult Origins pg. 16): The psychic can spend 2 points from her phrenic pool to sacrifice a linked spell of 2nd level or higher. Doing so grants the psychic a +1 enhancement bonus to Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution per level of the sacrificed spell. This bonus lasts for 1 minute per psychic level.
