Universal Monster Rules Distraction (Ex) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 292, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 299, Bestiary 2 pg. 296, Bestiary 3 pg. 294, Bestiary 4 pg. 293, Bestiary 5 pg. 292 A creature with this ability can nauseate creatures it damages. Any living creature that takes damage from a creature with the distraction ability is nauseated for 1 round; a Fortitude save (DC = 10 + 1/2 creature's HD + creature's Constitution modifier) negates the effect. Format: distraction (DC 14) Location: Special Attacks.
