Goggles of Night Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 226, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 515 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot eyes; Price 12,000 gp; Weight — Description The lenses of this item are made of violet crystal. Even though the lenses are opaque, when placed over the eyes of the wearer, they enable him to see normally and also grant him 60-foot darkvision. Both lenses must be worn for the magic to be effective. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, darkvision; Cost 6,000 gp
