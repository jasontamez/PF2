Curses Tormenting Visions Source Horror Adventures pg. 140 Type curse; Save Will DC 15 Effect The target is haunted by horrible phantasmal visions that distract him at critical moments. He takes a -4 penalty on concentration checks and on saving throws against effects that would impose any of the following conditions on him: confused, dazed, nauseated, shaken, sickened, or stunned. Once per day, when alone or confronted by something novel and strange, he must succeed at a Will save or take 1d3 points of Wisdom damage (or 1d6 points of sanity damage if using the sanity system). Cure The tormenting visions curse can be removed only by remove curse followed by banishment, each cast within 1 minute of each another.
