Caydenbrew - Miscellaneous - Equipment Caydenbrew Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 5 cp/mug; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Food/Drink Description This ale is the standard beer drunk in Absalom. The recipe was said to be the last beer Cayden Cailean had before he undertook the Test of the Starstone. The hops for this beer were originally grown on the Isle of Kortos, but the massive brewery now imports from Andoran and Taldor.
