Overwhelm - Feats Overwhelm (Combat, Teamwork) Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 21 You can overwhelm large foes with sheer numbers. Benefit: You are considered to be flanking an opponent if at least one ally who also has this feat is threatening that opponent and the opponent is at least two size categories larger than the larger of you or your ally, regardless of your actual positioning. Normal: You must be positioned opposite an ally to flank an opponent.
