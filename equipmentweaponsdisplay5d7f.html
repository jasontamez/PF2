Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range 20 ft.; Type B; Special — Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Hammers; Thrown Description The dwarven sphinx hammer is a long-handled hammer with a striking head that is artfully designed to look like a ram-headed criosphinx. Most commonly found among the Pahmet, the dwarven sphinx hammer is balanced for use as both a melee weapon and a thrown weapon.
