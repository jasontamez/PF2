Stone of Weight (Loadstone) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 351, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 543 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Description This dark, polished stone reduces the possessor's base land speed by half. Once picked up, the stone cannot be disposed of by any nonmagical means-if it is thrown away or smashed, it reappears somewhere on the possessor's person. If a remove curse spell is cast upon a loadstone, the item may be discarded normally and no longer haunts the individual.
