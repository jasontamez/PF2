Black fester (vial) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Black fester (vial) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 56 Price 30 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description This black paste is often applied on orc weapons before going on raids to stymie an enemy's healing magic. Like a poison, black fester stays on a weapon until the first time it strikes an opponent. It remains in the target's body for 1 hour. A creature exposed to black fester resists magical healing; a creature trying to magically heal the target must make a DC 10 caster level check to restore any hit points to the target. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
