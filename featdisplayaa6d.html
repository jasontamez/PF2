Versatile Summon Nature's Ally - Feats Versatile Summon Nature's Ally Source Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 18 You've learned to summon a wider array of creatures. Prerequisites: Augment Summoning, Spell Focus (conjuration), Knowledge (nature) 1 rank, Knowledge (planes) 1 rank. Benefit: When you summon one or more animals, humanoids, or vermin using a summon nature's ally spell (or an effect that mimics such a spell), instead of granting them the benefit from Augment Summoning, you can instead apply one of the following templates to them: aerial, aqueous, chthonic, fiery, or primordial. If you summon multiple creatures with one casting, they must all have the same template.
