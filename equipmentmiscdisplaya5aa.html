Wing oil - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wing oil Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 165 Price 1 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description Tengus mix special salves to protect their feathers from the elements. This one-ounce vial of wing oil gives a feathered creature a +1 bonus on all saving throws to resist the effects of cold weather. Its effects last 24 hours. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
