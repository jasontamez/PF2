Night Stalker - Feats Night Stalker Source Monster Codex pg. 153, Classic Monsters Revisited pg. 49 You excel at hunting humans in the dead of night and murdering them quietly. Prerequisites: Skill Focus (Stealth), darkvision, half-ogre or ogre, size Large or larger. Benefit: While in areas of dim lighting or natural darkness, you ignore all size penalties on Stealth checks and gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls against flat-footed enemies.
