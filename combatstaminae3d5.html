Slayer's Feint - Combat Stamina - Feats Slayer's Feint Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 Related Feat Slayer's Feint When attempting a feint using Acrobatics, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Dexterity bonus. If you do, you gain a bonus on that feint attempt equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
