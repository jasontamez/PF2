Sorcerer's Robe - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Sorcerer's Robe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 219 Aura moderate evocation; CL 10th Slot body; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This dapper robe enables a sorcerer to add the effects of her 1st-level bloodline power to spells she casts. As a swift action before casting a spell, the wearer expends one use of her 1st-level bloodline power and selects one target of the spell to be affected by that bloodline power. If the spell allows a saving throw, a successful save against the spell means the bloodline power deals half damage (if it normally deals hit point damage) or is negated (if it does not). The robe can be used up to three times per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Quicken Spell-Like Ability, creator must be a sorcerer; Cost 2,500 gp
