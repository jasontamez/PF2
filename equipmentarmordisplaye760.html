Kikko - Armor - Equipment Kikko Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Ultimate Combat pg. 128 Statistics Cost 250 gp Weight 25 lbs. Armor Bonus +5; Max Dex Bonus +4; Armor Check Penalty -3 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 20%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description Kikko armor consists of a clever arrangement of hexagonal plates made from iron and sewn to cloth, granting the wearer greater flexibility than that provided by many armors that afford similar defense. The plates may be left exposed or hidden by a layer of cloth.
