Turning Shield - Phrenic Amplifications - Psychic Turning Shield (Su) (Occult Adventures pg. 64): The psychic can turn her excess spell energy into a barrier that def lects weaker spells. She can spend a number of points from her phrenic pool equal to the linked spell's level to gain the benefit of spell turning for 1 round per psychic level.
