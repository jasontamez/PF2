Weapon Adept - Feats Weapon Adept (Combat) Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 11 You are skilled at using customized weaponry. Benefit: Choose a weapon modification (such as jagged hooks). You treat weapons with that modification as being of their normal category (simple, martial, or exotic). Normal: A modified weapon is treated as one category more difficult to wield. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times, choosing a different weapon modification each time.
