Boots of Levitation Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 228, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 503 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 7,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These soft leather boots are incredibly light and comfortable, with thin soles reinforced by strips of tough hide that provide an unexpected amount of support and protection to the foot. On command, these boots allow the wearer to levitate as if she had cast levitate on herself. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, levitate; Cost 3,750 gp
