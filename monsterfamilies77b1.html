Monster Families Ogrekin Source Bestiary 2 pg. 204 The result of an unfortunate union between an ogre and a humanoid, the ogrekin (or half-ogre) is cursed with horrific malformations due to its tragic ancestry. Shunned by both parents, ogrekin tend to form unstable clans of their own, often resorting to inbreeding to sustain the collective. While good-natured ogrekin are not entirely unheard of, they are far and few between. Members Kin Seeker (CR 6), Kreegwood Stalker (CR 3), Ogrekin (CR 2), Stroud Apprentice (CR 10), Thanegraves Looter (CR 4)
