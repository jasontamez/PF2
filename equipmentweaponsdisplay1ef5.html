Crystal chakram - Weapons - Equipment Crystal chakram Source Classic Horrors Revisited pg. 8 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type S; Special — Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Description Shaped and carved from quartz or stranger subterranean crystals, these circular throwing discs have jagged razor-sharp edges. When a crystal chakram strikes a foe, the weapon shatters into tiny sharp fragments; if it misses, there is a 50% chance the chakram shatters when it hits the ground or another solid object; otherwise it can be retrieved and used again. A crystal chakram is treated as ammunition for the purpose of creating magic weapons.
