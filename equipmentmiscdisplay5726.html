Armor ointment - Miscellaneous - Equipment Armor ointment Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 103 Price 30 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This thick lubricant makes the joints of armor move more freely. Applying the ointment takes 1 minute and reduces your armor's armor check penalty by 1 (to a minimum of -1) for 8 hours. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
