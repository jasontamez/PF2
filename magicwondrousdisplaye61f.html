Helm of Fearsome Mien - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Helm of Fearsome Mien Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 244, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 306 Aura faint necromancy; CL 1st Slot head; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This metal helm is made to look like the wearer has large fangs and other features resembling those of a fearsome predator. If the wearer is a barbarian, she can use the intimidating glare rage power whenever she rages. The helm has no effect if the wearer is not a barbarian (or another class with the rage class feature). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cause fear; Cost 2,500 gp
