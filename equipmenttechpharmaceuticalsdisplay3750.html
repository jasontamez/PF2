Vitality Serum - Pharmaceuticals - Technology Vitality Serum Source Pathfinder #85: Fires of Creation pg. 61 Price 500 gp Description The glass receptacle of this injector contains 1 dose of a bright-yellow serum. When injected into a creature (this is a standard action), the substance grants immunity to low radiation for 1 hour, and a +5 alchemical bonus on all saving throws against other radiation effects for that duration. In addition, it heals 1d4 points of Strength damage caused by radiation poisoning. Vitality serum cannot cure Constitution drain caused by radiation poisoning. Construction Craft DC 25; Cost 250 gp Craft Pharmaceutical, medical lab
