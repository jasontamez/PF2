Keen Senses - Spells Keen Senses Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 230 School transmutation; Level alchemist 1, druid 1, hunter 1, investigator 1, occultist 1, ranger 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, M/DF (a hawk's feather) Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description The subject gains a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks and gains low-light vision. Subjects that have low-light vision double the distance they can see under the effects of this spell.
