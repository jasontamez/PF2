Chalice of Communal Dweomer - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Chalice of Communal Dweomer Source Tears at Bitter Manor pg. 54 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot none; Price 3,750 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Once per day, a creature can pour a potion with a duration longer than instantaneous into the chalice, then drink it as a standard action that provokes attacks of opportunity. The bearer and up to two willing allies within 30 feet gain the benefits as though they had consumed the potion; however, the duration is divided evenly between all recipients (including the bearer), rounded down to the nearest round. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, alchemical allocation, amplify elixir; Cost 1,875 gp
