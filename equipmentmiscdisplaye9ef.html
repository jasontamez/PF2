Thinker's tipple - Miscellaneous - Equipment Thinker's tipple Source Potions and Poisons pg. 23 Price 300 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This orange liquid invigorates the mind. For 1 hour after drinking this tincture, you gain a +2 alchemical bonus on Knowledge checks and can attempt untrained Knowledge checks if the DC is 15 or lower. This saps your energy, imposing a -4 penalty on Fortitude saving throws for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
