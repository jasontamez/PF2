Dwarven Thrower - Weapons - Magic Equipment Dwarven Thrower Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 154, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 471 Aura moderate evocation CL 10th Slot none; Price 60,312 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This weapon functions as a +2 warhammer in the hands of most users. Yet in the hands of a dwarf, the warhammer gains an additional +1 enhancement bonus (for a total enhancement bonus of +3) and gains the returning special ability. It can be hurled with a 30-foot range increment. When hurled, a dwarven thrower deals an extra 2d8 points of damage against creatures of the giant subtype or an extra 1d8 points of damage against any other target. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, creator must be a dwarf of at least 10th level; Price 30,312 gp
