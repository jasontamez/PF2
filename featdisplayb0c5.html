Shadow Strike - Feats Shadow Strike (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 169 You accurately strike even those you cannot clearly see. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1. Benefit: You can deal precision damage, such as sneak attack damage, against targets with concealment (but not total concealment). Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 131 You can spend 5 stamina points when you hit a creature with total concealment to deal precision damage to that creature.
