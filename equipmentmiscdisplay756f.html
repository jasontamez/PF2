Celestial censer - Miscellaneous - Equipment Celestial censer Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 87 Price 50 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Tools Description This blessed thurible holds up to 10 pieces of incense, and burns at a rate of 1 stick per hour. If a smokestick is added to the incense in the censer while it burns, creatures with the evil subtype are dazzled for as long as they remain within the area of the resulting smoke.
