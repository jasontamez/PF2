Strangler (UC) - Feats Strangler (UC) (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 121 Throttling the life out of enemies is second nature to you. Prerequisites: Dex 13, sneak attack +1d6, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike. Benefit: Whenever you successfully maintain a grapple and choose to deal damage, you can spend a swift action to deal your sneak attack damage to the creature you are grappling.
