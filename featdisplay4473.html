Monstrous Disguise - Feats Monstrous Disguise Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 15 You can disguise yourself as a monstrous humanoid. Prerequisites: Disguise 5 ranks, Knowledge (nature) 5 ranks. Benefit: You can use the Disguise skill to emulate creatures of the monstrous humanoid type, at a -5 penalty. Creating a monstrous humanoid disguise takes 1d3 hours.
