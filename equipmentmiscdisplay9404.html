Yogurt - Miscellaneous - Equipment Yogurt Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 95 Price 1 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Food/Drink Description This thick, fermented milk has a tangier taste than unprocessed milk. It may be sweetened with fruit, honey, or jam; blended with chopped herbs and oil to create a sauce; or mixed with water and salt, sugar, fruit, or mint as a drink.
