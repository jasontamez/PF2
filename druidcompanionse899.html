Animal Companions Bustard Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 178 Companion Type Animal The bustard is a sizable bird with long legs. Bustards can fly, but they do so relatively poorly and prefer to walk or run. Starting Statistics: Size Medium; Speed 20 ft., fly 50 ft. (poor); AC +2 natural armor; Attack bite (1d6), 2 talons (1d4); Ability Scores Str 13, Dex 14, Con 13, Int 2, Wis 13, Cha 5; Special Qualities low-light vision. 4th-Level Advancement: Attack Str +2, Con +2.
