Chain Mastery - Feats Chain Mastery (Combat) Source Book of the Damned pg. 178 You wield spiked chains with effortless lethality. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Exotic Weapon Proficiency (spiked chain). Benefit: You treat a spiked chain as a one-handed martial weapon for all purposes. You can still choose to wield a spiked chain as a two-handed weapon, and if you do so, you gain a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks to disarm or trip foes with your spiked chain.
