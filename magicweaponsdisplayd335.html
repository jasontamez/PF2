Marrowcracker - Weapons - Magic Equipment Marrowcracker Source Magical Marketplace pg. 15 Aura moderate necromancy CL 9th Slot none; Price 28,312 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description Marrowcracker is a +2 mighty cleaving warhammer with a haft carved to look like a stout femur. Whenever the wielder confirms a critical hit against a corporeal creature with a skeletal system, the creature struck takes 1d4 points of Strength or Dexterity damage (wielder's choice).
