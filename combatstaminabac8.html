Moonlight Stalker Feint - Combat Stamina - Feats Moonlight Stalker Feint Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 127 Related Feat Moonlight Stalker Feint When you attempt a Bluff check to feint against an opponent from whom you have concealment, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on the Bluff check equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
