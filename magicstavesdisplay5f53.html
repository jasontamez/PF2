Staff of Slumber - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Slumber Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 200, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 298 Aura moderate varies CL 9th Slot none; Price 81,766 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description Smoky spirals adorn the length of this staff, and have an almost hypnotic effect. The staff allows use of the following spells:
  • Deep slumber (1 charge)
  • Ray of exhaustion (1 charge)
  • Dream (2 charges)
  • Nightmare (2 charges)
  • Waves of fatigue (2 charges)
  • Symbol of sleep (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, deep slumber, dream, nightmare, symbol of sleep, ray of exhaustion, waves of fatigue; Cost 49,216 gp
