Lantern Light - Feats Lantern Light (Combat) Source Distant Realms pg. 33 Your thrown weapons become pure light, overcoming enemy defenses. Prerequisites: Lantern Style, Point-Blank Shot, Precise Shot, good or lawful alignment, caster level 7th or monk level 7th. Benefit: When you make a single ranged attack with a thrown weapon, that weapon temporarily transforms into pure holy light, overcoming the target's damage reduction, if any.
