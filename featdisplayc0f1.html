Unsanctioned Knowledge - Feats Unsanctioned Knowledge Source Ultimate Magic pg. 159 You have searched though forbidden texts and are privy to powerful but proscribed magic. Prerequisites: Int 13, ability to cast 1st-level paladin spells. Benefit: Pick one 1st-level spell, one 2nd-level spell, one 3rd-level spell, and one 4th-level spell from the bard, cleric, inquisitor, or oracle spell lists. Add these spells to your paladin spell list as paladin spells of the appropriate level. Once chosen, these spells cannot be changed.
