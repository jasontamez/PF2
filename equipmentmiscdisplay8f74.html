Brewer's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Brewer's kit Source Alchemy Manual pg. 13 Price 25 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Category Alchemical Tools Description A brewer's kit includes a mash tun, as well as copper kettles and piping for boiling and cooling before casking it and allowing it to ferment. It also includes a variety of dried hops and spices for flavoring.
