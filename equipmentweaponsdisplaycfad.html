Pilum - Weapons - Equipment Pilum Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 0, Adventurer's Armory pg. 4, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 179, Ultimate Equipment pg. 19 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type P; Special see text Category Ranged; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Spears; Thrown Description The tip of this heavy javelin is designed to break off and embed itself into a shield once it hits. Like ammunition, a thrown pilum that hits its target is destroyed. If you hit a shieldbearing opponent with a pilum, he loses the AC bonuses from that shield until he takes a standard action to pry the remnants of the pilum from his shield.
