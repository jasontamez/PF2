Necklace of Air Adaptation - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Necklace of Air Adaptation Source Pathfinder #122: Into the Shattered Continent pg. 29 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot neck; Price 10,000 gp; Weight — Description This necklace grants an aquatic wearer the ability to breathe air, and renders it amphibious as per the universal monster rule, allowing it to survive on land indefinitely. The necklace also gives the wearer a base speed of 30 feet (or a +10 enhancement bonus to its base speed, if the wearer's speed is already at least 30 feet). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, air breathing, expeditious retreat; Cost 5,000 gp
