Relentless Butcher - Feats Relentless Butcher (Achievement) Source Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 29 You revel in the glory of dismemberment and maiming. Prerequisites: Confirm at least 50 critical hits. Benefit: When you confirm a critical hit, that opponent must make a Fortitude save (DC = 10 + your Strength or Dexterity modifier) or be stunned for 1 round.
