Carnivore feed (per day) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Carnivore feed (per day) Source Animal Archive pg. 12 Price 5 cp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description Consisting of various kinds of raw meat that have been jerked, smoked, or salted for preservation, a day's worth of carnivore feed is adequate food for any Small or Medium meat-eating animal such as a tiger, bear, or dog. Larger carnivores might require up to 2 to 4 days' worth of carnivore feed per day.
