Band of Toxic Nullification - Rings - Magic Equipment Band of Toxic Nullification Source Potions and Poisons pg. 30 Aura moderate necromancy CL 9th Slot ring; Price 8,500 gp; Weight — Description This platinum band has a smoky quartz set neatly into it. Once per day, the wearer can speak a command word and place the quartz on her skin as a full-round action to siphon a single dose of poison from her body and into the quartz, rendering the poison inert. This effect can be used only after the wearer has been poisoned. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, absorb toxicity; Cost 4,250 gp
