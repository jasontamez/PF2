Improved Plane Shift - Feats Improved Plane Shift Source Planar Adventures pg. 30 You're an expert at traveling the various realms of the Great Beyond via plane shift. Prerequisites: Wizard level 9th. Benefit: Add plane shift to your spell list and to your spellbook as a 5th-level wizard spell. The time required for you to identify a portal's destination is reduced to 1 round. In addition, you automatically identify any plane on which you arrive. Normal: Plane shift is a 7th-level wizard spell. Special: A wizard can select this as a wizard bonus feat.
