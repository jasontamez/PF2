Blistering Feint - Feats Blistering Feint (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 130 Your foes flinch from the heat of your weapon, giving you an opportunity to slip past their defenses. Prerequisites: Combat Expertise, Improved Feint, ifrit. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on feint checks made while wielding a weapon that deals fire damage. Anytime you successfully feint a creature while using such a weapon, you may deal its fire damage to the enemy. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114 When you attempt a feint while you're wielding a weapon that deals fire damage, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on the Bluff check equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
