Azlant Pendant - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Azlant Pendant Source Gods and Magic pg. 54 Aura moderate divination; CL 5th Slot necklace; Price 3,100 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This heavy necklace is usually made of gold and looks like a large winged eye. You gain a +5 competence bonus on one type of Knowledge checks (each pendant has a different specialty). If Aroden is your patron, the pendant counts as a holy symbol. Once per day as a free action, you may add a +10 insight bonus on a single skill check you are about to roll. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, moment of prescience, creator must have 5 ranks in the appropriate Knowledge skill; Cost 1,550 gp, 124 XP
