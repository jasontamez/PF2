Fey Spell Lore - Feats Fey Spell Lore Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 83 You have learned the treacherous spells employed by the fey. Prerequisites: Cha 13, Spellcraft 1 rank, ability to cast druid spells. Benefit: Add the following spells to your druid spell list at the indicated levels: 0-dancing lights, 1st-lesser confusion, 2nd-charm person, 3rd-invisibility, 4th-bestow curse, 5th-charm monster, 6th-major curse, 7th-cloak of dreams, 8th-insanity, 9th-irresistible dance.
