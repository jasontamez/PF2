Agile - Weapons - Magic Equipment Agile Source Pathfinder #100: A Song of Silver pg. 21, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 50 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot none; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A character with Weapon Finesse can apply her Dexterity modifier to damage rolls with an agile weapon in place of her Strength modifier. This modifier to damage is not increased for two-handed weapons, but is still reduced for off-hand weapons. This weapon special ability can be placed only on melee weapons usable with Weapon Finesse.
