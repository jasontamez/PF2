Past Life Evoker - Wild Talents - Kineticist Past Life Evoker Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 26 Element universal; Type utility (Su); Level 2; Burn 0 Prerequisite samsaran Once per day, you can accept 1 point of burn to choose one simple blast wild talent belonging to any element. You gain the chosen simple blast wild talent for 1 minute, using your kineticist level - 2 to determine the blast talent's damage. The simple blast wild talent you gain in this way does not allow you to meet any prerequisites.
