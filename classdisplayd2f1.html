Alchemist - Classes Aphorite (Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 15): Add 1 foot to the range increment of the alchemist's thrown splash weapons (including the alchemist's bombs). This option has no effect unless the alchemist has selected it 5 times (or another increment of 5). Catfolk (Blood of the Beast pg. 4): Gain 1/6 of a bonus discovery. Dhampir (Advanced Race Guide pg. 98): Add +10 minutes to the duration of the alchemist's mutagens. Drow (Advanced Race Guide pg. 103): Add +10 minutes to the duration of the alchemist's mutagens. Dwarf (Advanced Race Guide pg. 13): Add +1/4 to the alchemist's natural armor bonus when using his mutagen. Elf (Advanced Race Guide pg. 23): Add one extract formula from the alchemist's list to his formula book. This formula must be at least one level lower than the highest-level formula the alchemist can create. Fetchling (Blood of Shadows pg. 7): Add +1 to Craft (alchemy) checks to craft poison and +1/4 to the DCs of poisons the alchemist creates. Gathlain (Legacy of the First World pg. 8): Add 10 minutes to the duration of the alchemist's mutagens. Gnome (Advanced Race Guide pg. 33, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 15): Add +1/2 to the number of bombs per day the alchemist can create. Goblin (Advanced Race Guide pg. 115, Goblins of Golarion pg. 31): The alchemist gains fire resistance 1. Each time this reward is selected, increase fire resistance by +1. This fire resistance does not stack with fire resistance gained from other sources. Grippli (Advanced Race Guide pg. 190): Add +1/4 to the number of toxic skin uses per day. Half-Elf (Advanced Race Guide pg. 42): Add +1 foot to the range increment of the alchemist's thrown splash weapons (including the alchemist's bombs). This option has no effect unless the alchemist has selected it 5 times (or another increment of 5); a range increment of 24 feet is effectively the same as a range increment of 20 feet, for example. Halfling (Advanced Race Guide pg. 63): Add one extract formula from the alchemist's list to the alchemist's formula book. This formula must be at least one level below the highest formula level the alchemist can create. Half-Orc (Advanced Race Guide pg. 53, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 19): Add +1/2 to the alchemist's bomb damage. Half-Orc (Horror Adventures pg. 40): Gain a +1/2 bonus on Strength checks to break objects and on sunder combat maneuver checks when under the effects of a mutagen that increases the alchemist's Strength or Constitution score. Hobgoblin (Advanced Race Guide pg. 121): Add +1/2 to the number of bombs per day the alchemist can create. Human (Advanced Race Guide pg. 73, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 23): Add one extract formula from the alchemist formula list to the character's formula book. This formula must be at least one level below the highest formula level the alchemist can create. Ifrit (Advanced Race Guide pg. 127): Add +1/2 to the alchemist's bomb damage. Kitsune (Blood of the Beast pg. 12): Gain 1/6 of a new Magical Tail feat. Any kitsune character can choose this bonus upon gaining a level in her favored class. Kobold (Advanced Race Guide pg. 133): Add +1/2 to the number of bombs per day the alchemist can create. Nagaji (Advanced Race Guide pg. 196): Add +1 on Craft (alchemy) checks to craft poison and +1/3 on the DCs of poisons the alchemist creates. Orc (Advanced Race Guide pg. 139): Add +10 minutes to the duration of the alchemist's mutagens. Ratfolk (Advanced Race Guide pg. 151): The alchemist gains +1/6 of a new discovery. Svirfneblin (Advanced Race Guide pg. 204): Add one extract formula from the alchemist's list to the alchemist's formula book. This formula must be at least one level below the highest formula level the alchemist can create. Tiefling (Advanced Race Guide pg. 170): Add +1/2 to the alchemist's bomb damage. Vanara (Advanced Race Guide pg. 206): Add +1/2 to the alchemist's bomb damage. Vine Leshy (Ultimate Wilderness pg. 21): Add 1/4 to the alchemist's natural armor bonus when using his mutagen. Wyrwood (Heroes of Golarion pg. 6): Reduce the mental ability score penalty of the alchemist's mutagens by 1/5 (minimum 0).
