Sinuous Vines - Feats Sinuous Vines Source Wilderness Origins pg. 11 Your vines can wrap around foes you grapple. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +6, gathlain. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks to grapple, and your wings count as a free hand for the purpose of grappling a foe. You can maintain a grapple with your wings and still make attacks with your other limbs. You cannot fly while maintaining a grapple with your wings.
