Lion's Heart - Feats Lion's Heart Source Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 73 Inspired by tales of Cyricas the Leaping Lion vaulting from his sickbed, you refuse to allow ability damage to hinder you. Prerequisites: Wis 15, Great Fortitude. Benefit: Choose one ability. You ignore penalties from ability damage to that ability until you have taken an amount of ability damage equal to your score in that ability. Normal: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time, choose a different ability to which its benefits apply.
