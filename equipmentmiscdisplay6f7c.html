Soothe syrup - Miscellaneous - Equipment Soothe syrup Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 101, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 184 Price 25 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description This sweet and wholesome-tasting blue liquid creates a sense of warmth and comfort. Soothe syrup coats your stomach and makes it much more difficult for you to succumb to queasiness. For 1 hour after drinking soothe syrup, you gain a +5 alchemical bonus on saving throws made to resist effects that would make you nauseated or sickened. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
