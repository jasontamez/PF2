Stoneplate - Armor - Equipment Stoneplate Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 291, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 211 Statistics Cost 1,800 gp Weight 75 lbs. Armor Bonus +9; Max Dex Bonus +1; Armor Check Penalty -6 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 35%; Speed 15 ft./10 ft. Description This armor is crafted by dwarven stonesmiths from alchemically strengthened plates of basalt. Stoneplate is heavy and unwieldy, but offers incredible protection to its wearer. It is primarily used by dwarven druids who cannot wear metal armor.
