Brawler's brew - Miscellaneous - Equipment Brawler's brew Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Price 150 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This tincture is made of thick syrup dissolved in potent alcohol. For 1 hour after drinking this tincture, you gain DR 1/slashing or piercing and can ignore pain effects. This numbing of your body causes you to take a -2 penalty on Fortitude saving throws for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
