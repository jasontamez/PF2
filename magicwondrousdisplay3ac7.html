Defoliant Polish Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 291, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 304 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 800 gp; Weight — Description This container of sickly gray paste can coat the armor of one Medium or two Small creatures. Affected objects produce dark gray fumes for the next 24 hours. These fumes cause natural plants to wilt and diminish as soon as they come into contact with the affected armor. This allows the wearer to move through terrain made difficult due to the presence of vegetation as if it were normal terrain, though the creature leaves a clear path of ruined vegetation. In addition, creatures belonging to the plant type that target someone protected by defoliant polish with a natural attack or grapple take 1 point of damage with each successful attack or each round they maintain the grapple. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, diminish plants; Cost 400 gp
