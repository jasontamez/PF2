Combat Style Master - Feats Combat Style Master (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 93 You shift between combat styles, combining them to increased effect. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, two or more style feats, base attack bonus +6 or monk level 5th. Benefit: You can switch your style as a free action. At the start of combat, pick one of your styles. You start the combat in that style, even in the surprise round. Normal: It takes a swift action to begin or switch your styles. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 115 When it is not your turn, you can spend 2 stamina points to take the free action to switch your style.
