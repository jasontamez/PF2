Zipstick - Miscellaneous - Technology Zipstick Source Technology Guide pg. 53 Price 20 gp Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge Description A zipstick is a small pen-like device used to administer a small jolt of electricity. This jolt is enough to cause 1 point of nonlethal damage with a successful touch attack. A zipstick is used most often to activate or deactivate items that function on ion-bonding technology, such as ion tape (see page 48). Construction Craft DC 18; Cost 10 gp Craft Technological Item, production lab
