Amulet of Inescapable Location Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 342, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 538 Aura moderate abjuration CL 10th Slot neck; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This device appears to prevent location, scrying and detection, or influence by detect thoughts or telepathy, as per an amulet of proof against detection and location.
