Sailing ship - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sailing ship Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 10,000 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 2 sp Category Transport, Sea Description This large, seaworthy ship is 75 to 90 feet long, 20 feet wide, and has a crew of 20. It can carry 150 tons of cargo. It has square sails on its two masts and can make sea voyages. It moves about 2 miles per hour.
