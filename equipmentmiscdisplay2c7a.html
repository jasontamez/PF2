Masterwork flippers - Miscellaneous - Equipment Masterwork flippers Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 56 Price 50 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Tools Description These sleek and aquadynamic flippers grant their wearer a +2 circumstance bonus on Swim checks to perform complex maneuvers underwater or swim through rough waters. This bonus doesn't stack with racial bonuses on Swim checks from having a swim speed.
