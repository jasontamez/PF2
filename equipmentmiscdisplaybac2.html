Tepache (cup) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tepache (cup) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 95 Price 5 cp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Food/Drink Description This mildly alcoholic beverage is made of beer, pineapple (or other tropical fruit), sugar, and cinnamon, then fermented a few days and served cold with chili powder. It is sweet and pleasant but common only in warmer climates.
