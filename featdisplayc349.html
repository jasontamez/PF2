Tenacious Transmutation - Feats Tenacious Transmutation Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 172 Your mastery of the magic of changing makes your transmutations more durable. Prerequisites: Spell Focus (transmutation). Benefit: The DC of caster level checks to dispel or remove your transmutations increases by 2; even if the spell is negated, its effects persist for 1 additional round before dissipating.
