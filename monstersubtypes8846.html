Monster Subtypes Shapechanger Source Bestiary 6 pg. 307, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 312, Bestiary 2 pg. 309, Bestiary 3 pg. 307, Bestiary 4 pg. 308, Bestiary 5 pg. 309 A shapechanger has the supernatural ability to assume one or more alternative forms. Many magical effects allow some kind of shapeshifting, and not every creature that can change shapes has the shapechanger subtype.
