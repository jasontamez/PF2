Litany of Sight - Spells Litany of Sight Source Ultimate Combat pg. 235 School divination; Level antipaladin 3, inquisitor 4, paladin 3 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S, DF Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 round Saving Throw no; Spell Resistance yes Description This litany reveals the unseen to you. You can see invisible creatures and objects within 30 feet. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.
