Telekinetic Invisibility - Wild Talents - Kineticist Telekinetic Invisibility Source Occult Adventures pg. 28 Element aether; Type utility (Sp); Level 3; Burn 0 You weave strands of aether, bending light and dampening sound; this works as invisibility except that the aetheric bending is easier to notice than normal invisibility, so your bonus on Stealth checks is halved (+10 while moving and +20 while perfectly still). However, the dampened sound allows you to avoid automatic detection via sound-based blindsense and blindsight, but you do not receive the bonus on Stealth checks from this wild talent against a creature with such abilities.
