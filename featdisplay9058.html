Buffeting Wings - Feats Buffeting Wings (Combat) Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 53 You can create a gust of wind with your wings. Prerequisites: Hover, Powerful Wings. Benefit: As a full-round action, you can beat your wings to create a blast of air as the gust of wind spell. This is an extraordinary ability. Special: While the following feats are primarily taken by strix, other creatures with natural wings can also take them at the GM's discretion.
