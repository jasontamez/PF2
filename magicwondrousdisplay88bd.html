Map of the High Ice - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Map of the High Ice Source Pathfinder #51: The Hungry Storm pg. 62 Aura moderate divination; CL 11th Slot none; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This vellum map depicts the Crown of the World, and delineates a path across the High Ice. Studying the map provides a +2 competence bonus on Survival checks to avoid getting lost and to avoid hazards in the High Ice region of the Crown of the World, as well as a +5 bonus on caravan Security checks while on the High Ice (with the exception of checks made to avoid the effects of altitude, extreme cold, or severe weather). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, creator must have 5 ranks in Survival, find the path; Cost 1,500 gp
