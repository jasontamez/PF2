Singing - Armor - Magic Equipment Singing Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 26 Aura moderate evocation CL 10th Slot none; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can be placed only on a shield. Once per day, a singing shield's bearer can command the shield to sing for 10 rounds to counter magical effects that depend on sound (as the bard's countersong ability). Once per round during this time, the shield's bearer can roll 1d20+10, treating that as the result of the shield's Perform check for countersong. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, soundburst; Price +1 bonus
