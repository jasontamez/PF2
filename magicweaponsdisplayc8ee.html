Everflowing Aspergillum - Weapons - Magic Equipment Everflowing Aspergillum Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 154 Aura faint conjuration CL 5th Slot none; Price 7,805 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description The holy water in this +1 battle aspergillum never runs dry. However, the water vanishes 1 round after leaving the aspergillum, so it cannot be collected. As a full-round action, the wielder can swing the weapon to dispense a vial's worth of holy water as a ranged touch attack with a maximum range of 10 feet and no range penalty. An everflowing aspergillum counts as a good-aligned weapon for purposes of overcoming damage reduction. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bless water, create water; Price 3,905 gp
