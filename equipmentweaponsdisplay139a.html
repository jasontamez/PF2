Bolas - Weapons - Equipment Bolas Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 33, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143, Ultimate Equipment pg. 21 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range 10 ft.; Type B; Special nonlethal, trip Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Thrown Description A bolas is a pair of wooden, stone, or metal weights connected by a thin rope or cord. You can whirl and throw this weapon to make a ranged trip attack against an opponent. You can't be tripped during your own trip attempt when using a bolas.
