Moss Cloak - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Moss Cloak Source Villain Codex pg. 165, Heroes of the Wild pg. 29 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 7th Slot shoulders; Price 18,700 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description A tangled layer of green moss grown on a thin, damp sheet of sod, this heavy cloak can be wrapped around the wearer to provide a measure of camouflage. When donned in forest or jungle terrain, a moss cloak gives its wearer a +10 competence bonus on Stealth checks. In any terrain, the wearer can remove a single poison currently affecting him once per day as a standard action. This causes the moss to turn a sickly shade of brown as it draws the toxins from the wearer's body, negating the Stealth bonus until the cloak heals itself 24 hours later. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, invisibility, neutralize poison, restoration; Cost 9,350 gp
