Boons - Asura Ranas Source Book of the Damned pg. 111 Over time, the mightiest of the asuras complete enough cycles of reincarnation and build upon the atrocities committed over multiple lives to incarnate into the most powerful of their kind: the asura ranas. Asura ranas are lawful evil demigods that range in power from CR 26 to CR 30. Each asura rana detailed below is given an obedience, but the boons they grant are less complex than those granted by archdevils, manifesting as spell-like abilities usable once per day each. 1: deeper slumber 2: night terrors 3: dream voyage
