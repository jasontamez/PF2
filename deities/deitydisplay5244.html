You can use this ability for a number of rounds per day equal to your Hit Dice; these rounds need not be consecutive, but they must be used in 1-round increments. Each round a creature begins its turn within this aura, it must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC = 10 + half your Hit Dice + your Charisma modifier) or take 1d6 points of nonlethal damage and become fatigued from extreme hunger. The target itself takes 20d6 points damage. The target and creatures in the affected areas can attempt Fortitude saves (DC = 10 + half your Hit Dice + your Constitution bonus) to reduce the damage by half, but any creature that fails this saving throw is also nauseated for 1d4 rounds.
