Kols - Deities Kols Oath-Keeper Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 320 Pathfinder Wiki Kols Details Alignment LN Pantheon Dwarven Deities Areas of Concern Duty, honor, promises Domains Glory, Law, Nobility, Strength Subdomains Inevitable, Leadership, Loyalty, Resolve Favored Weapon Hammer Symbol Lizard perched on scroll Sacred Animal(s) Lizard Sacred Color(s) Brown, green Paladin Code The paladins of Kols are skyseekers, honorable servants of rightful dwarven lords, steadfast guardians of dwarven sites, and punishers of deserters and oathbreakers. Their tenets include the following affirmations.
  • My responsibilities to my people are a sacred duty to my god. I will never willingly shirk my duties.
  • Oathbreakers and deserters undermine the bonds that hold society together. I will never allow one to go unpunished if it is within my power.
  • A promise is sacred. I will never willingly break any oath or contract.
