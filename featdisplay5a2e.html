Crowd of Bullies - Feats Crowd of Bullies (Combat, Teamwork) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 204 Your failed attempt to humiliate a foe reveals its weaknesses to your allies. Prerequisites: Half-orc. Benefit: Whenever you fail a combat maneuver check, your allies within 30 feet who also have this feat gain a +2 circumstance bonus on combat maneuver checks of the same type against the same foe for 1 round.
