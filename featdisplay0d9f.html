Musketeer's Sidestep - Feats Musketeer's Sidestep (Combat, Panache) Source Villain Codex pg. 56 You're able to dodge attacks with increased panache. Prerequisites: Dodging panache deed, swashbuckler level 3rd. Benefit: When you use the dodging panache deed, the movement granted by that deed does not provoke attacks of opportunity from any creature, and you gain a +2 dodge bonus to your Armor Class.
