Robes of Xin-Bakrakhan - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Robes of Xin-Bakrakhan Source Pathfinder #138: Rise of New Thassilon pg. 70 Aura strong abjuration and conjuration; CL 16th Slot body; Price 198,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The traditional robes of the Runelord of Wrath are known as the robes of Xin-Bakrakhan. Woven of the finest of crimson silks and accented with precious gold, the robes are surprisingly light and easy to move in. The robes of Xin- Bakrakhan aid and accentuate their wearer's spellcasting ability. They grant a +5 shield bonus and spell resistance 24, and the wearer casts all spells at +1 caster level. Two of the robe's pockets function as handy haversacks. The wearer of these robes is immune to the effects of calm emotions and can use all skills and abilities that require patience or concentration when under the effects of a rage spell or similar effect. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, calm emotions, protection from spells, shield, secret chest; Cost 99,000 gp
