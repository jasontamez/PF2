Taldan Duelist - Combat Stamina - Feats Taldan Duelist Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 25 Related Feat Taldan Duelist When you make an attack with a falcata using two hands while wearing a buckler, you can spend 3 stamina points to ignore the -1 penalty on attack rolls for using your shield arm to wield a weapon while wearing a buckler and to retain the buckler's shield bonus for 1 round.
