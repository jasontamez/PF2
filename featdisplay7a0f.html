Pack Attack - Feats Pack Attack (Combat, Teamwork) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 112 You are skilled at surrounding your enemies. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1. Benefit: When you are adjacent to an ally with this feat, the first time you melee attack an opponent, you can spend an immediate action to take a 5-foot step, even if you have otherwise moved this round. Normal: You can take a 5-foot step only if you have not otherwise moved in a round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 128 When you are adjacent to an ally that also has this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to take a 5-foot step as a free action instead of an immediate action. You can still use this feat's ability only once per round.
