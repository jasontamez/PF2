Elven Battle Training - Combat Stamina - Feats Elven Battle Training Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119 Related Feat Elven Battle Training When you are wielding a traditional elven weapon as defined in the feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to make one additional attack of opportunity with that weapon. This stacks with additional attacks of opportunity granted by this feat, Combat Reflexes, and similar abilities.
