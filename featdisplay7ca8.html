Warren Digger - Feats Warren Digger Source Monster Codex pg. 81, Classic Horrors Revisited pg. 32 After years spent digging up graves, you are able to move through dirt almost as easily as a fish through water. Prerequisites: Ghoul. Benefit: You gain a burrow speed of 10 feet through earth, sand, or soil. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time you take it, add 10 feet to your burrow speed, up to a maximum of your base land speed.
