Spit Venom - Feats Spit Venom (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 197 You have mastered the nagaji warrior technique of spitting venom into your opponent's eyes. Prerequisites: Nagaji. Benefit: As a full-round action, you can spit poison up to 10 feet as a ranged touch attack. If you hit, the target must make a successful Fortitude save or be blinded for 1d6 rounds. The DC of this save is equal to 10 + 1/2 your total Hit Dice + your Constitution modifier. You can use this ability once per day plus one additional time per day for every three Hit Dice you have. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 When you spit poison, you can spend up to 5 stamina points. For each stamina point spent, you can increase the range of the attack by 5 feet.
