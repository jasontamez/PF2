Tap Inner Beauty - Spells Tap Inner Beauty Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 243, Faiths of Purity pg. 29 School divination; Level bard 1, cleric 1, oracle 1, skald 1, warpriest 1, witch 1 (Shelyn) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, M (a tiny mirror) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level Description This spell allows you to tap into the natural beauty of your soul and let it shine from your eyes and your speech. While the spell is active, you gain a +2 insight bonus on all Charisma ability checks and Charisma-based skill checks.
