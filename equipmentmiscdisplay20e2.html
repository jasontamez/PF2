Guard's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Guard's kit Source Villain Codex pg. 44 Price 20 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Category Kits Description The guard issues a standard set of supplies to each of its members. This kit consists of a backpack, a belt pouch, a flint and steel, manacles, 50 feet of rope, a signal whistle, soap, and 5 torches.
