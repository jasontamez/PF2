Field Medic's Breastplate Source Champions of Purity pg. 31 Aura moderate abjuration, faint conjuration CL 7th Slot armor; Price 52,900 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Description This battered yet lovingly polished +1 breastplate is marked by three chipped glass circles set over the heart, each of a different color. Three times per day, the wearer may tap a circle to activate it, enveloping her in a shimmering aura that protects her from harm. The wearer gains DR 10/- and is protected if she were under the effects of a globe of invulnerability spell. The effect ends as soon as the wearer makes an offensive action or casts an offensive spell, or after 1 minute. In addition, whenever the wearer casts a cure spell or administers a cure potion while under this effect, she may add one additional die to the total amount of damage healed. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cure light wounds, sanctuary; Price 26,450 gp
