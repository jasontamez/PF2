Monster Subtypes Augmented Source Bestiary 6 pg. 302, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 311, Bestiary 2 pg. 306, Bestiary 3 pg. 304, Bestiary 4 pg. 304, Bestiary 5 pg. 304 A creature receives this subtype when something (usually a template) changes its original type. Some creatures (those with an inherited template) are born with this subtype; others acquire it when they take on an acquired template. The augmented subtype is always paired with the creature's original type.
