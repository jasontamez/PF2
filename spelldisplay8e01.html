Spells Ash Storm Source Ultimate Magic pg. 207 School conjuration (creation) [fire]; Level arcanist 3, druid 3, hunter 3, sorcerer 3, witch 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M/DF (a pinch of ash) Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Area cylinder (40-ft. radius, 20 ft. high) Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description Driving ash blocks all sight (even darkvision) within the spell's area of effect, and falling cinders cause the ground in the area to become difficult terrain.
