Aspergillum Cloak - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Aspergillum Cloak Source Black Markets pg. 22 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot shoulders; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This drab brown, hooded cloak acts as a cloak of resistance +1 but also quickly absorbs any liquids poured onto it, drying in moments. Three times per day, the wearer can pour a vial of holy water onto the interior lining of an aspergillum cloak, causing its outer surface to glisten and drip. The holy water cannot be recovered once absorbed. For 7 minutes, the garment is soaked in holy water, dealing 2d4 points of damage to any undead or evil outsider that strikes the wearer with an unarmed strike or a natural weapon. An aspergillum cloak can also absorb unholy water instead, dealing damage to good outsiders instead. An aspergillum cloak gains no special abilities from absorbing any other liquid. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bless water; Cost 4,000 gp
