Peasant's outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Peasant's outfit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 1 sp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Clothing Description These clothes are of poor quality, the fabric little more than sack cloth, often patched and mended many times over. A peasant's outfit consists of a loose shirt and baggy breeches, or a loose shirt and skirt or overdress. Cloth wrappings are used for shoes, causing significantly more wear on the feet.
