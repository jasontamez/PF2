Chupar Pick - Weapons - Magic Equipment Chupar Pick Source Mystery Monsters Revisited pg. 12 Aura moderate evocation and transmutation CL 7th Slot none; Price 24,304 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description A curved yellow fang juts from the wooden haft of this light pick in place of a metal tip. Though the fang seems fragile, the enchantments on the weapon render it as hard as steel, and the fang evokes some of the powers of the beast from which it came. A chupar pick functions as a +1 light pick of wounding. When the chupar pick scores a critical hit, its wielder is affected by a haste effect for 7 rounds as if targeted by a haste spell. Subsequent critical hits made while the wielder is affected extend the duration of the haste effect but have no additional effect. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bleed, haste, one chupacabra fang; Price 12,304 gp
