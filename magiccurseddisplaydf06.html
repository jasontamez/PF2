Heavy Hammer - Cursed Items - Magic Equipment Heavy Hammer Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 346 Aura moderate transmutation CL 8th Slot none; Weight 20 lbs. Description This massive warhammer is even heavier than its size would normally warrant. A creature wielding the hammer takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls and a +4 bonus on damage rolls, as well as a -10 penalty to speed.
