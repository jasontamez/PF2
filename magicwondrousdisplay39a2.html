Traveling Master's Turban - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Traveling Master's Turban Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 107 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot head; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This turban consists of 30 feet of cloth held in place by a brooch. The owner can command the turban to tie or untie itself as a move action. When the turban is tied on the wearer's head, the wearer gains the benefits of endure elements. Once per day when the turban is untied, the owner can use the fabric to perform a rope trick (as per the spell rope trick). In addition, the owner can spend 1 ki point as a swift action to cast animate rope. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, animate rope, endure elements, rope trick; Cost 2,000 gp
