Chameleon suit - Armor - Technology Chameleon suit Source Technology Guide pg. 30 Statistics Cost 15,000 gp Type Light; Weight 5 lbs.; Armor Bonus +2 Max Dex Bonus +6; Armor Check Penalty 0 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 10% Speed (30 ft.) 30 ft.; Speed (20 ft.) 20 ft. Capacity 50 ; Usage 1 charge/minute Description This suit of light woven-fiber armor covers the wearer from ankle to wrist and includes a hooded cowl. Its surface consists of millions of tiny, scale-like lenses and LED screens. When powered on, a chameleon suit analyzes its environment and generates digital camouflage. The wearer gains a +10 competence bonus on Stealth checks and can hide in plain sight (as a ranger in his favored terrain).
