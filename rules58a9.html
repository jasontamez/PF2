Converting Existing Characters Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 67 When converting to the consolidated skills system in the middle of an ongoing campaign, it's typically best to allow each player to completely reset her character's skill ranks and spend them again from scratch. She should also be able to retrain any feats or other abilities that affect her skill bonuses.
