Diva Style - Feats Diva Style (Combat, Style) Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 176 You employ song, dance, or some other method of performance to distract your enemies from the battle at hand. Prerequisites: Cha 13; base attack bonus +1 or bard level 1st. Benefit: While using this style, you can use any Perform skill in place of Bluff to feint in combat. You can attempt a Perform check to feint as a move action when you begin a bardic performance.
