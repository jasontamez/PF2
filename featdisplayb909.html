Domain Strike - Feats Domain Strike (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 98 You unleash a domain power upon your enemy as part of your unarmed strike. Prerequisites: Domain class feature, Improved Unarmed Strike. Benefit: When you gain this feat, choose one domain-granted power that you can use to affect no more than one opponent. If you make a successful unarmed strike against an opponent, in addition to dealing your unarmed strike damage, you can use a swift action to deliver the effects of the chosen granted power to that opponent. Doing so provokes no attacks of opportunity. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you take it, you apply it to a different qualifying domain power. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118 You can spend 2 stamina points to apply the effects of your chosen domain power as a free action instead of a swift action.
