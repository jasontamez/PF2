Kunai - Weapons - Equipment Kunai Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 18 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range 10 ft.; Type B or P; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Simple Description Crafted from a single piece of iron, a kunai has a leaf-shaped blade and a ring for a pommel. Adapted from the common trowel, the kunai has a thick blade that is sharpened only near the tip. Unlike finer weapons, the kunai can readily substitute for a crowbar or piton without being damaged in the process.
