Expanded Enhance Arrows - Feats Expanded Enhance Arrows Source Chronicle of Legends pg. 8 Your magical arrows produce a wider range of powers. Prerequisites: Enhance arrows class feature. Benefit: You add corrosive to the list of special properties you can grant your nonmagical arrows with your enhance arrows ability at 3rd level. At 5th level, you can add the ghost touch, limning, or planar special ability instead of distance. At 7th level, you can add the corrosive burst special ability.
