Bomber's Eye - Spells Bomber's Eye Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 206 School transmutation; Level alchemist 1, investigator 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 round/level Description This extract allows you to throw weapons farther and more accurately. While this extract is in effect, increase the range of any thrown weapon by 10 feet. In addition, you receive a +1 insight bonus on attack rolls made with thrown weapons.
