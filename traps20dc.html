Spring-arm Spike Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 173 Associated Terrain any forests, jungles, or swamps Type mechanical; Perception DC 20; Disable Device DC 20 Effects Trigger location; Reset manual Effect A spring-arm spike is similar to a spring snare trap in that it incorporates a strong sapling bent into a taut arc. When a creature enters a square that contains the spring-arm spike's trigger, the sapling snaps forward, driving a pointed spear into the target. The spring-arm spike makes a melee attack +15 (2d10+4/??3) against the target, which is also knocked prone unless it succeeds at a DC 20 Reflex save.
