Blighted Critical Mastery - Feats Blighted Critical Mastery (Critical) Source Ultimate Magic pg. 143 You control the type of spellblight your critical hits give your opponent. Prerequisites: Blighted Critical, caster level 9th. Benefit: Whenever you apply a spellblight by way of the Blighted Critical or Greater Blighted critical feat, you can choose the spellblight you apply rather than determining it randomly.
