Uncanny Defense - Combat Stamina - Feats Uncanny Defense Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 135 Related Feat Uncanny Defense When fighting defensively or taking the total defense action, if you attempt a Reflex saving throw or are the target of a combat maneuver, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Dexterity bonus to gain a bonus on the Reflex save or to your CMD equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
