Moon Clock - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Moon Clock Source Blood of the Moon pg. 31 Aura moderate divination and illusion; CL 13th Slot none; Price 2,650 gp; Weight — Description This intricate mechanism opens like a pocket watch to reveal a set of dials indicating the current phase of the moon, as well as the previous and upcoming phases. A moon clock can be destroyed as a standard action to release a powerful blast of lunar energy in a 30-foot-radius burst. Lycanthropes in the area of this blast must succeed at a DC 20 Will save or revert to their humanoid forms. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, divination, lunar veil; Cost 1,325 gp
