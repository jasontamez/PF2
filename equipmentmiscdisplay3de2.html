Ritual bell - Miscellaneous - Equipment Ritual bell Source Occult Adventures pg. 250 Price 2 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This large, handheld silver bell has a long wooden handle. Ritual bells are typically used for ceremonial purposes, often as a means of getting the attention of large audiences. Each ritual bell is specifically engraved, often denoting its origin with a particular cult or sect.
