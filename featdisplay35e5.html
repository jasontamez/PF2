Shared Insight - Feats Shared Insight Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 169 You deftly direct others' attentions where you wish. Prerequisites: Wis 13, half-elf. Benefit: As a move action, you can grant all friendly creatures within 30 feet who can see or hear you a +2 bonus on Perception checks for a number of rounds equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1 round).
