Ring of Jumping (improved) - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Jumping (improved) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 172, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot ring; Price 10,000 gp; Weight — Description This ring functions as a ring of jumping, except it grants the wearer a +10 competence bonus all her Acrobatics checks made to make high or long jumps.
