Seven-Sided Coin - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Seven-Sided Coin Source Pathfinder: Runescars #4 pg. 27 Aura moderate necromancy; CL 9th Slot none; Price 5,000 gp; Weight — Description This brass coin is smooth on both faces. Once per week when touched to a creature, it affects that creature with a mark of justice (CL 9th). The user chooses the behavior that activates the mark, but it must be one of the seven deadly sins (greed, lust, pride, gluttony, envy, wrath, or sloth). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, mark of justice; Cost 2,500 gp
