Twinned Feint - Feats Twinned Feint (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 158 You can fool two opponents with a single feint. Prerequisites: Cha 13. Benefit: As a standard action, you can attempt to feint against a foe within your reach. If you succeed at the feint, you can make an additional feint attempt against a foe that is adjacent to the first one and also within reach. You can attempt only one additional feint per action with this feat. When you use this feat, you take a -2 penalty to your AC until your next turn. Special: If you have the Improved Feint feat, you can use this feat as a move action instead. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 134 After succeeding at the first feint while using this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to attempt a second feint against any foe within your reach, even if it's not adjacent to the first foe.
