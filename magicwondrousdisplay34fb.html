Pugwampi Braid - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pugwampi Braid Source Monster Codex pg. 93 Aura moderate necromancy; CL 8th Slot none; Price 9,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This braid, crafted from strips of pugwampi pelts, is 1 inch in diameter and 12 inches long. It can be carried in the hand like a riding crop or worn as an adornment. Once per day with a command word, the owner of the braid can create a 5-foot-radius aura around the braid for 1 minute. Creatures other than the owner within the aura must roll twice and use the lower result whenever a situation calls for a d20 roll. This is a mind-affecting effect that does not work on humanoids with the gnoll subtype, animals, or gremlins. A creature under the effect of a luck bonus is immune to the aura. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bestow curse; Cost 4,500 gp
