Improved Steal - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Steal Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 124 Related Feat Improved Steal You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. When you are the target of a steal combat maneuver, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Strength or Dexterity bonus, whichever is greater. Your CMD against that steal attempt increases by that number.
