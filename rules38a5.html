Summoner Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 91 A character who chooses summoner as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features. Summon Monster: At 3rd level, he gains the summoner's summon monster spell-like ability as a summoner of his character level - 2. He can use this ability once per day; the casting time is 1 full round, and the duration is 1 round per effective summoner level. Eidolon: At 7th level, he gains the ability to summon an eidolon, using the statistics of an eidolon for a summoner of his character level - 4, except with half as many evolution points. This eidolon can only be summoned using his summon monster spell-like ability. Additional Summons: At 11th level, he can use his summon monster spell-like ability three times per day. Shield Ally: At 15th level, he gains the shield ally class feature. Aspect: At 19th level, he gains the aspect class feature, except he can divert only 1 evolution point from his eidolon to himself.
