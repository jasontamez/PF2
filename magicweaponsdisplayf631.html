Sword of Life Stealing - Weapons - Magic Equipment Sword of Life Stealing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 162, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 471 Aura strong necromancy CL 17th Slot none; Price 25,715 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This black iron +2 longsword bestows a negative level when it deals a critical hit. The sword wielder gains 1d6 temporary hit points each time a negative level is bestowed on another. These temporary hit points last for 24 hours. One day after being struck, subjects must succeed at a DC 16 Fortitude save for each negative level gained or any such levels become permanent. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, enervation; Price 13,015 gp
