Ferocious Horde - Feats Ferocious Horde (Teamwork) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 206 Seeing a fellow orc bleed makes you fight harder. Prerequisites: Orc, ferocity racial trait. Benefit: Whenever you are at or below half of your full hit points and an orc ally within 30 feet who also has this feat is at or below half of her full hit points, you gain a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls.
