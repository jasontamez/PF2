Clockwork Puppet - Wild Talents - Kineticist Clockwork Puppet Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 26 Element earth; Type utility (Su); Level 5; Burn 0 Prerequisite metal blast You can weave metal into clockwork parts, creating clockwork constructs to attack your foes. This functions as the aether puppet wild talent, except the object animated gains the clockwork subtype and cannot have the stone Construction Point ability.
