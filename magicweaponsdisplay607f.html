Smuggler's Sling - Weapons - Magic Equipment Smuggler's Sling Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 29 Aura faint conjuration CL 5th Slot none; Price 5,300 gp; Weight — Description Smuggler's slings are favored in Chu Ye, where they are employed by freedom fighters sneaking contraband weapons past oni slave masters. The wielder of this +1 sling gains a +5 competence bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal the sling. Up to five times per day, the wearer can load the sling with a conjured bullet as a swift action. A conjured bullet counts as magical for the purposes of overcoming damage reduction and attacking incorporeal creatures, but is otherwise a standard sling bullet. This bullet must be fired by the end of the round or it vanishes. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, reloading hands; Price 2,650 gp
