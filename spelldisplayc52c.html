Underbrush Decoy - Spells Underbrush Decoy Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 228 School transmutation; Level druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1, shaman 1 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one non-creature plant of size Tiny, Small, or Medium Duration 1 round Saving Throw Will negates (object); Spell Resistance yes (object) Description You cause a plant to rustle noisily, distracting nearby creatures. You can attempt a Bluff check to create a distraction to hide, using your caster level + your Wisdom modifier in place of your total Bluff skill bonus and applying the result to all creatures within 30 feet. You count the target as distracted, as do any creatures that knew about your distraction in advance. Creatures might not be distracted if they detect you casting the spell or otherwise anticipate your subterfuge.
