Necrotic talisman - Miscellaneous - Equipment Necrotic talisman Source Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 213 Price 500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description These unholy symbols have been desecrated by clerics of Urgathoa and imbued with negative energy. When worn by an undead creature with an Intelligence score, a necrotic talisman provides +1 turn resistance.
