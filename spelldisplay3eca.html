Enhanced Diplomacy - Spells Enhanced Diplomacy Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 24 School divination; Level cleric 0, druid 0, hunter 0, oracle 0, warpriest 0 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration 1 minute or until discharged Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You imbue the subject with divine diplomacy skills. The creature gets a +2 competence bonus on a single Diplomacy or Intimidate check. It must choose to use the bonus before making the roll to which it applies.
