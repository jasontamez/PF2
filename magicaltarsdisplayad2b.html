Altar of Rovagug - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Rovagug Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 249 Aura moderate abjuration and transmutation [chaotic, evil] CL 10th Slot none; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 150 lbs. Description This altar is usually built around the lip of a pit that resembles a linked ring of claws and fangs made from copper. Praying at the altar grants a +1 bonus on damage rolls and a +2 bonus on attack rolls to confirm critical threats. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, keen edge, magic circle against good, magic circle against law, creator must worship Rovagug; Cost 5,000 gp
