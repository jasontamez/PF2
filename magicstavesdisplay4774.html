Staff of Many Rays - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Many Rays Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 198, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 297 Aura moderate evocation and necromancy CL 11th Slot none; Price 57,200 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description The elaborate swirls and delicate flecks of shining metal adorning the head of this pale wooden shaft make it seem almost as much a long mace as a staff. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Ray of enfeeblement (1 charge)
  • Ray of exhaustion (1 charge)
  • Scorching ray (1 charge)
  • Enervation (2 charges)
  • Disintegrate (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, disintegrate, enervation, ray of enfeeblement, ray of exhaustion, scorching ray; Cost 28,600 gp
