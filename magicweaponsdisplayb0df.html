Dimensional Grenade Source Pathfinder #71: Rasputin Must Die! pg. 60 Aura moderate conjuration CL 7th Slot none; Price 1,450 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This M1914 concussion grenade (see page 66) can be thrown up to 680 feet away, as it teleports itself directly to the target location when thrown. This allows a dimensional grenade to be thrown even at targets behind cover, including total cover. The wielder does not need to attempt an attack roll; the dimensional grenade simply appears in the target location and detonates at the beginning the wielder's next turn. A dimensional grenade deals 3d6 points of force damage in a 15-foot radius (Reflex DC 15 for half). In addition, any creature damaged by the grenade's blast must succeed at a DC 16 Will save or be dazed for 1 round. The grenade is consumed in the attack. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, dimension door, mass daze; Price 750 gp
