Good Hope - Spells Good Hope Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 291 School enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting]; Level bard 3, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Targets one living creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart Duration 1 min./level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description This spell instills powerful hope in the subjects. Each affected creature gains a +2 morale bonus on saving throws, attack rolls, ability checks, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls. Good hope counters and dispels crushing despair.
