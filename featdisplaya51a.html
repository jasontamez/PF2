Low Profile - Feats Low Profile (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 164 Yours small stature helps you avoid ranged attacks. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Small size or smaller. Benefit: You gain a +1 dodge bonus to AC against ranged attacks. In addition, you do not provide soft cover to creatures when ranged attacks pass through your square. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125 When targeted by a ranged attack, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Dexterity bonus. For each stamina point you spend in this way, your dodge bonus to AC against that attack increases by 1.
