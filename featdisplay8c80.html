Wrist Grab - Feats Wrist Grab (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 22 When an enemy overextends to attack you, you quickly catch its arm and knock its weapon loose. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Int 13, Combat Expertise, Combat Reflexes, Improved Disarm, Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: When an opponent's attack misses you by 5 or more, you can attempt a disarm combat maneuver check against that foe as an immediate action.
