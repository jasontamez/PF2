Illusory Maze - Spells Illusory Maze Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 41 School illusion (figment); Level arcanist 2, bard 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Area 20-ft.-radius spread Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will disbelief (if interacted with); Spell Resistance yes Description This spell creates the illusion of a translucent, ever-changing maze of glass in the target area. Creatures in the area who believe the maze to be real cannot move more than 5 feet continuously in any one direction and can't move diagonally, forcing them to take a circuitous route to their destination.
