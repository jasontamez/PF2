Four-mirror - Armor - Equipment Four-mirror Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Ultimate Combat pg. 128 Statistics Cost 125 gp Weight 45 lbs. Armor Bonus +6; Max Dex Bonus +2; Armor Check Penalty -5 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 30%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description This armor consists of four plates harnessed together with leather shoulder straps. Two round plates protect your front and back, while two smaller rectangular plates cover the sides of the torso. Four-mirror armor is worn over chainmail to provide added protection, and comes with a spiked helmet with a chainmail hood.
