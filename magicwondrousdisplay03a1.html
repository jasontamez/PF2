Wavecutter's Figurine - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Wavecutter's Figurine Source Pathfinder #124: City in the Deep pg. 81 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This rune-carved wooden statuette of a fish feels very slippery. When placed at the helm of any Large water vehicle (such as a rowboat or ship's boat), the friction between the boat and water is reduced considerably, allowing the boat to glide through the water more smoothly and swiftly. The boat gains a foot enhancement bonus to its maximum speeds. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, touch of the sea; Cost 3,000 gp
