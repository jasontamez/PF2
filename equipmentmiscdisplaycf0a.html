Harrow mat - Miscellaneous - Equipment Harrow mat Source The Harrow Handbook pg. 30 Price 80 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Entertainment Description This thick leather mat is large enough to fit all the cards necessary for a standard harrow reading. If you use a harrow mat as part of a reading, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus on Bluff or Diplomacy checks (harrower's choice) to convince the subjects of your harrowing that your words are true.
