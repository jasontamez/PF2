Shrillshaft javelin - Weapons - Equipment Shrillshaft javelin Source Gnomes of Golarion pg. 22 Statistics Cost 35 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range 30 ft.; Type P; Special see text Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Description This single-use javelin bears a thunderstone in its haft, just behind the weapon's head. If you hit with one of these javelins, the target takes javelin damage as normal and is the center of the thunderstone's effect. If you miss, roll to see where the javelin lands (as if determining a miss with a splash weapon); that square becomes the center of the thunderstone's effect.
