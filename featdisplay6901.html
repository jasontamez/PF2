Ferocious Resolve - Feats Ferocious Resolve Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 57 Your orc heritage allows you to fight on. Prerequisites: Con 13, half-orc, orc ferocity racial trait. Benefit: You gain the ferocity universal monster ability, allowing you to continue fighting at negative hit points. When using this ability, you gain a +2 bonus on Intimidate checks. Normal: A half-orc with the orc ferocity racial trait can fight for 1 more round after he is brought below 0 hit points.
