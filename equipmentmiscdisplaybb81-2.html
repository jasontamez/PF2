Miscellaneous - Equipment Thieves' tool extenders Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 25 Price 70 gp (common), 820 gp (mithral); Weight 3 lbs. (common), 1 1/2 lbs. (mithral) Description These long-reaching extenders can be attached to or detached from thieves' tools as a standard action, allowing the user to make Disable Device checks on devices in any space he could reach with a reach weapon. High-quality mirrors on the shafts let him see what he's doing, but he takes a -4 penalty on Disable Device checks when working on a device more than 5 feet away.
