Bolstering - Armor - Magic Equipment Bolstering Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 116 Aura faint enchantment CL 5th Slot armor quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description Anytime the wearer of bolstering armor damages a creature with a melee or ranged attack, he gains a +2 competence bonus on saving throws against that creature's abilities until the start of his next turn. The bolstering ability can be applied to medium armor, heavy armor, light shields, heavy shields, and tower shields, but not light armor or bucklers.
