Sack - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sack Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 1 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This heavy canvas sack, useful for carrying adventuring gear and other supplies, holds about 4 cubic feet. A leather thong threaded through loops at the bag's end allows it to be tied shut easily.
