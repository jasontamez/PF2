Staff of Courage - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Courage Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 195, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 54 Aura moderate enchantment CL 8th Slot none; Price 20,800 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This sturdy-looking hickory staff is capped with a figurine of an angel or other benevolent spirit, wings and arms outstretched as if in welcome. This staff allows use of the following spells:
  • Bless (1 charge)
  • Remove fear (1 charge)
  • Prayer (2 charges)
  • Remove paralysis (2 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, bless, prayer, remove fear, remove paralysis; Cost 10,400 gp
