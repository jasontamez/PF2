Self-Sufficient - Feats Self-Sufficient Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 133 You know how to get along in the wild and how to effectively treat wounds. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Heal checks and Survival checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in one of these skills, the bonus increases to +4 for that skill. Mythic Self-Sufficient Source Mythic Adventures pg. 72 Independence comes naturally to you, and you have mastered taking care of yourself. Prerequisites: Self-Sufficient. Benefit: The bonus on Heal and Survival skill checks from Self-Sufficient increases by 2. In addition, you can expend one use of mythic power to treat a Heal or Survival check as if you had rolled a natural 20. You must decide to use this ability before making the roll.
