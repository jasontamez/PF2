Redistributed Might - Feats Redistributed Might (Combat) Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 26 You've gained enough control over your body to translate strength into swiftness. Prerequisites: Con 13 or Wis 13, Enhanced Morale, Exotic Weapon Proficiency (Aldori dueling sword), Iron Will, base attack bonus +4. Benefit: Whenever an effect would grant you an enhancement or morale bonus to your Strength score (such as via bull's strength or a skald's inspired rage performance), you can instead apply that bonus to your Dexterity score. You make this choice when you first gain the bonus. The bonus type does not change.
