Harrying Partners - Combat Stamina - Feats Harrying Partners Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 20 Related Feat Harrying Partners When using the aid another action to help an ally who also has this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to grant a +4 bonus instead of the normal +2. Alternatively, you can grant your ally a +2 bonus both on her next attack roll and to her AC against the next attack against her. (Melee Tactics Toolbox 10)
