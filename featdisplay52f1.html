Terrifying Strike - Feats Terrifying Strike Source Pathfinder #81: Shifting Sands pg. 73 Your touch spreads fear. Prerequisites: Despair special ability, mummy. Benefit: Any creature struck by your slam attack must succeed at a Will save at the same DC as your despair ability or be shaken for 1d4 rounds. If the creature is already shaken, frightened, or panicked, it is paralyzed for 1 round instead. This is a mind-affecting fear effect.
