Disheartening Display - Feats Disheartening Display (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 144 Your show of prowess further demoralizes others. Prerequisites: Dazzling Display, Weapon Focus, base attack bonus +6, proficiency with chosen weapon. Benefit: When you successfully use Dazzling Display against any shaken, frightened, or panicked opponents, their fear increases by one step. An already panicked creature demoralized by this feat cowers. Once affected by this feat, a creature cannot be affected by it again (by you or anyone else) for 24 hours. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118 When you affect a creature with this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to gain the ability to affect that creature with this feat a second time during the same 24-hour period.
