Amulet of Eldest's Blessing (Gnome) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Amulet of Eldest's Blessing (Gnome) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 228 Aura strong abjuration; CL 13th Slot neck; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The following wondrous item is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity. This crystalline amulet holds a fragment from the First World domain of one of the Eldest. A gnome wearing the amulet does not count as extraplanar on the First World. The wearer heals 1 point of lethal damage every minute and an equal amount of nonlethal damage, up to a maximum each day equal to her Hit Dice or character level. Finally, she gains a +4 insight bonus on saving throws against the Bleaching. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bleaching resistance (see page 218), planar adaptation, regeneration, creator must be a gnome; Cost 5,000 gp
