Arrow (bleeding) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Arrow (bleeding) Source Alchemy Manual pg. 20, Elves of Golarion pg. 22 Price 160 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Weapons Description This sharpened hollow tube looks like the narrow proboscis of some giant insect, but it actually comes from a carnivorous plant.
