Falcata - Weapons - Equipment Falcata Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, Adventurer's Armory pg. 33, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 178 Statistics Cost 18 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/x3; Range —; Type S; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy Description This heavy blade has a single sharp edge, concave at the bottom and convex at the top, with a hook-shaped hilt. Its shape distributes the weight to give it the momentum of an axe with the cutting edge of a sword, much like a larger version of a kukri. The hooked hilt is sometimes carved to resemble the head of a horse or bird of prey, and may have a thin chain stretched across its opening, acting as a partial guard for the hand and fingers.
