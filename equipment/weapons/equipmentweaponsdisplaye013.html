Piston maul, gnome - Weapons - Equipment Piston maul, gnome Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 9, Gnomes of Golarion pg. 22 Statistics Cost 70 gp Weight 15 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special see text Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Hammers Description An alchemically fired piston in the head of this two-handed hammer strikes targets with astounding force. Successful sunder attacks made with a piston maul deal an additional 4 points of damage. A piston maul requires a thunderstone to function; otherwise, it functions as a greatclub. Inserting a thunderstone into the weapon's compartment is a standard action, and powers the weapon for 24 hours, after which the thunderstone is consumed.
