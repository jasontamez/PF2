Kumade - Weapons - Equipment Kumade Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 19 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special grapple Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Axes Description Adapted from a garden rake, the kumade consists of a wood or bamboo pole topped with a metal claw. The rake head doubles as a grappling hook. When a kumade is secured to a surface, such as a rafter or window sill, its handle can be climbed as a 5-foot length of rope.
