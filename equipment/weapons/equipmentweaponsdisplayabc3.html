Dire flail - Weapons - Equipment Dire flail Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 90 gp Weight 10 lbs. Damage 1d6/1d6 (small), 1d8/1d8 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special disarm, double, trip Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Double; Flails Description A dire flail consists of two spheres of spiked iron dangling from chains at opposite ends of a long haft. This weapon excels at short but powerful strikes, and is typically swung in a constant churning motion. The wielder of a dire flail must have great strength, both to use the weapon effectively and to keep from tiring out.
