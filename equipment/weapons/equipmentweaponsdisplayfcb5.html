Spiked heavy shield - Weapons - Equipment Spiked heavy shield Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 17 gp/30 gp Weight special Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Close Description Spiked shields are intimidating weapons, and can have a single protruding central spike, razored shield edges, or a whole forest of deadly protrusions. You can bash with a spiked heavy shield instead of using it for defense. A spiked heavy shield can't be disarmed. See the armor spikes entry on page 10 for details.
