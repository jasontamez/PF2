Tanglefoot bundle - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tanglefoot bundle Source Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 72 Price 250 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Category Alchemical Weapons Description This larger version of a tanglefoot bag entangles its target as normal and requires a successful DC 20 Reflex saving throw to avoid being stuck to the floor. In addition, it can affect Huge and smaller creatures, and has a range increment of 60 feet. A creature that is glued to the floor can break free by succeeding at a DC 30 Strength check or by dealing 25 points of damage to the goo with a slashing weapon. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 35
