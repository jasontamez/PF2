Switchblade knife - Weapons - Equipment Switchblade knife Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 5 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range 10 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Martial Description This knife has a springloaded blade hidden inside the handle. It can be designed to look like another type of object (a DC 15 Perception check reveals the deception). If held in your hand, you can activate it as a free action. This weapon is treated as a dagger.
