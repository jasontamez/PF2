Quarterstaff - Weapons - Equipment Quarterstaff Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 17, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost — Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d4/1d4 (small), 1d6/1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special double, monk Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Double; Monk Description A quarterstaff is a simple piece of wood, about 5 feet in length. In battle, it's held with one hand at the butt and the other a quarter of the way up the staff, allowing it to block blows and make quick strikes.
