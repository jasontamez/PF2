Tekko-kagi - Weapons - Equipment Tekko-kagi Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight — Damage 1d2 (small), 1d3 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special disarm, see text Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Close Description Also known as an iron claw, this device consists of a fanlike structure of three or more 10-inch blades secured to a sturdy handle strapped to the forearm of the off hand. It can be used as an offensive weapon or defensively like a buckler, or used to disarm an opponent without provoking an attack of opportunity. It provides its owner with a +2 circumstance bonus on attempts to disarm or sunder swords or other slender-bladed weapons.
