Blowgun darts (10) - Weapons - Equipment Blowgun darts (10) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 211, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 5 sp Weight — Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description The thin, light darts used in a blowgun are usually made of hardwood with a stabilizing fletching of down or fur.
