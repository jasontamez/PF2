Ogre hook - Weapons - Equipment Ogre hook Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Rise of the Runelords Player's Guide pg. 11, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 290, Adventurer's Armory pg. 0 Statistics Cost 24 gp Weight 10 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special trip Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Polearms Description This large crook of sharpened metal makes it easy to trip and catch fleeing opponents. Invented by ogres, most ogre hooks are Large and crudely fashioned-often from metal torn from previous victims' weapons and armor. Despite their poor workmanship, the rarity and peril involved in getting ogre hooks make such items quite valuable.
