Naginata - Weapons - Equipment Naginata Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 133 Statistics Cost 35 gp Weight 9 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x4; Range —; Type S; Special reach Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Polearms Description The naginata consists of a 6-foot staff affixed to a 2-foot-long, slightly curved, swordlike blade. The shaft is designed to keep the wielder out of reach from swords and shorter weapons.
