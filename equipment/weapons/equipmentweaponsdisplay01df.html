Kumade, collapsible - Weapons - Equipment Kumade, collapsible Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 19 Statistics Cost 10 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special grapple Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Axes Description This weapon acts as a kumade, but the handle consists of bamboo sections held together by a taut rope running through the center. Releasing the rope is a standard action that provokes attacks of opportunity and causes the weapon to collapse into a bundle that fits into a backpack. Reassembling the collapsible kumade requires a full-round action that provokes attacks of opportunity.
