Arrows (20) - Weapons - Equipment Arrows (20) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 3 lbs. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description An arrow used as a melee weapon is treated as a light improvised weapon (-4 penalty on attack rolls) and deals damage as a dagger of its size (critical multiplier x2). Arrows come in a leather quiver that holds 20 arrows.
