Coat pistol - Weapons - Equipment Coat pistol Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 43, Ultimate Combat pg. 139 Statistics Cost 750 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x3; Range 10 ft.; Type B and P; Special — Misfire 1 (5 ft.); Capacity 1 Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description Though less powerful than other, larger firearms, this pistol is small enough to be easily concealed in a jacket or coat. You get a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks made to conceal a coat pistol on your body. A coat pistol uses either a bullet and 1 dose of black powder or a single alchemical cartridge as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
