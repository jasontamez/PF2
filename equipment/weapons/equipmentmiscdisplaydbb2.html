Shriek bomb - Miscellaneous - Equipment Shriek bomb Source Alchemy Manual pg. 7 Price 45 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Weapons Description This yellow ball of volatile resin has a hemp wick. Lighting the fuse is a move action; 1d3-1 rounds later (a result of 0 rounds takes place at the end of your turn), the shriek bomb explodes, dealing 2d6 points of sonic damage to creatures in a 10-footradius burst (Fortitude DC 15 half). A shriek bomb can be thrown as a splash weapon with a range of 10 feet. Alchemical Recipe Recipe (20 magnesium + 30 myrrh + 5 saltpeter)/congelation; Craft 20 Time 10 minutes; Tools alchemist's lab; Type alchemical weapon
