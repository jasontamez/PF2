Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special reach, trip Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Polearms Description This common herder's tool is a wooden pole with a hooked end. When using a crook, you can ignore the penalty for not having two hands free when making a combat maneuver check to initiate a grapple against a creature you threaten.
