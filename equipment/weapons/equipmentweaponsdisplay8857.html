Doru - Weapons - Equipment Doru Source Distant Shores pg. 12 Statistics Cost 4 gp Weight 6 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range 20 ft.; Type P; Special brace Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description Balanced by a metal butt, this large spear is weighted so that it can be used in one hand by a proficient wielder.
