Heavy slaver's crossbow - Weapons - Equipment Heavy slaver's crossbow Source Monster Codex pg. 45 Statistics Cost 400 gp Weight 12 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/see "bolas bolts" text; Range 80 ft.; Type B; Special nonlethal, trip, see text Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Description This crossbow shoots ammunition called bolas bolts instead of crossbow bolts. It comes in both light and heavy varieties (but not double, hand, or repeating crossbow varieties) with the same reload action as a conventional crossbow of its type.
