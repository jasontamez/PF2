Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type P; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description You can use the Weapon Finesse feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier to attack rolls with a rapier sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon.
