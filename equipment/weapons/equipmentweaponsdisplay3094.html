Kasatha spinal sword - Weapons - Equipment Kasatha spinal sword Source People of the Stars pg. 9 Statistics Cost 50 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P or S; Special fragile Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Description This light one-handed sword is covered in bony spines that make it easier to poison opponents. The save DC of a nonmagical injury poison applied to a kasatha spinal sword increases by 2. Kasathas treat kasatha spinal swords as martial weapons.
