Chain shot - Weapons - Equipment Chain shot Source Ultimate Combat pg. 164 Statistics Cost 50 gp Weight 30 lbs Category Ammunition; Proficiency Siege Engine Description This siege engine firearm ammunition can be loaded in a cannon or a fiend's mouth cannon. It is especially good at tearing through sails and dirigibles (see the Vehicle rules), dealing double its normal damage to those forms of propulsion. When fired at a creature, on a hit, the creature must succeed at a DC 20 Fortitude saving throw or be knocked prone.
