Light mace - Weapons - Equipment Light mace Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 17, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Hammers Description A mace is made up of an ornate metal head attached to a simple wooden or metal shaft. The mace's head is usually flanged to cause more pain or damage. Some more primitive maces are made with stone bound to a wooden haft with leather straps.
