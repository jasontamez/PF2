Jolting dart - Weapons - Equipment Jolting dart Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 205 Statistics Cost 100 gp Weight 1/2 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type P; Special see text Category Ranged; Proficiency Martial Description This alchemically grown crystal dart builds up an electrical charge when thrown. A creature struck by the dart takes normal piercing damage and 1d6 points of electricity damage. Anyone proficient in darts can use a jolting dart. Once thrown, the dart is destroyed.
