Halfling sharpstones (10) - Weapons - Equipment Halfling sharpstones (10) Source Halflings of Golarion pg. 23 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 5 lbs. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description A thin layer of baked clay covers the spikes and jagged ridges covering this specially designed bullet. A proficient user knows how to crack the clay (a free action) so that it peels off from either the spikes or ridges when fired, dealing either piercing or slashing damage.
