Spring blade - Weapons - Equipment Spring blade Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 232 Statistics Cost 70 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range 10 ft.; Type P or S; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Simple Description This 4-inch blade is attached via a hinge to a wooden handle with a slot in the center, into which the blade can be folded. With a click of a switch, a spring thrusts the blade outward, where it locks into place until it is reset. Releasing the blade is a free action, and resetting it is a move action. The handle must be in your hand for the blade to be released; placing it in your hand requires the same type of action as drawing a weapon. A spring blade is easy to conceal: an observer must succeed at a DC 20 Perception check to notice the blade hidden within the wooden handle, and you gain a +4 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal a spring blade on your body.
