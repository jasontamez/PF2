Air crystals - Miscellaneous - Equipment Air crystals Source Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 48 Price 50 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description These unpleasant-tasting, alchemically grown crystals release breathable air when chewed. A pouch of air crystals provides 1 minute of breathable air. Placing air crystals in your mouth takes a standard action; chewing them each round is a free action. Any attempt to speak while chewing air crystals negates any remaining duration.
