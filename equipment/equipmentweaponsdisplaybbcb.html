Dwarven urgrosh - Weapons - Equipment Dwarven urgrosh Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 50 gp Weight 12 lbs. Damage 1d6/1d4 (small), 1d8/1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P or S; Special brace, double Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Double Description A dwarven urgrosh is a double weapon- an axe head and a spear point on opposite ends of a long haft. The urgrosh's axe head is a slashing weapon that deals 1d8 points of damage. Its spear head is a piercing weapon that deals 1d6 points of damage. You can use either head as the primary weapon; the other becomes the off-hand weapon. If you use an urgrosh against a charging character, the spear head is the part of the weapon that deals damage. Dwarves treat dwarven urgroshes as martial weapons.
