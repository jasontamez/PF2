Double-barreled pistol - Weapons - Equipment Double-barreled pistol Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 43, Ultimate Combat pg. 139 Statistics Cost 1,750 gp Weight 5 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x4; Range 20 ft.; Type B and P; Special — Misfire 1-2 (5 ft.); Capacity 2 Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description This pistol has two parallel barrels; each barrel can be fired independently as separate attacks, or both can be fired at once as a standard action (the attack action).. If both barrels are shot at once, they must both target the same creature or object, and the pistol becomes wildly inaccurate, imposing a -4 penalty on each shot. This is an early firearm.
