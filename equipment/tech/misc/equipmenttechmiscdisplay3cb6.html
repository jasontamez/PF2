Commset - Miscellaneous - Technology Commset Source Technology Guide pg. 42 Price 6,000 gp Slot none; Weight 2 lbs. Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge/hour Description This handheld device allows for communication between two commset users. A commset carries audio and visual signals, and includes a builtin camera that can record all communications being broadcast. Communication between two commsets requires both users to tune their individual devices to the same frequency. A commset has a range of 1 mile-beyond this range, communication is impossible without enhancing the signal strength with a signal booster (see page 51). The price and cost listed for this item are for a single commset. Construction Craft DC 27; Cost 3,000 gp Craft Technological Item, production lab
