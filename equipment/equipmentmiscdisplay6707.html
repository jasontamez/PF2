Folding ladder - Miscellaneous - Equipment Folding ladder Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 2 gp; Weight 16 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description The hook, pole, and rungs of this 10-foot ladder are hinged, allowing you to fold it into a 3-foot-by-1-foot-by-1-foot bundle. Folding or unfolding it is a standard action.
