Animal harness - Miscellaneous - Equipment Animal harness Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 185, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 2 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description This leather or hemp harness allows a holder to restrain and control a domesticated animal. A harness is standard equipment for anyone trying to teach an animal tricks with the Handle Animal skill. Ready-made harnesses are available in most markets for common domesticated animals such as cats, dogs, horses, and oxen, but harnesses can be made for nearly any creature.
