Dog sled - Miscellaneous - Equipment Dog sled Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 185, Pathfinder #51: The Hungry Storm pg. 67 Price 20 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 3 cp Category Transport, Land Description This sled is designed to be pulled over snow and ice by a team of trained riding dogs. Most sleds have runners at the back for a musher to stand on. A dog sled can carry up to the capacity of all the dogs that pull it.
