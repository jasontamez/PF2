Razor, drow - Weapons - Equipment Razor, drow Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 9 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type S; Special see text Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description Meant for quick strikes and slashes, this long, slender knife is incredibly sharp along one edge. Drow swashbucklers and assassins are particularly fond of these weapons. A confirmed critical hit or successful sneak attack with a drow razor deals an additional 2 points of damage; this damage is precision damage and is not multiplied on a critical hit. A character with the drow's weapon familiarity racial trait treats the drow razor as a martial weapon.
