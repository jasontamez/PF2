Charcoal - Miscellaneous - Equipment Charcoal Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 12, Seekers of Secrets pg. 42 Price 5 sp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description Sticks of charcoal are useful for marking floors and walls, writing on paper or parchment, and making rubbings of engravings or other markings. In a pinch, they can even be burned to stay warm. A good-quality rubbing generally takes 1 minute per sheet of paper.
