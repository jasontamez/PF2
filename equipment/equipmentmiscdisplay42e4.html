Soldier’s uniform - Miscellaneous - Equipment Soldier’s uniform Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89 Price 1 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Clothing Description The exact details of this item varies from country to country, but typically includes sturdy boots, leather breeches or a kilt, a belt, a shirt, gloves, a cloak or jacket, and a hat. The belt includes several loops or rings for tying pouches, a waterskin, a scabbard, and similar things a traveling soldier requires.
