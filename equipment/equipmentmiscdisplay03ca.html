Reversible cloak - Miscellaneous - Equipment Reversible cloak Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89 Price 2 sp-100 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Clothing Description This cloak has an outer layer of fabric and an inner layer of a different color. They are worn for the sake of fashion, in theater performances, or to aid a quick appearance change as part of a disguise. The price varies depending on the cloak's quality, with the low end being a simple linen cloak and the high end being made of silk or decorated with fur trim.
