Miscellaneous - Equipment Skillet Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 8 sp (common), 2,001 gp (mithral); Weight 4 lbs. (common), 2 lbs. (mithral) Description This 8-inch-diameter skillet has a long handle and feet so you can rest it on a fire or hot coals. Adventurers prefer models with a hinged handle that folds over the top of the skillet for easier storage and transport. A mithral skillet is lighter and food rarely sticks to it.
