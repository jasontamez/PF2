Purity test - Miscellaneous - Equipment Purity test Source Alchemy Manual pg. 18 Price 10 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Black Market Description This set of special disposable reagents can be used in conjunction with an alchemist's lab to detect whether a drug has been cut with inferior-grade material or filler, granting a +5 competence bonus on Craft (alchemy) checks to detect such chicanery.
