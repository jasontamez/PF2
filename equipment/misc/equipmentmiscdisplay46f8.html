Alchemist's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Alchemist's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 184 Price 40 gp; Weight 24 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit includes an alchemy crafting kit, a backpack, a bedroll, a belt pouch, a flint and steel, ink, an inkpen, an iron pot, a mess kit, soap, torches (10), trail rations (5 days), and a waterskin. The kit does not contain a formula book because an alchemist begins play with a formula book and does not need to purchase one.
