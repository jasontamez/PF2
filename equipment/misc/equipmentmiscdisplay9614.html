Iron spike - Miscellaneous - Equipment Iron spike Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 181, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 5 cp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This foot-long iron spike is used to keep doors open or closed and to secure ropes for climbing. Hearing a spike being hammered in requires a DC 5 Perception check.
