Anointing oil - Miscellaneous - Equipment Anointing oil Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 87 Price 25 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This sacred oil, infused with aromatic spices and distilled holy water, may be applied to a creature while casting a harmless divine spell with a range of touch, increasing the casting time to a full-round action but also increasing the caster's effective caster level by +1 for that spell. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
