Rake's friend - Miscellaneous - Equipment Rake's friend Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 236 Price 120 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description You can accept alchemical burn when you imbibe this draught to gain 1 grit point or 1 panache point. The effects of rake's friend last for 1 hour; if not spent within 1 hour, this point is lost. Rake's friend has no effect if you don't have the ability to use grit or panache. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
