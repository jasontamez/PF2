Compressed air - Miscellaneous - Equipment Compressed air Source People of the Stars pg. 19 Price 50 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Tools Description Longer lasting than an air bladder (Ultimate Equipment 58), a jar of compressed air comes with a breathing apparatus that can be strapped over the mouth with a leather cord and provides enough air for a Medium character to breathe normally for 10 minutes (20 minutes for a Small character).
