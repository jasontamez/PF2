Hound's blood (vial) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Hound's blood (vial) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 36 Price 110 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description If you apply the thick, red paste known as hound's blood to your nostrils or upper lip, it greatly enhances your sense of smell. For most creatures, this grants a +2 alchemical bonus on Perception checks for 1 hour. For gnomes, it briefly grants a super-heightened sense of smell, granting the scent ability for 5 minutes, before the potency is reduced to a +2 alchemical bonus on Perception checks for an additional 1 hour. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
