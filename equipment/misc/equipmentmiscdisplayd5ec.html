Shield sconce - Miscellaneous - Equipment Shield sconce Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This metal frame holds a torch and is designed to be strapped to the front of a light, heavy, or tower shield, allowing you to carry a torch without giving up your shield or occupying your weapon hand.
