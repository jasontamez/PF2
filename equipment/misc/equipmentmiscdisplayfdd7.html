Marbles - Miscellaneous - Equipment Marbles Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 181, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 sp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description Marbles can be used to slow opponents. One 2-pound bag of marbles covers an area 5 feet square. A creature entering a square with marbles scattered on it must succeed at a DC 10 Reflex save or fall prone (the creature's stability bonus to trip applies to this save). Any creature moving at half speed or slower can pick its way through a square of marbles with no trouble.
