Cold-weather outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Cold-weather outfit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89, Pathfinder #51: The Hungry Storm pg. 67, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 8 gp; Weight 7 lbs. Category Clothing Description This outfit is designed for mountaineering or hunting and traveling in icy climates. It includes a coat of wool or thick animal fur, a linen shirt, a wool cap, a heavy cloak, a heavy skirt or pants, and waterproof leather boots. In some regions, the outfit may be composed primarily of fur and animal pelts. This outfit grants a +5 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saving throws against exposure to cold weather.
