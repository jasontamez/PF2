Toad (vision) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Toad (vision) Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 10 gp; Weight 5 oz. Category Mounts/Pets Description These animals are found in areas of the Mwangi Expanse. They have a psychoactive skin venom which acts as a mild hallucinogen if the toad's skin is licked,-though breeding programs for these creatures outside of Mwangi produce venomless offspring.
