Skeleton slayer's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Skeleton slayer's kit Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 15 Price 120 gp; Weight 32 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit contains all the tools necessary for clearing out small packs of skeletons from tombs and crypts. It includes three flasks of acid, a club, a sling, 10 sling bullets, two flasks of holy water, a tanglefoot bag, 50 feet of hemp rope, a crowbar, and a wooden holy symbol.
