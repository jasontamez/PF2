Stench-gas bladder - Miscellaneous - Equipment Stench-gas bladder Source Pathfinder #115: Trail of the Hunted pg. 41 Price 30 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Alchemical Weapons Description This thin bladder splits when it hits a target. You can throw a gas bladder as a ranged weapon with a range increment of 10 feet. Any creature struck by a gas bladder must succeed at a DC 14 Fortitude save or be sickened for 1d6 rounds.
