Miscellaneous - Equipment Woolly mammoth Source Belkzen, Hold of the Orc Hordes pg. 39 Price 4,500 gp (combat-trained), 3,000 gp (normal); Weight 6 tons (combat-trained), 6 tons (normal) Description Poached from the Realm of the Mammoth Lords, these hairy pachyderms are used by the orcs of Belkzen as mounts and living siege engines. As if these mounts weren't intimidating enough, the orcs outfit them with spiked metal barding and tusk blades.
