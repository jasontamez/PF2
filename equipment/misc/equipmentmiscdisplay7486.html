False-bottomed cup - Miscellaneous - Equipment False-bottomed cup Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description The tiny compartment in the thick bottom of this cup is an excellent place to store a small item or substance. The most insidious are designed with a weighted catch that opens when the cup is tilted back, releasing the hidden substance into the contents of the cup. Spotting the secret compartment in an empty cup requires a DC 15 Perception check.
