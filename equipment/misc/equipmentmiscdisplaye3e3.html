Brewer's kit, masterwork - Miscellaneous - Equipment Brewer's kit, masterwork Source Alchemy Manual pg. 13 Price 75 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Category Alchemical Tools Description This exceptionally crafted kit provides extra spices, a wider variety of herbs, and higher quality tools than a normal brewer's kit. A masterwork brewer's kit grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Craft (alchemy) checks to produce alcoholic beverages.
