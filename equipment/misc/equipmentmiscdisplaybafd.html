Collapsible rowboat - Miscellaneous - Equipment Collapsible rowboat Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 19 Price 100 gp; Weight 40 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This boat has a canvas hull and wooden frame that can be easily disassembled. When collapsed, the boat fits into a pack that can be carried by one person. When expanded, this boat has the same base statistics as a rowboat (Core Rulebook 163). Expanding or collapsing it takes 5 minutes.
