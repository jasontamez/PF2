Coffee pot - Miscellaneous - Equipment Coffee pot Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56 Price 3 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This tall, teapotlike device contains a small chamber for coffee grounds and a large chamber for water, connected by a small tube. Heating the pot forces boiling water through the tube and into the grounds. A glass knob at the top of the tube allows you to see the color of the brew and stop when it is sufficiently strong. It can brew up to 4 cups of coffee at a time. It can also be used to make tea, steep medicinal herbs, or just boil water.
