Dodger's draught - Miscellaneous - Equipment Dodger's draught Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Price 300 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This dark green liquid has a pungent odor. For 1 hour after you drink this tincture, your muscles instinctively react to danger and you gain a +2 dodge bonus to AC. Your involuntary muscle movement causes you to take a -2 penalty on attack rolls for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
