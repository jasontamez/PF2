Driftwood icon - Miscellaneous - Equipment Driftwood icon Source Faiths of Balance pg. 25 Price 25 gp; Weight — Category Channel Foci Deity Gozreh Description This is a hand-carved icon of a human figure. When the icon is activated and a flower is placed in the niche, the icon grants the bearer a +1 luck bonus to Armor Class for 1 hour, but only against ranged attacks.
