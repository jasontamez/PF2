Leaper's libation - Miscellaneous - Equipment Leaper's libation Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Price 200 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This bubbly drink has a zesty scent and invigorates the muscles. For 1 hour after drinking this tincture, you gain a +4 alchemical bonus on Acrobatics checks to jump horizontally, you are always treated as having a running start for the purpose of these checks, and your jump distance can exceed your base speed. Due to your exaggerated movement, you take a -2 penalty on attack rolls and cannot take 5-foot steps for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
