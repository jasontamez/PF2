Caltrops, cold iron - Miscellaneous - Equipment Caltrops, cold iron Source Demon Hunter's Handbook pg. 18 Price 2 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description Cold iron caltrops function in all ways as ordinary caltrops (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 155) except that they are made of cold iron, and affect creatures with DR/cold iron accordingly.
