Scroll case - Miscellaneous - Equipment Scroll case Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 183 Price 1 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description A leather or wooden scroll case easily holds four scrolls; you can cram more inside, but retrieving any of them becomes a full-round action rather than a move action. You must destroy the scroll case to damage its contents (hardness 2 for leather or 5 for wood, 2 hit points, break DC 15). A scroll case is not watertight, and must still be protected from the elements.
