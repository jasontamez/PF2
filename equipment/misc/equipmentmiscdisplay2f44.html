Tusk blades - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tusk blades Source Belkzen, Hold of the Orc Hordes pg. 39 Price 50 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description These metal caps must be specially fitted to a creature's horns or tusks; a blade reminiscent of a sword or axe head projects from each cap. If the creature makes a gore attack (including as part of a powerful charge), the attack deals both piercing and slashing damage, and has a critical threat range of 19-20 (this range can be increased by other effects). Tusk blades can be enhanced as melee weapons; the enhancement is applied to the creature's gore attack.
