Spirit-vision ink - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spirit-vision ink Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 28 Price 25 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description Originally developed by kami-revering priests to write prayer scrolls, this pale ink is now often used by ninjas to mark locations or leave messages. Once spirit-vision ink dries, it becomes completely invisible. To invisible creatures, however, writing in spirit-vision ink shimmers with a rich azure hue. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 30
