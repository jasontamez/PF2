Calistria's kindness - Miscellaneous - Equipment Calistria's kindness Source Inner Sea Intrigue pg. 50 Price 3 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description This tonic reduces menstrual pains and dramatically reduces the chances of conception during sexual intercourse if taken daily for 1 month.
