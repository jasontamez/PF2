Bit and bridle - Miscellaneous - Equipment Bit and bridle Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 2 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Animal Gear Description This is headgear used by a rider to control a mount. Bit and bridles for horses, ponies, and riding dogs can be readily purchased in most settlements and bits and bridles can be crafted for any creature that serves as a mount.
