Miscellaneous - Equipment Gecko Source 300 gp (riding), 400 gp (riding, combat trained); Weight 1,200 lbs. (riding), 1,500 lbs. (riding, combat trained) Description Larger than even giant geckos, these mammoth lizards have been specifically bred to be used as mounts. Prized for their ability to run along cave ceilings and sheer walls, they require exotic saddles to ride. A riding gecko has the same statistics as a giant gecko with the giant template (see Bestiary 3).
