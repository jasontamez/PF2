Poisoned locket - Miscellaneous - Equipment Poisoned locket Source Potions and Poisons pg. 29 Price 75 gp; Weight — Category Tools Description This heart-shaped locket is attached to a thin metal chain made of looped strands of gold or silver. Inside the locket is a small, spring-loaded vial that can hold a single dose of powdered poison. Loading the locket with poison carries the same risks as applying poison to a weapon. When the locket is opened, the vial is shattered by a ball bearing, releasing the powder into the air and treating the poison's type as if it were inhaled. The airborne poison affects all creatures within 10 feet of the locket.
