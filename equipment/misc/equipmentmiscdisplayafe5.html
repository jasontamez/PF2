Folding table - Miscellaneous - Equipment Folding table Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56 Price 10 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This wooden table has jointed legs and a hinge down the middle. When fully extended, it is a 2-foot-by-4-foot table, but collapses down to 2 feet by 2 feet by 2 inches. Setting up the table is a full-round action; collapsing it is 2 full-round actions.
