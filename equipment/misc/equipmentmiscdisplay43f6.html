Scroll box - Miscellaneous - Equipment Scroll box Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 5 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This wooden box easily holds 10 scrolls and has small clips or bookmarks for easier indexing. Retrieving a scroll from a held scroll box is a move action. A scroll box has hardness 5, 5 hit points, a break DC of 20. A scroll box is water-tight.
