Compass - Miscellaneous - Equipment Compass Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 184, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 10 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description An ordinary compass that points to the magnetic north pole grants you a +2 circumstance bonus on Survival checks made to avoid becoming lost. You can also use it to grant the same bonus on Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks made to navigate underground.
