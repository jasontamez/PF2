Stretch cords - Miscellaneous - Equipment Stretch cords Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 244 Price 5 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description Sold in pairs, these cords have an elastic quality. Both cords measure 2 feet long but can be extended to 4 feet. Each end of a cord has a small metal hook. Employed together, these cords can be used to tie down all sorts of equipment and prevent damage from wind or other inhospitable weather.
