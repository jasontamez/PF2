Chirurgeon's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Chirurgeon's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 47 Price 400 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Kits Description This sturdy leather case straps to the waist or thigh, or can be slung over a shoulder. It consists of a leather pouch, a healer's kit, a bottle of strong brandy, a potion of cure light wounds, a vial of smelling salts, and 2 doses each of alchemist's kindness, antiplague, antitoxin, bloodblock, and soothe syrup.
