Arrow (tangleshot) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Arrow (tangleshot) Source Alchemy Manual pg. 21, Elves of Golarion pg. 23 Price 20 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Weapons Description This arrow is tipped with a tiny vial of tanglefoot goo. Firing a tangleshot arrow is a ranged touch attack; the arrow deals no damage when it hits, but the target is splashed with the alchemical adhesive. This effect is similar to that of a tanglefoot bag, but with the following adjustments: Reflex DC 10, Strength DC 12 to break, 10 points of slashing damage to cut through, concentration DC 10 to cast spells. A tangleshot arrow imposes a -1 penalty on attack rolls because of its weight. Alchemical Recipe Recipe (18 myrrh + 26 urea)/congelation; Craft 25 Time 10 minutes; Tools alchemist's lab; Type alchemical weapon
