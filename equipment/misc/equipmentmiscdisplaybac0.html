Hammock - Miscellaneous - Equipment Hammock Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 sp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This blanket or net is attached to strong ropes, allowing you to hang it from a heavy branch or two trees and sleep above the ground. Climbing into or out of a hammock is a full-round action. A successful DC 5 Dexterity check reduces this to a move action.
