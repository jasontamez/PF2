Printing press (1-page) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Printing press (1-page) Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 250 gp; Weight 150 lbs. Category Tools Description A hand-cranked press uses a plate on which all of the text on a page is carved in reverse. Changing the plate is a simple process, but creating a new one is expensive and laborintensive. The press prints one page at a time, at a rate of about five pages per minute. A stationer then needs to cut and trim the pages and bind the book.
