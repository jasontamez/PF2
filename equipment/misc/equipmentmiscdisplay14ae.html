Monk's outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Monk's outfit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 5 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Clothing Description This simple outfit includes sandals, loose breeches, and a loose shirt, and is bound together with sashes. The outfit is designed to give you maximum mobility, and it's made of high-quality fabric. You can conceal small weapons in pockets hidden in the folds, and the sashes are strong enough to serve as short ropes.
