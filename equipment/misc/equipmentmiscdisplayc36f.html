Sucker's barrel - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sucker's barrel Source Villain Codex pg. 117 Price 4 gp; Weight 35 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description A sucker's barrel is constructed to appear to the casual eye exactly like a normal barrel. However, an inner barrier installed near one of the barrel's ends hides a cavity containing a bladder filled with water. This creates a false bottom such that, when the barrel is full, it weighs and feels the same as a barrel filled completely with more valuable liquid, such as alcohol or oil. In this way, a merchant can "fill" a barrel with a smaller quantity of a liquid (typically 75% of the barrel's total capacity) and then sell it as if it were a full barrel's worth.
