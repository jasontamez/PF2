Formula book - Miscellaneous - Equipment Formula book Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77 Price 15 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Tools Description A formula book has 100 pages of parchment, and each alchemical extract formula takes up one page per extract level. A newly purchased formula book contains no extract formulas. An alchemist character begins play with a formula book containing the extract formulae he knows (Advanced Player's Guide 32). This is the alchemist equivalent of a spellbook for wizards (a spellbook and a formula book are essentially the same item).
