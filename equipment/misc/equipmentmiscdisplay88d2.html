Spiritual leader's vestments - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spiritual leader's vestments Source Disciple's Doctrine pg. 28 Price 100 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Category Clothing Description These fine garments come in assorted vibrant colors and are used in the most important religious or spiritual ceremonies. The vestments typically include a robe or cloak embroidered with a religion's holy symbol, as well as a number of extravagant accessories, such as fine jewelry, long sashes, tall hats, and other appropriate attire.
