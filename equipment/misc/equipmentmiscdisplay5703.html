Antidote kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Antidote kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77 Price 100 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Tools Description This small box contains a wide variety of remedies and treatments against not only specific poisons but also their most common methods of delivery, including such things as purgatives to eliminate ingested toxins. It grants a +3 circumstance bonus on Heal checks made to treat poison. An antidote kit is exhausted after 10 uses.
