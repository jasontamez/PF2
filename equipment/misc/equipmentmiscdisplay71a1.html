Waterproof bag - Miscellaneous - Equipment Waterproof bag Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 5 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This leather sack sealed with tar or pitch keeps delicate items from being ruined by water. Items kept inside remain relatively dry, making the bag ideal for carrying maps, scrolls, spellbooks, and the like, although the bag is not impervious and can only be completely immersed for 10 rounds before enough water seeps in to ruin such items.
