Explorer's emulsion - Miscellaneous - Equipment Explorer's emulsion Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Price 150 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This tincture contains powdered plant roots and comes in cold-weather and hot-weather varieties. For 6 hours after drinking the tincture, you gain a +5 alchemical bonus on Fortitude saving throws to resist the effects of the chosen temperature. The tincture interferes with blood flow, causing you to take a -2 penalty on all other Fortitude saving throws for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
