Sherrif's whistle - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sherrif's whistle Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 37 Price 1 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description Used most often by gnome sheriffs and rangers, a sheriff's whistle gives off a piercing shriek that can easily be heard up to 1,000 feet away under ordinary circumstances. However, the frequency of the shriek is such that it can only be heard by badgers, dogs, and gnomes.
