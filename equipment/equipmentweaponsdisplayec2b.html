Sansetsukon - Weapons - Equipment Sansetsukon Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 8 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type B; Special blocking, disarm, monk Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Flails; Monk Description Also called a three-section staff, this weapon is divided into three 2-footlong segments chained together, allowing the wielder greater flexibility than a normal staff and allowing the weapon to be swung rapidly to create a defensive block, wrap and catch weapons, or harness momentum via a whipping motion for powerful strikes.
