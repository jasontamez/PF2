Red flame torch - Miscellaneous - Equipment Red flame torch Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 246 Price 20 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This otherwise normal-looking torch is encrusted with lithium salts and burns with a dark red flame. It sheds only dim light in a 10-foot radius, and the light it casts is undetectable outside this radius by normal vision, darkvision, or lowlight vision. This torch burns for 1 hour.
