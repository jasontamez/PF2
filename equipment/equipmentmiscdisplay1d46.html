Saddlebags - Miscellaneous - Equipment Saddlebags Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 4 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description These sturdy, weatherproof bags are draped over a saddle to add extra carrying capacity. Each side of a saddlebag can typically carry 20 pound of items that can fit in the bags. Saddlebags do not increase the amount of weight a mount can carry; they merely give riders a place to stow their equipment.
