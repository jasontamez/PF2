Handaxe - Weapons - Equipment Handaxe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 6 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Axes; Tribal Description This one-handed axe is short (roughly 1 foot long) and designed for use with one hand. Unlike throwing axes, it is not well balanced for a graceful tumbling motion, and is instead heavier at its head. Tomahawks, war hatchets, and other such names usually refer to hand axes.
