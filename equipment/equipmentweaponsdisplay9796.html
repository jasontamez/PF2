Spiral rapier - Weapons - Equipment Spiral rapier Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 9 Statistics Cost 80 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type P; Special blocking, disarm, see text Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description This rapier like weapon has a thicker blade than normal, which is shaped into a corkscrewing spiral of sharpened edges. These edges can catch enemy weapons, making it ideal for disarming or parrying. You can use the Weapon Finesse feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier to attack rolls with a spiral rapier sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon. You can't wield a spiral rapier in two hands in order to apply 1-1/2 times your Strength modifier to its damage. Any effects that apply to rapiers also apply to a spiral rapier.
