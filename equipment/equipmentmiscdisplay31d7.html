Trapper's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Trapper's kit Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 7 Price 263 gp; Weight 90 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit is particularly useful for dungeon explorers who specialize in trapping or taming whatever vile quarry they find. A trapper's kit contains an average lock, a bear trap, a container of bone paste (see page 26), a Small cage, a set of manacles, a bag of marbles, 50 feet of silk rope, two tanglefoot bags, 50 feet of twine, and a wire.
