Doctor's mask - Miscellaneous - Equipment Doctor's mask Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Pathfinder #8: Seven Days to the Grave pg. 61, Curse of the Crimson Throne Player's Guide pg. 12 Price 50 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Tools Description This protective mask gives you a +1 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves made against airborne toxins and scent-based effects. In Korvosa, it is a minor crime to wear a doctor's mask in public if you are not a healer or physician.
