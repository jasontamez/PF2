Swift hands tonic - Miscellaneous - Equipment Swift hands tonic Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 236 Price 250 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description When imbibed, this bitter, rust-colored concoction grants you 1d4 uses of alchemical inspiration that can be used only on Disable Device checks and Sleight of Hand checks. These benefits last for 1 hour; during this time, you must roll all Acrobatics checks and Escape Artist checks twice and take the worse result. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
