Bayonet - Weapons - Equipment Bayonet Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 17, Adventurer's Armory pg. 2, Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176, Skull and Shackles Player's Guide pg. 6 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special — Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Blades, Light; Close Description Bayonets are close combat weapons designed to fit into the grooves or muzzles of crossbows and firearms. They allow you to make melee attacks with these weapons but render them temporarily useless as ranged weapons. Attaching or removing a bayonet is a move action.
