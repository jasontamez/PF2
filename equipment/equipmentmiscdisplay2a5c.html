Battle mask - Miscellaneous - Equipment Battle mask Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 141 Price 50 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Clothing Description Made from wood, bone, or similar materials, this mask covers its wearer's actual appearance and identity by depicting a hateful, leering face instead. Because of a battle mask's excellent craftsmanship and exquisite details, the wearer gains a +1 bonus on Intimidate checks made to demoralize an opponent.
