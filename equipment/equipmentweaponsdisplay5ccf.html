Flight arrows (20) - Weapons - Equipment Flight arrows (20) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight 3 lbs. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description These arrows have light shafts and special fletching to give them greater range. A flight arrow's range increment is 20 feet greater with longbows and 10 feet greater with shortbows. They deal damage as if one size category smaller.
