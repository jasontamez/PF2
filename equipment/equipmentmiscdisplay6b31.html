Animal disguise kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Animal disguise kit Source Blood of the Moon pg. 30 Price 100 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Category Tools Description This leather pouch contains dyes, odorous balsams, colorchanging eye drops, reversible collars, and a host of other tools to disguise one animal as another. The kit grants a +4 circumstance bonus on Disguise checks to disguise an animal as another specific animal of the same species, and a +2 circumstance bonus on Disguise checks to disguise an animal as an animal of a different species. An animal disguise kit is exhausted after 10 uses.
