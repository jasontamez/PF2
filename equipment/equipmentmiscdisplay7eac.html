Stove can - Miscellaneous - Equipment Stove can Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 10 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This little metal container holds a waxy fuel that creates enough heat to cook with but only as much light as a candle. To snuff the flame, close the metal lid on the container; it cools enough that it can be carried in a few minutes. Each lasts 5 hours (enough to cook about 10 meals).
