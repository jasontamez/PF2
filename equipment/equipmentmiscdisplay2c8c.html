Phrenologist's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Phrenologist's kit Source Occult Adventures pg. 249 Price 50 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Kits Description This leather doctor's bag contains all the accoutrements a phrenologist needs to measure skulls. This kit contains several metal skull calipers of various sizes, a labeled chalkware bust of a humanoid head, and numbered charts of skulls of various species. The kit provides a +2 circumstance bonus on Knowledge (arcana) checks for the phrenology occult skill unlock.
