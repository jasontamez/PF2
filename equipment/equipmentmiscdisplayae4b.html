Shaman's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Shaman's kit Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 205 Price 15 gp; Weight 44 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit includes a backpack, a bedroll, a belt pouch, candles (10), a flint and steel, an iron pot, a mess kit, rope, soap, a spell component pouch, torches (10), trail rations (5 days), a waterskin, and a wooden holy symbol.
