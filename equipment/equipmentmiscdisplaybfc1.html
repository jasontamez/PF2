Blight tonic - Miscellaneous - Equipment Blight tonic Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 153 Price 150 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description Blight tonic increases the potency of a creature's disease attack transmitted by physical contact, such as a dire rat's filth fever. The next time a creature must save against the drinker's disease save DC, the DC for the initial infection increases by +2. The tonic lasts for 10 minutes or until the next time a creature must save against the drinker's disease DC, whichever comes first. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
