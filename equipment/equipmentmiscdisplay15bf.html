Nautical chart - Miscellaneous - Equipment Nautical chart Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 13, Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 21 Price 25 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description Nautical charts map waterways, showing the depth of water and height of coastlines, as well as currents, harbors, navigational hazards, reefs, and tides. A nautical chart grants a +4 circumstance bonus on Profession (sailor) checks to navigate in the area detailed by the chart.
