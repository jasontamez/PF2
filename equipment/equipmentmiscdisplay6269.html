Silent piton - Miscellaneous - Equipment Silent piton Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 244 Price 5 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description A silent piton is identical to a regular piton, except it has been alchemically treated to reduce the noise it generates when driven into a surface. A silent piton staked into a piece of terrain generates sound out to a radius of only 10 feet from the point of impact.
