Lantern honey - Miscellaneous - Equipment Lantern honey Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 114 Price 150 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description This alchemically enhanced honey is typically transported in small, lantern-shaped containers capable of holding up to 3 doses of honey. A single dose of lantern honey functions as normal antitoxin, except that it also provides immunity to drow poison for 1 hour after consumption.
