Slow-heart concoction - Miscellaneous - Equipment Slow-heart concoction Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 27 Price 50 gp; Weight — Category Concoctions Description This inky concoction slows the flow of blood, reducing any bleed damage you would suffer by 2 and causing you to automatically stabilize when dying. The concoction dulls your response speed, however, imparting a -1 penalty on your Reflex saving throws.
