Juggler's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Juggler's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 15 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Tools Description This kit includes multiple sets of well-balanced objects, such as balls, clubs, knives, rings, and torches. It provides a +1 bonus on Perform (act or comedy) checks and checks involving juggling.
