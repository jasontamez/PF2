Miscellaneous - Equipment Cane Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 12 Price 1 sp (common), 25 gp (elegant), 2 gp (simple); Weight 3 lbs. (common), 4 lbs. (elegant), 3 lbs. (simple) Description A cane is a walking stick fashioned from wood. A cane sports either a metal cap or a curved top that's easy to grip. Simple canes are no more than a sturdy branch, while high quality canes are made from fine materials and bear intricate carvings or adornments.
