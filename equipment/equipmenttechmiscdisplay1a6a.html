Nanite canister - Miscellaneous - Technology Nanite canister Source Technology Guide pg. 50 Price 500 gp Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Capacity —; Usage disposable Description A nanite canister is used to power technological devices that use nanites'such items generally don't require other sources of power, as the nanites themselves do the bulk of the work when the object is activated. The canister contains a small, independent power source and a supply of nanites. A single canister contains enough nanites to provide 10 charges for a nanotech item or weapon. Construction Craft DC 25; Cost 250 gp Craft Technological Item, nanotech lab
