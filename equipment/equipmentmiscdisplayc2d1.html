Miscellaneous - Equipment Dungeoneering kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 47 Price 15 gp (common), 130 gp (deluxe); Weight 25 lbs. (common), 15 lbs. (deluxe) Description This bundle of essential supplies for exploring ruins and abandoned cities includes two candles, chalk, a hammer and four pitons, 50 feet of hemp rope, a hooded lantern with 5 flasks of oil, two sacks, two torches, and four tindertwigs. A deluxe dungeoneering kit contains two candles, chalk, a hammer and four pitons, 50 feet of silk rope, two sacks, three sunrods, four tindertwigs, and an everburning torch.
