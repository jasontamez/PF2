Tropical kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tropical kit Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 9 Price 120 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit is intended for hot and moist environments such as jungles and warm marshes. It includes 1 dose each of antiplague and antitoxin, five fishhooks, a hammock, a kukri, 1 dose of vermin repellent, and a water purification sponge.
