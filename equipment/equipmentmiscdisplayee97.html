Arctic kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Arctic kit Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 9 Price 27 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit is designed for use in cold climates, where the weather can be deadly and the terrain treacherous. It includes 10 candles, a candle lamp, cleats, a cold-weather outfit, a handaxe, a shovel, a signal whistle, a small iron pot, snowshoes, and five tindertwigs, all wrapped in a winter blanket.
