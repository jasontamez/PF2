Swordbreaker dagger - Weapons - Equipment Swordbreaker dagger Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Adventurer's Armory pg. 5, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 179 Statistics Cost 10 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type S; Special disarm, see text Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description You can use this dagger to disarm or sunder enemy blades by catching them in the grooves on the back of the blade and using your weapon as a lever. If you are proficient with this weapon, add a +4 bonus on combat maneuver checks to disarm or sunder bladed weapons while using it.
