False-bottomed wagon - Miscellaneous - Equipment False-bottomed wagon Source Heroes of the Streets pg. 12 Price 375 gp; Weight 400 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This wagon has a hollow below its apparent bottom. The hollow is 6 inches deep and has a capacity of 75 cubic feet. The hollow can be noticed during a search of the wagon with a successful DC 20 Perception check. The wagon otherwise resembles a normal medium wagon.
