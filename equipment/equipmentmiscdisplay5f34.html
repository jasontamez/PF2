Titan's tonic - Miscellaneous - Equipment Titan's tonic Source Potions and Poisons pg. 23 Price 200 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description Gravel floats in this umber liquid. For 1 hour after drinking this tincture, you gain a +1 alchemical bonus to natural armor and a +2 alchemical bonus on Strength checks and Strength-based skill checks. The tonic swells your musculature, decreasing your movement speed by 5 feet, to a minimum of 5 feet, and causing you to take a -4 penalty on Reflex saving throws for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
