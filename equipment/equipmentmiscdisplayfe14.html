Tattoo - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tattoo Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 183, Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 21 Price 1 cp-20 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description The price of a tattoo depends on the quality, size, and number of colors used. A coin-sized tattoo in blue ink that will blur over a decade costs 1 cp, a hand-sized one in black ink that won't fade costs 1 sp, and a tattoo covering an entire back takes several sessions and costs 10 gp. Each additional color costs as much as a single tattoo of its size. While these prices represent the baseline, tattoos-like any other artwork-can be worth far more depending on the prestige of the artist.
