Wire - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wire Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58 Price 5 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This 6-foot length of copper wire is strong and flexible. By attaching it to two pitons or iron spikes across an area, you can create a simple tripwire trap. Tripwire Trap - CR 1/3 Type mechanical; Perception DC 15; Disable Device DC 10 Effects Trigger location; Reset automatic Effect trip combat maneuver +5 (+10 if the target is running)
