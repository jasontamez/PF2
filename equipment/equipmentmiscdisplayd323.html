Hoodwink cowl - Miscellaneous - Equipment Hoodwink cowl Source Occult Adventures pg. 249 Price 75 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This uncomfortable mask consists of a black cloth hood, padded leather earmuffs, and shuttered goggles that block vision. A creature wearing a hoodwink cowl is blinded and deafened, and it takes a -10 penalty on all other Perception checks.
