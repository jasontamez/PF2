Bottled lightning - Miscellaneous - Equipment Bottled lightning Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 107 Price 40 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Weapons Description Electricity crackles along a metal filament inside this small glass bottle. You can open the bottle as a standard action, unleashing a small bolt of lightning toward an enemy within 20 feet of you. This is a ranged touch attack that deals 1d8 points of electricity damage. Any creature in a line between you and the target (including the target) takes 1 point of sonic damage from the terrific clap of thunder the bolt generates (Reflex DC 15 negates). Crafting bottled lightning is a DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
