Breaker's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Breaker's kit Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 7 Price 353 gp; Weight 40 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit contains all manner of items useful for smashing down doors, creating diversions, and blowing things up. It includes a dose of alchemist's glue, a flask of alchemist's fire, a crowbar, a drill, a fuse grenade, a glass cutter, a jetcaster (see page 24), a vial of phosphorescent gel (see page 27), 4 pints of oil, a portable ram, a dose of rusting powder, 5 tindertwigs, and a wire.
