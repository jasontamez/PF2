Harrow carrying case - Miscellaneous - Equipment Harrow carrying case Source The Harrow Handbook pg. 30 Price 10 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Entertainment Description This small darkwood case contains a recessed area to hold a harrow deck and a harrow mat, as well as a compartment for incense. Nonmagical harrow cards are protected against rips, tears, water damage, bending, and flames so long as they are stored in a harrow carrying case.
