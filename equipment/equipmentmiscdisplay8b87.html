Wild-blood concoction - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wild-blood concoction Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 27 Price 100 gp; Weight — Category Concoctions Description This coppery-tasting substance causes your adrenaline production to spike, granting you a +2 alchemical bonus on melee damage rolls with natural and manufactured weapons. The concoction weakens your self-preservation urges and imparts a -2 penalty to your AC.
