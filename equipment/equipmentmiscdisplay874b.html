Wall hook - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wall hook Source Blood of the Sea pg. 19 Price 2 sp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description While surface dwellers sometimes use all manner of wall hooks to hang items for decoration or convenience, wall hooks are essential underwater to prevent loose objects from simply drifting away with the currents and tides. These hooks are found throughout underwater settlements and buildings to help the aquatic inhabitants keep track of their belongings.
