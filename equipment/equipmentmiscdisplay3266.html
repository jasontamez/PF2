Singer's solution - Miscellaneous - Equipment Singer's solution Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 236 Price 120 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description When you imbibe this draught, you can accept alchemical burn to gain an additional 4 rounds of bardic performance or raging song for the day. The effects of singer's solution last for 1 hour; if not spent within 1 hour, these additional rounds are lost. Singer's solution has no effect if you don't have the bardic performance or raging song class features. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
