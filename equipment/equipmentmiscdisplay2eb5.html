Stonechipper salve - Miscellaneous - Equipment Stonechipper salve Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 147 Price 150 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This gritty, gray ointment weakens rocky materials. When applied to an object made of stone, it reduces the object's hardness by 3 for 10 minutes. As a standard action, a creature can apply the paste to affect one 5-foot-square or a Medium or smaller creature. The paste only affects hardness, not damage reduction or similar defenses. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
