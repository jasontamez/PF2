Warhammer - Weapons - Equipment Warhammer Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 12 gp Weight 5 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type B; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Hammers Description This weapon consists of a wooden haft and a heavy, metal head. The head may be single (like a carpenter's hammer) or double (like a sledgehammer). The haft is long enough that you may wield it one- or two-handed. Though heavy and relatively slow to wield, warhammers are capable of delivering immense blows, crushing armor and flesh alike.
