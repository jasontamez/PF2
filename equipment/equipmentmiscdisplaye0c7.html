Light kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Light kit Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 11 Price 170 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Category Kits Description Ideal for use in subterranean environments or in combat against light-averse creatures, this small kit provides ample alchemical and magical sources of illumination. It contains an ioun torch, flash powder, a pair of sunrods, four torches, and six tindertwigs.
