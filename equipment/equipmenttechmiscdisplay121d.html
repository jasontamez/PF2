Chemalyzer - Miscellaneous - Technology Chemalyzer Source Technology Guide pg. 40 Price 2,500 gp Slot none; Weight 8 lbs. Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge Description A chemalyzer is a handheld unit with an extendable wand tipped with a sampling nozzle. It grants a +5 competence bonus on all Craft (alchemy) or Knowledge (engineering) checks to identify an unknown pharmaceutical, poison, chemical, or substance.
