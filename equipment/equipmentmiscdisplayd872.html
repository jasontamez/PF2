Pig grease - Miscellaneous - Equipment Pig grease Source Goblins of Golarion pg. 12 Price 1 sp; Weight 1 lb. Category Tools Description Maybe it's the wide heads, but more likely it's the lack of common sense that causes goblins to so often get stuck in narrow spaces. In such cases, a dose of pig grease is really helpful. An application of pig grease applied to the body grants a +2 circumstance bonus on all Escape Artist checks for 1 hour.
