Nodachi - Weapons - Equipment Nodachi Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 133 Statistics Cost 60 gp Weight 8 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type S or P; Special brace Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy; Polearms Description This very long, two-handed weapon has a slender but wickedly sharp 4-foot-long blade at one end. It is predominantly used by ground troops to attack mounted warriors, and can be used to hack riders from their mounts or set to receive a charge.
