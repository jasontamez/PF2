Poison ward salve - Miscellaneous - Equipment Poison ward salve Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 206 Price 15 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description This herbal salve can be applied directly to the skin to prevent the effects of contact poisons. If a creature touches a contact poison, but you apply the salve within 1 round of contact, the victim rolls the save twice and uses the higher result. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
