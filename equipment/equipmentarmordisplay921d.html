Hide - Armor - Equipment Hide Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 15 gp Weight 25 lbs. Armor Bonus +4; Max Dex Bonus +4; Armor Check Penalty -3 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 20%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description Hide armor is made from the tanned skin of particularly thickhided beasts, stitched with either multiple overlapping layers of crude leather or exterior pieces of leather stuffed with padding or fur. Damage to the armor is typically repaired by restitching gashes or adding new pieces of hide, giving the most heavily used suits a distinctively patchwork quality.
