Ghost hunter's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Ghost hunter's kit Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 13 Price 670 gp; Weight 22 lbs. Category Kits Description This sturdy satchel is the quintessential toolkit for detecting ghosts and sending them to their final slumber. This kit includes 10 candles, three flasks of holy water, one potion of lesser restoration, a crowbar, a folding shovel, four sunrods, one spiritbane spike (see above), and a wooden holy symbol.
