Enraging whip - Miscellaneous - Equipment Enraging whip Source Dungeon Denizens Revisited pg. 36 Price 10 gp; Weight — Category Animal Gear Description This whip has small caltrops or metal barbs woven into its length and a quartet of metal whistles attached to the fall; when snapped, the whistle sounds like screaming owlbear young. An owlbear's rider uses this noise to enrage the monster, prodding it to attack the nearest creature. Anyone who attacks an owlbear with such a whip draws its attack in preference to all other targets.
