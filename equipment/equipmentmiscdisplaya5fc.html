Pyramid buster's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Pyramid buster's kit Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 9 Price 310 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit contains items helpful in navigating the winding corridors of pyramids, but is also useful in other crypts and tombs. It contains three sticks of chalk, five sheets of parchment, 1 dose of synthetic mumia, a flask of alchemist's fire, a tangleburn bag, and a pair of sunrods.
