Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special nonlethal Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Close Description This weapon consists of a soft wrapping around a hard, dense core, typically a leather sheath around a lead rod. The head is wider than the handle and designed to spread out the force of the blow, making it less likely to draw blood or break bones.
