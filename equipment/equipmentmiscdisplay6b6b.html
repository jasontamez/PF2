Blain bane - Miscellaneous - Equipment Blain bane Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 9 Price 10 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description This alchemically infused wax is spread over exposed skin to protect it from chapping, cold, dry winds, heat, and the loss of moisture. Applying it takes 5 minutes; once applied, it reduces any nonlethal damage taken from cold or hot environments by 1 point for 6 hours. Crafting blain bane requires a successful DC 19 Craft (alchemy) check. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 19
