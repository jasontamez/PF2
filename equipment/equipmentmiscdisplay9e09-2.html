Melee contingency kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Melee contingency kit Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 20 Price 150 gp; Weight 14 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit contains items necessary for a melee combatant to overcome the most common forms of damage resistance. The kit includes a cold iron morningstar, a silver sickle, two flasks of acid, two flasks of alchemist's fire, and two flasks of holy water.
