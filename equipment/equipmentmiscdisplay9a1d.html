Anesthetic wine - Miscellaneous - Equipment Anesthetic wine Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 29 Price 50 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description This alchemical anesthetic, developed from ancient recipes for Sarenite sacred wine, grants a powerful resistance to pain. For 1 hour after drinking anesthetic wine, you gain a +5 bonus on saving throws against pain effects, and when you would take nonlethal damage, reduce the amount of damage taken by 1. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
