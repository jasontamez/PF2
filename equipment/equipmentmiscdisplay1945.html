Training sleeve - Miscellaneous - Equipment Training sleeve Source Animal Archive pg. 12 Price 100 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description This thick, heavily padded sleeve fits over the wearer's arm and serves as a target for animals being trained to attack. When used in conjunction with the Handle Animal skill to train an animal for a general purpose, this item grants the user a +5 competence bonus on her Handle Animal check to successfully combat train an animal.
