Padded - Armor - Equipment Padded Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 10 lbs. Armor Bonus +1; Max Dex Bonus +8; Armor Check Penalty 0 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 5%; Speed 30 ft./20 ft. Description More than simple clothing, padded armor combines heavy, quilted cloth and layers of densely packed stuffing to create a cheap and basic protection. It is typically worn by those not intending to face lethal combat or those who wish their maneuverability to be impacted as little as possible.
