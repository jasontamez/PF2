Hellknight plate - Armor - Equipment Hellknight plate Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 97, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 291 Statistics Cost 2,000 gp Weight 50 lbs. Armor Bonus +9; Max Dex Bonus +1; Armor Check Penalty -5 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 35%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description These distinctive suits of armor are a special type of masterwork full plate that grant additional effects when worn by a character with levels in the Hellknight prestige class. Apart from its distinctive look (each order of Hellknights has its own style of armor), a suit of Hellknight plate functions the same as a suit of masterwork full plate.
