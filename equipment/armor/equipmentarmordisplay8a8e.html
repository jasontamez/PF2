Spider-silk bodysuit - Armor - Equipment Spider-silk bodysuit Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 6 Statistics Cost 850 gp Weight 4 lbs. Armor Bonus +3; Max Dex Bonus +6; Armor Check Penalty -1 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 10%; Speed 30 ft./20 ft. Description This exotic form-fitting garment is woven entirely from alchemically treated silk harvested from giant spiders or other arachnid creatures. If you have the drow's weapon familiarity racial trait, you treat the spider-silk bodysuit's maximum Dexterity bonus as 2 higher.
