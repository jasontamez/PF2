Wooden - Armor - Equipment Wooden Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 179 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 25 lbs. Armor Bonus +3; Max Dex Bonus +3; Armor Check Penalty -1 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 15%; Speed 30 ft./20 ft. Description This suit of leather armor has plates of fire-treated wood sewn over vital areas. Though not as effective as metal armor, it offers better protection than leather alone. Unlike metal armor, the wood is slightly buoyant, and the armor check penalty for swimming in this armor is 0.
