Light wooden quickdraw - Armor - Equipment Light wooden quickdraw Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 179 Statistics Cost 53 gp Weight 6 lbs. Armor Bonus +1; Max Dex Bonus —; Armor Check Penalty -2 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 5%; Speed —/— Description A wooden quickdraw shield is essentially the same as a steel quickdraw shield, except it responds differently to some spells and effects (such as rusting grasp). A druid can use a heavy wooden quickdraw shield, but not a steel quickdraw shield.
