Tatami-do - Armor - Equipment Tatami-do Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Ultimate Combat pg. 129 Statistics Cost 1,000 gp Weight 45 lbs. Armor Bonus +7; Max Dex Bonus +3; Armor Check Penalty -6 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 35%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description Worn by samurai as a lighter-weight alternative to o-yoroi, tatami-do is a full-body field armor that combines both metal lamellar and kikko components into a suit of mail with a cloth backing. It typically includes a collapsible kabuto helmet or an armored hood, as well as arm, shoulder, and thigh guards.
