Crossbow bolts (10) - Weapons - Equipment Crossbow bolts (10) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 1 lb. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description A crossbow bolt used as a melee weapon is treated as a light improvised weapon (-4 penalty on attack rolls) and deals damage as a dagger of its size (critical multiplier ??2). Bolts come in a case or quiver that holds 10 bolts. All crossbows except repeating crossbows use these kinds of crossbow bolts, which are sometimes called "quarrels."
