Feed (per day) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Feed (per day) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 5 cp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Animal Gear Description Horses, donkeys, mules, and ponies can graze in some regions to sustain themselves, but feed provides more reliable nourishment. Riding dogs and other carnivores can be fed meat for the same cost. Except for horses, other Large creatures need to eat twice the feed of other animals each day. Huge creatures need four times the feed.
