Intuition serum - Miscellaneous - Equipment Intuition serum Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 236 Price 190 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description By drinking this clear, fizzy liquid, you gain 1d4 uses of alchemical inspiration that can be used only on Sense Motive checks. The benefits of intuition serum last for 1 hour; during this time, you must roll all Bluff checks twice and take the worse result. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
