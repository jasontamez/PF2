Rowboat - Miscellaneous - Equipment Rowboat Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 50 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 2 cp Category Transport, Sea Description This 8- to 12-foot-long boat with two oars holds two or three Medium passengers, and is either carried on the deck of a larger ship or moored to a dock onshore. A rowboat moves about 1-1/2 miles per hour.
