Brass knuckles - Weapons - Equipment Brass knuckles Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 17, Adventurer's Armory pg. 2, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d2 (small), 1d3 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special monk, see text Category Light; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Close; Monk Description These weapons fit snugly around the knuckles and allow you to deal lethal damage with an unarmed strike. You may hold, but not wield, a weapon or other object in a hand wearing brass knuckles. You may cast a spell with a somatic component while wearing brass knuckles if you make a successful concentration check (DC 10 + the level of the spell you're casting). Monks are proficient with brass knuckles. Brass knuckles can't be disarmed.
