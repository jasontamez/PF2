Windup music box - Miscellaneous - Equipment Windup music box Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 245 Price 25 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description A tiny crank protrudes from this small wooden box. After its crank is turned for 1 round, the box produces a pleasing melody for 4 rounds, as the pins on a revolving cylinder inside the box pluck the tuned teeth of a steel comb.
