Miscellaneous - Equipment Wire saw Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58 Price 150 gp (adamantine), 30 gp (common); Weight — (adamantine), — (common) Description This flexible saw consists of an abrasive wire hooked to two metal rings. By feeding the wire around a deadbolt or small door bar, you can use the rings to quietly saw through the obstruction. The saw takes 10 minutes to cut through a 2-inch piece of wood or a 1-inch iron bar. A wire saw can cut through items with hardness 10 or less. After five uses, the abrasive wire is worn smooth and must be replaced (costing 25 gp). Adamantine Wire Saw: This works twice as fast as a normal saw when cutting materials with hardness 10 or less, or at normal speed when cutting materials with hardness 11-20. Replacing an adamantine wire saw's wire costs 145 gp.
