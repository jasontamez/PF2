Candle lamp - Miscellaneous - Equipment Candle lamp Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56 Price 5 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description A candle lamp is a small lanternlike device with glass panels and a slot to hold a candle. It protects the candle from drafts and catches wax drippings. It provides the same light as a candle, but can be shuttered such that it only illuminates 1 to 3 adjacent squares. You can use a candle lamp to keep your hands warm.
