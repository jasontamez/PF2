Gray maiden plate - Armor - Equipment Gray maiden plate Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 84 Statistics Cost 1,500 gp Weight 50 lbs. Armor Bonus +9; Max Dex Bonus +1; Armor Check Penalty -6 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 35%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description The Gray Maidens' distinctive armor is one of their most iconic identifiers. Gray Maiden plate is mechanically identical to full plate, and any effect that functions with full plate functions with Gray Maiden plate. Certain options for Gray Maidens function only when they wear Gray Maiden plate.
