Throwing arrow - Weapons - Equipment Throwing arrow Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 19 Statistics Cost 5 sp Weight 1/2 lb. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description Too long to use in a bow, the throwing arrow has no nock at the tail. Instead, it has a notch ahead of the fletching where a throwing arrow cord can be attached (see above). If thrown without the use of a cord, treat a throwing arrow as a dart.
