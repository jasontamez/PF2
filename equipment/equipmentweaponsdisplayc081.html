Sling bullets (10) - Weapons - Equipment Sling bullets (10) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 1 sp Weight 5 lbs. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description Sling bullets are shaped metal balls, designed to be used by a sling or halfling sling staff. Bullets come in a leather pouch that holds 10 bullets.
