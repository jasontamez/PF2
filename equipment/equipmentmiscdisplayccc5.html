Arrow (grappling) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Arrow (grappling) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This small grappling hook is designed to be tied to a silk rope and fired from a bow. When fired, it has a range increment of 30 feet. An equivalent item exists for crossbows.
