Gravity rifle - Weapons - Technology Gravity rifle Source Technology Guide pg. 21 Statistics Cost 165,000 gp Type two-handed ranged; Proficiency exotic (firearms); Weight 6 lbs. Damage 1d10 (small), 2d6 (medium); Damage Type Force; Critical x2 Range 150 ft.; Capacity 20; Usage 1 charge; Special Automatic, touch Description A gravity rifle is a larger, more powerful version of the gravity pistol, and functions as such except for the differences listed here. When a gravity rifle is on sustained setting, it can move objects weighing no more than 350 pounds up to 20 feet per round, and the wielder gains a +4 bonus on combat maneuver checks; she still substitutes her Intelligence modifier in place of her Strength or Dexterity modifier when calculating her CMB for these maneuvers.
