Sunblock kohl - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sunblock kohl Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 15 Price 10 gp; Weight — Category Clothing Description Made from ground antimony, this sooty cosmetic is used to line the eyes. It grants the wearer a +2 circumstance bonus on saves against light-based effects that would dazzle or blind him for 8 hours. A container of kohl holds 10 applications.
