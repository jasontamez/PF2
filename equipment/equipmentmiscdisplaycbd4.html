Miscellaneous - Equipment Meal Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 95, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 3 sp (common, per day), 5 sp (good, per day), 1 sp (poor, per day); Weight - (common, per day), - (good, per day), - (poor, per day) Description The listed price is for a day's worth of meals. Poor meals might consist of bread, baked turnips, onions, and water. Common meals might consist of bread, chicken stew, carrots, and watered-down ale or wine. Good meals might consist of bread and pastries, beef, peas, and ale or wine.
