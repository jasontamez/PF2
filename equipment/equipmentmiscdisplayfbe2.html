Vest - Miscellaneous - Equipment Vest Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 sp-50 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Clothing Description Much like hats, vests appear in almost all cultures. Though the basic design remains the same, they vary wildly in cut, color, and function. The difference between the court vests of Taldor and the dancing vests of Varisia is quite striking.
