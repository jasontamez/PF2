Sonic pistol - Weapons - Technology Sonic pistol Source Technology Guide pg. 21 Statistics Cost 13,000 gp Type one-handed ranged; Proficiency exotic (firearms); Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Damage Type Sonic; Critical x2 Range 50 ft.; Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge; Special Semi-automic, touch Description A sonic pistol emits a blast of devastating sound waves that shatter and blast flesh, bone, and anything else they happen to strike. A creature critically hit by a sonic pistol must succeed at a DC 15 Fortitude save to resist being permanently deafened.
