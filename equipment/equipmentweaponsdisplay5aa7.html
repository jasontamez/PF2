Blade boot - Weapons - Equipment Blade boot Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 2 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special see text Category Light; Proficiency Martial Description Blade boots come with a spring-mounted knife that pops out when triggered with the right combination of toe presses. You can use a blade boot as an off-hand weapon. Releasing the knife is a swift action; rearming it is a full-round action. When the blade is extended, you treat normal terrain as difficult and difficult terrain as impassable.
