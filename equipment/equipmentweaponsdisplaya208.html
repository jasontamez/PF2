Bamboo shaft arrows - Weapons - Equipment Bamboo shaft arrows Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, Ultimate Combat pg. 130 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 1/2 lb. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description These foot-long arrows are used as ammunition for arrow tubes. As an improvised melee weapon, they deal damage equivalent to that of a spiked gauntlet. They come in a bamboo tube that holds 10 shafts.
