Gandasa - Weapons - Equipment Gandasa Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 19 Statistics Cost 15 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 2d4 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Axes Description A gandasa is a wide, single-edged dagger on a 2-foot handle and is wielded in combat like an axe. This weapon is also known as an elephant knife because the connection between the blade and the handle is often decorated to resemble a stylized elephant head.
