Spyglass - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spyglass Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 1,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description Objects viewed through a spyglass are magnified to twice their size. Characters using a spyglass take a -1 penalty on Perception skill checks per 20 feet of distance to the target, if the target is visible.
