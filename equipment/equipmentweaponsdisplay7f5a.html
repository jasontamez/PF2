Underwater light crossbow - Weapons - Equipment Underwater light crossbow Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 0, Advanced Race Guide pg. 195, Ultimate Equipment pg. 17 Statistics Cost 70 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range 80 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Ranged; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Crossbows Description An underwater light crossbow functions like its normal counterpart above water, and can be used underwater. When fired underwater, the crossbow has a range increment of 20 feet. Anyone proficient with a normal light crossbow can use an underwater light crossbow.
