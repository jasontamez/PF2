Axe musket - Weapons - Equipment Axe musket Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 43, Ultimate Combat pg. 137 Statistics Cost 1,600 gp Weight 6 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x4; Range 30 ft.; Type B and P; Special — Misfire 1-2 (5 ft.); Capacity 1 Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description This short musket features an axe blade at the end of its barrel. It can be used as both a musket and a battleaxe. It is considered a double weapon for the purposes of creating masterwork or magical versions of this weapon. If this firearm gains the broken condition, both the firearm component and the axe are considered broken. An axe musket uses either a bullet and a single dose of black powder or an alchemical cartridge as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
