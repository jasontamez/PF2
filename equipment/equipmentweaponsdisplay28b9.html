Totem spear - Weapons - Equipment Totem spear Source Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 14, Pathfinder #10: A History of Ashes pg. 35 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight 6 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range 10 ft.; Type P or B; Special — Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description Favored by the Shoanti of the Sklar-Quah, totem spears combine deadly weapons with the mournful music of Varisia's displaced natives. The shovel-like heads of these broad spears are decorated with variously oriented hollows. In addition to its use as a weapon, a totem spear can be played using the Perform (wind instrument) skill.
