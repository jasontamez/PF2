Signal jammer - Miscellaneous - Technology Signal jammer Source Technology Guide pg. 52 Price 10,000 gp Slot none; Weight 8 lbs. Capacity 24; Usage 1 charge/hour Description This small device emits a low, hissing sound when activated. While active, a white noise generator creates a 60-foot-radius spread in which all signals are completely blocked. For more information on signals, see the On Signals sidebar on page 43.
