Portable altar - Miscellaneous - Equipment Portable altar Source Demon Hunter's Handbook pg. 18, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Ultimate Equipment pg. 77 Price 250 gp; Weight 40 lbs. Category Tools Description A portable altar is typically made of wood and is ornately carved and decorated with the iconography of a specific religion. The top of the altar lifts up, and items such as candles, incense, and oil can be stored within. These altars are modest, but give traveling priests a center from which to say their daily prayers, perform minor rituals, or preach to an assembled crowd.
