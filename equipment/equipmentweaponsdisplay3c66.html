Dire bolas - Weapons - Equipment Dire bolas Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 24 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 4 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range 10 ft.; Type B; Special nonlethal, trip Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Description This specialized bolas can be used to perform ranged trip combat maneuvers against creatures far larger than the wielder. It consists of a pair of metal weights connected by a silk rope 20 feet long. Because of the great size and weight of the bolas, you need both hands to swing it around your head, and considerable time to get the weights up to speed. Making a ranged attack with it is a full-round action. You can use a dire bolas to trip a creature whose size is up to three size categories larger than yours.
