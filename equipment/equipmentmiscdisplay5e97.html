Alkali salt - Miscellaneous - Equipment Alkali salt Source Dungeon Denizens Revisited pg. 19 Price 30 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description These tiny granules can be dissolved in water, creating a slimy material used to coat metallic items such as weapons or armor. Such a coating neutralizes the metaleating acid of black puddings and gray oozes, protecting the item from 1d3 contacts with an ooze's acidic touch. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
