Shinobi shozoku - Miscellaneous - Equipment Shinobi shozoku Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 28 Price 50 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Clothing Description This close-fitting bodysuit of soft, dark fabric is favored by ninja and assassins. When worn, it grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks. Wearing armor with a base armor bonus higher than +1 negates this bonus, as does additional heavy clothing or equipment worn under the shinobi shozoku (at the GM's discretion).
