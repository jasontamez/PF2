Wakizashi - Weapons - Equipment Wakizashi Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 35 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical 18-20/x2; Range —; Type P or S; Special deadly Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description These short, slender blades are between 1 to 2 feet long. They are primarily used as back-up weapons, to decapitate defeated enemies, or to commit seppuku (ritual suicide) as a matter of honor. These blades are specifically worn by samurai and are intended to be paired with a katana. Together, this set of blades is referred to as a daisho.
