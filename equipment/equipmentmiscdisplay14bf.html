Monochrome Camouflage - Miscellaneous - Equipment Monochrome Camouflage Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 30 Price 40 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This slimy gray unguent distorts wavelengths of light beyond of the normal visual spectrum, causing you to appear blurry to creatures using darkvision. Applying the camouflage takes 1 minute and provides a +4 alchemical bonus on Stealth checks against creatures relying on darkvision to see. One dose of monochrome camouflage lasts for 1 hour.
