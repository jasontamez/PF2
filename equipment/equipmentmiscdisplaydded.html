Ambrosia (vial) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Ambrosia (vial) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 87 Price 100 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description Upon consumption, this heavenly elixir, brewed from holy water and blessed herbs, grants a +2 sacred bonus on saving throws against negative energy, energy drain, and death effects for 1 hour, including saves to remove negative levels. Ambrosia affects undead and evil outsiders as holy water. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
