Flayleaf - Miscellaneous - Equipment Flayleaf Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 20 gp; Weight — Category Black Market Description These narrow, rust-colored leaves produce a mildly hallucinogenic smoke that also serves as powerful sedative. Users are immune to pain for 4 hours after smoking flayleaf, but during this time they take a -5 penalty on saves against mind-altering effects.
