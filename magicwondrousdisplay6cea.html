Steelbone Frame - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Steelbone Frame Source People of the River pg. 29 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot armor; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 35 lbs. Description Fashioned from the salvaged mechanical "bones" of the first gearsmen that issued forth from Silver Mount, this gear-jointed, metal exoskeleton can be affixed to or removed from a suit of medium or heavy armor in 10 minutes. While attached, the enchanted frame bears some of the armor's burden, reducing the armor's check penalty by 2. In addition, three times per day, the wearer of a medium or heavy suit of armor attached to a steelbone frame can ignore the armor's reduction to his speed for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bull's strength, longstrider; Cost 3,000 gp
