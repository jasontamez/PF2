Sneaking Critical - Feats Sneaking Critical (Combat, Critical) Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 11 Your critical hits are particularly deadly. Prerequisites: Critical Focus, base attack bonus +9, sneak attack class feature. Benefit: Whenever you confirm a critical hit on a sneak attack, you can roll an additional number of sneak attack dice equal to your weapon's critical modifier. Special: You can apply the effects of only one critical feat to a given critical hit unless you possess Critical Mastery.
