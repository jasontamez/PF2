Focused Aberration Expertise - Feats Focused Aberration Expertise (Combat) Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 22 You hold strong against aberrations. Prerequisites: Favored enemy (aberration) class feature. Benefit: Choose either Fortitude saves against transformation effects (including transmutation spells and spell-like abilities) or Will saves against mind-affecting effects. You gain a bonus on the selected saving throw equal to half your favored enemy (aberration) bonus. Against aberrations' spells and abilities, you gain this bonus on Fortitude saves against transmutation effects and on Will saves against mind-affecting effects.
