Staff of Tricks - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Tricks Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 203 Aura moderate illusion CL 8th Slot none; Price 8,800 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This thin staff is made of stiff wood that has been wrapped in colorful bands of cloth and topped with a number of feathers, bells, and other decorative baubles. The staff allows use of the following spells:
  • Ghost sound (1 charge)
  • Mage hand (1 charge)
  • Prestidigitation (1 charge)
  • Silent image (2 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, ghost sound, mage hand, prestidigitation, silent image; Cost 4,400 gp
