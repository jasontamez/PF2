Impress Audiences Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 62 By performing a speech, song, play, or other artistic work, you can impress an audience. If you're in a prosperous city, you gain money for spending a day at this task. The amount depends on the result of your Performance check.

Action: Anywhere between one evening and a full day. Try Again: Yes, though the DC increases by 2 for each previous routine performance the same audience has witnessed you make. Masterwork Instrument: A masterwork instrument grants a +2 bonus on Performance checks to impress an audience.
