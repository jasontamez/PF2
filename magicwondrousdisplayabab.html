Black Feather Fan - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Black Feather Fan Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 166 Aura faint evocation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This elaborate fan is made with a dozen black feathers set into a metal handle embossed with a stylized crest. The bearer can use it to create a gust of wind, whispering wind, or wind wall. The fan's effects can be used 3 times per day, in any combination. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, gust of wind, whispering wind, wind wall; Cost 5,000 gp
