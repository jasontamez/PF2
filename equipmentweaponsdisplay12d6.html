Blowgun - Weapons - Equipment Blowgun Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 0, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142, Ultimate Equipment pg. 17 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1 (small), 1d2 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Ranged; Proficiency Simple Weapon Groups Thrown Description Blowguns are generally used to deliver debilitating (but rarely fatal) poisons from a distance. They are nearly silent when fired. For a list of appropriate poisons, see Poisons on page 110.
