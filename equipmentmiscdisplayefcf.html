Censer - Miscellaneous - Equipment Censer Source Occult Adventures pg. 248 Price 5 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This metal container comes with a small removable lid, and holds a small crucible in which to burn incense. Used in religious ceremonies or rituals, a censer can hold up to 10 pieces of incense, and burns 1 piece per hour when lit.
