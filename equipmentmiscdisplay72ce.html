Companion cold-weather outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Companion cold-weather outfit Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 242 Price 15 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This vestment works just like a cold-weather outfit, granting a +5 circumstance bonus on Fortitude saving throws against exposure to cold weather, except it is custom made for an animal companion. The added cost represents custom tailoring to ensure that the furs used don't cause unwanted reactions from the animal wearing the outfit.
