Extra Mercy - Feats Extra Mercy Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 124 Your lay on hands ability adds an additional mercy. Prerequisites: Lay on hands class feature, mercy class feature. Benefit: Select one additional mercy for which you qualify. When you use lay on hands to heal damage to one target, it also receives the additional effects of this mercy. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take this feat, select a new mercy.
