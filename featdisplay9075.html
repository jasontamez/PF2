Bonebreaker - Feats Bonebreaker (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 90 When your opponent is unable to adequately defend against them, your precise unarmed strikes break bone and tear tissue. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike, Jawbreaker, Stunning Fist, Heal 9 ranks. Benefit: When you make a successful Stunning Fist attempt against an opponent that is grappled, helpless, or stunned, you can forgo any other Stunning Fist effect to deal 1d6 Strength or Dexterity damage to that opponent. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114 When you make a successful Stunning Fist attempt against a creature that is dazed or staggered, you can spend 5 stamina points to forgo any other Stunning Fist effects and deal 1d6 points of Strength or Dexterity damage to that opponent instead.
