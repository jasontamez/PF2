Great Hatred - Feats Great Hatred (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 37 Your rage burns with vast intensity. Prerequisites: Gnome, hatred racial trait. Benefit: You gain an additional +1 bonus on melee and thrown weapon attacks against targets of your hatred racial trait. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 When you hit an opponent of a subtype subject to your hatred racial trait, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to deal an amount of extra damage equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
