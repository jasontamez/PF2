Performing Combatant - Feats Performing Combatant (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 112 You treat every combat as a performance, bringing flare and showmanship. Prerequisites: Dazzling Display, any one performance feat. Benefit: You can make performance combat checks in any combat. When making a performance check outside of performance combat, you can pick a single performance feat to use. You automatically gain any bonus on the performance combat check the feat grants, and then you make a DC 20 performance combat check. On a success, you gain the full effect of the performance feat you chose. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 128 When using this feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on your performance combat check equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
