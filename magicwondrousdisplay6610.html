Boots of the Mire - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of the Mire Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 229, Pathfinder #3: The Hook Mountain Massacre pg. 33, Rise of the Runelords Anniversary Edition pg. 420 Aura faint abjuration and transmutation; CL 5th Slot feet; Price 3,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These soft leather boots confer several powers upon the wearer. First, he is granted the power to walk on water in swampy environments, provided the water is no deeper than 5 feet-this effectively lets him move through swampy terrain and mud at no cost to his speed. He leaves no tracks or other signs of his passage as long as he's in swampy terrain, and never becomes uncomfortable or wet from rain, fog, or other forms of non-freezing precipitation. Finally, the boots grant him a +2 resistance bonus on all Fortitude saves made against poison and disease. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, endure elements, pass without trace, resistance, water walk; Cost 1,750 gp
