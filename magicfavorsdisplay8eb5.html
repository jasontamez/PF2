Kerchief of Remembrance - Favors - Magic Equipment Kerchief of Remembrance Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 13 Aura faint divination CL 3rd Slot arm or wrist; Price 600 gp; Weight — Description This favor is a simple checked kerchief worn around the arm or wrist. For 1 week, the recipient can automatically call to mind a detailed recollection of the presenter's features, even if his memory has been impaired or modified (such as by modify memory). In addition, the recipient can take a standard action to know the presenter's status in the same manner that the presenter can take a standard action to know the recipient's status. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, eagle's splendor, status; Cost 300 gp
