Riving Strike - Feats Riving Strike (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 155 When you infuse your weapon with arcane might, your attacks make foes more susceptible to magic. Prerequisites: Arcane Strike, ability to cast arcane spells. Benefit: If you have a weapon that is augmented by your Arcane Strike feat, when you damage a creature with an attack made with that weapon, that creature takes a -2 penalty on saving throws against spells and spell-like abilities. This effect lasts for 1 round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130 When you use this feat, you can spend up to 3 stamina points to increase the duration of the effect by 1 round for each stamina point spent.
