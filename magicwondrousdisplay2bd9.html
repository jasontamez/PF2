Mattock of the Titans Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 310, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 523 Aura strong transmutation; CL 16th Slot none; Price 23,348 gp; Weight 120 lbs. Description This digging tool is 10 feet long. Any creature of at least Huge size can use it to loosen or tumble earth or earthen ramparts (a 10-foot cube every 10 minutes). It also smashes rock (a 10- foot cube per hour). If used as a weapon, it is the equivalent of a Gargantuan +3 adamantine warhammer, dealing 4d6 points of base damage. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Craft Magic Arms and Armor, move earth; Cost 13,348 gp
