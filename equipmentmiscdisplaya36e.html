Pyrography kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Pyrography kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 1 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Kits Description Also called a "woodburning kit," this set of metal needles, styluses, and brands is used to draw on leather or wood by burning it. Using the kit requires a fire source; the needle or stylus is heated in the fire and held in a thick leather handle, then used like a pen.
