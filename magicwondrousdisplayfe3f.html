Earthquake Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Earthquake Boots Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 30 Aura strong evocation; CL 15th Slot feet; Price 67,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These thick hide boots allow the wearer to detect unseen enemies, and they provide a final line of defense against overwhelming foes. The wearer gains tremorsense with a range of up to 60 feet as long as she is standing on solid ground. Additionally, once per day, by stomping repeatedly on an earthen or stone surface as a full-round action that provokes attacks of opportunity, the wearer can cast earthquake as a spell-like ability. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, earthquake, see invisibility; Cost 33,750 gp
