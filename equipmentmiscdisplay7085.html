Moonrod - Miscellaneous - Equipment Moonrod Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 37 Price 10 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This 1-foot-long, silver-tipped tin rod glows dimly when struck as a standard action. It sheds dim light in a 30-foot radius. However, the light given off by a moonrod is particularly easily to perceive for creatures with low-light vision. For such creatures, a moonrod increases the light level by one step (to a maximum of normal) for a 60-foot radius. It glows for 6 hours, after which the silver tip is burned out and worthless. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
