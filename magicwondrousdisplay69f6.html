Book of the Loremaster - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Book of the Loremaster Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 284, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 300 Aura moderate divination; CL 7th Slot none; Price 15,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This digest-sized book contains a seemingly random collection of words, phrases, and strange mnemonic aids. Three times each day, a bard can consult it while using the lore master class feature in order to gain a +5 competence bonus when taking 10 or taking 20 on a Knowledge check. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, clairaudience/clairvoyance; Cost 7,500 gp
