Magister Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 202 The Magister guides the kingdom's higher learning and magic, promoting education and knowledge among the citizens and representing the interests of magic, science, and academia. In most kingdoms, the Magister is a sage, a wizard, or a priest of a deity of knowledge, and oversees the governmental bureaucracy except regarding finance. Benefit: Add your Charisma modifier or Intelligence modifier to Economy. Vacancy Penalty: Economy decreases by 4.
