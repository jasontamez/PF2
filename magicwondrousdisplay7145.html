Nightdrops - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Nightdrops Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 312 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 250 gp; Weight — Description This narrow vial holds 5 doses of viscous liquid. When dripped onto a willing creature's eyes, it gives the creature low-light vision for 1 hour. The affected creature takes a -2 penalty on saves against light-based spells and effects and gains the light sensitivity weakness. A dose affects one creature, regardless of how many eyes it has. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, beast shape I; Cost 125 gp
