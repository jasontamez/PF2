Mountain pattern - Armor - Equipment Mountain pattern Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Ultimate Combat pg. 128 Statistics Cost 250 gp Weight 40 lbs. Armor Bonus +6; Max Dex Bonus +3; Armor Check Penalty -4 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 30%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description This medium armor consists of hundreds of small, interlocking pieces of steel shaped to resemble an ancient symbol for the word "mountain." The mail is then riveted to a cloth or leather backing. It is worn like a mail coat and covers your torso, shoulders, and thighs.
