Shadow Jumper's Tunic - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Shadow Jumper's Tunic Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 27 Aura faint conjuration; CL 3rd Slot chest; Price 16,200 gp; Weight — Description This dark-gray tunic allows a character to make a single jump of up to 40 feet per day, or four jumps of 10 feet each per day. All jumps must begin and end in an area that has at least some dim light. If the wearer has at least 4 levels in the shadowdancer prestige class, the tunic instead adds 40 feet to the total number of feet he can shadow jump in 1 day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, dimension door; Cost 8,100 gp
