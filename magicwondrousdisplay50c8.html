Gloves of Swimming and Climbing - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gloves of Swimming and Climbing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 237, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 515 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 6,250 gp; Weight — Description These apparently normal lightweight gloves are obviously made for use outdoors.
