Necklace of Construct Aversion - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Necklace of Construct Aversion Source Construct Handbook pg. 19 Aura moderate divination and enchantment; CL 11th Slot neck; Price 132,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description A creature wearing this thick necklace is automatically aware of all constructs within 60 feet as per the lifesense ability but only sensing constructs. Constructs can't see, hear, or otherwise detect the presence of the wearer, even when using extraordinary or supernatural sensory capabilities such as blindsense, blindsight, scent, or tremorsense. If the wearer touches a construct or attacks any creature, this concealing ability ceases to function for 1 minute. Constructs with an Intelligence score of 3 or greater can attempt a DC 18 Will save to detect the wearer. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, invisibility, locate object; Cost 66,000 gp
