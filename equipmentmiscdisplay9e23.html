Snake oil - Miscellaneous - Equipment Snake oil Source Occult Adventures pg. 252 Price 20 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description This mildly toxic liniment numbs flesh and can soothe injuries. Though the tincture is actually poisonous, an imbiber who avoids becoming poisoned recovers from minor injuries. Snake Oil Type poison (contact); Save Fortitude DC 12 Onset 1 round; Frequency 1/minute for 5 minutes Effect On a failed save, snake oil deals 1 point of nonlethal damage. However, on a successful save, the toxin heals 1d6 points of nonlethal damage; Cure 1 save Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
