Surgeon's tools - Miscellaneous - Equipment Surgeon's tools Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77, Pathfinder #8: Seven Days to the Grave pg. 61, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 20 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Category Tools Description When used in conjunction with a healer's kit, surgeon's tools raise the kit's bonus to a +3 circumstance bonus on Heal checks to treat wounds or deadly wounds.
