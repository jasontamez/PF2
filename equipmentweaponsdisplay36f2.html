Starknife - Weapons - Equipment Starknife Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, Rise of the Runelords Player's Guide pg. 11, Curse of the Crimson Throne Player's Guide pg. 13, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142, Varisia, Birthplace of Legends pg. 14 Statistics Cost 24 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x3; Range 20 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Light; Thrown Description From a central metal ring, four tapering metal blades extend from this weapon like points on a compass rose. By gripping the crossbar that runs through the weapon's open middle, a wielder can strike with it as a deadly melee weapon. Alternatively, by gripping it by the outer rim, a starknife's light, aerodynamic design allows it to be thrown short distances in a manner similar to a chakram.
