Expert Explorer - Feats Expert Explorer Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 110 You are trained to seek out the unknown places of the wild and can explore more efficiently than your untrained rivals. Prerequisites: Knowledge (nature) or Survival 5 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on skill checks when using the exploration rules presented on pages 124-125 to detect features in a territory. If you succeed at such a skill check by 5 or more, you gain an additional 1d4 Discovery Points.
