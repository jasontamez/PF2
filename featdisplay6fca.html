Calculating Mind - Feats Calculating Mind Source Occult Mysteries pg. 49 You see potential in the world around you that nobody else notices. Your numerological abilities are heightened by this uncommon aptitude. Prerequisites: Sacred Geometry, Knowledge (engineering) 5 ranks. Benefit: Anytime you use the Sacred Geometry feat, you can use d8s instead of d6s when rolling your dice pool. You can use any combination of d6s and d8s that you wish, as long as the number of dice does not exceed the number of ranks you possess in Knowledge (engineering).
