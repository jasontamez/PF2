Hellin Tallhallow Short, stout for a halfling, and powerful, Hellin sees himself as the quintessential paladin-and does not suffer fools who doubt his prowess or speak condescendingly about his size. Most of the time he is a cheerful defender of everything lawful and good, but he is quick to anger if small folk or innocents are threatened, abused, or mocked. Combat Encounters: Hellin picks fights with slavers or slave owners, even in lands where slavery is legal or ignored. In his mind, those who turn a blind eye are just as guilty as those who forge the chains. Roleplaying Suggestions: Hellin may need assistance with freeing or protecting a particularly large band of slaves.
