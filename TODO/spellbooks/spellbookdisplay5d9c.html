The Formulae of Master Gebr - Spellbooks - Equipment The Formulae of Master Gebr (Formula Book, Level 20 Alchemist) Source Ultimate Magic pg. 127 These four cloth-bound books are neatly printed-as with a printing press or some other form of mechanical transcription-with a title page, a table of contents, and a useful cross-referenced index. They have been treated well and are clearly reference works, rather than laboratory notes; the writer was obviously a most disciplined and focused inventor.
