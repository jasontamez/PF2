Arctic Call - Spellbooks - Equipment Arctic Call (Spellbook, Level 11 Evoker) Source Ultimate Magic pg. 124 Fine vellum sheets fill these two well-worn books. Beautiful illustrations depict the effects of each spell, many with a distinctly arctic theme. Though the pages and their wintry illustrations are of high quality, the protective leather case that encloses both books smells and feels greasy, as if the hide has been crudely waterproofed with animal fat.
