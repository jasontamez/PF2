Dying (Negative Hit Points) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 189 If your hit point total is negative, but not equal to or greater than your Constitution score, you're dying. A dying character immediately falls unconscious and can take no actions. A dying character loses 1 hit point every round. This continues until the character dies or becomes stable.
