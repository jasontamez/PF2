Creation Time Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 181 The default amount of time it takes to create an item with this system is the same as in the normal item creation rules. The process can't be accelerated by increasing check DCs as with the normal rules. Space the challenges out evenly. For instance, having four challenges for a 10,000 gp item (two base, two random) means having a challenge at the beginning, another after 3 days of work, the third challenge 7 days in, and the final on day 10.
