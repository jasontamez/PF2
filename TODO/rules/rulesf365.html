An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Major Artifacts Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 547 Major artifacts are unique items-only one of each such item exists. These are the most potent of magic items, capable of altering the balance of a campaign. Unlike all other magic items, major artifacts are not easily destroyed. Each should have only a single, specific means of destruction.
