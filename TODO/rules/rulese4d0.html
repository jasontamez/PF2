An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Getting Into and Out of Armor Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 153 The time required to don armor depends on its type; see Table 6-7. Don: This column tells how long it takes a character to put the armor on. (One minute is 10 rounds.) Readying (strapping on) a shield is only a move action. Don Hastily: This column tells how long it takes to put the armor on in a hurry. The armor check penalty and armor bonus for hastily donned armor are each 1 point worse than normal. Remove: This column tells how long it takes to get the armor off. Removing a shield from the arm and dropping it is only a move action. Table 6-7: Donning Armor
