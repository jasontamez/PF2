Fighter Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 89 A character who chooses fighter as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features. Bravery: At 3rd level, he gains the bravery class feature as a fighter of his character level - 1. Armor Training 1: At 7th level, he gains armor training 1. Weapon Training 1: At 11th level, he gains weapon training 1. Armor Training 2: At 15th level, he gains armor training 2. Weapon Training 2: At 19th level, he gains weapon training 2.
