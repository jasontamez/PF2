An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Grass Fire (Hill, Plain) Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 159 Grass fires are often caused by lightning or careless camp fires. A grass fire is similar to a forest fire, except it can be spotted at twice the normal distance, and a PC caught in its area saves against heat damage only every 10 rounds.
