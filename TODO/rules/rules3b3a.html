Treasurer Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 203 The Treasurer monitors the state of the kingdom's Treasury and citizens' confidence in the value of their money and investigates whether any businesses are taking unfair advantage of the system. The Treasurer is in charge of the tax collectors and tracks debts and credits with guilds and other governments. Benefit: Add your Intelligence modifier or Wisdom modifier to Economy. Vacancy Penalty: Economy decreases by 4. The kingdom cannot collect taxes-during the Edict phase, when you would normally collect taxes, the kingdom does not collect taxes at all and the taxation level is considered "none."
