Step 6: Skills and Feats Source Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 292 Using Table 1-4, determine how many skill ranks your creature has based on its type and Hit Dice. Assign these ranks as determined by the creature's concept. A creature's class skills are determined by its type (see the creature types). Creatures with a low Intelligence typically only have ranks in Dexterity- and Strength-based skills. After you have assigned skills, it's time to assign the creature's feats. Each creature with an Intelligence score receives a number of feats equal to 1 + 1 per every 2 Hit Dice after the first (so, 1 at 1 HD, 2 at 3 HD, etc.).
