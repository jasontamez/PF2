Settlement Population Ranges Source GameMastery Guide pg. 203 A settlement's population is left to the GM to assign, but you can use a settlement's type to help you determine just how many folks live in the city. Since the actual number of people who dwell in a settlement has no impact on game play, the number you choose is largely cosmetic-feel free to adjust the suggested values below to fit your campaign.
Settlement TypePopulation Range
