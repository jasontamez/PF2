Curses Perilous Demesne, Major Source Horror Adventures pg. 145 Type regional curse; Save Will DC 16 Effect As per minor perilous demesne, except this can involve up to four hazards, each of CR 6 or less, imperil creatures in the area. If any hazards are encountered together, the encounter's effective CR from hazards must be no more than 7 (for instance, two CR 5 hazards could be encountered together).
