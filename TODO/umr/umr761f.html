Universal Monster Rules Hardness (Ex) Source Bestiary 5 pg. 294 When a creature with hardness takes damage, subtract its hardness from the damage. Only damage in excess of its hardness is subtracted from its hit points. A creature with hardness doesn't further reduce damage from energy attacks, ranged attacks, or other types of attacks as objects typically do. Adamantine weapons bypass hardness of 20 or less. Format: hardness 10 Location: Defensive Abilities.
