Universal Monster Rules Trip (Ex) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 300, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 305, Bestiary 2 pg. 302, Bestiary 3 pg. 300, Bestiary 5 pg. 301 A creature with the trip special attack can attempt to trip its opponent as a free action without provoking an attack of opportunity if it hits with the specified attack. If the attempt fails, the creature is not tripped in return. Format: trip (bite) Location: individual attacks.
