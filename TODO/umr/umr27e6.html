Universal Monster Rules Mythic Magic (Su) [Mythic] Source Bestiary 5 pg. 296, Bestiary 4 pg. 296, Mythic Adventures pg. 227 Up to three times per day, when the creature casts a spell, it can cast the mythic version instead (as with all mythic spells, the creature must expend mythic power to cast a mythic spell in this way). Format: mythic magic 3/day Location: Special Attacks.
