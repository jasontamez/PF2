Universal Monster Rules Poisonous Blood (Ex) [Mythic] Source Bestiary 5 pg. 297, Bestiary 4 pg. 297, Mythic Adventures pg. 227 Any creature that confirms a critical hit against the monster with a piercing or slashing melee weapon is sprayed with poison. (Melee weapons with reach don't endanger their users in this way.) The type of poison depends on the monster. Unless otherwise stated, this poison uses the poison's normal DC, though some monsters might have a poison DC that is not Constitution-based. Format: poisonous blood (dragon bile) Location: Defensive Abilities.
