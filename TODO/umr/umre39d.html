Universal Monster Rules Ability Damage and Drain (Ex or Su) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 290, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 298, Bestiary 2 pg. 294, Bestiary 3 pg. 292, Bestiary 4 pg. 290, Bestiary 5 pg. 290 Some attacks or special abilities cause ability damage or drain. While ability damage can be healed naturally, ability drain is permanent and can be restored only through magic. Format: 1d4 Str drain Location: Special Attacks and individual attacks.
