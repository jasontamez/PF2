Universal Monster Rules Darkvision (Ex or Su) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 292, Bestiary 4 pg. 292, Bestiary 5 pg. 292 A creature with darkvision can see in total darkness, usually to a range of 60 feet. Within this range, the creature can see as clearly as a sighted creature could see in an area of bright light. Darkvision is black and white only but otherwise like normal sight. Format: darkvision 60 ft. Location: Senses.
