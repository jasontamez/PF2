Universal Monster Rules Greensight (Su) Source Bestiary 6 pg. 294, Bestiary 4 pg. 295, Bestiary 5 pg. 294, Mythic Adventures pg. 227 The monster can see through thick plant matter as though it were transparent, usually with a range of 60 feet. Greenery, leaves, undergrowth, and vines offer no concealment to the monster's sight, though solid wood still blocks its line of sight. Format: greensight 60 ft. Location: Senses.
