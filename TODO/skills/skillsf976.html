Skills Skills Skills represent some of the most basic and yet most fundamental abilities your character possesses. As your character advances in level, he can gain new skills and improve his existing skills dramatically. Unchained - Skill Unlock Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 86 With sufficient ranks in Spellcraft, you earn the following. 5 Ranks: Identifying magic items takes 1 full round, and the time required to learn a spell from a spellbook is halved. 10 Ranks: You can identify magic items without using detect magic, though the DC is increased by 10. 15 Ranks: Identifying magic items is a standard action, and the time required to learn a new spell from a spellbook is reduced to 1 minute per spell level. 20 Ranks: Whenever you attempt a caster level check, attempt a Spellcraft check at a -20 penalty at the same DC. If the spellcraft check succeeds, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on your caster level check.
