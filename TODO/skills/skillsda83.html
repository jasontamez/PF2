Skills Skills Skills represent some of the most basic and yet most fundamental abilities your character possesses. As your character advances in level, he can gain new skills and improve his existing skills dramatically. Unchained - Skill Unlock Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 84 With sufficient ranks in Heal, you earn the following. 5 Ranks: When you treat deadly wounds, the target recovers hit points and ability damage as if it had rested for a full day. 10 Ranks: When you treat deadly wounds, the target recovers hit points as if it had rested for a full day with long-term care. 15 Ranks: When you treat deadly wounds, the creature recovers hit point and ability damage as if it had rested for 3 days. 20 Ranks: When you treat deadly wounds, the target recovers hit point and ability damage as if it had rested for 3 days with long-term care.
