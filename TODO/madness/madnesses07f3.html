Madnesses Psychosomatic Loss Source Horror Adventures pg. 187 Type greater madness; Save DC 20 Onset immediate Effect The afflicted character becomes blinded or deafened, loses another special sense (like scent or blindsense), loses the use of limbs, or loses a special movement speed (like a fly or swim speed). The GM chooses how the psychosomatic loss manifests. Dormancy Effect None Description While there is nothing physically wrong with the afflicted character, some kind of mental trauma has forced her brain to shut down sensory or motor systems. Furthermore, magical effects that typically remove these conditions (such as remove blindness/ deafness) have no effect on this madness; the madness must be cured in order to regain the senses or motor skills.
