Jorngarl's Harm - Weapons - Magic Equipment Jorngarl's Harm Source Classic Treasures Revisited pg. 57 Aura strong necromancy [evil] and transmutation CL 18th Slot none; Price 150,320 gp; Weight 24 lbs. Description This Large +2 keen vorpal greataxe has an adamantine haft and a chipped, pitted guillotine blade instead of a curved axehead. Obviously made for a giant's hands, the axe laughs evilly whenever it kills. Like the final blades of Galt, any creature killed by it (whether from decapitation or simple damage) finds its soul trapped within the blade, and it cannot be raised from the dead while so trapped. Presumably the Gray Gardeners would know how to release a soul from the blade. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, circle of death, enervation, keen edge; Price 75,320 gp
