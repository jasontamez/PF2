Athletic - Feats Athletic Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 118 You possess inherent physical prowess. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on Climb and Swim skill checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in one of these skills, the bonus increases to +4 for that skill. Mythic Athletic Source Mythic Adventures pg. 60 You excel far beyond others at acts of physical prowess. Prerequisites: Athletic. Benefit: The bonus on Climb and Swim skill checks from Athletic increases by 2. In addition, you can expend one use of mythic power to treat a Climb or Swim check as if you had rolled a natural 20. You must decide to use this ability before making the roll.
