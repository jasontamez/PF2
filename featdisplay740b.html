Assisted Ascension - Feats Assisted Ascension (Teamwork) Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 5 You and your allies have practiced scaling heights together. Prerequisites: Climb 5 ranks. Benefit: When you succeed at a Climb check, allies within 20 feet who also have this feat can climb 5 feet along the same surface as an immediate action. You gain a +5 bonus on Climb checks to catch any falling ally who also has this feat.
