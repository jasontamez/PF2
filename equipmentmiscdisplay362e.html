Fortifying brew - Miscellaneous - Equipment Fortifying brew Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 206 Price 20 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description This liquid causes a pleasant warming sensation when consumed. For the next hour, you gain a +2 morale bonus on saving throws against fear. Using more than 1 dose within 24 hours causes you to become nauseated for 1 hour. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
