Sunlit Strike - Feats Sunlit Strike Source Blood of Angels pg. 25 Your melee blows deal extra damage to creatures sensitive to light, making you adept at hunting foul monsters of the night. Prerequisites: Arcane Strike, able to prepare or cast daylight. Benefit: Whenever you successfully hit an enemy that is vulnerable to light (such as by the light blindness, light sensitivity, or sunlight powerlessness universal monster rules) with a melee weapon you've imbued with your Arcane Strike feat, you deal an additional 1d6 points of damage to the target of your attack. The struck creature must also succeed at a Fortitude saving throw (DC 10 + your character level + your Strength modifier) or become staggered for 1 round.
