Breastplate - Armor - Equipment Breastplate Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 200 gp Weight 30 lbs. Armor Bonus +6; Max Dex Bonus +3; Armor Check Penalty -4 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 25%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description A breastplate protects a wearer's torso with a single piece of sculpted metal, similar to the core piece of a suit of full plate. Despite its sturdiness, its inflexibility and open back make it inferior to complete suits of metal armor, but still an improvement over most non-metal armors.
