Girdle of Opposite Gender - Cursed Items - Magic Equipment Girdle of Opposite Gender Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 346, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 310 Aura moderate transmutation CL 10th Slot belt; Weight 1 lb. Description When this magical belt is put on, the wearer must immediately make a DC 20 Fortitude saving throw or be transformed into a person of the opposite gender. The character's abilities, mind, and spirit remain unaffected; only the character's sex changes. If the character's saving throw is a natural 1, the item actually removes all gender from the wearer, giving him an androgynous, neutered appearance. The change is permanent unless undone with curse-removing magic. Once its magic takes effect, the belt can be removed without effort. A creature can only be affected by a particular girdle once, though other girdles of this type can cause further transformations. Construction Requirements any belt; Cost —
