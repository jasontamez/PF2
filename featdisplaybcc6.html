Belier's Bite - Feats Belier's Bite (Combat) Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 26 Your unarmed strikes cause your opponent to bleed. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike. Benefit: When you damage an opponent with an unarmed strike, you deal an extra 1d4 bleed damage. Special: This ability does not stack with other special abilities, attacks, or items that allow you to deal bleed damage. The belier devil appears in Pathfinder Adventure Path volume #12.
