Inexplicable Luck - Feats Inexplicable Luck Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 77 Others are often dumfounded by your luck. Prerequisites: Defiant Luck, human. Benefit: Once per day, as a free action before a roll is made, you gain a +8 bonus on any single d20 roll. You can also use this ability after the roll is made, but if you do, this bonus is reduced to +4.
