Iomedae's Needle - Weapons - Magic Equipment Iomedae's Needle Source Pathfinder #141: Last Watch pg. 49 Aura strong divination and necromancy CL 18th Slot weapon; Price 8,016 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This brilliant mithral ballista bolt radiates a blinding sheen. Its fletching is made of white and gold feathers. This +1 light ballista bolt functions as a greater undead slaying arrow, except its enchantment is so potent that it deals 50 points of damage even if the saving throw is successful. If the wielder utters the name of his target as the bolt is fired, he gains the benefits of true strike on the attack roll. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, true strike, undeath to death; Price 4,016 gp
