Dispelling Fist - Feats Dispelling Fist Source Ultimate Combat pg. 97 By focusing on your knowledge of magic and spells that negate its powers, you use your bare hands to rip magical defenses from your enemy. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +11, ability to cast dispel magic, caster level 7th. Benefit: If you have dispel magic prepared or can cast it spontaneously, you can cast it as a swift action after hitting an opponent with an unarmed strike. Treat this as a targeted dispel against the opponent you hit.
