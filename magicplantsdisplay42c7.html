Plants - Magic Equipment Portal Oak Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 249 Aura moderate conjuration CL 9th Slot none; Price 45,000 gp; Weight 500 lbs. Description These large oak trees are always grown in a pair. With a command word, a creature can enter one portal oak and exit from the other. A character can determine the command word with a successful DC 20 Knowledge (nature) check. The two trees must be grown within 1 mile of each other. Portal oaks produce no fruit or other products tied to their effect and are constantly active. Construction Requirements Cultivate Magic Plant, tree stride, cultivator must have 3 ranks in Knowledge (nature); Cost 22,500 gp
