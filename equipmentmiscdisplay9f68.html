Firework (paper candle) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Firework (paper candle) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 107, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 1 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Weapons Description This finger-sized explosive detonates noisily 1 round after lighting. Anyone in the same square as a paper candle when it explodes must make a DC 15 Fortitude save or be dazzled for 1d4 rounds. Crafting this item is a DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
