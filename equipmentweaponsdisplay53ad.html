Damage 1d10 (small), 2d6 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type B; Special — Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Hammers Description The crude metal of this massive hammer's head ends in multiple blunt spikes that help concentrate the force of a powerful swing down into a smaller, more focused area, allowing it to smash through armor with greater ease. Due to the tremendous size and weight of its head, an earth breaker is poorly balanced, and requires two hands to properly lift and control.
