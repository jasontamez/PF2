Horseshoes of Great Burden - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Horseshoes of Great Burden Source Seekers of Secrets pg. 55 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 2,500 gp; Weight 24 lbs. (for four) Description These sturdy iron horseshoes come in sets of four like ordinary horseshoes, but are much heavier. When affixed to an animal's hooves, they double the animal's carrying capacity. All four shoes must be worn by the same animal for the magic to be effective. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bear's endurance, bull's strength; Cost 1,250 gp
