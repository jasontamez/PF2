Arrowbreak Bow Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 342 Aura moderate evocation CL 6th Slot none; Weight 2 lbs. Description This shortbow is beautifully made and inlaid with iridescent designs, appearing to be a +2 shortbow. When it fires its arrows, though, it does it with so much force that the arrows are always broken on either a hit or a miss. This bow must be used as its owner's primary ranged weapon and can only be discarded after the owner is subject to a remove curse spell or similar effect. Construction Requirements +2 shortbow; Cost —
