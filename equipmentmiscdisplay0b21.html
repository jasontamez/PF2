Consecrated weapon - Miscellaneous - Equipment Consecrated weapon Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 24 Price +150 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Channel Foci Deity Any Description Consecrated weapons may be of any type'swords, axes, masterwork, mithral, and even magical-and function appropriately. When activated, the cleric charges the weapon with positive or negative energy (as appropriate) so it deals extra damage when it hits a target. This functions like the Channel Smite feat, except the additional damage is equal to half the cleric's channel energy damage (the target makes a Will save as normal, based on the cleric's channel energy DC). The weapon remains charged with this energy until it successfully strikes a creature or 24 hours pass, whichever comes first. Creatures other than the cleric are able to use the charged weapon and this ability.
