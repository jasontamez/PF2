Dwarven double waraxe - Weapons - Equipment Dwarven double waraxe Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 17 Statistics Cost 60 gp Weight 12 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special see text Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description This hefty waraxe is similar to the common dwarven waraxe, but its recurved blade spans forward and back from its head like a deadly butterfly. A dwarven double waraxe grants a +1 bonus on all attack rolls after the first when using Cleave or Great Cleave.
