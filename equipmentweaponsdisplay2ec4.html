Khopesh - Weapons - Equipment Khopesh Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, Adventurer's Armory pg. 33, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 178 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 8 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type S; Special trip Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy Description This heavy blade has a convex curve near the end, making its overall shape similar to that of a battleaxe. A typical khopesh is 20 to 24 inches in length. Its curved shape allows the wielder to hook around defenses and trip foes. The elegant shape of a khopesh leads some artisans to cover them in ornate decorations.
