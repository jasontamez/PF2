Banner of the Rushing Horde - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Banner of the Rushing Horde Source Monster Codex pg. 165 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot none; Price 60,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description The banner of the rushing horde is a tattered cloth flag, typically 3 feet wide and 6 feet long, depicting the device or insignia of an orc tribe or unit. It can be carried on a staff, affixed to a pole that's planted in the ground, or hung from the harness of a great beast. Allies within 60 feet of the banner gain the benefits of the Improved Bull Rush and Improved Overrun feats. If planted, the flag does not need a bearer, but if it is toppled or touched by an enemy, it loses its effectiveness until reclaimed and replanted by allies of its owner. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bull's strength; Cost 30,000 gp
