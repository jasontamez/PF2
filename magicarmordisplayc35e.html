Fangtide Scale Mail - Armor - Magic Equipment Fangtide Scale Mail Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 251 Aura moderate enchantment, evocation, and illusion CL 10th Slot armor; Price 4,890 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Description This +1 glamered scale mail is decorated with fangs and is usually made from the hide of a reptilian beast such as a basilisk. The wearer can use burst bonds and wrath each once per day; as a full-round action, the wearer can activate both of these abilities at the same time. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, burst bonds, disguise self, wrath; Price 2,590 gp
