Arcane Talent - Feats Arcane Talent Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 151 Magic is in your blood, and at your fingertips. Prerequisites: Cha 10; elf, half-elf, or gnome. Benefit: Choose a 0-level spell from the sorcerer/wizard spell list. You can cast this spell three times per day as a spell-like ability. The caster level is equal to your character level. The save DC is 10 + your Charisma modifier.
