Crepuscular Trinket - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Crepuscular Trinket Source Planar Adventures pg. 53 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot neck; Price 4,000 gp; Weight — Description This simple necklace contains an hourglass-shaped pendant with one black half and one white half. Once per day, the wearer can speak a command word to gain spell resistance 15 against spells and spell-like abilities with the light or shadow descriptors, as well as spells and spell-like abilities of the shadow subschool. This effect lasts for 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, dancing lights, darkness; Cost 2,000 gp
