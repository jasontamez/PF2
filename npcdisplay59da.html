Kiro Kiro, the mud shaman of a remote tribe, sees outsiders as a threat to his culture and people. He acquired a green dragonhide breastplate years ago from a trader, but keeps it hidden under cloaks and wraps, ashamed that he had to buy it rather than earn it through combat. Combat Encounters: Kiro uses mud pits as traps to entangle his enemies. Roleplaying Suggestions: A stubborn xenophobe, Kiro does not like speaking with those who are not of his tribe.
