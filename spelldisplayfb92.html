Protection from Spells - Spells Protection from Spells Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 328 School abjuration; Level arcanist 8, psychic 8, sorcerer 8, summoner 6, wizard 8 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (diamond worth 500 gp), F (One 1,000 gp diamond per target. Each subject must carry the gem for the duration of the spell. If a subject loses the gem, the spell ceases to affect him.) Effect Range touch Targets up to one creature touched per four levels Duration 10 min./level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description The subject gains a +8 resistance bonus on saving throws against spells and spell-like abilities (but not against supernatural and extraordinary abilities).
