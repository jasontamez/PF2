Awe-Inspiring Smash - Combat Stamina - Feats Awe-Inspiring Smash Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24 Related Feat Awe-Inspiring Smash After successfully performing a bull rush or sunder combat maneuver, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the bonus on performance combat checks that you gain from Awe-Inspiring Smash by 1 per stamina point spent.
