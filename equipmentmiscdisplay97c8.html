Boar's bellow - Miscellaneous - Equipment Boar's bellow Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 236 Price 190 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description When imbibed, this bitter, rust-colored concoction grants you 1d4 uses of alchemical inspiration that can be used only on Intimidate checks. The benefits of boar's bellow last for 1 hour; during this time, you must roll all Diplomacy checks twice and take the worse result. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 15
