Firearm bullet, silver - Weapons - Equipment Firearm bullet, silver Source Ultimate Combat pg. 141 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight — Category Ammunition; Proficiency Exotic Description This ammunition is specifically crafted from silver, and though nonmagical, it is particularly detrimental to lycanthropes, automatically confirming any critical threats against such creatures. A firearm that is shooting a silver bullet takes a -1 penalty on damage rolls (with a minimum of 1 point of damage).
