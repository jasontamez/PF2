Crystal-sweet concoction - Miscellaneous - Equipment Crystal-sweet concoction Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 25 Price 50 gp; Weight — Category Concoctions Description This sugary blue drink, often cut with mint to reduce the cloying sweetness, brightens your demeanor and sweetens your voice, granting a +2 alchemical bonus on Diplomacy checks. It renders you naive and perilously trusting, however, imparting a -4 penalty on your Sense Motive checks.
