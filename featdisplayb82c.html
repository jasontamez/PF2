Stunning Irruption - Feats Stunning Irruption (Combat) Source Champions of Corruption pg. 23 When you smash your way into a room, you gain more than just the element of surprise. Prerequisites: Str 15, Power Attack, base attack bonus +5. Benefit: Before starting combat, you can attempt to break through a door, window, or wall to enter a room. If you succeed, the violence of your arrival is so great that all characters within 20 feet of your entry point must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw (DC = 10 + your base attack bonus) or be stunned instead of acting in the surprise round (if there is one) plus 1 round thereafter. Characters who succeed at this save are instead shaken for 1d4 rounds.
