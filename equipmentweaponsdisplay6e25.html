Broken-back seax - Weapons - Equipment Broken-back seax Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 30 Statistics Cost 40 gp Weight 4 lbs Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type P & S; Special See text Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description Used in Avistan since before Earthfall, seaxes are straight, single-edged blades. The broken-back seax is the size of a short sword, and has a broad blade with a distinctive trapezoidal shape. If your Strength score is less than 15, you take a -2 penalty on attack rolls with a broken-back seax, as you're unable to put enough force into the blows to land them effectively.
