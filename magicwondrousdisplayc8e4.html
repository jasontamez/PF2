Mask Of A Thousand Tomes - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mask Of A Thousand Tomes Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 245 Aura faint divination; CL 5th Slot head; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The random snippets of script that write across the surface of this parchment-colored eyeless mask hint at the knowledge of the many tomes stored within. The mask grants its wearer a +10 competence bonus on all Knowledge skill checks, but the wearer is blinded while wearing the mask. The mask must be worn for 10 minutes before the wearer gains its bonus. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, clairaudience/clairvoyance; Cost 5,000 gp
