Siphon ray - Miscellaneous - Equipment Siphon ray Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 6 Price 5 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description This parasitic, hand-sized, cartilaginous fish attaches to a creature and filters nutrients directly from its host's bloodstream. However, a siphon ray reoxygenates the blood in the process, allowing the host to hold her breath for twice as long while underwater. A siphon ray must be attached for at least 1 minute before providing this benefit, and every hour a ray remains attached per day, the host must succeed at a DC 15 Fortitude saving throw or become fatigued. While attached, a siphon ray avoids most attacks as though it were a piece of equipment unless specifically targeted. A siphon ray must be kept in at least 1 gallon of salt water when not in use. If it is out of salt water for 1 hour, the siphon ray expires.
