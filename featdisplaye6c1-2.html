Stunning Croak - Feats Stunning Croak Source Monster Codex pg. 9 The terrifying force of your croak disorients and debilitates your enemies. Prerequisites: Sonic Croak, Throat Pouch, boggard, terrifying croak. Benefit: When you use your terrifying croak ability, instead of imparting its normal effect you can choose to stun one creature within 30 feet for 1 round. If the target succeeds at a Fortitude save, it is instead staggered for 1 round. The DC of this Fortitude save is the same as that of your terrifying croak ability.
