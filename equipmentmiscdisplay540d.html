Merchant's scale - Miscellaneous - Equipment Merchant's scale Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 2 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Tools Description A merchant's scale is a simple lever on a fulcrum, from which two trays hang. It grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Appraise checks involving items that are valued by weight, including anything made of precious metals.
