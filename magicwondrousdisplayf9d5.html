Scrap Collector's Strap - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Scrap Collector's Strap Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 23 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 500 gp; Weight — Description When this leather strap is tied around the grip of a broken weapon, the user can ignore the effects of the broken condition on that weapon. If the weapon loses its remaining hit points, it is destroyed as normal. Tying or untying the strap is a full-round action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, mending; Cost 250 gp
