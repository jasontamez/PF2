Celestial Plate Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Celestial Plate Armor Source Pathfinder #11: Skeletons of Scarwall pg. 29 Aura faint transmutation (good) CL 8th Slot armor; Price 28,650 gp; Weight 25 lbs. Description Celestial plate armor is a sturdier version of the standard celestial armor. This bright silver suit of +3 full plate is remarkably light, and is treated as medium armor. It has a maximum Dexterity bonus of +6, an armor check penalty of -3, and an arcane spell failure chance of 20%. It allows the wearer to use fly on command (as the spell) once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, creator must be good, fly; Price 15,150 gp, 1,080 XP
