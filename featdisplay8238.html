Pinch Time - Feats Pinch Time Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 21 Often having one parent with a human lifespan and one with an elven lifespan, many half-elves have a skewed perception of time, and none more so than the Spireborn, who also study the subject of time academically. Prerequisites: Character level 5th, Spireborn half-elf. Benefit: Once per day, as a standard action, you can gain the benefits of haste until the end of your next turn.
