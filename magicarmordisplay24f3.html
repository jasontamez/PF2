Folding - Armor - Magic Equipment Folding Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 30 Aura strong conjuration and transmutation CL 13th Slot shield quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description On command, a folding shield transforms to take on the form of another shield. As a move action, the wearer of a folding shield can change the shield from its current form into a buckler, heavy shield, or tower shield. The wearer of a folding shield must be proficient with the type of shield into which he wishes to transform the folding shield.
