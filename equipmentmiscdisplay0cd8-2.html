Meditation tea - Miscellaneous - Equipment Meditation tea Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 101 Price 30 gp; Weight - Category Alchemical Remedies Description Drinking this cloudy tea has a soothing effect that clarifies your thoughts. For 10 minutes after drinking the tea, you gain a +2 alchemical bonus against mind-affecting effects. If you drink meditation tea while suffering from a mind-affecting affect, you may immediately roll another saving throw (with the +2 bonus) against the effect; you may gain this particular benefit only once per day. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 20
