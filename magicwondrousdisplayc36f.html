Dyso's Travel Canopy - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Dyso's Travel Canopy Source Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 26 Aura faint evocation; CL 7th Slot none; Price 750 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This 5-foot-square cloth canopy is brightly colored with gold fringe around the edges, something a slightly foppish sultan might use while resting on the seashore. When you speak the command word, the canopy unfolds and floats 3 feet above your head, positioning itself to keep you shaded. The travel canopy cannot hold any weight. It reverts to its 1-foot-square folded form at your command, or automatically when it is out of the sun for more than 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, floating disk; Cost 375 gp, 30 XP
