Archetypes Wave Rider Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 47 Throughout the Inner Sea region, wave riders patrol harbors, keeping them safe from dangerous sea creatures and watching for smugglers. The most famous of these is the hippocampus-riding Sea Cavalry of Absalom. Weapon and Armor Proficiency: Wave riders are proficient with all simple and martial weapons and with light and medium armor, but not with shields. This replaces the cavalier's weapon and armor proficiencies. Seafaring Companion: At 1st level, a wave rider gains Monstrous Mount as a bonus feat. The wave rider can use this feat only to select a hippocampus mount. Otherwise, this ability functions identically to and replaces the cavalier's mount ability.
