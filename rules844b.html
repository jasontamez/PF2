Advanced Other Traits Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 239 Grabbing Appendages (6 RP): Prerequisites: Any type except humanoid, Large size; Benefit: Members of this race have a small group of appendages that are useful for little more than to aid in grappling. Members of this race gain Improved Grapple as a bonus feat, and can maintain a grapple and still make attacks with their main appendages.
