Vulpine Pounce - Feats Vulpine Pounce (Combat) Source Dragon Empires Primer pg. 5 You can change shape mid-charge and pounce on an opponent in the same round. Prerequisites: Swift Kitsune Shapechanger, base attack bonus +10, kitsune. Benefit: When you change shape into your kitsune form and use the charge action in the same round, you can make a full attack against your opponent.
