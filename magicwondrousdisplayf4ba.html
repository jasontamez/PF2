Agile Alpenstock Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 276 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 2,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This gnarled wooden walking stick aids a creature's travel, granting a +5 foot enhancement bonus to speed and a +2 enhancement bonus to CMD against trip attempts and on saving throws to avoid being tripped. These bonuses apply as long as the agile alpenstock is held in hand. It can be wielded in combat as a quarterstaff. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, longstrider; Cost 1,000 gp
