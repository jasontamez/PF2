Animal Divining Pot - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Animal Divining Pot Source Occult Adventures pg. 256 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 1,400 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description These small clay vessels are fired in shapes representing various small animals-fish, lizards, mice, and sparrows are the most common. If a pot's user places a living animal matching the pot's form within, the user can communicate with the animal through whispers, as speak with animals. This ability functions three times per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, speak with animals; Cost 700 gp
