Invisible enemies kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Invisible enemies kit Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 17 Price 900 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit is designed to help adventurers deal with surprise encounters against magically concealed enemies, and comes in a small, waterproof satchel. The kit contains 2 scrolls of glitterdust, 6 1/2-pound packages of powder such as flour, and one packet of dust of appearance.
