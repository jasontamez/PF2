Spirited Charge - Combat Stamina - Feats Spirited Charge Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 Related Feat Spirited Charge When making a mounted charge, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Strength bonus. If your charge attack hits, you deal an amount of extra damage equal to the number of stamina points you spent. This additional damage is doubled by this feat (or tripled if you're using a lance).
