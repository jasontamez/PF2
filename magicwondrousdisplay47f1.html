Elixir of Spirit Sight - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Elixir of Spirit Sight Source Pathfinder #39: The City of Seven Spears pg. 55 Aura faint divination and transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 1,000 gp; Weight — Description This elixir seems to be made of thick vapor rather than liquid, and constantly emits soft whispers when uncorked.
