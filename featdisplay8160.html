Death or Glory - Feats Death or Glory (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 94 Even when facing a larger foe, you aren't afraid to take great risks in order to finish the fight. Prerequisites: Str 13, Power Attack, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: Against a creature of size Large or larger, you can make a single melee attack as a full-round action, gaining a +4 bonus on the attack roll, damage roll, and critical confirmation roll. You gain an additional +1 on this bonus at base attack bonus +11, +16, and +20 (for a maximum of +7 at base attack +20). After you resolve your attack, the opponent you attack can spend an immediate action to make a single melee attack against you with the same bonuses. Special: You can combine the full-round action attack this feat allows with the benefit of Vital Strike, Improved Vital Strike, or Greater Vital Strike. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 117 When you make a Death or Glory attack, you can spend 5 stamina points to automatically negate the opponent's immediate-action attack against you after it takes the action but before it rolls the attack.
