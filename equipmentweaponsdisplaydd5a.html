Spiked chain - Weapons - Equipment Spiked chain Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 21, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 25 gp Weight 10 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 2d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special disarm, trip Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Flails Description A spiked chain is about 4 feet in length and covered in wicked barbs. Some have metal hoops to use as handgrips. You can use the Weapon Finesse feat to apply your Dexterity modifier instead of your Strength modifier to attack rolls with a spiked chain sized for you, even though it isn't a light weapon.
