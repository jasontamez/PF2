Diseases Tetanus Source Pathfinder #1: Burnt Offerings pg. 43 Type injury; Fortitude Save DC 14 Onset 1d6 days; Frequency 1/day Effect 1d4 Dex. Each time someone takes Dexterity damage from tetanus, there's a 50% chance his jaw muscles stiffen, preventing speech and the use of spells with verbal components for the next 24 hours. Cure 2 consecutive saves
