Mad Hallucination - Spells Mad Hallucination Source Ultimate Magic pg. 227 School illusion (phantasm); Level arcanist 2, bard 2, mesmerist 2, psychic 2, redmantisassassin 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one humanoid creature Duration 5 minute/level (maximum 1 hour) Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell induces a hallucinogenic reality in the mind of your target. Surfaces seem to swim, and movement constantly distracts the eye. The target takes a -2 penalty on Will saving throws, caster level checks, Intelligence-based skill checks, and Wisdom-based skill checks.
