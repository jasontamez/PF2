Special Attacks blood drain (1d2 Con damage), poison Statistics Str 20, Dex 13, Con 16, Int -, Wis 13, Cha 1 Base Atk +3; CMB +8; CMD 19 (can't be tripped) Skills Swim +13 SQ compression Ecology Environment temperate oceans Organization solitary, pack (2-4), or bloom (5-12) Treasure none Special Abilities Poison (Ex) Tendrils-contact; save Fort DC 15; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d4 Str damage; cure 2 consecutive saves. Tentacle (Ex) A crimson jellyfish's tentacle is a primary natural attack that deals bludgeoning damage. Description The crimson jellyfish is a blooddrinking creature whose red color comes from the blood absorbed throughout its body after it feeds. A large fish or sea mammal encountering a pack or bloom of these creatures can be drained of blood in a matter of minutes. Fortunately, a crimson jellyfish's bright color makes it fairly easy to avoid under well-lit conditions
