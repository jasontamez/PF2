Diseases Fungal Flu Source Pathfinder #13: Shadow in the Sky pg. 81 Type inhaled; Fortitude Save DC 12 Onset 3d6 days; Frequency 1/day Effect 1d4 Con. Rolling a 1 on any Fortitude save to resist taking damage from fungal flu results in blindness. The blindness persists - even resisting spells like remove blindness/deafness - until the disease is cured. Cure 2 consecutive saves
