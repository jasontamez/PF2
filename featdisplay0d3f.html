Orc Rampage - Feats Orc Rampage (Combat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 13 You effortlessly savage fearful enemies. Prerequisites: Bullying Blow, Intimidating Prowess, Orc Fury Style, Intimidate 5 ranks, weapon familiarity racial trait. Benefit: While using Orc Fury Style, you gain a +1 bonus on attack rolls and damage rolls against shaken opponents, and you don't take an attack penalty when using the Bullying Blow feat.
