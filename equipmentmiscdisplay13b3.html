Chronicler's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Chronicler's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 47 Price 40 gp; Weight 4-1/2 lbs. Category Kits Description This bundle contains a map case, two vials of ink, two inkpens, 10 sheets of paper, two blank journals, a pound of fine powder for drying ink, and a 20-foot measuring cord. The supplies usually suffice for chronicling a single expedition of not more than 2 months' duration.
