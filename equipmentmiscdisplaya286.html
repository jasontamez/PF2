Magus's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Magus's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 22 gp; Weight 31 lbs. Category Kits Description This includes a backpack, a bedroll, a belt pouch, a flint and steel, ink, an inkpen, an iron pot, a mess kit, rope, soap, a spell component pouch, torches (10), trail rations (5 days), and a waterskin. The kit does not contain a spellbook because a magus begins play with a spellbook.
