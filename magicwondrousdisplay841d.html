Vagrant's Hood - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vagrant's Hood Source Villain Codex pg. 129 Aura faint illusion; CL 3rd Slot head; Price 4,400 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This linen hood is simply decorated with the colors of the forest and designed to cast a dramatic shadow upon the wearer's face, obscuring identifiable features. A vagrant's hood grants the wearer a +4 competence bonus on Disguise checks and makes the wearer's voice sound deep and throaty, as per vocal alteration. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, disguise self, vocal alteration; Cost
