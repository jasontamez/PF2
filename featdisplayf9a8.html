Wood Crafter - Feats Wood Crafter Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 121 Eschewing the use of metals in your craft, you excel at crafting armor from wood. Prerequisites: Craft (armor) 3 ranks, Knowledge (nature) 3 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +4 bonus on Craft (armor) checks to create armor and shields from wood or special materials containing wood (such as darkwood). If you succeed at the check by 5 or more, you decrease the cost to make the item by 10%.
