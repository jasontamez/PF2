Blessed Fist - Spells Blessed Fist Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 175 School transmutation [good]; Level cleric 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description The target is considered to be armed even when unarmed, so it doesn't provoke attacks of opportunity when it attacks foes with unarmed strikes. Its unarmed strikes can deal lethal or nonlethal damage (target's choice). If the target already has this ability (such as from the monk unarmed strike ability or the Improved Unarmed Strike feat), its unarmed strikes gain a +1 enhancement bonus on attack rolls and damage rolls, and they count as good-aligned weapons for the purposes of overcoming damage reduction.
