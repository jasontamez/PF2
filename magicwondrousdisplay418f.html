Wound Paste - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Wound Paste Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 125 Aura faint conjuration; CL 1st Slot none; Price 50 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description Prized by slavers as an affordable way to stop a captive from bleeding to death, a dose of wound paste acts as a stabilize spell when slathered on a dying creature. Applying wound paste is a standard action that provokes an attack of opportunity. A pot of wound paste contains 5 doses. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, stabilize; Cost 25 gp
