Errani Kamaki (Gillmen) - Weapons - Magic Equipment Errani Kamaki (Gillmen) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 226 Aura moderate transmutation CL 10th Slot none; Price 35,005 gp; Weight 16 lbs. Description The following weapon is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity. This +2 wounding harpoon has a head carved from whalebone, and has been fitted with a grip akin to that of a calvary lance. It deals double damage to and ignores the hardness of wooden objects, including most ships. These weapons frequently appear among Absalom's Sea Cavalry, and are responsible for many of the wrecks in the Flotsam Graveyard. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bleed, warp wood, creator must be a gillman; Price 17,655 gp
