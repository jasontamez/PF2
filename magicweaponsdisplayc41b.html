Arachnid's Fang - Weapons - Magic Equipment Arachnid's Fang Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 25 Aura moderate transmutation CL 9th Slot none; Price 18,302 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Poison applied to this +1 dagger seeps into the blade, awaiting the perfect moment for release. After a hit with the dagger but before damage is determined, the wielder of an arachnid's fang can release a stored dose of poison into the creature struck by the blade as a free action. Up to 8 doses of poison can be stored in the dagger at a time, but only 1 dose can be released on a given attack. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, touch injection; Price 9,302 gp
