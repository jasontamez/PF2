Erinyes Company Cloak - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Erinyes Company Cloak Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 86 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot shoulders; Price 22,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This crimson cloak is trimmed with feathers taken from erinyes devils. Three times per day on command, the cloak transforms into a pair of black-feathered wings that grant the wearer a fly speed of 30 feet (average maneuverability) for 10 minutes. If the wearer is wearing Gray Maiden plate, its armor check penalty does not apply to the wearer's Fly checks. In addition, the wearer gains a +2 resistance bonus on all saving throws. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fly, resistance; Cost 11,000 gp
