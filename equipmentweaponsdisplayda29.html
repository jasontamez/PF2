Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type S; Special see text Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Heavy; Close Description The traditional form of this tribal weapon is a short metal blade bound to the skull of a large horned lizard, but a skilled smith can craft one entirely out of metal. A traditional klar counts as a light wooden shield with armor spikes; a metal klar counts as a light steel shield with armor spikes. The klar's shield entry appears on page 12.
