Armor Bonus +1; Max Dex Bonus —; Armor Check Penalty -1 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 5%; Speed —/— Description A light wooden shield is essentially the same as a light steel shield, except it responds differently to some spells and effects (such as rusting grasp). A druid can use a light wooden shield, but not a light steel shield.
