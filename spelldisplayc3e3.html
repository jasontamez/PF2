Know the Enemy - Spells Know the Enemy Source Ultimate Magic pg. 226 School divination; Level cleric 1, hunter 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, ranger 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 minute Components S, V, DF Effect Range personal Target you Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You commune with the divine, reflecting on one type of creature you encountered in the last day. You may make a Knowledge check regarding that creature type with a +10 insight bonus. Mythic Know the Enemy Source Mythic Adventures pg. 99 The insight bonus increases to +15 plus your tier. If the check is successful, you gain a +5 insight bonus on your next attack roll or saving throw against a creature of that type, as long as the roll occurs within 24 hours of casting the spell.
