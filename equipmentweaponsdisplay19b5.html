Battleaxe - Weapons - Equipment Battleaxe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 142 Statistics Cost 10 gp Weight 6 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special — Category One-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Axes Description The handle of this axe is long enough that you can wield it one-handed or two-handed. The head may have one blade or two, with blade shapes ranging from half-moons to squared edges like narrower versions of woodcutting axes. The wooden haft may be protected and strengthened with metal bands called langets.
