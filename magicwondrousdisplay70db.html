Lantern of Revealing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 307, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 522 Aura faint evocation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 30,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This lantern operates as a normal hooded lantern. While it is lit, it also reveals all invisible creatures and objects within 25 feet of it, just like the spell invisibility purge. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, invisibility purge; Cost 15,000 gp
