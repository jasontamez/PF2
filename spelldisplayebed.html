Spells Bless Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 249 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level adept 1, cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, shaman 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 50 ft. Area The caster and all allies within a 50-ft. burst, centered on the caster Duration 1 min./level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description Bless fills your allies with courage. Each ally gains a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and on saving throws against fear effects. Bless counters and dispels bane. Mythic Bless Source Mythic Adventures pg. 85 The +1 morale bonus applies on attack rolls, weapon damage rolls, and all saving throws. Once during the spell's duration, an affected creature can roll an attack roll or saving throw twice and take the higher result. The target must decide to use this ability before the first roll is attempted.
