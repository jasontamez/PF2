Kestros - Weapons - Equipment Kestros Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 33, Ultimate Equipment pg. 21 Statistics Cost 1 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x3; Range 50 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Thrown Description The kestros is an oddly shaped Bronze Age sling used for launching kestros darts. Your Strength modifier applies on damage rolls when you use a kestros, just as it does for thrown weapons. You can fire-but not load-a kestros with one hand. Loading a kestros is a move action that requires two hands and provokes attacks of opportunity. Halflings treat the kestros as a martial weapon.
