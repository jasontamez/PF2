Dueling dagger - Weapons - Equipment Dueling dagger Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 8 Statistics Cost 12 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range 10 ft.; Type P or S; Special see text Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Light; Thrown Description This dagger is used in the off hand to block attacks. When you fight defensively or use Combat Expertise while wielding a dueling dagger, your bonus to AC increases by 1. This benefit applies even if you did not attack with the dueling dagger. If you aren't proficient with a dueling dagger, you treat it as a dagger and gain no special benefits. Any effects that apply to daggers apply to dueling daggers.
