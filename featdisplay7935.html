Smiting Reversal - Feats Smiting Reversal (Combat) Source Agents of Evil pg. 19 Your can withstand the onslaught of holy or unholy forces. Prerequisites: Power Attack, Toughness. Benefit: Three times per day after being targeted by the smite attack of an enemy, you can immediately make an attack of opportunity against the target. You gain a bonus on this attack roll equal to your Charisma bonus. If this attack of opportunity hits, you gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to your character level. This attack of opportunity ignores all damage reduction the creature possesses.
