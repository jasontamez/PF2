Pepperbox - Weapons - Equipment Pepperbox Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 43, Ultimate Combat pg. 138 Statistics Cost 3,000 gp Weight 5 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x4; Range 20 ft.; Type B and P; Special — Misfire 1-2 (5 ft.); Capacity 6 Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description This pistol has six barrels instead of one. The entire barrel housing can be quickly rotated by hand between shots (a free action requiring one free hand), allowing all six bullets to be fired before the weapon must be reloaded. Each barrel of a pepperbox uses either a bullet and 1 dose of black powder or a single alchemical cartridge as ammunition. This is an early firearm.
