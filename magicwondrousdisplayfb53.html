Hydrodaemon Runestone - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Hydrodaemon Runestone Source Book of the Damned pg. 197, Book of the Damned - Volume 3: Horsemen of the Apocalypse pg. 41 Aura moderate conjuration [evil]; CL 11th Slot none; Price 3,300 gp; Weight — Description This water-polished stone is carved in the shape of a frog-like head and decorated with evil runes. It feels lighter than it should, as if hollow and partially filled with liquid. When a hydrodaemon runestone is crushed, smashed, or broken (a standard action), a hydrodaemon appears as if summoned by a summon monster VI spell. Normally, the daemon is under the control of the creature that broke the runestone, but there is a 25% chance that the daemon is uncontrolled and attacks its summoner. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, summon monster VI; Cost 1,650 gp
