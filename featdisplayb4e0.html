Skilled Rager - Feats Skilled Rager Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 156 With practice, you've mastered your rage to allow yourself more versatility. Prerequisites: Rage class feature. Benefit: Choose one Charisma-, Dexterity-, or Intelligence-based skill. While you are raging, you can use this skill. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, it applies to a different skill.
