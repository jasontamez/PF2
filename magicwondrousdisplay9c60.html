Goblin Fire Drum Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 300 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 2,000 gp (Normal), 4,500 gp (Greater); Weight 1 lb. Description This small crude-looking drum is attached to a strap long enough to loop around the wearer's neck. A successful DC 12 Perform (percussion) check activates the drum, which enhances any nearby natural or magical fire as long as the bearer spends a move action each round to continue playing. All fires (including fire spells and effects) within 30 feet of the bearer deal an additional 1 point of fire damage (or 2 points for the greater version). All unattended flammable alchemical objects such as tindertwigs, flasks of oil or alchemist's fire, and fireworks within this range have a 10% chance of spontaneously igniting or exploding (as appropriate to the item) when the drum activates. This chance increases by 5% each round and is checked at the start of the performer's turn as long as the playing continues. A greater goblin fire drum has all the above abilities and can create pyrotechnics once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, pyrotechnics, spark (Advanced Player's Guide); Cost 1,000 gp (Normal), 2,250 gp (Greater)
