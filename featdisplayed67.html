Improved Feint - Feats Improved Feint (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 127 You are skilled at fooling your opponents in combat. Prerequisites: Int 13, Combat Expertise. Benefit: You can make a Bluff check to feint in combat as a move action. Normal: Feinting in combat is a standard action. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123 You can select this feat even if you don't meet the Ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefit of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to feint as a move action.
