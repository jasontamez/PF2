Fleeting Defect - Spells Fleeting Defect Source Black Markets pg. 31 School transmutation; Level arcanist 2, bard 2, bloodrager 2, magus 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one solid object that does not have the broken condition, or one construct Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Fortitude negates (harmless, object), see text; Spell Resistance no Description You cause a single object to temporarily gain the broken condition. The target must be a single solid object, regardless of its composition, weighing up to 10 pounds per caster level. Attended objects are allowed a Fortitude save. When this spell ends, the object reverts to its original quality. An item destroyed while under the effects of this spell remains destroyed when this spell ends. This spell can be cast on a single construct to disrupt its animating force. A construct that fails its saving throw takes a -2 circumstance penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, skill checks, and ability checks for the duration of the spell.
