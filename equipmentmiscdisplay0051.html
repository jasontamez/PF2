Miscellaneous - Equipment Lock Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 40 gp (average), 80 gp (good), 20 gp (simple), 150 gp (superior); Weight 1 lb. (average), 1 lb. (good), 1 lb. (simple), 1 lb. (superior) Description This can be a padlock, an inset lock in a door or chest, or any other type of keyed lock. The DC to open a lock with the Disable Device skill depends on the lock's quality: simple (DC 20), average (DC 25), good (DC 30), or superior (DC 40).
