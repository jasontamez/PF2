Divine Dignity - Feats Divine Dignity Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 210 Secrets of the First Vault help you perfect your spellcasting. Prerequisites: Combat Casting, ability to cast divine spells, worshiper of Abadar. Benefit: Three times per day, as a free action when you cast a harmless divine spell or a domain spell, you can choose for the casting to not provoke attacks of opportunity. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. When you do, you can use this feat three more times per day.
