Jatha Ventoth Jatha champions the benign nature spirits. He spends his time patrolling his beloved forests, chasing unicorns, and conversing with treants. When danger threatens his home, he has the charisma necessary to unite the creatures of the woods into an army of focused chaos. Combat Encounters: Jatha might attack the PCs if he believes they or foes that pursue them are a threat to his home or the creatures within it. He attempts to use detect evil before attacking. Roleplaying Suggestions: Jatha engages in mock battles with mischievous sprites, possibly leading the PCs to think he is under attack.
