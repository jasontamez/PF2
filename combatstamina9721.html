Gnome Weapon Focus - Combat Stamina - Feats Gnome Weapon Focus Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 Related Feat Gnome Weapon Focus When you make a successful attack with a gnome weapon against an opponent that is at least one size category larger than you, you can spend up to 5 stamina points. If you do, you gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
