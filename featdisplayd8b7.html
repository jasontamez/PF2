Let Them Come - Feats Let Them Come (Combat, Faction) Source Faction Guide pg. 57 You have trained to fight onrushing enemy hordes. With a carefully timed strike you ensure that their reckless charge will be their undoing. Prerequisites: Dwarf, Ninth Battalion TPA 25. Benefit: You treat all one- or two-handed melee weapons as if they had the brace special feature. Normal: Only weapons with the brace special feature can be readied against charges.
