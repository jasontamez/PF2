Golden Holy Symbol - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Golden Holy Symbol Source Mythic Adventures pg. 152 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 10th Slot neck; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This holy symbol is always warm to the touch, and whenever it strikes metal it rings out with the sound of a choir of angels. When used as a divine focus for a divine spell, the symbol emits a bright light that heals its wearer of a number of points of damage equal to the spell's level. If the wearer expends one use of mythic power when casting a divine spell that uses this holy symbol as its divine focus, the emblem glows for a number of rounds equal to the wearer's mythic tier, shedding light like a torch. Instead of this item's normal effect, at the start of each of the wearer's turns, she and all her allies within 30 feet heal a number of points of damage equal to 1d6 + the wearer's tier. This effect lasts as long as the light persists. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, cure critical wounds; Cost 5,000 gp
