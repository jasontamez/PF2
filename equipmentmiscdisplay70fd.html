Ectoplasmic residue - Miscellaneous - Equipment Ectoplasmic residue Source Occult Adventures pg. 251 Price 20 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Alchemical Weapons Description This flask is filled with the detritus of minor ectoplasmic phantoms and similar creatures. You can throw a flask of ectoplasmic residue as a splash weapon. Treat this attack as a ranged touch attack with a range increment of 10 feet. A direct hit deals no damage, but the target must succeed at a DC 13 Will save or be shaken for 1d4 rounds. Every creature within 5 feet of the point where the flask hits must succeed at a DC 13 Will save or be shaken for 1 round. This is a mind-affecting fear effect. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
