Vest of Surgery Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 223 Aura faint conjuration; CL 5th Slot chest; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This emerald vest has four prominent and bulging pockets along its front. The wearer is always treated as if using a healer's kit when making Heal checks (Core Rulebook 161). Once per day, the wearer can make a DC 20 Heal check as if attempting to treat deadly wounds. If the check is successful, it cures 1d4 points of ability damage instead of hit point damage. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, lesser restoration, creator must have 5 ranks in Heal; Cost 1,500 gp
