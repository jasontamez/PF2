Cane, hollow - Miscellaneous - Equipment Cane, hollow Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 12 Price +1 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description A hollow cane has a false top that conceals a small compartment within the cane. The compartment is large enough to house a potion or vial. Spotting the secret compartment of a hollow cane requires a successful DC 18 Perception check.
