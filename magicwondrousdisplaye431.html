Mantle of Life - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mantle of Life Source Horror Adventures pg. 220 Aura moderate necromancy; CL 10th Slot shoulders; Price 48,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This short cape shimmers faintly under light. The wearer is immune to the energy drain attacks made by undead creatures. She still suffers any other effects of the attack, but ignores the energy drain. If a corpse is wearing the mantle, it can't rise as an undead creature for any reason. This doesn't prevent creatures that were already undead from using abilities like rejuvenation to return to unlife. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, death ward; Cost 24,000 gp
