Emerald-Eyed Blade - Weapons - Magic Equipment Emerald-Eyed Blade Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 17 Aura moderate necromancy CL 9th Slot none; Price 24,315 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This +3 scimitar has an emerald gem set into its hilt. When the wielder confirms a critical hit with the emerald-eyed blade, as a free action the wielder can cause the weapon to inflict a terrible curse upon the foe. The affected creature cannot benefit from spells, potions, or other magic items with the word "cure" in their names until the curse is lifted with a remove curse or break enchantment spell (DC 20) or a limited wish, wish, or miracle. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bestow curse; Price 21,315 gp
