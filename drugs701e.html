Drugs Slaver's Drops Source Rival Guide pg. 53 Type ingested; Addiction minor, Fortitude DC 13 Price 20 gp Effect 3 hours; -3 on Will saves, +3 on Perception checks Damage 1d2 Str damage Dropped into the eyes, this clear liquid awakens the senses but makes the user mentally pliant and open to suggestion. This drug is often used during interrogations or by slavers who seek to force compliance via enchantment effects or similar methods of mind-control.
