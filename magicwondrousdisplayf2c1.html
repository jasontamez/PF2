Horseshoes of Speed - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Horseshoes of Speed Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 232, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 519 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 12 lbs. Description When affixed to an animal's hooves, these iron horseshoes increase the animal's base land speed by 30 feet; this counts as an enhancement bonus. As with other effects that increase speed, jumping distances increase proportionally. All four shoes must be worn by the same animal for the magic to be effective. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, haste; Cost 1,500 gp
