Tri-point double-edged sword - Weapons - Equipment Tri-point double-edged sword Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 12 gp Weight 14 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type P; Special reach Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Monk Description Topping the head of this 5-foot-long shaft are three long, backward-curved blades fashioned in the appearance of a blossoming lotus. The unusually large head is designed to land heavy blows and cause multiple wounds that open away from each other.
