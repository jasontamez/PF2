Slow Time - Feats Slow Time (Combat, Meditation) Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 19 Meditation allows you to perceive time as if it were moving more slowly. Prerequisites: Wis 15, Combat Meditation, Meditation Master, character level 7th. Benefit: Once per day, when you meditate as a full-round action using the Combat Meditation feat, you gain the effects of haste for 1 round per 2 character levels. These effects do not stack with the haste spell, the speed magic weapon special ability, or other effects granting the same result. This is an extraordinary ability.
