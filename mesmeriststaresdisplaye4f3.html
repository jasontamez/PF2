Devilbane Impediment - Stares - Mesmerist Devilbane Impediment(Occult Origins pg. 13): The target of your hypnotic stare must succeed at a concentration check (DC = 15 + the level of the spell) to activate any of its spell-like abilities (but not standard spellcasting). If the target fails the check, the spelllike ability doesn't function but is still expended.
