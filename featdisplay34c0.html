Urdefhan Bladebreaker - Feats Urdefhan Bladebreaker (Combat) Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 59 You can use your rhoka sword to destroy your foes' blades. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, proficiency with the rhoka sword, urdefhan. Benefit: When wielding a rhoka sword, you can attempt to sunder an opponent's sword or other slender-bladed weapon without provoking an attack of opportunity. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus on the combat maneuver check to do so.
