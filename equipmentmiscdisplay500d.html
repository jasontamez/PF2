Sentry mannequin - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sentry mannequin Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 30 gp; Weight 35 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description After 5 minutes of assembly and coverage with the included loose hood and baggy clothing, this wooden frame looks like a vigilant humanoid figure. Creatures more than 30 feet distant from it must succeed at a DC 10 Perception check in order to realize it is not someone standing watch.
