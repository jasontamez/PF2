Improved Day Job - Feats Improved Day Job Source Pathfinder Society Primer pg. 8 You learned the importance of an honest day's work while training as an initiate of the Pathfinder Society. Prerequisites: Ability to attempt Day Job checks (see the Guide to Pathfinder Society Organized Play). Benefit: You gain a +3 bonus on Day Job checks using one skill you choose when you take this feat. This bonus stacks with other feats that grant a bonus to the chosen skill, including Skill Focus.
