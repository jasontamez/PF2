Difficult Swings - Feats Difficult Swings (Weapon Mastery) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 22 You can force foes to move cautiously when near you. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +5, weapon training class feature with a melee weapon. Benefit: When you make a full attack with an appropriate melee weapon, you can force creatures to treat squares adjacent to you as difficult terrain until the beginning of your next turn. You can choose to allow any creature you are aware of to ignore the difficult terrain you effectively create with this feat.
