Headband of the Wolf - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Headband of the Wolf Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 230 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot headband; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This silver band is cast to resemble a pack of hunting wolves. On command, the wearer can gain the scent ability with a range of 10 feet (20 feet upwind, 5 feet downwind) as the wolf aspect of the hunter's animal focus class feature. If the wearer is a hunter with that aspect, this headband instead increases the wearer's effective hunter level for the wolf aspect by 8 levels. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bloodhound, creator must have the animal focus class feature; Cost 2,500 gp
