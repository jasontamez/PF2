Armored Rider - Feats Armored Rider Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 15 You are accustomed to riding while wearing armor, and your armor doesn't hinder your riding skills. Prerequisites: Mounted Combat, Ride 3 ranks. Benefit: You don't take the usual armor check penalty on Ride checks. If you are knocked unconscious while in a saddle, you always remain in the saddle. Normal: If you are knocked unconscious while riding, you have a 50% chance to stay in the saddle (75% if you're in a military saddle).
