Snowshoes - Miscellaneous - Equipment Snowshoes Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89, Pathfinder #51: The Hungry Storm pg. 67, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 185 Price 5 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Clothing Description These high-tension nets of rope or sinew in wooden frames are lashed to the feet to spread your weight across the snow, making you much less likely to break through the crust and rendering walking much easier. Snowshoes reduce the penalty for walking through heavy snow by 50%; for example, if moving through snow normally costs you 2 squares of movement (1 square plus a 1 square penalty) per square traveled, snowshoes reduce this cost to 1.5 squares per square traveled. Snowshoes may supplement another outfit.
