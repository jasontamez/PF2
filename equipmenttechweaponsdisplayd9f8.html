Dart gun - Weapons - Technology Dart gun Source Technology Guide pg. 21 Statistics Cost 3,000 gp Type one-handed ranged; Proficiency exotic (firearms); Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Damage Type P; Critical x2 Range 30 ft.; Capacity 1 nanite canister; Usage 1 charge; Special — Description A dart gun makes use of nanotechnology to craft a small pointed projectile that does minimal damage on its own. Unlike other nanotech weapons, the nanites that make up a dart do not have any additional effect on the target. Instead, a dart gun contains a small reservoir above its handle into which a single dose of a pharmaceutical, drug, poison, or even potion can be poured as a standard action. The next dart fired from the dart gun delivers that liquid into the target immediately upon a hit. Construction Craft DC 24; Cost 1,500 gp Craft Technological Arms and Armor, nanotech lab
