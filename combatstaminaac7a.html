Bonebreaker - Combat Stamina - Feats Bonebreaker Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114 Related Feat Bonebreaker When you make a successful Stunning Fist attempt against a creature that is dazed or staggered, you can spend 5 stamina points to forgo any other Stunning Fist effects and deal 1d6 points of Strength or Dexterity damage to that opponent instead.
