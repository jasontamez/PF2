Falconry gauntlet - Miscellaneous - Equipment Falconry gauntlet Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 10 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Animal Gear Description This gauntlet, made from layers of thick leather, is worn on a falconer's off hand to give a falcon a place to stand before and after the hunt. The gauntlet also has a tassel and a ring to attach to a falcon's tether.
