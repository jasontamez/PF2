Cunning - Feats Cunning Source Villain Codex pg. 128 You are particularly devious, and can master more skills than others in the same amount of time. Benefit: You gain 1 additional skill point per Hit Die. When you take this feat, you gain a number of skill points equal to your Hit Dice right away, and every time your Hit Dice increase in the future, you will gain an additional skill point as well.
