Darkness Arrow - Weapons - Magic Equipment Darkness Arrow Source Villain Codex pg. 21 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot none; Price 80 gp ; Weight — Description This +1 arrow is as black as the darkest night and is fletched with sleek raven feathers. If the arrow strikes a foe, magical darkness infuses the creature's sight. The creature counts the light level as darkness for 1 round, regardless of the actual light level, and if the creature has darkvision, it must succeed at a DC 13 Fortitude saving throw or lose its darkvision for 1 round.
