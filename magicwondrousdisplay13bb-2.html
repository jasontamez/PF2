Blouse of the Boastful Bastard - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Blouse of the Boastful Bastard Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 227 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot chest; Price 8,000 gp; Weight — Description This stylish, purple silk shirt trimmed with designs embroidered in golden thread brings out reckless behavior in even the most conservative swashbucklers. If the wearer has the panache class feature, when she takes damage from a successful critical hit, she regains 1 panache point. This shirt has no effect on someone who is not a panache user.
