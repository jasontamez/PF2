Hunga munga - Weapons - Equipment Hunga munga Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 0, Adventurer's Armory pg. 3, Ultimate Equipment pg. 19 Statistics Cost 4 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range 15 ft.; Type P; Special — Category Ranged; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Light; Thrown Description Also called a mambele, danisco, goleyo, njiga, or any of many lesser-known names, this three-bladed dagger is intended for throwing but can be used as a melee weapon. Though the basic three-blade design is most common, hunga mungas can take a wide variety of forms.
