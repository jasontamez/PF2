True Deception - Feats True Deception Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 95 Time spent learning the mystic arts of disguise has given you insight into the anonymity of the everyday. Prerequisites: Cha 17, Disguise 17 ranks, master disguise master ninja trick or master of disguise advanced rogue talent, ninja level 10th or rogue level 10th. Benefit: When using the master disguise master ninja trick or master of disguise advanced rogue talent to take on the appearance of a specific individual, you can fool divination magic and effects designed to locate the subject of your disguise as per the vigilante's any guise social talent. In addition, you gain a +10 bonus on Bluff checks to act like that individual.
