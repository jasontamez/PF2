Stunstone - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Stunstone Source Pathfinder #39: The City of Seven Spears pg. 57 Aura faint evocation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 350 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This naturally occurring, glowing purple crystal can be thrown as a ranged attack with a range increment of 20 feet. When it strikes a target or hard surface (or is struck hard), the crystal shatters in a burst of sound and light. Every creature in a 10-foot-radius spread is outlined in faerie fire, takes 1d8 points of sonic damage, and must make a DC 13 Fortitude save or be stunned for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Craft (alchemy) 5 ranks, faerie fire, sound burst; Cost 175 gp
