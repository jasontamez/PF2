Whetstone - Miscellaneous - Equipment Whetstone Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 2 cp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description A whetstone allows you to sharpen a blade by sliding it against the stone at a precise angle. Honing a blade with a whetstone requires about 15 minutes of work and grants the weapon a +1 bonus on your damage roll the first time you hit with it. This only works on nonmagical blades.
