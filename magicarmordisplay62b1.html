Cushioned - Armor - Magic Equipment Cushioned Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 24 Aura faint transmutation CL 1st Slot armor quality; Price +1,000 gp; Weight — Description Armor with this special ability reduces the damage its wearer takes from falling as if the distance were 20 feet less. The cushioned special ability can be applied only to armor made primarily from cloth and padding, such as padded, quilted cloth, and silken ceremonialarmor.
