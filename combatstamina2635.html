Surprise Follow-Through - Combat Stamina - Feats Surprise Follow-Through Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133 Related Feat Surprise Follow-Through When you hit a foe that has been denied its Dexterity bonus by this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to deal an extra 1d6 points of damage. This damage is precision damage and is not multiplied on a critical hit.
