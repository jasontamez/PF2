Miser's Mask Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 246 Aura faint divination; CL 1st Slot head; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This wooden mask depicts the sunken face of a miser with an upturned nose. The mask grants the wearer the scent ability (Bestiary 304), but she can use it only to sense coins and gems. Each type and quality of gem has its own unique aura to the wearer, granting her a +5 competence bonus on Appraise checks made to ascertain the value of gems. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bloodhound (Advanced Player's Guide), the creator must have 4 ranks in Appraise; Cost 1,500 gp
