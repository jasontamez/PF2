War razor - Weapons - Equipment War razor Source Book of the Damned pg. 93, Rise of the Runelords Player's Guide pg. 11, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 209, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 290, Adventurer's Armory pg. 0 Statistics Cost 8 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type S; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Blades, Light Description To all appearances, a war razor is an oversized razor or flip knife. Since the razor folds into the handle, no sheath is required, making the weapon easy to hide and thus granting the wielder a +2 bonus on Sleight of Hand checks to conceal the weapon on her body.
