Hairline Fractures - Spells Hairline Fractures Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 235, Faiths of Purity pg. 28 School transmutation [earth]; Level cleric 1, druid 1, hunter 1, oracle 1, shaman 1, warpriest 1, witch 1 (Torag) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a broken twig) Effect Range touch Target 5-foot-square section of stone or a creature with the earth subtype Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Fortitude negates (object); Spell Resistance yes (object) Description With a single touch, you create temporary hairline fractures in a piece of stone or a creature with the earth subtype. If you cast this spell on a section of stone, you reduce its hardness to 5 and its hit points to 10/inch of thickness. If you cast this spell on a creature with the earth subtype, that creature takes a -2 penalty to AC for the spell's duration. Make whole reverses this spell's effect.
