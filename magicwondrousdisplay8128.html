Fungal Slippers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Fungal Slippers Source Villain Codex pg. 141 Aura faint conjuration; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 2,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description As a swift action up to three times per day, the wearer can create fungi in every square through which she walks during her turn. The fungi in these squares count as difficult terrain. After 1 minute, most of the fungi disappear; those that remain provide no impediment. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, entangle; Cost 1,000 gp
