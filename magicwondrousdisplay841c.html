Earth Root Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Earth Root Boots Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 230 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description The longer the wearer of these boots stays in one place-as long as that place is on solid ground-the harder it is to move her from that place. The wearer gains a cumulative +1 circumstance bonus to her CMD (maximum of +10) for each round she does not move from her space. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, elemental body I; Cost 4,000 gp
