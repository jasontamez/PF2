Rewarding Mythic Characters Source Mythic Adventures pg. 129 Mythic characters should be rewarded with experience points and treasure based on the higher overall CR of the encounters they face. In general, this means mythic characters will earn experience points and treasure at a faster rate than their normal counterparts.
