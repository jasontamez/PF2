An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Determine Bonuses Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 15 Each ability, after changes made because of race, has a modifier ranging from -5 to +5. Table 1-3 shows the modifier for each score. The modifier is the number you apply to the die roll when your character tries to do something related to that ability. You also use the modifier with some numbers that aren't die rolls. A positive modifier is called a bonus, and a negative modifier is called a penalty.
