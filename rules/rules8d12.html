An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Force Words Source Ultimate Magic pg. 178 These words use raw magic to create pure force. Force Bolt (Force) School evocation [force]; Level magus 2, sorcerer/wizard 2 Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance Yes Target Restrictions selected This effect word deals 1d4 points of force damage per caster level (maximum 5d4). This effect is treated like a magic missile for the purposes of spells that negate or absorb force effects. Force Blast (Force) School evocation [force]; Level magus 5, sorcerer/wizard 5 Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Reflex half; Spell Resistance Yes This effect word deals 1d6 points of force damage per caster level (maximum 10d6).
