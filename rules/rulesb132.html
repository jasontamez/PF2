Moving Capital Source Ultimate Campaign pg. 80 Some types of capital-in particular Influence-might be specific to a particular settlement or region. Other types may be used at any settlement, though the GM might rule that there is a delay in transporting Goods or Labor to a new location before you can spend it there.
