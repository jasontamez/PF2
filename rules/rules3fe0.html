Witch Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 91 A character who chooses witch as her secondary class gains the following secondary class features. Patron: At 1st level, she chooses a patron. She never qualifies for the Extra Hex feat. Witch's Familiar: At 3rd level, she gains a familiar, treating her character level as her effective witch level. Hex: At 7th level, she gains a hex of her choice as a 1st-level witch. If she chooses the slumber hex, it can affect only creatures with Hit Dice less than or equal to her character level. Cantrip: At 11th level, if she has an Intelligence score of 10 or higher, she chooses a witch cantrip and can cast that cantrip as a spell-like ability at will. She uses her character level as the caster level and Intelligence as the cantrip's key ability score. Improved Hex: At 15th level, she gains a second hex as a 1st-level witch, and her first hex advances as if she were an 8th-level witch. Major Hex: At 19th level, she gains a major hex as a 10th-level witch. She cannot gain the ice tomb hex.
