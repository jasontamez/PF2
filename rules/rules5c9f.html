Squeeze Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 56 A successful DC 30 Acrobatics check allows you to squeeze through a gap through which your head fits but your shoulders don't. You can't fit through spaces smaller than your head. Action: 1 minute or longer, possibly requiring multiple checks. Try Again: Yes. You can even take 20 if you're not being actively opposed.
