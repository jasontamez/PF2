An entry marked with this has additional sections within it. Mount/Dismount a Steed Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 187 Mounting or dismounting a steed requires a move action. Fast Mount or Dismount: You can mount or dismount as a free action with a DC 20 Ride check. If you fail the check, mounting or dismounting is a move action instead. You can't attempt a fast mount or fast dismount unless you can perform the mount or dismount as a move action in the current round.
