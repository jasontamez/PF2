Constitution Poison Track Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 139 Healthy-Weakened-Impaired-Disabled-Unconscious-Dead Weakened: A character whose health is weakened by Constitution poison takes a -2 penalty on all Fortitude saves and Constitution checks. Every time she attempts a Fortitude save against the poison, whether she succeeds or fails, she takes damage as on initial exposure. Impaired: A character impaired by Constitution poison takes an additional -2 penalty on the above checks. Disabled: A character disabled by Constitution poison gains the disabled condition. If she takes a standard action, her hit points drop by 1 or to -1, whichever is worse. Unconscious: A character rendered unconscious by Constitution poison enters a state of shock and can't be woken by any means as long as she remains in this state. Dead: The character's immune system is ravaged by the poison, and she expires.
