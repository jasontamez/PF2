Jitterbugs - Spells Jitterbugs Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 39 School illusion (figment) [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 2, bard 1, mesmerist 1, psychic 2, redmantisassassin 2, skald 1, sorcerer 2, wizard 2 (gnome) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range short (25 ft. +5 ft. 2/levels) Target one creature Duration 1 round/level Description You cause the target to perceive itself as being covered in creeping, crawling, stinging bugs. This causes the target to become jittery and unable to stay still, forcing it to constantly move and twitch. The target takes a -4 penalty on all Dexterity checks and Dexterity-based skill checks, and cannot take the delay, ready, or total defense actions. Mythic Jitterbugs Source Mythic Adventures pg. 99 Starting on the second round of the spell, the affected target must succeed at a Fortitude save on your turn or be nauseated for 1 round, as if distracted by an actual swarm.
