Traveler's Wet Suit - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Traveler's Wet Suit Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 63 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot body; Price 2,750 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This wet suit protects against environmental cold damage and hypothermia. Anything stored in its waterproof pockets is kept magically separate from any surrounding water, even if the wearer opens the pocket underwater. Finally, the wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Swim checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, endure elements; Cost 2,500 gp
