Steadfast Urumi - Weapons - Magic Equipment Steadfast Urumi Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 57 Aura moderate abjuration and evocation CL 8th Slot none; Price 18,830 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description This weapon consists of a flexible blade attached to a handle by a fine coil of steel, and is favored by tieflings and other outcasts in Jalmeray. This +1 mighty cleaving urumi grants its wielder a deflection bonus to AC equal to the number of enemies within her reach with the urumi (maximum +4). The wielder also gains a +5 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks to move through a threatened area. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shield of faith; Price 9,580 gp
