Intended Level Source Ultimate Magic pg. 128 Before you start, you typically need an idea of the general power level of the new spell-probably because you have a specific PC or NPC in mind who-d like to use it, and making the level too low to be significant or too high for that character to cast defeats the purpose of designing the spell. Once you know the general level of the spell-a two-level range is close enough at this point-you can progress to the next issue.
