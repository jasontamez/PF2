Armored kilt - Armor - Equipment Armored kilt Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 18, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 211 Statistics Cost 20 gp Weight 10 lbs. Armor Bonus +1; Max Dex Bonus +6; Armor Check Penalty 0 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 0%; Speed 30 ft./20 ft. Description When you add an armored kilt to a suit of light armor, the set counts as medium armor. Likewise, a kilt and medium armor counts as heavy armor. Adding an armored kilt to heavy armor has no effect.
