Toughness - Feats Toughness Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 135 You have enhanced physical stamina. Benefit: You gain +3 hit points. For every Hit Die you possess beyond 3, you gain an additional +1 hit point. If you have more than 3 Hit Dice, you gain +1 hit points whenever you gain a Hit Die (such as when you gain a level). Mythic Toughness Source Mythic Adventures pg. 75 You have legendary resilience and durability. Prerequisites: Toughness. Benefit: Toughness provides you twice as many hit points. In addition, when you're below 0 hit points, you gain DR 10/epic. This DR stacks with any other DR/epic you might possess.
