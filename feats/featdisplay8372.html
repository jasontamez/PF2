Improved Great Fortitude - Feats Improved Great Fortitude Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 127 You can draw upon an inner reserve to resist diseases, poisons, and other grievous harm. Prerequisites: Great Fortitude. Benefit: Once per day, you may reroll a Fortitude save. You must decide to use this ability before the results are revealed. You must take the second roll, even if it is worse.
