Armor of the Pit - Feats Armor of the Pit Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 173 Your fiendish traits take the form of a protective scaly skin. Prerequisites: Tiefling. Benefit: You gain a +2 natural armor bonus. Special: If you have the scaled skin racial trait, you instead gain resistance 5 to two of the following energy types that you don't have resistance to already: cold, electricity, and fire.
