Improved Grapple - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Grapple Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123 Related Feat Improved Grapple When you are the target of a grapple combat maneuver to initiate or maintain a grapple, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Strength or Dexterity bonus, whichever is greater. Your CMD against that grapple attempt increases by that number.
