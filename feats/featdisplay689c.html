Half-Drow Paragon - Feats Half-Drow Paragon Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 47 Your drow blood is particularly strong. Prerequisites: Drow-blooded and drow magic racial traits, half-elf. Benefit: You count as a drow for any effects that relate to race. Furthermore, the spell-like abilities granted to you by your drow magic racial trait count as drow spell-like abilities for the purposes of any feat prerequisites.
