Goblin Gunslinger - Combat Stamina - Feats Goblin Gunslinger Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 Related Feat Goblin Gunslinger When wielding a firearm that deals fire damage, when you threaten but fail to confirm a critical hit, you can spend 5 stamina points to regain 1 grit point. You still can't gain a grit point if the target is immune to critical hits.
