Reverse-Feint - Feats Reverse-Feint (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 142 You can goad an opponent into attacking you in order to make your counter attack all the more powerful. Prerequisites: Toughness, base attack bonus +1, orc. Benefit: As a move action, you can leave a gap in your defenses for one adjacent opponent to use. If the opponent attacks you on its next turn, it gains a +4 bonus on its attack roll. Whether or not the opponent successfully hits, you may attack it as an immediate action with a single melee attack, gaining a +2 bonus on your attack roll. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130 You can spend 2 stamina points to make the attack granted by this feat as a free action rather than an immediate action. You can still make the attack only once per use of this feat.
