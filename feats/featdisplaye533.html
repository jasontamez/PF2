Mounted Skirmisher - Feats Mounted Skirmisher (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 165 You are adept at attacking from upon a swift moving steed. Prerequisites: Ride rank 14, Mounted Combat, Trick Riding. Benefit: If your mount moves its speed or less, you can still take a full-attack action. Normal: If your mount moves more than 5 feet, you can only take an attack action. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 127 When your mount moves its speed or less, you can spend 5 stamina points to spur it on 10 additional feet. This is bonus movement, and you can still make a full attack when your mount takes this bonus movement.
