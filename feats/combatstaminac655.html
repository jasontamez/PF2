Arcane Armor Mastery - Combat Stamina - Feats Arcane Armor Mastery Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 113 Related Feat Arcane Armor Mastery When casting a spell while wearing armor, you can spend 1 stamina point to activate Arcane Armor Mastery as a free action, or spend a number of stamina points equal to the spell's level (minimum 1) to ignore your armor's arcane spell failure chance entirely for that casting.
