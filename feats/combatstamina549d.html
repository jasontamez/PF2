Imposing Bearing - Combat Stamina - Feats Imposing Bearing Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 20 Related Feat Imposing Bearing You can spend 5 stamina points as a swift action to add half your armor bonus to your next combat maneuver check this turn. Alternatively, you can spend 5 stamina points as an immediate action to add half your armor bonus to your CMD against a single combat maneuver this turn. (See page 17.)
