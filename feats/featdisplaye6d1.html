Raging Hurler - Feats Raging Hurler Source Ultimate Combat pg. 114 An opponent can do little to evade your wrathful pitching of weapons and objects. Prerequisites: Rage class feature, Throw Anything. Benefit: While raging, you can throw a two-handed weapon as a standard action, and you double the range increment for weapons you throw. If you also have the Quick Draw feat, you can throw two-handed weapons at your full normal rate of attacks. Further, you can pick up an unattended object that you can use as a improvised weapon within your reach as part of the attack action to throw that item.
