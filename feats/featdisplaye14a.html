Kudzu Grappler - Feats Kudzu Grappler Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 24 You climb all over creatures you're grappling like kudzu, blocking their vision. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike, Greater Grapple, base attack bonus +6, vine leshy. Benefit: You add an option to blind the grappled creature for 1 round to the list of actions you can take when you succeed at a check to maintain a grapple.
