Mounted Blade - Feats Mounted Blade Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 15, Qadira, Gateway to the East pg. 9, Qadira, Jewel of the East pg. 15 You can use the momentum of your mount to carry your weapon through one foe and into another. Prerequisites: Mounted Combat, Ride-By Attack, Ride 3 ranks. Benefit: When you use the Ride-By Attack feat, if your attack hits, you can also make an attack against a target adjacent to your original target. You take a -5 penalty on this additional attack, which does not benefit from any effects of a charge.
