Forceful Charge - Feats Forceful Charge Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 16 This animal's charges move its enemies. Prerequisites: Improved Bull Rush, Power Attack, animal companion. Benefit: Once per round when this animal companion hits an opponent with a charge attack, it can initiate a bull rush against that target as a free action. If it moves its full speed as part of the charge, it gains a +4 bonus on its combat maneuver check to resolve this bull rush.
