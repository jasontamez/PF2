Cosmopolitan - Feats Cosmopolitan Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 156, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 86 Living in large, exotic cities has put you in touch with many diverse civilizations, cultures, and races. Benefit: You can speak and read two additional languages of your choice. In addition, choose two Intelligence-, Wisdom-, or Charisma-based skills. Those skills always count as class skills for you.
