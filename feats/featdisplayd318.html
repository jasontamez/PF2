Street Style - Feats Street Style (Combat, Style) Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 92 You use the urban environment to your advantage, thrashing and bashing your foe and driving it back. Prerequisites: Str 15, Improved Bull Rush, Improved Unarmed Strike, Power Attack, base attack bonus +4 or monk level 3rd. Benefit: While using this style, once per round as a swift action when you hit a target with an unarmed strike, you can deal an extra 1d6 points of damage and attempt a bull rush combat maneuver against that target. Special: You can enter the style stance for street style only while in urban terrain, and the stance ends immediately if you cease to be in urban terrain.
