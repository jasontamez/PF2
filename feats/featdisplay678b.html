Improved Horse Whisperer - Feats Improved Horse Whisperer Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 15 Your understanding of horses goes so deep that you can communicate with them as if you shared a language. Prerequisites: Horse Whisperer, Handle Animal 5 ranks, Ride 5 ranks. Benefit: With a successful DC 20 Handle Animal check, you can communicate with a horse as if you were using speak with animals for 1 round. The horse's ability to communicate and take actions is still limited by its intelligence.
