Pursuit of Glory - Feats Pursuit of Glory (Combat, Teamwork) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 208 You know that daring deeds and heroics live forever in song, and sagas will carry your deeds into immortality. Prerequisites: Human (Ulfen). Benefit: Whenever you start or end a charge attack in a space adjacent to an ally who also has this feat, you gain a +2 morale bonus on one attack roll you make as part of the charge. Furthermore, you can charge through a space containing an ally who also has this feat.
