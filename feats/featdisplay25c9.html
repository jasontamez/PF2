Fortified Armor Training - Feats Fortified Armor Training (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 102 You have learned to let your armor bear the brunt of the worst attacks. Prerequisites: Proficient with armor or shield. Benefit: If an opponent scores a critical hit against you, you can turn the critical hit into a normal hit. If you do, either your armor or your shield gains the broken condition (your choice). Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 When you use this feat to negate a critical hit, you can spend 5 stamina points to give your armor or shield a 50% chance to avoid gaining the broken condition.
