Revered Guidance - Feats Revered Guidance Source Blood of Angels pg. 25 You were selected by your celestial ancestor to be a guide for a chosen people, and serve as a beacon of hope in times of despair. Prerequisites: Aasimar, must be taken at 1st level. Benefit: Select one of the following creature subtypes: dwarf, elf, gnome, halfling, human, or orc. You gain a +4 racial bonus on Diplomacy checks made to influence creatures of the selected subtype.
