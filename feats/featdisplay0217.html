Oread Burrower - Feats Oread Burrower Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 147 The ground parts for you at the slightest touch, allowing you to dig with great speed. Prerequisites: Stony Step, character level 9th, oread. Benefit: You gain a burrow speed equal to 1/2 your base speed. You can burrow through sand, dirt, clay, gravel, or similar materials, but not solid stone. You do not leave a hole behind, nor is your passage marked on the surface.
