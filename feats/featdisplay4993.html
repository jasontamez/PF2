Taldan Squire - Feats Taldan Squire Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 29 You spent time as a squire to a Taldan knight and understand how to fight alongside him. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, proficient in a martial weapon. Benefit: When within 50 feet of a mounted ally with the Mounted Combat feat, you get a +2 bonus on initiative. If this ally is injured, you gain a +1 dodge bonus on AC as long as you are within 10 feet of him.
