Spellbreaker - Combat Stamina - Feats Spellbreaker Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 Related Feat Spellbreaker When you hit a caster with an attack of opportunity it provoked by failing to cast defensively, you can spend 5 stamina points to daze the caster until the end of its next turn. If the caster succeeds at a Fortitude save with a DC equal to 10 + your base attack bonus, it is staggered instead.
