Giant's Crush - Feats Giant's Crush (Combat) Source Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 69 When you strike hard, you knock smaller foes off their feet. Prerequisites: Str 13, Power Attack, Vital Strike, base attack bonus +6, giant subtype. Benefit: If you hit a creature at least one size category smaller than you with a melee attack while using both Power Attack and Vital Strike, the creature that you attacked is also knocked prone. A successful Reflex save negates this effect (DC = 10 + your base attack bonus). You must choose to use this feat before making the attack roll. Special: This effect stacks with the effect of Giant's Wallop.
