Improved Position of Strength - Feats Improved Position of Strength (Combat) Source Villain Codex pg. 188 You are particularly skilled in pressing your advantage when negotiating with noncombatants. Prerequisites: Position of Strength; Two-Weapon Fighting or Multiattack; Intimidate 5 ranks. Benefit: As long as you are wielding one or more weapons with which you are proficient, you gain the bonus on Intimidate checks from Position of Strength against creatures that are armed with fewer weapons than you are wielding.
