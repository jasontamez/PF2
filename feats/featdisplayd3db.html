Fleshwarper - Feats Fleshwarper (Item Creation) Source Horror Adventures pg. 87 You can create creatures through an abominable alchemical process. Prerequisites: Craft (alchemy) 5 ranks, Heal 5 ranks, evil alignment. Benefit: You can create fleshwarped creatures and fleshcraft grafts (see Fleshwarping for full details). A newly created fleshwarped creature has average hit points for its Hit Dice.
