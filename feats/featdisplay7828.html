Acrobatic - Feats Acrobatic Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 113 You are skilled at leaping, jumping, and flying. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Acrobatics and Fly skill checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in one of these skills, the bonus increases to +4 for that skill. Mythic Acrobatic Source Mythic Adventures pg. 54 Your grace and fluidity are beyond compare. Prerequisites: Acrobatic. Benefit: The bonus on Acrobatics and Fly skill checks from Acrobatic increases by 2. In addition, you can expend one use of mythic power to treat an Acrobatics or Fly check as if you had rolled a natural 20. You must decide to use this ability before making the roll.
