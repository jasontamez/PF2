Giant-Killer Stance - Feats Giant-Killer Stance (Combat) Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 23 You can prepare a crippling strike against larger foes that rush into your weapon's reach. Prerequisites: Power Attack. Benefit: You can treat any reach weapon you wield as having the brace property against creatures that are Large or larger. If your readied attack hits, the creature takes a -2 penalty on melee attack rolls for 1 round.
