Spider Step - Feats Spider Step Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 170 Your physical mastery grants you an impossible stride. Prerequisites: Acrobatics 6 ranks, Climb 6 ranks, monk level 6th. Benefit: As a move action, you can move up to half your slow fall distance across a wall or ceiling or across ropes, branches, or even water or other surfaces that cannot support your weight. You must reach a solid, level surface by the end of your turn or you will fall.
