Aquatic Squires - Feats Aquatic Squires Source Blood of the Sea pg. 15 When you call for aid from the sea, your summoned creatures remain to serve you longer. Prerequisites: Triton, summon nature's ally II spell-like ability, character level 5th. Benefit: The duration of your summon nature's ally II spell-like ability is 1 minute per level. Normal: Summon nature's ally II has a duration of 1 round per level.
