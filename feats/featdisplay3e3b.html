Incredible Healer - Feats Incredible Healer Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 20 You are able to treat deadly wounds with an almost supernatural skill. Prerequisites: Heal 5 ranks. Benefit: When you use the Heal skill to treat deadly wounds, the target heals a number of hit points equal to either the result of your Heal check or the normal amount, whichever is higher. A creature can still benefit from having deadly wounds treated no more than once per day.
