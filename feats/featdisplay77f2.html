Improved Swarm Form - Feats Improved Swarm Form Source Blood of the Night pg. 17 You can assume the form of various varmints. Prerequisites: Vampire. Benefit: Choose one of the following forms: bat swarm, centipede swarm, cockroach swarm, leech swarm, rat swarm, or spider swarm. As a standard action, you can change into this form. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time you take this feat, choose another swarm type.
