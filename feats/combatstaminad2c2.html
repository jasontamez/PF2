Blazing Aura (ARG) - Combat Stamina - Feats Blazing Aura (ARG) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114 Related Feat Blazing Aura (ARG) When you create an aura of heat with this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to increase the fire damage dealt to any creature that begins its turn in the aura to 2d6 points of fire damage.
