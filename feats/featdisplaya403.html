Maximize Spell - Feats Maximize Spell (Metamagic) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 130 Your spells have the maximum possible effect. Benefit: All variable, numeric effects of a spell modified by this feat are maximized. Saving throws and opposed rolls are not affected, nor are spells without random variables. A maximized spell uses up a spell slot three levels higher than the spell's actual level. An empowered, maximized spell gains the separate benefits of each feat: the maximum result plus half the normally rolled result.
