Net and Trident - Feats Net and Trident (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 111 Your skill with lighter weapons allows you to wield one alongside your net. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Exotic Weapon Proficiency (net), Net Adept, Two-Weapon Fighting. Benefit: You can treat a net as a one-handed ranged weapon, allowing you to wield a light or one-handed melee weapon and still make ranged attacks with your net. When you use your light or one-handed melee weapon to attack an entangled opponent, you gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls and on attack rolls to confirm a critical hit. Normal: A net is a two-handed ranged weapon. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 127 When you use a light or one-handed melee weapon to attack an opponent entangled by your net, you can spend up to 5 stamina points when you hit the opponent to gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
