Osirionology - Feats Osirionology Source People of the Sands pg. 7 You have a broad interest in Osirion and are something of an authority in one specialized field. Prerequisites: Knowledge (history) 1 rank, Knowledge (local) 1 rank, must be able to speak Osiriani and Ancient Osiriani. Benefit: Pick one Intelligence-based skill. You gain a +3 bonus on all checks made using that skill in relation to Osirion or its people. In addition, you gain a +1 bonus on all other Intelligence-based skill checks made in relation to Osirion or its people.
