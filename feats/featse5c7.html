Feats Coven Feats Description Source Blood of the Coven pg. 13 Two of the feats below are coven feats, special teamwork feats whose effects require three participants. While such feats often represent the connection between coven members and refer to the group as a coven, any group of three individuals who meet the prerequisites can select these feats.
