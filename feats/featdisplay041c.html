Armor Proficiency, Medium - Feats Armor Proficiency, Medium (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 118 You are skilled at wearing medium armor. Prerequisites: Light Armor Proficiency. Benefit: See Armor Proficiency, Light. Normal: See Armor Proficiency, Light. Special: Barbarians, clerics, druids, fighters, paladins, and rangers automatically have Medium Armor Proficiency as a bonus feat. They need not select it.
