Channeled Revival - Feats Channeled Revival Source Ultimate Combat pg. 92 You can expend a large portion of your channeling power to reverse death itself. Prerequisites: Channel energy 6d6 (positive energy). Benefit: As a full-round action that provokes attacks of opportunity, you can expend three uses of your channel energy class feature to restore a dead creature to life as if you had cast the breath of life spell (Core Rulebook 251).
