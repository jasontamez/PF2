Firebrand - Feats Firebrand (Combat) Source Faiths of Corruption pg. 24 You can make use of a torch as a deadly, macelike weapon. Prerequisites: Worshiper of Asmodeus Benefit: You treat a torch as a light weapon that deals bludgeoning damage equal to that of a light mace of its size, plus 1 point of fire damage, and you do not incur penalties as you would for using it as an improvised weapon. Normal: A torch used in combat is treated as a one-handed improvised weapon that deals bludgeoning damage equal to that of a gauntlet of its size, plus 1 point of fire damage.
