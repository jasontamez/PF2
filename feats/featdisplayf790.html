Shield Material Mastery - Feats Shield Material Mastery (Combat, Shield Mastery) Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 19 Your extraordinary understanding of the special materials from which your shield is made lets you take advantage of it more often. Prerequisites: Shield Focus, Shield Material Expertise, base attack bonus +11 or fighter level 8th. Benefit: You can use the ability granted by Shield Material Expertise two additional times per day.
