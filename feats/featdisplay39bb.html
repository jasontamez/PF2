Cerberus Crush - Feats Cerberus Crush (Combat) Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 9 When you strike from three directions, you channel deadly force into each blow. Prerequisites: Cerberus Snare, Cerberus Style, Vital Strike, base attack bonus +13 or monk level 13th. Benefit: You can apply the benefits of Vital Strike, Improved Vital Strike, and Greater Vital Strike to all attacks made using Cerberus Style.
