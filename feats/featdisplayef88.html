Fiend Sight - Feats Fiend Sight Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 174 Your eyes develop keener sight in dim light and darkness. Prerequisites: Darkvision 60 ft., tiefling. Benefit: You gain low-light vision and your darkvision improves to 120 ft. Special: You can take this feat twice. When you take it a second time, you gain the see in darkness universal monster ability.
