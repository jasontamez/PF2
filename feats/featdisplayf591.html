Snapping Turtle Style - Feats Snapping Turtle Style (Combat, Style) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 120 Your deft unarmed style allows you to shield your body from harm. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +1 or monk level 1st. Benefit: While using the Snapping Turtle Style feat with at least one hand free, you gain a +1 shield bonus to AC. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 When using this style, as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool, your shield bonus to AC increases by 1.
