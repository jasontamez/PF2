Sharpclaw - Feats Sharpclaw (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 154 Your nails are large and strong. Prerequisites: Ratfolk. Benefit: You gain two claw attacks. These are primary natural attacks that deal 1d4 points of damage. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 131 You can spend 2 stamina points to increase the damage die of your claw attacks to 1d6 until the start of your next turn.
