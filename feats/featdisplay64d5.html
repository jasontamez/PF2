Wave Master - Feats Wave Master Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 217 Gozreh has seen fit to bless you as an uncommonly skilled swimmer. Prerequisites: Worshiper of Gozreh. Benefit: When you succeed at a Swim check, you can move your full speed as a full-round action or half your speed as a move action. This does not grant you the benefit of having a swim speed. Normal: A successful Swim check allows you to move up to half your speed as a full-round action or up to a quarter your speed as a move action.
