Kudzu Invasion - Feats Kudzu Invasion (Combat) Source Wilderness Origins pg. 14 In addition to obscuring creatures' vision, your vines crush their bodies. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Greater Grapple, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike, Kudzu Grappler, base attack bonus +6 or monk level 6th, vine leshy. Benefit: When you blind a creature using your Kudzu Grappler feat, you can also deal your unarmed strike damage to it.
