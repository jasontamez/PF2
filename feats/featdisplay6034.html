Step of the Flighty Fey - Feats Step of the Flighty Fey Source Legacy of the First World pg. 8 Your spell-like ability taps the magic and resilience of the First World. Prerequisites: Gathlain, feather step spell-like ability. Benefit: A creature under the effect of your feather step spell-like ability gains DR 1/cold iron for the duration of the effect. If you have at least 8 Hit Dice, the creature instead gains DR 2/cold iron, and if you have at least 16 Hit Dice, the creature instead gains DR 3/cold iron.
