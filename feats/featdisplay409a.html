Hammer the Gap - Feats Hammer the Gap (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 103 You repeatedly strike the same location, causing increasing amounts of damage. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +6. Benefit: When you take a full-attack action, each consecutive hit against the same opponent deals extra damage equal to the number of previous consecutive hits you have made against that opponent this turn. This damage is multiplied on a critical hit. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 When you use this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points to increase the amount of extra damage dealt by 1 point per consecutive hit you make.
