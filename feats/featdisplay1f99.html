Masterful Display - Feats Masterful Display (Combat, Performance) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 109 You craft a special victory performance that causes the crowd to go wild. Prerequisites: Dazzling Display, any two performance feats. Benefit: Choose the effects of any two performance feats you have. When you make a performance combat check, you gain the benefits of those two feats, but you only gain a +2 bonus on the performance combat check. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 126 When choosing the effects of any two performance feats you possess, you can spend 2 stamina points to gain the bonus from both feats on your performance combat check.
