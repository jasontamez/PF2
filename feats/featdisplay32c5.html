Channeling Scourge - Feats Channeling Scourge Source Ultimate Combat pg. 92 Your zeal for hunting your faith's enemies empowers your ability to channel divine energy, as long as you channel that energy for harm. Prerequisites: Channel energy class feature, inquisitor level 1st. Benefit: When you use channel energy to deal damage, your inquisitor levels count as cleric levels for determining the number of damage dice and the saving throw DC.
