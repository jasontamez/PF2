Slurk Rider - Feats Slurk Rider (Combat) Source Monster Codex pg. 129 You are skilled at riding slurks into battle. Prerequisites: Kobold. Benefit: You are able to control, guide, and communicate with slurks (Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 2 251), as if they understood Draconic. You can also spend a standard action to manipulate the nodules on the slurk's back to activate its slime ability. Both you and the slurk can activate this ability during the same round.
