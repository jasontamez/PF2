Sense Metals and Minerals - Feats Sense Metals and Minerals Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 5 You have a sixth sense for the valuable treasures of the earth Benefit: You gain a +4 bonus on Perception checks to notice natural deposits of precious metals or minerals, and a +4 bonus on Appraise checks to determine their worth. By spending 5 minutes scrutinizing a discovered vein of metals or minerals, you can determine the worth of the full vein to within 1,000 gp, to a maximum of 50,000 gp.
