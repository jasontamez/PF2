Vigilant Phantom - Feats Vigilant Phantom Source Occult Adventures pg. 141 Your phantom is highly observant, and its link with you increases your own watchfulness. Prerequisites: Phantom class feature. Benefit: While your phantom is within your reach, you gain a +4 bonus on Perception checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in Perception, this bonus increases to +8. This doesn't apply if your phantom is helpless or unconscious.
