Felling Escape - Combat Stamina - Feats Felling Escape Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119 Related Feat Felling Escape You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefit of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to make the trip attempt from this feat as a free action instead of a swift action.
