Cornugon Shield - Feats Cornugon Shield (Combat) Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 26 You can use your spiked chain as both armor and weapon. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Weapon Focus (spiked chain). Benefit: When wielding a spiked chain, you gain a +1 shield bonus to your AC. When you are fighting defensively or using the total defense action, this shield bonus increases to +2.
