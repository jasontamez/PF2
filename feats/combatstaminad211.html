Haunted Gnome Shroud - Combat Stamina - Feats Haunted Gnome Shroud Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 Related Feat Haunted Gnome Shroud When an opponent misses you due to the miss chance from Haunted Gnome Shroud, you can spend 5 stamina points to impose a -2 penalty on all that opponent's attack rolls against you until that opponent deals damage to you. This ability doesn't stack with itself.
