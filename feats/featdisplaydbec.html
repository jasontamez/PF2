Focused Plant Expertise - Feats Focused Plant Expertise (Combat) Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 23 You are quick to dodge and slash anything that would ensnare you. Prerequisites: Favored enemy (plant) class feature. Benefit: You gain a bonus equal to half your favored enemy (plant) bonus on Reflex saves to avoid being entangled and to your CMD whenever an opponent attempts to grapple you. Your weapons' critical hit multipliers increase by one against creatures with the plant type.
