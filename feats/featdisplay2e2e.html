Orc Snarl - Feats Orc Snarl (Combat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 13 Your attacks leave your opponent quivering in fear. Prerequisites: Bullying Blow, Intimidating Prowess, Orc Fury Style, Orc Rampage, Intimidate 7 ranks, weapon familiarity racial trait. Benefit: While using Orc Fury Style, you gain a +4 bonus on saving throws against effects created by shaken foes.
