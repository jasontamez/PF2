Iron Will - Feats Iron Will Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 129 You are more resistant to mental effects. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Will saving throws. Mythic Iron Will Source Mythic Adventures pg. 68 Your will is incorruptible. Prerequisites: Iron Will. Benefit: Whenever you roll a Will saving throw against a spell, spell-like ability, or supernatural ability from a non-mythic source, roll twice and take the higher result.
