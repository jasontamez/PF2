Staggering Critical - Feats Staggering Critical (Combat, Critical) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 134 Your critical hits cause opponents to slow down. Prerequisites: Critical Focus, base attack bonus +13. Benefit: Whenever you score a critical hit, your opponent becomes staggered for 1d4+1 rounds. A successful Fortitude save reduces the duration to 1 round. The DC of this Fortitude save is equal to 10 + your base attack bonus. The effects of this feat do not stack. Additional hits instead add to the duration. Special: You can only apply the effects of one critical feat to a given critical hit unless you possess Critical Mastery. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 When you score a critical hit and attempt to stagger an opponent with this feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the DC of this feat's saving throw by an amount equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
