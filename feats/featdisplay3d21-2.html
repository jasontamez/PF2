Fast Crawl - Feats Fast Crawl Source Faction Guide pg. 56 You are skilled at moving while prone. Benefit: While prone, you can move at half speed. This movement provokes attacks of opportunity as normal. You can take a 5-foot step while crawling. This benefit does not stack with the rogue crawl talent. Normal: You can crawl 5 feet as a move action. You cannot take a 5-foot step while crawling.
