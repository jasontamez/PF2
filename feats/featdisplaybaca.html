Stone Cover - Feats Stone Cover Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 9 You can use stone around you to help you avoid attacks. Prerequisites: Wis 15, Stone Shroud, Stealth 6 ranks, dwarf, stonecunning racial trait. Benefit: While in areas of rocky terrain or while underground, you can gain 10% miss chance as a swift action. Alternatively, you can take a moment to better position yourself and gain concealment as a move action. The miss chance or concealment you gain from this feat does not stack with similar effects and remains until the start of your next turn.
