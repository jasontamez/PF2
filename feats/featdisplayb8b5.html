Hurtful - Feats Hurtful (Combat) Source Monster Codex pg. 20 You know how to add injury to insult. Prerequisites: Str 13, Power Attack. Benefit: When you successfully demoralize an opponent within your melee reach with an Intimidate check, you can make a single melee attack against that creature as a swift action. If your attack fails to damage the target, its shaken condition from being demoralized immediately ends.
