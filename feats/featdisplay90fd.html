Janni Rush - Feats Janni Rush (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 106 When you leap to the attack, your blows are like bolts from on high. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Janni Style, Janni Tempest, Acrobatics 8 ranks, Perform (dance) 8 ranks. Benefit: While using Janni Style, you are always considered to have a running start when jumping. Further, if you jump as part of a charge and make an unarmed strike against the designated opponent, a hit allows you to roll the unarmed strike's damage dice twice and add the results together before adding modifiers (such as from Strength) or extra dice (such as precision-based damage or dice from weapon abilities). The extra damage dice are not multiplied on a successful critical hit. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 124 You can spend 5 stamina points to multiply the extra damage dice from this feat on a critical hit.
