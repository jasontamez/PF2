Electric Eel Style - Feats Electric Eel Style (Combat, Style) Source Blood of the Sea pg. 11 You have learned how to harness electrical energy to momentarily disorient your foes. Prerequisites: Wis 13, Elemental Fist, Improved Unarmed Strike, base attack bonus +9 or monk level 5th. Benefit: You gain one additional Elemental Fist (Advanced Player's Guide 158) attempt per day. While you are in this style, your Elemental Fist attack deals electricity damage. When a creature takes electricity damage from your Elemental Fist attack, you gain a +4 bonus for 1 round on combat maneuver checks to grapple the creature.
