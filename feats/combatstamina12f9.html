Whirlwind Attack - Combat Stamina - Feats Whirlwind Attack Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 135 Related Feat Whirlwind Attack When using this feat, you can spend stamina points to still take bonus or extra attacks granted by other feats, spells, or abilities. You must spend 5 stamina points per extra attack you take in this way. This combat trick allows you to make extra attacks to which you have access-it doesn't by itself grant extra attacks.
