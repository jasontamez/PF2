Imperial Prankster - Feats Imperial Prankster Source Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 73 You model your behavior on that of the legendary trickster, Grand Prince Beldam I, to confound proponents of law and order. Prerequisites: Bluff 5 ranks, any chaotic alignment. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on Bluff checks and combat maneuver checks against lawful creatures. Against creatures with the lawful subtype, this bonus increases to +4.
