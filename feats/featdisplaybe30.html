Wild Vigor - Feats Wild Vigor Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 120 The forces of nature bolster your vitality when you use wild shape. Prerequisites: Wild shape class feature. Benefit: A number of times per day equal to your Wisdom bonus (minimum 1), when you use wild shape, you can gain a number of temporary hit points equal to your base attack bonus. These temporary hit points last for the duration of the wild shape or until they are lost.
