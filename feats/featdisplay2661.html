Greater Subjective Slam - Feats Greater Subjective Slam (Combat) Source Planar Adventures pg. 28 Your gravitational impact is meteoric, and your ability to use the act of falling devastating. Prerequisites: Con 15; Wis 15; Subjective Mobility; Subjective Slam; base attack bonus +8 or monk level 7th. Benefit: When using Subjective Slam, you take only a -1 penalty on the combat maneuver check for every 100 feet of falling speed, and the maximum damage for such a slam increases to 12d6.
