Great Expectations - Feats Great Expectations Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 25 Burdened with heavy responsibility at a young age, you must mature faster than most. Prerequisites: Rainkin half-orc, skilled alternate racial trait. Benefit: Choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma when you take this feat. This choice cannot be changed. As an immediate action a number of times per day equal to your modifier in the chosen attribute, you can gain a +2 circumstance bonus on any skill check that uses the chosen attribute. You can use this ability after rolling, but must use it before the results of the rolls are declared.
