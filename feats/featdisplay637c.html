Fearless Curiosity - Feats Fearless Curiosity Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 77 Your desire to see and experience the world overrides healthy caution. Prerequisites: Cha 13, human. Benefit: You gain a +1 bonus on saving throws against effects with the emotion descriptor. In addition, for any round in which you begin your turn affected by a fear effect, you gain a new save at the beginning of your turn to reduce the severity of the fear effect, from panicked to frightened, frightened to shaken, and shaken to unaffected.
