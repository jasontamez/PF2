Mutated Shape - Feats Mutated Shape Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 115 Your wild shape form gains an additional appendage you can use to attack your foes. Prerequisites: Wis 19, base attack bonus +6, wild shape class feature. Benefit: When you use wild shape, you grow an additional appendage of your choice that can be used to make one of the following attacks: bite, claw, gore, slam, sting, or talons. The appendage can be used as part of a full attack using your highest base attack bonus, and it deals damage as described in Table 3-2: Mutated Shape Appendage Attacks. This appendage lasts for as long as you stay in the same form with this use of wild shape.
