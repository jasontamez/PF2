Rapid Shot - Feats Rapid Shot (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 132 You can make an additional ranged attack. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Point-Blank Shot. Benefit: When making a full-attack action with a ranged weapon, you can fire one additional time this round at your highest bonus. All of your attack rolls take a -2 penalty when using Rapid Shot. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130 When using this feat, you can spend a number of stamina points up to the number of shots taken using this feat. If you do, you decrease the penalty of a single Rapid Shot ranged attack by 2 for each stamina point you spent. Mythic Rapid Shot Source Mythic Adventures pg. 72 You can fire many times each round with amazing precision. Prerequisites: Rapid Shot. Benefit: When using Rapid Shot, you can either ignore the feat's -2 penalty on attack rolls or make two additional attacks instead of one.
