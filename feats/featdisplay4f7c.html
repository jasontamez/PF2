Disruptive Spell - Feats Disruptive Spell (Metamagic) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 157 Your magical energies cling to enemies, interfering with their spellcasting. Benefit: Targets affected by a disruptive spell must make concentration checks when using spells or spell-like abilities (DC equals the save DC of the disruptive spell plus the level of the spell being cast) for 1 round. Targets that avoid the spell's effects avoid this feat's effect as well. A disruptive spell uses up a spell slot one level higher than the spell's actual level.
