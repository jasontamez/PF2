Read the Room - Feats Read the Room Source Inner Sea Taverns pg. 59 You can gain some insight into the mood of a crowd with just a look. Prerequisites: Drunken Sing-Along, Diplomacy 1 rank, Performance (sing) 1 rank, Sense Motive 1 rank. Benefit: Before you use the Drunken Sing-Along feat, you can attempt a DC 20 Sense Motive check as a free action to pick the exact right song for that crowd. If you are successful, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus on your next Diplomacy check to gather information in that tavern.
