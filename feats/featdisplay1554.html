Signature Deed - Feats Signature Deed (Grit) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 119 You are known for performing a particular deed, and can perform it with greater ease. Prerequisites: Grit class feature, gunslinger level 11th. Benefit: Pick a deed that you have access to and that you must spend grit to perform. Once per round, you can perform this deed for 1 fewer grit point (minimum 0). You can reduce the cost of a deed in this way only if you have at least 1 grit point.
