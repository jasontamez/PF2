Merciless Butchery - Feats Merciless Butchery (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 152 Your talent for slaughter lets you dispatch helpless opponents before they can recover. Prerequisites: Dastardly Finish, sneak attack +5d6, studied target class feature. Benefit: As a standard action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity, you can attempt a coup de grace against a cowering, helpless, or stunned opponent that you have designated as your studied target.
