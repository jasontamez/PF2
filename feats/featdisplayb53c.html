Rebounding Leap - Feats Rebounding Leap (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 115 Your riding and lancing expertise allows you to enter and leave the saddle with great speed. Prerequisites: Leaping lance class feature, Acrobatics 5 ranks, Ride 11 ranks. Benefit: When you succeed at the Acrobatics check to jump as part of your leaping lance class feature, you can remount your steed as a swift action. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 130 When attempting an Acrobatics check to jump as part of your leaping lance class feature, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on that check equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
