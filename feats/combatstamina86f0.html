Improvised Weapon Mastery - Combat Stamina - Feats Improvised Weapon Mastery Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 124 Related Feat Improvised Weapon Mastery When making an improvised weapon attack, you can spend 2 stamina points to increase the damage of the attack by an additional step (to a maximum of 1d10, or 2d8 if the improvised weapon is two-handed).
