Shocking Bellow - Feats Shocking Bellow (Combat) Source Monster Codex pg. 20 You can demoralize a foe with a sudden ferocious scream. Prerequisites: Intimidating Prowess. Benefit: If you act in the surprise round of a combat, as a free action you can attempt a single Intimidate check to demoralize a creature within 30 feet that can clearly see or hear you.
