Demonic Possession - Feats Demonic Possession Source Demons Revisited pg. 32 You can possess creatures. Prerequisites: Demon or half-demon, Cha 21, Wis 17 Benefit: You gain the ability to use magic jar as a spell-like ability once per day. Special: You may take this feat more than once. Each time you select this feat, you gain the ability to use your magic jar spell-like ability one additional time per day.
