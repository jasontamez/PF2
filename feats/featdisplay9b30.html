Bloody Vengeance - Feats Bloody Vengeance (Combat) Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 208, Faiths of Balance pg. 24 Your retaliatory strikes have long-lasting effects. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, worshiper of Calistria. Benefit: If an opponent within line of sight has damaged you within the last minute, you may study that opponent as a standard action. Thereafter, if you hit that opponent with a melee attack, you deal 1 point of bleed damage to that creature in addition to the normal damage dealt by your attack.
