Druidic Decoder - Feats Druidic Decoder Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 11 You have a natural understanding of the Druidic language. Prerequisites: Linguistics 1 rank, cannot be a druid. Benefit: You gain a +5 bonus on Linguistics checks made to decipher writings in the Druidic language. If you have 10 or more ranks in Linguistics, add Druidic to the list of bonus languages available to you.
