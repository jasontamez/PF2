Slaying Sprint - Feats Slaying Sprint Source Rival Guide pg. 11 With swift feet and deadly intent, you finish off your target before anyone sees your blade. Prerequisites: Dodge, Mobility, Spring Attack, base attack bonus +13. Benefit: You can move up to half your speed instead of taking a 5-foot step when taking a full-round action to perform a coup de grace. Performing a coup de grace does not provoke attacks of opportunity. You can use this feat only when wielding a light or onehanded weapon.
