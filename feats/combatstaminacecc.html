Improved Feint - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Feint Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123 Related Feat Improved Feint You can select this feat even if you don't meet the Ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefit of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to feint as a move action.
