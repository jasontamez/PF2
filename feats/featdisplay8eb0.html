Juju Way - Feats Juju Way (Teamwork) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 207 You can use spirits of the unseen world to carry your spells for you. Prerequisites: Knowledge (religion) 3 ranks, human (Mwangi). Benefit: You can cast spells with the range of "touch" at a range of 20 feet if the recipient is a willing Mwangi ally who also has this feat.
