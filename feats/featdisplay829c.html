Dwarven Fury - Feats Dwarven Fury (Combat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 12 You can channel your hatred against many opponents. Prerequisites: Dwarven Hatred Style, Dwarven Seething, base attack bonus +7, defensive training and hatred racial traits, Medium size. Benefit: When using Dwarven Seething, you can maintain bonuses from both your defensive training racial trait and hatred racial trait's against any number of opponents.
