Team Up - Feats Team Up (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 172 When you are ganging up against an enemy, you can aid an ally with a quick feint. Prerequisites: Int 13, Combat Expertise, Gang Up, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: When you and at least two of your allies are adjacent to an opponent, you can attempt the aid another action as a move action. Normal: Aid another is a standard action. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133 When you and at least two of your allies are adjacent to an opponent, you can spend 2 stamina points to attempt to aid another as a swift action.
