Octopus Thrash - Feats Octopus Thrash (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 15 Your arms flail about in a confusing pattern of strikes that trips and batters your foes. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Dodge, Improved Trip, Octopus Focus, Octopus Style, Acrobatics 7 ranks, Perception 7 ranks. Benefit: While using Octopus Style, when you threaten two enemies who are adjacent to each other, you can attempt a trip combat maneuver check against both targets at once in place of a melee attack. You attempt a single combat maneuver check at a -5 penalty and apply the result to both targets.
