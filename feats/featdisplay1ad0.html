Surprise Follow-Through - Feats Surprise Follow-Through (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 57 When striking one opponent, you catch its ally off guard. Prerequisites: Str 13, Cleave, Power Attack, base attack bonus +1. Benefit: When using Cleave or Great Cleave, the second foe you attack on your turn is denied its Dexterity bonus against you. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133 When you hit a foe that has been denied its Dexterity bonus by this feat, you can spend 2 stamina points to deal an extra 1d6 points of damage. This damage is precision damage and is not multiplied on a critical hit.
