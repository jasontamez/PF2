Bleeding Stare - Feats Bleeding Stare (Combat, Stare) Source Occult Adventures pg. 129 Your stare causes your foe to bleed out of its eyes. Prerequisites: Mesmerist level 5th, painful stare class feature. Benefit: When you trigger your painful stare, the target takes an amount of bleed damage equal to 1/3 your mesmerist level. Bleed damage from multiple uses of Bleeding Stare doesn't stack.
