Feats Meditation Feats Description Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 19 Meditation provides some of the advantages of a monastic refuge anywhere. While meditation can help any character center herself or calm a troubled mind, those specially trained in the art can use it to unlock fantastic abilities within themselves, ref lected in the following feats. These feats can be taken by any character who meets the prerequisites, but are most commonly encountered among those who have trained in a monastery. Monks and Meditation Feats: A monk may take a meditation feat in place of a bonus feat. She must meet all of the prerequisites to take the feat as normal.
