Breaker of Barriers - Feats Breaker of Barriers (Combat) Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 208 You see it as your sacred duty to destroy all obstacles. Prerequisites: Str 17, worshiper of Rovagug. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on Strength checks to break down doors, walls, dams, and other barriers and on combat maneuver checks to bull rush and overrun opponents. You ignore the first 5 points of hardness when you deal damage to doors, walls, dams, and other barriers.
