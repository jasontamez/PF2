Black Powder Spectacle - Feats Black Powder Spectacle (Combat, Performance) Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 12 You can sacrifice ammunition to make a grand spectacle. Prerequisites: Amateur Gunslinger or the grit class feature, base attack bonus +4. Benefit: When you spend a swift action to attempt a performance combat check, you can sacrifice one cartridge or an amount of black powder required for a single shot to make either a loud sound or an impressive flash. If you choose to make your weapon's report louder, you gain a +4 bonus on all Intimidate checks you attempt for 1 round. If you choose to create an impressive flash, you gain a +4 bonus on any Perform checks you attempt for 1 round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24 You can spend 2 stamina points when using Black Powder Spectacle in order to gain a +4 bonus on Intimidate checks and Perform checks for 1 round.
