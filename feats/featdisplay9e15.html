Eldritch Assault - Feats Eldritch Assault Source Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 5 You make deadly strikes when magic cloaks your location. Prerequisites: Blind-Fight, Improved Initiative. Benefit: Whenever you attack from total concealment provided by a magical source (such as the displacement or invisibility spells), the first attack roll you make each round that is a critical threat is automatically confirmed as a critical hit.
