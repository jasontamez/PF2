Potion Glutton - Feats Potion Glutton Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 215 You gulp down potions with unsettling speed. Prerequisites: Worshiper of Urgathoa. Benefit: You can drink potions, elixirs, or other potables (but not extracts) as a move action without provoking an attack of opportunity. Normal: Drinking potions is a standard action that provokes an attack of opportunity.
