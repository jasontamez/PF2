Shark Tear - Feats Shark Tear (Combat) Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 59 You tear into bleeding foes and can smell their blood in the water. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Shark Style, Swim 6 ranks, base attack bonus +6 or monk level 6th. Benefit: While in Shark Style, you gain scent while in water, but only against bleeding creatures. You also gain a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls against bleeding creatures.
