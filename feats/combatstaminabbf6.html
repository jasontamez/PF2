Snap Shot - Combat Stamina - Feats Snap Shot Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 Related Feat Snap Shot At the end of your turn, you can spend 2 stamina points to increase the range at which you threaten squares with ranged weapons to 10 feet until you take an attack of opportunity against an opponent in the expanded threat range or the beginning of your next turn, whichever comes first.
