Guardian of Tradition - Feats Guardian of Tradition Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 35 Your deity rewards your devotion to the duty of your race. Prerequisites: Domain class feature, girtablilu. Benefit: You can replace one of the domains offered by your deity with one of the following druid domains: Desert (Ultimate Magic 34), Ruins, or Vermin. You gain a +1 bonus to your caster level when casting domain spells from the selected domain.
