Lost in the Crowd - Feats Lost in the Crowd Source Osirion, Land of the Pharaohs pg. 29 You are used to dealing with the teeming masses in the markets, and are capable of disappearing into them at a moment's notice. Benefit: You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Hide checks in urban areas; this bonus increases to +4 in crowds. You gain a +1 dodge bonus to AC when threatened by 2 or more enemies.
