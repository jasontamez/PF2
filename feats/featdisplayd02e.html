Relic Breaker - Feats Relic Breaker (Combat) Source Path of the Hellknight pg. 39 You are furious when destroying dangerous objects. Prerequisites: Str 13, Gate Breaker, Improved Sunder, Power Attack, base attack bonus +1. Benefit: Whenever you sunder a held object, its bearer must succeed at a Reflex saving throw or the bearer catches on fire (taking 1d6 points of fire damage immediately, and an additional 1d6 points of fire damage each subsequent round the creature fails a Reflex saving throw). When your total Hit Dice reach 5, this damage increases by 1d6, and it increases by 1d6 every 5 levels thereafter, to a maximum of 5d6 at 20th level. The saving throw's DC is equal to 10 + 1/2 your HD + your Charisma modifier.
