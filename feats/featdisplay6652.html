Voracious Blade - Feats Voracious Blade (Combat) Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 59 You can channel your daemonic energies through the vile blade you wield. Prerequisites: Weapon Focus (rhoka sword), base attack bonus +4, urdefhan. Benefit: When wielding a rhoka sword, you can channel your daemonic energies through the blade as a swift action, causing it to deal Strength damage as your bite attack. You can use this ability a number of times per day equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum 1).
