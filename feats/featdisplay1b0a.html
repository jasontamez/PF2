Exquisite Sneak - Feats Exquisite Sneak Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 82 You know how to move silently even while wearing some forms of armor. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Careful Sneak, Stealth 6 ranks. Benefit: You do not apply the armor check penalty for light and medium armor on Stealth skill checks no matter how fast you move. You still cannot use Stealth while running or charging.
