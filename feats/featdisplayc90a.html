Fortitude of Giants - Feats Fortitude of Giants Source Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 69 Your great mass and vigor allows you to shrug off the effects of poison and fatigue. Prerequisites: Con 14, Great Fortitude, giant subtype. Benefit: When you attempt a saving throw against a poison effect or an effect that inflicts the fatigued condition, roll twice and take the higher result.
