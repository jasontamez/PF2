Ancient Draconic - Feats Ancient Draconic Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 24 You empower spells using the ancient language of dragons. Prerequisites: Arcane caster level 5th, speaks Draconic. Benefit: You gain a +1 bonus on caster level checks to overcome spell resistance when casting arcane spells that have a verbal component. If you speak Draconic when casting an arcane language-dependent spell, all intelligent creatures with a language can understand your words.
