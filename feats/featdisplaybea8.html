Graceful Flier - Feats Graceful Flier Source Inner Sea Monster Codex pg. 53 When unencumbered, you fly with exceptional speed and agility. Prerequisites: Skill Focus (Fly), fly speed. Benefit: You are a gifted flier. While wearing light or no armor, your fly speed increases by 5 feet and your maneuverability is one step better than normal, to a maximum of good. You lose the benefits of this feat if you carry a medium or heavy load. Special: While the following feats are primarily taken by strix, other creatures with natural wings can also take them at the GM's discretion.
