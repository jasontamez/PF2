Improved Back to Back - Feats Improved Back to Back (Teamwork) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 105 After much practice, you and an ally have become adept at fighting in close proximity to one another. Prerequisites: Back to Back, Perception 5 ranks. Benefit: While you are adjacent to an ally who is flanked and also has this feat, you can spend a swift action to gain a +2 bonus to AC against all flankers until the start of your next turn.
