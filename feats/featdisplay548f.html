Horn of the Criosphinx - Feats Horn of the Criosphinx (Combat) Source People of the Sands pg. 13 You end your charge in a devastating two-handed attack. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +6 or monk level 6th. Benefit: Whenever you make a successful charge attack while wielding a two-handed weapon in both hands, add two times your Strength bonus to the damage roll. Normal: A character wielding a two-handed weapon adds 1-1/2 times her Strength bonus to damage rolls. Special: A monk can use this feat as long as he is wielding a two-handed weapon or both his hands are empty. Note: A monk can take this feat as a bonus feat at 6th level. To benefit from this feat, monks must have both hands free and be capable of delivering an unarmed strike.
