Improved Two-Weapon Feint - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Two-Weapon Feint Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 124 Related Feat Improved Two-Weapon Feint You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to increase the duration for which the opponent is denied its Dexterity bonus until the start of your next turn.
