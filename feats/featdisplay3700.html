Natural Charmer - Feats Natural Charmer Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 100 You possess some of the dominating powers of your vampire progenitor. Prerequisites: Cha 17, dhampir. Benefit: You can take 20 on any Charisma-based skills to charm, convince, persuade, or seduce humanoids whose attitude is at least friendly to you. Taking 20 still requires 20 times the normal time to perform the skill. Normal: You cannot take 20 on any check where you incur penalties for failure.
