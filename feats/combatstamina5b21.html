Improved Charging Hurler - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Charging Hurler Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 Related Feat Improved Charging Hurler When using Charging Hurler against a target within 30 feet, you can spend up to 2 stamina points. When you do, the bonus on your damage roll for that attack increases by double the number of stamina points you spent.
