Natural Jouster - Feats Natural Jouster (Combat) Source Pathfinder #69: Maiden, Mother, Crone pg. 91 Nearly all svathurims have the following feat, which is common among tauric creatures. Prerequisites: Centaur (or any tauric creature at the GM's discretion) Benefit: You gain proficiency with the lance. You can wield a lance one-handed as if you were mounted, and you deal double damage with a lance while charging.
