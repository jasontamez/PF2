Greater Drag - Feats Greater Drag (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 161 Foes that you drag are thrown out of balance. Prerequisites: Str 13, Improved Drag, Power Attack, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: You receive a +2 bonus on checks made to drag a foe. This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by Improved Drag. Whenever you drag a foe, his movement provokes attacks of opportunity from all of your allies (but not you). Normal: Creatures moved by drag do not provoke attacks of opportunity. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 121 When you succeed at a drag combat maneuver check, you can spend 5 stamina points to gain up to 10 feet of movement for your drag. You can use this movement in place of your normal movement, but only to drag an opponent.
