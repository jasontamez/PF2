Tiger Pounce - Combat Stamina - Feats Tiger Pounce Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 133 Related Feat Tiger Pounce When using Tiger Style, you can spend 2 stamina points and take a swift action to move closer to an enemy you've hit with an unarmed strike or made a successful combat maneuver against at any point during this combat, instead of one you hit on this or your last turn.
