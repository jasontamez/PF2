Cracking the Shell - Feats Cracking the Shell (Combat, Combination) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 27 The tip of your weapon pierces both the body and the spirit. Prerequisites: Weapon Focus with piercing weapon. Benefit: When you deal damage with a melee attack with a piercing weapon to an opponent you have already damaged this round, you can also reduce the value of that target's spell resistance or energy resistances by 1 until the start of your next turn. This reduction stacks with itself.
