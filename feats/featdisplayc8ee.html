Terrifying Assault - Feats Terrifying Assault (Combat) Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 13 Your foes flee when you appear suddenly. Prerequisites: Frightening Ambush, Intimidate 5 ranks. Benefit: When using Intimidate to demoralize an opponent during a surprise round, if you exceed the DC by 10 or more, you can make the target frightened for 1 round instead of shaken.
