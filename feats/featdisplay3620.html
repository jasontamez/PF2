Improved Precise Strike - Feats Improved Precise Strike (Combat, Teamwork) Source Legacy of the First World pg. 19 Your precise timing and smooth teamwork allow you to strike a foe where it hurts. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Precise Strike, base attack bonus +4. Benefit: Whenever you deal additional damage with Precise Strike, you also deal 1 point of bleed damage. This bleed damage does not stack with itself.
