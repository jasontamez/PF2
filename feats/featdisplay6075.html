Thuvian Grenadier - Feats Thuvian Grenadier (Teamwork) Source People of the Sands pg. 9 Training with alchemically inclined allies helps you avoid friendly fire. Prerequisites: Precise Shot or Throw Anything. Benefit: Whenever you hit a creature with a splash weapon, you may choose to exclude one creature within the weapon's splash area from taking splash damage. If a creature within the splash area also has this feat, it automatically ignores splash damage from your splash weapons on a successful hit. In addition, if you miss with a splash weapon and the errant weapon lands in a square occupied or adjacent to an ally with this feat, your ally can attempt a DC 20 Reflex save. If the ally succeeds, he is able to catch the weapon. If your ally also has the Snatch Arrows feat, he can toss the splash weapon back to you as an immediate action.
