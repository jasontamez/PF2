Coaxing Spell - Feats Coaxing Spell (Metamagic) Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 10 Your charms and enchantments reach the minds of even the lowliest dungeon denizens. Prerequisites: Spell Focus (enchantment), Knowledge (dungeoneering) 6 ranks. Benefit: This feat only works with mind-affecting effects. A coaxing spell affects mindless oozes and vermin as if they weren't mindless, but has no effect on other creature types. A coaxing spell uses a spell slot 2 levels higher than the spell's actual level.
