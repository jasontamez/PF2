Starlight Summons - Feats Starlight Summons Source Ultimate Magic pg. 157 Your summoned minions slink along under the shadows of the stars. Prerequisites: Spell Focus (conjuration), able to cast summon nature's ally. Benefit: Creatures you summon gain the Blind-Fight feat, a +5 bonus to Perception and Stealth checks in dim light or darkness, and their natural weapons are treated as cold iron for overcoming damage reduction.
