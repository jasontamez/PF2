Sly Draw - Feats Sly Draw Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 31 You can draw your weapon with such speed and finesse that your opponents don't realize they're threatened. Prerequisites: Quick Draw. Benefit: When you draw a light weapon, you may make a Sleight of Hand check instead of a Bluff check to feint in combat. Other feats and abilities that affect a feint still apply to your feint.
