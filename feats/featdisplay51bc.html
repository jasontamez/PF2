Great Fortitude - Feats Great Fortitude Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 124 You are resistant to poisons, diseases, and other maladies. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Fortitude saving throws. Mythic Great Fortitude Source Mythic Adventures pg. 66 Your fortitude is unmatched. Prerequisites: Great Fortitude. Benefit: Whenever you roll a Fortitude saving throw against a spell, spell-like ability, or supernatural ability from a non-mythic source, roll twice and take the higher result.
