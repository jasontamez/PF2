Painful Collision - Feats Painful Collision (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 24 You shove your opponents into each other, causing painful and damaging collisions. Prerequisites: Str 13, Improved Bull Rush, Power Attack. Benefit: When you bull rush an enemy into another creature, both creatures take 1d6 points of bludgeoning damage, plus an additional 1d6 points of bludgeoning damage for every 5 feet your original target has moved as a result of your bull rush. For instance, if you successfully bull rush a goblin 10 feet and it then collides with another goblin, both creatures would take 3d6 points of bludgeoning damage.
