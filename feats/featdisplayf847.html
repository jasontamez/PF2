Water Skinned - Feats Water Skinned Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 178 Your touch extinguishes small flames. Prerequisites: Undine. Benefit: As a standard action, you can extinguish a small nonmagical fire with a touch, affecting anything up to the size of a large campfire. This ability does not affect fires with a total area greater than 5 square feet. Touching the fire in this way does not harm you.
