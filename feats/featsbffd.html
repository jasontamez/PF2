Feats Origin Feats Description Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 16 Duskwalker Origin Feats Description Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 16 Origin feats are duskwalker racial feats that grant additional powers based on the duskwalker's original cause of death. Generally, a duskwalker can qualify for only a single origin feat, but at the GM's discretion, a duskwalker whose death matches multiple origin feats may be allowed to take more than one.
