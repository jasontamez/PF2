Uncanny Concentration - Feats Uncanny Concentration Source Ultimate Magic pg. 158 You have learned to enter a deeper state when casting spells, allowing you to shrug off distractions, damage, weather effects, and even the effects of other spells. Prerequisites: Combat Casting. Benefit: You do not need to make concentration checks when affected by vigorous or violent motion or by violent weather. You gain a +2 bonus on all other concentration checks.
