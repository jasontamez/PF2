Demonologist - Feats Demonologist Source Pathfinder #74: Sword of Valor pg. 71 You have immersed yourself deeply in studies of the nature of the Abyss and its inhabitants. Prerequisites: Knowledge (planes) 3 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on all Knowledge checks related to the Abyss or to demons, and you may take 10 on Knowledge checks made to identify demons.
