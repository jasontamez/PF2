Giant's Wallop - Feats Giant's Wallop (Combat) Source Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 69 When you strike, you knock the sense out of smaller foes. Prerequisites: Str 13, Power Attack, Vital Strike, base attack bonus +6, giant subtype. Benefit: If you hit a creature at least one size category smaller than you with a melee attack while using both Power Attack and Vital Strike, the creature you attacked is also dazed for 1 round. A successful Fortitude save negates this effect (DC = 10 + your base attack bonus). You must choose to use this feat before making the attack roll. Special: This effect stacks with the effect of Giant's Crush.
