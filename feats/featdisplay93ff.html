Haunted Gnome - Feats Haunted Gnome (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 104 You use your gnome magic to take on an eerie illusory appearance. Prerequisites: Cha 13, gnome magic racial trait, Knowledge (arcana) 1 rank. Benefit: You add haunted fey aspect (page 230) to your list of gnome magic spell-like abilities, and you can use this spell-like ability twice per day. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 You can spend 5 stamina points to use haunted fey aspect an additional time in a day.
