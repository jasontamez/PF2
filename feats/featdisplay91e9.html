Rhino Charge - Feats Rhino Charge (Combat) Source Sargava, the Lost Colony pg. 24 Your charges are both violent and unpredictable. Prerequisites: Power Attack, Improved Bull Rush, base attack bonus +5. Benefit: You may ready a charge, though you may only move up to your speed on the charge. Normal: Charging is a full-round action and allows you to move twice your speed.
