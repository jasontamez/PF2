Greater Whip Mastery - Combat Stamina - Feats Greater Whip Mastery Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 Related Feat Greater Whip Mastery When you initiate a grapple with a whip, you can spend 5 stamina points to attempt a second grapple check as a swift action to either move or damage the creature you are grappling. This is not a check to maintain the grapple.
