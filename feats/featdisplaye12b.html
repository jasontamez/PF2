Improved Fury of the Tainted - Feats Improved Fury of the Tainted (Combat) Source Blood of Fiends pg. 25 Your hatred for goodness is rivaled only by your ability to repel it. Prerequisites: Cha 13, Fury of the Tainted, base attack bonus +8, rage class feature, tiefling. Benefit: While raging, you gain spell resistance equal to 10 + your class level against spells with the good descriptor.
