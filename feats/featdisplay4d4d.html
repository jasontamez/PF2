Musketeer's Dodge - Feats Musketeer's Dodge (Combat, Grit) Source Villain Codex pg. 56 You dodge ranged attacks with greater proficiency than most. Prerequisites: Exotic Weapon Proficiency (firearms), gunslinger level 3rd, gunslinger's dodge deed. Benefit: When you use the gunslinger's dodge deed to move, the movement granted by that deed does not provoke attacks of opportunity, and you gain a +4 dodge bonus to your Armor Class.
