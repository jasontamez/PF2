Shapeless Familiar - Feats Shapeless Familiar Source Wilderness Origins pg. 22 A familiar is more than just an animal. It's a spirit and an intelligence, and your familiar can choose to take more than one physical form. Prerequisites: Familiar class feature, caster level 7th. Benefit: Your familiar gains the change shape universal monster ability. This ability functions as beast shape II and grants access to a single alternate form, which must be the same size as the familiar. Once chosen, this form cannot be changed. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time you take this feat, your familiar gains an additional alternate form. If taken by a familiar that already has the change shape universal monster ability (like an imp), this grants a single additional form.
