Shikigami Style - Feats Shikigami Style (Combat, Style) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 9 Your skill with improvised weapons makes them more dangerous than they would otherwise be. Prerequisites: Catch Off-Guard or Throw Anything. Benefit: While in this style, you deal damage with improvised weapons as if they were one size category larger. For every style feat you have that lists Shikigami Style as a prerequisite, treat the improvised weapon as an additional size category larger, to a maximum of three sizes larger than its actual size.
