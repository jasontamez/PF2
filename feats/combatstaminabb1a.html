Snake Sidewind - Combat Stamina - Feats Snake Sidewind Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 132 Related Feat Snake Sidewind When you threaten a critical hit with your unarmed strike while using Snake Style and choose to attempt a Sense Motive check in place of the confirmation roll, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on the Sense Motive check equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
