Widen Spell - Feats Widen Spell (Metamagic) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 137 You can cast your spells so that they occupy a larger space. Benefit: You can alter a burst, emanation, or spread-shaped spell to increase its area. Any numeric measurements of the spell's area increase by 100%. A widened spell uses up a spell slot three levels higher than the spell's actual level. Spells that do not have an area of one of these four sorts are not affected by this feat.
