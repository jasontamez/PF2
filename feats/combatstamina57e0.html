Greater Grapple - Combat Stamina - Feats Greater Grapple Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 121 Related Feat Greater Grapple After you take a move action to successfully maintain a grapple, you can spend 5 stamina points before the end of your turn to maintain that grapple as a swift action. This allows you to make up to three grapple checks to maintain a grapple during a round, but you still can't maintain a grapple until the round after you initiate it.
