Improved Awesome Blow - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Awesome Blow Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 Related Feat Improved Awesome Blow When a creature attempts an awesome blow combat maneuver against you, you can spend up to 3 stamina points to increase your Combat Maneuver Defense against that combat maneuver by an amount equal to the number of stamina points you spent. This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by Improved Awesome Blow.
