Shapeshifter Twist - Feats Shapeshifter Twist (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 19 Your form flexes and contorts in unexpected ways, making it difficult for enemies to move you against your will. Prerequisites: Shapeshifter Style, base attack bonus +5, change shape ability, shapechanger subtype. Benefit: While you are using Shapeshifter Style, you gain a +2 bonus to your CMD versus bull rush, drag, grapple, overrun, reposition, and trip combat maneuvers. If you begin your turn with the grappled condition, you can attempt a combat maneuver check or an Escape Artist check as a move action to attempt to break the grapple. Special: A character with the wild shape class feature counts as having the change shape ability and shapechanger subtype for the purpose of meeting this feat's prerequisites.
