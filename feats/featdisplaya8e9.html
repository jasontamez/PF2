Extra Spontaneous Spell Mastery - Feats Extra Spontaneous Spell Mastery Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 124, Arcane Anthology pg. 14 You are able to spontaneously cast spells you have mastered more frequently than your peers. Prerequisites: Magaambyan arcanist spontaneous spell mastery class feature. Benefit: You gain one additional use of your spontaneous spell mastery ability per day. Special: You can take this feat multiple times.
