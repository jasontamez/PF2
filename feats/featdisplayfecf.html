Gun Twirling - Feats Gun Twirling (Grit) Source Champions of Balance pg. 19 You can spin and juggle a small firearm, making it difficult to predict where you will shoot. Prerequisites: Amateur Gunslinger feat or grit class feature, Dazzling Display, Weapon Focus. Benefit: You can spend 1 grit point to make a feint attempt using a one-handed firearm for which you have Weapon Focus (instead of a melee weapon). The target of this feint must be within 30 feet of you and be able to see you. If you have the Quick Draw feat, you can holster a onehanded firearm as a free action as long as you have at least 1 grit point.
