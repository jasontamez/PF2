Empathy - Feats Empathy Source Pathfinder #85: Fires of Creation pg. 72, People of the Stars pg. 7 You have learned to experience emotion. Prerequisites: Cha 13, android. Benefit: You lose the emotionless special quality. You can gain morale bonuses, and can be affected by emotionbased effects and fear effects. You lack the +4 racial bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting effects.
