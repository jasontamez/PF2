Dodge - Combat Stamina - Feats Dodge Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118 Related Feat Dodge When you move your speed or farther, you can spend a number of stamina points up to double your Dexterity bonus. If you do, until the start of your next turn, you gain an increase to the dodge bonus granted by the Dodge feat equal to half the number of stamina points you spent.
