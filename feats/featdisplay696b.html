Stone Read - Feats Stone Read Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 216, Faiths of Purity pg. 25 Faint vibrations in stone can alert you to trouble. Prerequisites: Knowledge (dungeoneering) 4 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +3 bonus on Perception checks in underground or mountainous environments. If you have 10 or more ranks in Knowledge (dungeoneering), this bonus increases to +6.
