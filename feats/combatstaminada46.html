Disengaging Feint - Combat Stamina - Feats Disengaging Feint Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 117 Related Feat Disengaging Feint You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to attempt a Disengaging Feint as a move action instead of a standard action.
