Sneaky Vagabond - Feats Sneaky Vagabond Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 29 You have a well-practiced ability at going unnoticed. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on Hide checks when others are actively trying to find you. In crowds, this bonus increases to +4. Additionally, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on Gather Information and Knowledge (local) checks when trying to find a place to hide.
