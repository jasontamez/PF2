Arcane Armor Training - Feats Arcane Armor Training (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 118 You have learned how to cast spells while wearing armor. Prerequisites: Light Armor Proficiency, caster level 3rd. Benefit: As a swift action, reduce the arcane spell failure chance due to the armor you are wearing by 10% for any spells you cast this round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 114 When casting a spell while wearing armor, you can spend 1 stamina point to activate Arcane Armor Training as a free action. Mythic Arcane Armor Training Source Mythic Adventures pg. 55 You've adapted your spellcasting to overcome limitations of wearing armor. Prerequisites: Arcane Armor Training. Benefit: You don't have to spend a swift action to gain the reduction to arcane spell failure chance from Arcane Armor Training. Furthermore, if you're wearing light armor, reduce your arcane spell failure chance by 20%.
