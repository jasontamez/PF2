Babau Rogue Talent - Feats Babau Rogue Talent Source Demons Revisited pg. 8 Your instinct for combat is much like that of a rogue. Prerequisites: Dex 17, babau. Benefit: Choose one rogue talent that adds to sneak attacks (such as bleeding attack or slow reactions). Special: You may select this feat multiple times. Each time you take the feat, you gain a new rogue talent.
