Profane Studies - Feats Profane Studies Source Horror Adventures pg. 91 Your study of fiends grants you advantages when summoning or fighting them. Prerequisites: Int 13, Knowledge (planes) 4 ranks, ability to cast a summon monster spell. Benefit: You gain a +5 bonus on Knowledge (planes) checks involving evil outsiders. Additionally, when you cast a summon monster spell to summon an evil outsider, treat your caster level as 2 levels higher for the purpose of determining the duration.
