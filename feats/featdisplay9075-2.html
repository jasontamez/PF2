Lastwall Phalanx - Feats Lastwall Phalanx (Teamwork) Source Champions of Purity pg. 23 When battling the terrifying hordes of Belkzen, you find strength in your shield brothers and sisters. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +3, good alignment. Benefit: You gain a sacred bonus to your AC against the attacks of evil creatures and a sacred bonus to saves against the spells and abilities of evil creatures equal to the number of adjacent allies who also have this feat.
