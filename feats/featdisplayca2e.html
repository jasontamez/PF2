Channel Viciousness - Feats Channel Viciousness Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 209 You can channel brutality into the weapons of your allies. Prerequisites: Channel negative energy 4d6, worshiper of Gorum. Benefit: As a standard action, you can expend one of your uses of channel energy to charge the melee weapons of all allies within 30 feet of you with negative energy. Affected melee weapons gain the vicious magic weapon special ability for 1 minute ?? your Charisma bonus (minimum 1 minute).
