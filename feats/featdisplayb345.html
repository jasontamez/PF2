Multiweapon Specialist - Feats Multiweapon Specialist Source Demons Revisited pg. 38 You gain bonuses on damage when you wield identical one-handed weapons. Prerequisites: Three or more arms, Dex 21 Benefit: When you wield one-handed or light weapons in each of your arms, and all of those weapons belong to the same weapon group (as defined for the fighter class), all attacks with those weapons gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls.
