Perturbing Stare - Feats Perturbing Stare (Combat, Stare) Source Psychic Anthology pg. 17 Your stare dulls your foe's reactions. Prerequisites: Mesmerist level 5th, painful stare class feature. Benefit: When you trigger your painful stare, the target must succeed at a Will save (DC = 10 + half your mesmerist level + your Charisma modifier) or lose its Dexterity modifier to AC for 1 round.
