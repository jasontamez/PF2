Favored Defense - Feats Favored Defense Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 160 Your cunning is your shield against your quarry's attacks. Prerequisites: Favored enemy class feature. Benefit: Choose one of your favored enemy types. You add half your favored enemy bonus to your CMD and as a dodge bonus to AC when attacked by a favored enemy. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take this feat, it applies to a new favored enemy type.
