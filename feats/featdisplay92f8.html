Twin Fang Lunge - Feats Twin Fang Lunge (Combat) Source Villain Codex pg. 105 You strike with the force of a serpent's lunge. Prerequisites: Dex 15; Quick Draw; Twin Fang Strike; Twin Fang Style; Two-Weapon Fighting; Acrobatics 8 ranks; base attack bonus +8 or monk level 8th. Benefit: While using Twin Fang Style, you can spend a full-round action to move up to twice your speed and then use Twin Fang Strike as if you were taking the attack action.
