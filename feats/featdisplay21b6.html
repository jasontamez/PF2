Shatter Resolve - Feats Shatter Resolve Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 216, Faiths of Corruption pg. 25 Your negative energy causes despair in those you harm. Prerequisites: Channel energy class feature, worshiper of Urgathoa. Benefit: When you damage a creature by channeling negative energy, you fill your foe with fear. A creature that fails its Will save against your channeled energy also becomes shaken for a number of rounds equal to the number of dice you roll for your channel energy ability.
