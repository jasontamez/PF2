Reject Poison - Feats Reject Poison Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 215, Faiths of Corruption pg. 24 You are a careful poisoner, and know how to protect yourself against exposure to the ill effects of most substances. Prerequisites: Con 13, poison use class feature, worshiper of Norgorber. Benefit: Whenever you fail the initial saving throw against poison, you have a 20% chance of naturally rejecting the toxin, negating its effects and wasting its use.
