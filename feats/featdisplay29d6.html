Two-Fisted Drinker - Feats Two-Fisted Drinker Source Mythic Adventures pg. 76 You can pound down drinks with amazing verve. Prerequisites: Quick Draw. Benefit: You can draw two potions or other beverages-mundane or magical-from a pouch, bandolier, or similar holder (but not from a backpack) as a move action. As a standard action, you can drink two potions or other beverages. You must have two free hands to use this feat.
