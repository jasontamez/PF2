Undermining Exploit - Feats Undermining Exploit Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 217, Faiths of Purity pg. 25 Your familiarity with earth and stone gives you a distinct advantage on rocky battlefields. Prerequisites: Knowledge (dungeoneering) 4 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +4 insight bonus on combat maneuver checks against opponents occupying a square of rocky difficult terrain such as rubble, broken ground, or steep stairs.
