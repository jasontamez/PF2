Camouflaged Trap - Feats Camouflaged Trap Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 108 You excel at hiding your traps in the wilderness, making them more difficult for your enemies to locate and avoid. Prerequisites: Craft (traps) 4 ranks, Survival 4 ranks. Benefit: When you craft and set a trap in a wilderness environment, you increase the DC of the Perception check required to find it by 5.
