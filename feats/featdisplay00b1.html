Quah Bond - Feats Quah Bond (Teamwork) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 208 Your bond with your totem spirit increases around others of the same quah. Prerequisites: Totem Spirit (The Inner Sea World Guide 289), human (Shoanti). Benefit: If at least one Shoanti ally from the same quah who also has this feat is within 30 feet, double your benefits from the Totem Spirit feat.
