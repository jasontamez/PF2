Disengaging Flourish - Combat Stamina - Feats Disengaging Flourish Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118 Related Feat Disengaging Flourish You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 5 stamina points to make a Disengaging Flourish attempt as a move action instead of a standard action.
