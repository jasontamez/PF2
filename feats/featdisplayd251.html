Multitalented Mastery - Feats Multitalented Mastery Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 47 You are adept at numerous disciplines. Prerequisites: Character level 5th, half-elf, multitalented racial trait. Benefit: All of your classes are considered favored classes. You gain either +1 hit point or +1 skill point whenever you take a level in any class. Apply these bonuses retroactively for all class levels that have not yet gained one of these bonuses. Normal: Half-elves with the multitalented racial trait have two favored classes.
