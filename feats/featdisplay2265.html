Commander of Goblinkind - Feats Commander of Goblinkind Source Monster Codex pg. 116 You employ ruthlessness and malice to command other races of goblinoids to do your bidding. Prerequisites: Cha 13, hobgoblin. Benefit: You receive a +5 competence bonus on Knowledge (local) checks and Charisma-based skill checks regarding other goblinoids. If you have the Leadership feat, treat your leadership score as 2 higher when taking followers or a cohort with the goblinoid subtype.
