Janni Tempest - Combat Stamina - Feats Janni Tempest Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 124 Related Feat Janni Tempest When you hit an opponent with an unarmed strike while using Janni Style, you can spend 2 stamina points to increase this feat's bonus by 2. You can also spend 2 stamina points to apply the feat's bonus on checks to a drag or reposition combat maneuver instead of a bull rush or trip.
