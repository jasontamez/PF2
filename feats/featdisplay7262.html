Designated Antagonist - Feats Designated Antagonist (Teamwork) Source Antihero's Handbook pg. 10 When you antagonize your foes, you can redirect their enmity to your ally. Prerequisites: Antagonize. Benefit: When you succeed at a skill check using Antagonize, you can cause the target creature to focus its efforts on a willing ally who also has this feat, as if the ally had been the one to use the ability.
