Exceptional Pull - Feats Exceptional Pull (Combat) Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 10 You have mastered techniques to get the most out of composite bows. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Deadly Aim, base attack bonus +3. Benefit: When you wield a ranged weapon that you are proficient with and that has a strength rating, add 2 to the weapon's strength rating. You don't take a penalty on attack rolls for having a Strength modifier lower than the strength rating of a weapon, provided you're proficient with that weapon.
