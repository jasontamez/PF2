Psychic Adept - Feats Psychic Adept Source Occult Adventures pg. 137 You have honed your mind to harness psychic power. Prerequisites: Int 11, Psychic Sensitivity. Benefit: You gain the ability to cast a 0-level knack from the psychic class's spell list. You can cast this spell three times per day as a spell-like ability. The caster level for this ability is equal to your character level, and the save DC is equal to 10 + your Intelligence modifier. Special: You can choose this feat multiple times, choosing a different knack each time.
