Lightning Stance - Feats Lightning Stance (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 130 The speed at which you move makes it nearly impossible for opponents to strike you. Prerequisites: Dex 17, Dodge, Wind Stance, base attack bonus +11. Benefit: If you take two actions to move or a withdraw action in a turn, you gain 50% concealment for 1 round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125 When you take a single move action and move no less than your speed, you can spend 5 stamina points to benefit from a 50% miss chance until the start of your next turn.
