Jaguar Pounce - Feats Jaguar Pounce (Combat) Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 115, Sargava, the Lost Colony pg. 24 Your ambushes are especially lethal. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +4. Benefit: When making a charge or using Spring Attack against an opponent who is flat-footed or helpless, you treat your first melee attack against that target as if you had the Improved Critical feat.
