Improved Drag - Feats Improved Drag (Combat) Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 162 You are skilled at dragging foes around the battlefield. Prerequisites: Str 13, Power Attack, base attack bonus +1. Benefit: You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a drag combat maneuver. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks made to drag a foe. You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Defense when an opponent tries to drag you. Normal: You provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a drag combat maneuver. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123 When you are the target of a drag combat maneuver, you can spend a number of stamina points up to your Strength or Dexterity bonus, whichever is greater. Your CMD against that drag attempt increases by that number. Mythic Improved Drag Source Mythic Adventures pg. 67 You pull your enemies around the battlefield with great ease. Prerequisites: Improved Drag. Benefit: You gain a bonus equal to half your tier on checks to attempt a drag combat maneuver, and to your CMD when an opponent attempts a drag combat maneuver against you. These bonuses stack with those granted by Improved Drag. Furthermore, you can make an attack of opportunity against any creature that attempts a drag maneuver against you, unless it also has this feat.
