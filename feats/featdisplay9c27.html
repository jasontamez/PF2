Master Delver - Feats Master Delver Source Cities of Golarion pg. 12 You've spent some time in Cassomir's Locker and have learned a little bit to help you survive. Prerequisites: Explored part of Cassomir's locker. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Knowledge (dungeoneering) checks and Perception checks made to notice traps. Knowledge (dungeoneering) is always a class skill for you.
