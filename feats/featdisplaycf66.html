Wolf Style - Feats Wolf Style (Combat, Style) Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 121, Blood of the Moon pg. 25 While in this style, you hamper foes that turn their backs on you. Prerequisites: Wis 13, Improved Unarmed Strike, Knowledge (nature) 3 ranks. Benefit: While using this style, whenever you deal at least 10 points of damage to a foe with an attack of opportunity, that foe's base speed decreases by 5 feet until the end of its next turn. For every 10 points of damage your attack deals beyond 10, the foe's base speed decreases by an additional 5 feet. If the penalty meets or exceeds the total base speed of the foe, you can attempt to trip the foe as a free action after the attack of opportunity is resolved.
