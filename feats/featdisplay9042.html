Blinding Sneak Attack - Feats Blinding Sneak Attack (Combat) Source Blood of Fiends pg. 24 When you strike from the cover of darkness, you inject foes with a fraction of your foul magic. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +5, darkness spell-like ability, sneak attack class feature, tiefling. Benefit: When you successfully deal sneak attack damage to a foe while you are within an area of magical darkness, you temporarily blind your opponent for 1 round. A successful Fortitude save (DC 10 + the amount of sneak attack damage dealt) negates this effect.
