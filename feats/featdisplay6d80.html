Brash Stride - Feats Brash Stride Source Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 72 You have studied the bold fighting style of Grand Prince Cyricas and emulate his intrepid tread through jungles and other dangerous lands. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Acrobatic Steps, Nimble Moves. Benefit: When you charge or run, you treat difficult terrain as normal terrain.
