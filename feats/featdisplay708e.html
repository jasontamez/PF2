Lantern Style - Feats Lantern Style (Combat, Style) Source Distant Realms pg. 33 Your thrown weapons shine with a divine light that dazzles their targets. Prerequisites: Point-Blank Shot, good or lawful alignment, caster level 1st or monk level 1st. Benefit: Your thrown weapons glow with holy light, illuminating a 20-foot-radius area where they fall for 1 round. A target damaged by one of these weapons becomes dazzled for 1 round.
