Punishing Step - Feats Punishing Step (Combat) Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 15 You can hurt a foe that uses a combat maneuver against you. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Dodge. Benefit: Your bonus to AC from the Dodge feat increases to +2 when calculating your CMD. When a creature attempts a combat maneuver against you and fails, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on attack rolls against that creature until the end of your next turn.
