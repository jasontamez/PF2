Cruelty - Feats Cruelty Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 209 The suffering of others compels you to greater cruelties. Prerequisites: Worshiper of Zon-Kuthon. Benefit: Whenever you deal bleed damage to an opponent, cause an opponent to suffer a fear or pain effect, or reduce an opponent's hit points to 0 or fewer, you gain a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls and weapon damage rolls for 1 round.
