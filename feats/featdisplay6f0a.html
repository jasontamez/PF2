Technology Adept - Feats Technology Adept (Combat) Source Technology Guide pg. 7 You utilize high-tech firearms to maximum effect. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Exotic Weapon Proficiency (firearms), Point-Blank Shot Benefit: When you attack with a technological firearm that consumes charges, the save DC of any effect caused by that firearm increases by 1. If your bonuses from Point-Blank Shot apply, it increases by 2 instead.
