Night Sky Hex - Feats Night Sky Hex Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 116 You use the mystical network of power that blazes in the night sky to empower your hexes. Prerequisites: Hex class feature; moon, stars, or winter patron. Benefit: When you gain this feat, choose one hex that you can use to affect no more than one opponent. When you are in view of the night sky and use that hex, increase the save DC of that hex by 2.
