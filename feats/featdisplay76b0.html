Patient Strike - Feats Patient Strike (Combat) Source Pathfinder Society Primer pg. 8 Your training under the Master of Swords has taught you that a well-timed strike is worth waiting for and that patience will serve you well in the long run. Prerequisites: Int 13. Benefit: You can choose to ready an attack as a fullround action instead of a standard action. When you do so, you gain a +2 bonus on your attack roll when your readied action triggers. Normal: Readying an attack is a standard action and doesn't grant a bonus on your attack roll.
