Fox Style - Feats Fox Style (Combat, Style) Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 84 Your martial training helps you manipulate foes. Prerequisites: Int 13. Benefit: While using this style, you can use your base attack bonus in place of your ranks in Bluff on Bluff checks to feint in combat and to create a distraction to hide. If your Intelligence is at least 19, you gain a +4 bonus on such Bluff checks when adding your Charisma modifier.
