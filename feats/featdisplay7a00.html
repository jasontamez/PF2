Tower Shield Specialist - Feats Tower Shield Specialist (Combat, Shield Mastery) Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 19 You wield tower shields with ease. Prerequisites: Shield Focus, Tower Shield Proficiency, base attack bonus +11 or fighter level 8th. Benefit: You reduce the armor check penalty for tower shields by 3, and if you have the armor training class feature, you modify the armor check penalty and maximum Dexterity bonus of tower shields as if they were armor.
