Improved Charging Hurler - Feats Improved Charging Hurler (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 105 Every muscle in your body adds its force to your thrown weapons. Prerequisites: Charging Hurler, Point-Blank Shot. Benefit: When you use Charging Hurler, your target can be at any range up to your weapon's maximum range. If your target is within 30 feet, you gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls. Normal: Using Charging Hurler requires you to end your movement within 30 feet of your opponent. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 When using Charging Hurler against a target within 30 feet, you can spend up to 2 stamina points. When you do, the bonus on your damage roll for that attack increases by double the number of stamina points you spent.
