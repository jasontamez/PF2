Shield Focus - Feats Shield Focus (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 133 You are skilled at deflecting blows with your shield. Prerequisites: Shield Proficiency, base attack bonus +1. Benefit: Increase the AC bonus granted by any shield you are using by 1. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 131 When an attack is made against you while you are using a shield, you can spend up to 2 stamina points. If you do, the shield's bonus to AC against that attack increases by an amount equal to the number of stamina points you spent. Mythic Shield Focus Source Mythic Adventures pg. 73 You're a master of using your shield for protection. Prerequisites: Shield Focus. Benefit: Add your shield bonus and your shield's enhancement bonus to your touch AC. As an immediate action, you can expend one use of mythic power to add your shield bonus and your shield's enhancement bonus on a Fortitude or Ref lex save just before you roll it.
