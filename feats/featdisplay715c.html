Human Spirit - Feats Human Spirit Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 47 Your blood burns with the passion and unyielding quest for self-improvement displayed so prominently by your human relatives. Prerequisites: Half-elf. Benefit: You receive 1 bonus skill rank. Whenever you gain another Hit Die, you gain an additional skill rank. You cannot gain more than four skill ranks in this way. Special: You can only take this feat at 1st level. If you take this feat, you cannot take the Elven Spirit feat.
