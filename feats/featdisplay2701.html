Darting Viper - Feats Darting Viper (Combat) Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 11, Dwarves of Golarion pg. 21 When using a dorn-dergar, you can quickly attack both near and distant foes. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +4, proficiency with the dwarven dorn-dergar. Benefit: You can change whether you're using the dorndergar as a normal or reach weapon as a swift action. Normal: Changing whether a dorn-dergar is a normal or reach weapon is a move action.
