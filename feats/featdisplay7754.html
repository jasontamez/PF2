Trailblazing Channel - Feats Trailblazing Channel Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 217 Your positive energy makes trailblazers of those you heal. Prerequisites: Channel energy 3d6, worshiper of Desna. Benefit: Once per day when you channel positive energy, you can cause healed creatures to be unimpeded by difficult terrain for a number of minutes equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum 1 minute).
