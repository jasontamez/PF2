Greater Bless Equipment - Feats Greater Bless Equipment Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 7 Your blessings are longer lasting, and you can bless more than one item at a time. Prerequisites: Bless Equipment, Improved Bless Equipment, caster level 12th, channel energy class feature. Benefit: The duration of your equipment blessings doubles. In addition, as a standard action you may bless up to two pieces of equipment within range with a single blessing, expending uses of channeled energy as if you had only blessed one item.
