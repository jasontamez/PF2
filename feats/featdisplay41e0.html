Born Alone - Feats Born Alone Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 141 You are so tough and vicious that you killed and ate the rest of your litter while still in the womb. Prerequisites: Orc. Benefit: Whenever you kill or knock unconscious an opponent with a melee attack, you gain temporary hit points equal to your Constitution bonus (minimum 1) until your next turn. These temporary hit points do not stack. You do not gain this bonus if the opponent is helpless or has less than half your Hit Dice.
