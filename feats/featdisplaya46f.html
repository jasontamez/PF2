Cultivate Magic Plants - Feats Cultivate Magic Plants (Item Creation) Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 109 You combine a natural green thumb and knowledge of magic in order to grow magic plants. Prerequisites: Brew Potion, Craft Wondrous Item, Knowledge (nature) 1 rank. Benefit: You can cultivate magic plants. Cultivating a magic plant takes 1 week per 1,000 gp in its base price. When you create a magic plant, you make the same choices that you would normally make when casting the spell. Whoever consumes the fruit of the magic plant is the target of the spell.
