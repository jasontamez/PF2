Meditative Concentration - Feats Meditative Concentration (Meditation) Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 19 Meditation clears your mind, allowing you to concentrate on your spells as if not distracted. Prerequisites: Wis 13, Combat Meditation, Meditation Master, character level 5th. Benefit: For 1 round per character level after you perform combat meditation, whenever you attempt a concentration check to avoid losing a spell or a Fortitude save to resist a distraction effect (such as the distraction ability of a swarm), you gain a +4 bonus on the check. If you choose to use the benefits of Combat Meditation on the check, the bonus applies to both die rolls.
