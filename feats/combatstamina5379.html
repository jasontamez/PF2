Duelist of the Roaring Falls - Combat Stamina - Feats Duelist of the Roaring Falls Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24 Related Feat Duelist of the Roaring Falls When you hit a foe with a melee attack made using an Aldori dueling sword, you can spend 4 stamina points to treat that foe as being shaken for the purpose of gaining a bonus from Duelist of the Roaring Falls until the start of your next turn.
