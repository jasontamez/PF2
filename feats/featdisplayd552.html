Merciful Takedown - Feats Merciful Takedown (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 26 You are adept at removing your defeated opponents from the fight while avoiding their deaths. Benefit: When you perform a coup de grace and deal only nonlethal damage, instead of attempting a Fortitude save to avoid death, the target must instead attempt a Fortitude save to avoid falling unconscious for 1 hour. The DC of this save is the same as that of a lethal coup de grace.
