Expert Salvager - Feats Expert Salvager Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 111 You are adept at foraging and salvaging raw materials from even the most seemingly sparse of environments. Prerequisites: Craft (any) 2 ranks, Spellcraft 2 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +4 bonus on Craft checks for Craft skills in which you have at least 2 ranks and Spellcraft checks when crafting items by foraging alchemical supplies and material components, salvaging raw crafting materials, and salvaging raw magical item materials.
