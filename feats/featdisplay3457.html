Medusa's Wrath - Feats Medusa's Wrath (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 130 You can take advantage of your opponent's confusion, delivering multiple blows. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Gorgon's Fist, Scorpion Style, base attack bonus +11. Benefit: Whenever you use the full-attack action and make at least one unarmed strike, you can make two additional unarmed strikes at your highest base attack bonus. These bonus attacks must be made against a dazed, flat-footed, paralyzed, staggered, stunned, or unconscious foe. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 126 While using this feat, you can spend 10 stamina points to make the bonus attacks against a foe that you are flanking, instead of only a dazed, flat-footed, paralyzed, staggered, stunned, or unconscious foe. Mythic Medusa's Wrath Source Mythic Adventures pg. 69 Your barrage of unarmed attacks builds to a single, staggering strike. Prerequisites: Medusa's Wrath. Benefit: You may forgo the two additional unarmed strikes of Medusa's Wrath to instead make a single unarmed strike at your highest base attack bonus. If you successfully hit your opponent, it must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw (DC 10 + 1/2 your character level + your Wisdom modifier) or be staggered for 1 round. You can expend one use of mythic power when you stagger a target with Medusa's Wrath to extend the duration of the staggered condition by a number of rounds equal to half your tier.
