Craft Rod - Feats Craft Rod (Item Creation) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 120 You can create magic rods. Prerequisites: Caster level 9th. Benefit: You can create magic rods. Crafting a rod takes 1 day for each 1,000 gp in its base price. To craft a rod, you must use up raw materials costing half of its base price. See the magic item creation rules in Magic Items for more information.
