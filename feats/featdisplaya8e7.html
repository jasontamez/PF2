Vanth's Scythe - Feats Vanth's Scythe Source Pathfinder #139: The Dead Road pg. 71 You've seen the reapers many times, and have learned something of their methods. Prerequisites: Character level 9th, duskwalker, ghost hunter racial trait. Benefit: When you use your ghost hunter racial trait to grant a scythe, quarterstaff, whip, or bow the ghost touch weapon special ability, you also grant the undead-bane weapon special ability. You also gain an additional daily use of ghost hunter.
