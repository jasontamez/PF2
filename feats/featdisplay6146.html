Shifter's Edge - Feats Shifter's Edge Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 117 You use your shapechanging powers to make your natural attacks especially lethal. Prerequisites: Dex 13, Weapon Finesse, shifter claws class feature. Benefit: Whenever you use Weapon Finesse to make a melee attack with your claws or a natural attack augmented by your claws, and you use your Dexterity bonus on attack rolls and your Strength modifier on damage rolls, you also add half your shifter level to the damage.
