Extra Cantrips or Orisons - Feats Extra Cantrips or Orisons Source Ultimate Magic pg. 150 You are a master of minor spells. Prerequisites: Ability to cast cantrips or orisons. Benefit: Add two cantrips to your cantrips known or two orisons to your orisons known. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, add two cantrips or orisons to your spells known.
