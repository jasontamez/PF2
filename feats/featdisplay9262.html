Spirit of the Wild - Feats Spirit of the Wild Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 28 Your mystic connection with one type of wilderness has grown even stronger. Prerequisites: Attuned to the Wild, Guardian of the Wild, elf. Benefit: When you are in a terrain type you have selected the Attuned to the Wild feat for, you gain a +4 bonus on Perception checks made to act in the surprise round of combat. If you act in the surprise round, you gain blindsense out to a range of 30 feet during the surprise round. If you are in an area that qualifies as more than one kind of terrain, these bonuses do not stack; you receive the bonus for only one terrain type.
