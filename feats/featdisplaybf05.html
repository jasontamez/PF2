Mantis Style - Feats Mantis Style (Combat, Style) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 108 You have learned to target vital areas with crippling accuracy. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Stunning Fist, Heal 3 ranks. Benefit: You gain one additional Stunning Fist attempt per day. While using this style, you gain a +2 bonus to the DC of effects you deliver with your Stunning Fist. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 125 While using this style, you can spend 5 stamina points to use Stunning Fist without expending a use of Stunning Fist for the day.
