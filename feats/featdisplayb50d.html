Prone Shooter - Feats Prone Shooter (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 113 Take a reduced penalty to your AC against melee attacks while prone Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1. Benefit: If you have been prone since the end of your last turn, the penalty to your Armor Class against melee attacks made against you is reduced to -2. In addition, the bonus to your Armor Class against ranged attacks made against you is increased to +6. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 129 As long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool, you gain the bonuses from this feat even if you haven't been prone since the end of your last turn.
