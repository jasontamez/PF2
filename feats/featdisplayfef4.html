Agile Tongue - Feats Agile Tongue Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 191 Your long pink tongue is capable of manipulating small items and even stealing objects. Prerequisites: Grippli. Benefit: You have a prehensile tongue with a range of 10 feet. You can pick up items weighing no more than 5 pounds, make Sleight of Hand checks, perform the steal or disarm combat maneuvers, or make melee touch attacks with your tongue.
