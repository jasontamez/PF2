Ability Focus - Feats Ability Focus (Monster) Source Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 314 One of this creature's special attacks is particularly difficult to resist. Prerequisites: Special attack. Benefit: Choose one of the creature's special attacks. Add +2 to the DC for all saving throws against the special attack on which the creature focuses. Special: A creature can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time the creature takes the feat, it applies to a different special attack.
