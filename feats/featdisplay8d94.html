Atheist Abjurations - Feats Atheist Abjurations Source Faiths and Philosophies pg. 6 Your critical attitude toward the divine allows you to determine the best ways to remove divine influence. Prerequisites: Divine Defiance. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus to your caster level whenever you use an abjuration spell to dispel or counter a divine spell, or send an extraplanar outsider summoned or called by a divine caster back to its home plane.
