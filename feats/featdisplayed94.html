Moonlight Stalker - Feats Moonlight Stalker (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 110 You are adept at using shadows to conceal your attacks. Prerequisites: Int 13, Blind-Fight, Combat Expertise, Bluff 3 ranks, darkvision or low-light vision racial trait. Benefit: While you have concealment from an opponent, you gain a +2 bonus on attack and damage rolls against that opponent. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 127 When you have total concealment from an opponent, you can spend 2 stamina points to increase your bonuses on attack and damage rolls from this feat to +4.
