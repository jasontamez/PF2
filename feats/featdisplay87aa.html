Lunging Spell Touch - Feats Lunging Spell Touch Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 152 You can extend your reach to touch foes that would normally be too far away. Prerequisites: Spellcraft 6 ranks. Benefit: You can increase the reach of your spells' melee touch attacks by 5 feet until the end of your turn by taking a -2 penalty to your AC until your next turn. You must decide to use this ability before you attempt any attacks on your turn.
