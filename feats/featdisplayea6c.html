Master Combat Performer - Feats Master Combat Performer (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 109 You are a master of the techniques and weapons of the arena and the stage. Prerequisites: Performing Combatant or at least three performance feats, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: You can make performance combat checks as a free action. You are proficient in all weapons with the performance special quality. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 126 When you attempt a performance combat check, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to gain a bonus on the check equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
