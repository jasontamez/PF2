Morrigna's Wrappings - Feats Morrigna's Wrappings Source Pathfinder #139: The Dead Road pg. 71 One of the Boneyard's psychopomp bounty hunters has taught you the trick of using spider webs to defend against the undead. Prerequisites: Character level 9th, duskwalker, ward against corruption racial trait. Benefit: So long as you have a spider web wrapped around some part of your body, the bonus provided by your ward against corruption racial trait increases to +4.
