Spider Climber - Feats Spider Climber Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 106 You tread where only arachnids dare. Prerequisites: Character level 3rd, drow. Benefit: You can cast spider climb once per day as a spell-like ability, using your character level as the caster level. Furthermore, you gain a +4 bonus on saving throws against the web special attacks of spiders and the effects of web and other similar spells (such as the web cloud spell).
