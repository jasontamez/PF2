Expert Boarder - Feats Expert Boarder Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 76, Andoran, Birthplace of Freedom pg. 32 You are as comfortable dangling from a ship's rigging as you are on stable land. Prerequisites: Affiliated with the Gray Corsairs. Benefit: Acrobatics and Climb are class skills for you. You gain a +2 bonus on Acrobatics and Climb checks to climb or swing on a rope or ship's rigging. You do not provoke attacks of opportunity from climbing or swinging on a rope or ship's rigging.
