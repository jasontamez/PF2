Burrowing Teeth - Feats Burrowing Teeth Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 154 You have teeth and claws that suit you well to digging. Prerequisites: Sharpclaw, Tunnel Rat, ratfolk. Benefit: You gain a burrow speed equal to 1/2 your base speed. You can burrow through sand, dirt, clay, gravel, or similar materials, but not solid stone. You do not leave a hole behind, nor is your passage marked on the surface.
