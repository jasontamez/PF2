Improved Channel - Feats Improved Channel Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 126 Your channeled energy is harder to resist. Prerequisites: Channel energy class feature. Benefit: Add 2 to the DC of saving throws made to resist the effects of your channel energy ability. Mythic Improved Channel Source Mythic Adventures pg. 66 As a divine conduit for energy, you're unmatched. Prerequisites: Improved Channel. Benefit: Non-mythic creatures that take damage from your channel energy must roll their saving throws twice and take the lower result.
