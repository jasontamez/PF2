Scholar - Feats Scholar Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 125, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 288 You have graduated from one of the many colleges, universities, and specialized schools of higher learning scattered throughout the Inner Sea region. Benefit: Pick any two Knowledge skills. You gain a +2 bonus on checks with these two skills. If you have 10 or more ranks in one of these Knowledge skills, the bonus increases to +4 for that skill. Special: This feat is unaffiliated; a creature need not be affiliated with the Magaambya to select this feat.
