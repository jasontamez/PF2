Focused Dragon Expertise - Feats Focused Dragon Expertise (Combat) Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 23 Your understanding of dragons helps you to easily avoid breath weapons. Prerequisites: Favored enemy (dragon) class feature. Benefit: You gain a bonus on saving throws against breath weapons and fear effects equal to half your favored enemy (dragon) bonus. Whenever you succeed at a Reflex saving throw against a dragon's extraordinary or supernatural ability that would deal half damage, you instead take no damage. If you already have the evasion class feature, you instead gain an inquisitor's stalwart ability (Pathfinder RPG Advanced Player's Guide 41) against dragons' extraordinary and supernatural abilities.
