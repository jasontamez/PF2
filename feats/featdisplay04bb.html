Ghost Hunting Team - Feats Ghost Hunting Team Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 16 Your loathing of undead is so great that even your allies can feel it. Prerequisites: Duskwalker, ghost hunter racial trait. Benefit: When you use your ghost hunter ability to grant your weapons the ghost touch special ability, you can also affect the weapons of allies within 30 feet of you. You can activate ghost hunter an additional time each day.
