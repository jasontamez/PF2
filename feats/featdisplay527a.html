Final Embrace Master - Feats Final Embrace Master (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 102 Few creatures can survive the crushing horror of your Final Embrace. Prerequisites: Str 17, Int 3; naga, serpentfolk, or creature that has the constrict special attack; Final Embrace; Final Embrace Horror; base attack bonus +9. Benefit: Double the number of damage dice for your constrict special attack. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119 Each time you constrict, you can spend 5 stamina points to triple the damage dice of your constrict ability instead of doubling them.
