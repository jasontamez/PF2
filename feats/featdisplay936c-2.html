Gray Dwarf Magic - Feats Gray Dwarf Magic Source Monster Codex pg. 44 You gain a new spell-like ability from the list of duergar racial traits. Prerequisites: Duergar. Benefit: Choose one spell-like ability that is usable once per day and is granted by a duergar racial trait you don't have. You can use that spell-like ability once per day, with a caster level equal to your character level. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, you must select a spell-like ability that's usable once per day from a different duergar racial trait.
