Unraveler of Secrets - Feats Unraveler of Secrets Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 24 You have a knack for learning what others would like to remain hidden. Prerequisites: Int 15, Knowledge (history) 1 rank, gloomkin half-orc. Benefit: You gain a +2 circumstance bonus on Knowledge (history) checks. In addition, you can cast object reading once per day as a spell-like ability, attempting a Knowledge (history) check in place of an Appraise check.
