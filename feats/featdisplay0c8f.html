Fearless Aura - Feats Fearless Aura Source Ultimate Magic pg. 150 Your aura of courage becomes more potent, as your steadfast resolve is also manifested by your allies. Prerequisites: Aura of courage class feature, caster level 8th. Benefit: Your aura of courage expands to a 20-foot-radius emanation. Allies within the aura are immune to fear effects. Normal: The aura of courage affects each ally within 10 feet, and grants a +4 morale bonus on saving throws against fear effects.
