Notorious Vigilante - Feats Notorious Vigilante (Combat) Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 87 You are widely known as dangerous and fearsome. Prerequisites: Dazzling Display, Weapon Focus, any nongood alignment, great renown social talent, proficiency with chosen weapon. Benefit: Your presence can act as a fast and effective Dazzling Display. As a standard action, you use the benefit of Dazzling Display even when you are not wielding a weapon in which you have Weapon Focus. If you are wielding such a weapon, you gain a +2 bonus on the Intimidate check.
