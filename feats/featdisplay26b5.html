Beastmaster Salvation - Feats Beastmaster Salvation (Combat) Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 107 Your presence grants your companion the toughness and will to resist all manner of threats. Prerequisites: Cha 13, Alertness, Beastmaster Style, Handle Animal 5 ranks, Sense Motive 5 ranks. Benefit: If you are adjacent to your animal companion when it attempts a saving throw, you can attempt a Handle Animal check as an immediate action. Your animal companion does not attempt the saving throw, but instead uses your Handle Animal result as its result for the save.
