Goblin Gunslinger - Feats Goblin Gunslinger (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 118 You have learned how to fire the big guns. Prerequisites: Goblin. Benefit: You can wield Medium firearms without taking the penalty for an inappropriately sized weapon. Normal: You take a -2 penalty when using an inappropriately sized weapon. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 When wielding a firearm that deals fire damage, when you threaten but fail to confirm a critical hit, you can spend 5 stamina points to regain 1 grit point. You still can't gain a grit point if the target is immune to critical hits.
