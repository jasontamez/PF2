Swamp Dweller - Feats Swamp Dweller Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 30 You have trained with the Muckmouth lizardfolk of the Hao Jin Tapestry and learned their ways. Prerequisites: Str 13, must have visited the Hao Jin Tapestry. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on Swim checks. If you have 10 or more ranks in Swim, the bonus increases to +4. This bonus doubles while you're in marshy terrain. In addition, you can move normally into squares with a shallow bog, and it costs you only 2 squares of movement to move into a square with a deep bog.
