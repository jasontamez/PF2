Grand Duchy Familiarity - Feats Grand Duchy Familiarity Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 17 You have spent significant time in the Grand Duchy of Alkenstar, and your proximity to so many firearms has given you a familiarity with their delicate handling. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +3, proficiency with at least one firearm. Benefit: Once per day as an immediate action, you can reroll an attack roll with a firearm that would have resulted in a misfire. You must take the results of the new roll, even if it results in a misfire as well.
