Arcane Insight - Feats Arcane Insight Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 207, Faiths of Balance pg. 24 Magical insight helps you evade attacks. Prerequisites: Arcane Shield, arcane spellcaster, caster level 10th, worshiper of Nethys. Benefit: Whenever you use Arcane Shield, you also gain a +1 insight bonus to AC for 1 round.
