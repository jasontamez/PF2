Net and Trident - Combat Stamina - Feats Net and Trident Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 127 Related Feat Net and Trident When you use a light or one-handed melee weapon to attack an opponent entangled by your net, you can spend up to 5 stamina points when you hit the opponent to gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
