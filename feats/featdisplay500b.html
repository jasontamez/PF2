Damned Disciple - Feats Damned Disciple Source Book of the Damned pg. 178 Your diverse experiences influence your choice of boons. Prerequisites: Fiendish Obedience. Benefit: When you gain boons through the Fiendish Obedience feat, you can choose an evangelist boon or an exalted boon, but once you select this boon, it can't be changed. In addition, choose two skills; you gain a +2 profane bonus on checks with those skills.
