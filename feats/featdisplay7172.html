Pesh Healing - Feats Pesh Healing Source Black Markets pg. 18, Dark Markets - A Guide to Katapesh pg. 56 Consuming pesh makes you feel healthier. Prerequisites: Sahir-Afiyun or pesh addict. Benefit: When you take a dose of pesh, in addition to its normal initial effect, you gain a number of temporary hit points equal to your total Hit Dice for 1 hour. Temporary hit points gained from additional doses of pesh do not stack.
