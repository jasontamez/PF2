Master Hammer Throw - Feats Master Hammer Throw (Combat) Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 9 Your ability to knock down foes with thrown weapons is second to none. Prerequisites: Int 13, Combat Expertise, Hammer Throw, Improved Hammer Throw, Improved Trip, Point-Blank Shot, Precise Shot, base attack bonus +6, dwarf. Benefit: You can immediately attempt a trip combat maneuver against a target that you successfully hit and damage with a thrown bludgeoning weapon with any attack, even as part of a full-attack action.
