Explosive Weapon - Feats Explosive Weapon (Combat) Source Chronicle of Legends pg. 8 You can channel magical power through your weapon to devastating effect. Prerequisites: Arcing Weapon, diverse training class feature. Benefit: As a standard action, you can cast a spell with an area of effect and a duration of instantaneous, such as fireball, and release it through your melee weapon as part of a melee attack. This functions as the magus spellstrike ability, except the spell damage does not multiply on a critical hit, the spell effect is centered on you, and you are excluded from its effects.
