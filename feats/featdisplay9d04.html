Cartwheel Dodge - Feats Cartwheel Dodge Source Ultimate Combat pg. 91 You use your knack for avoiding damage to reposition yourself in combat. Prerequisites: Evasion class feature, improved evasion class feature, acrobatics 12 ranks. Benefit: When you successfully use improved evasion to avoid taking damage, you can move up to half your speed as an immediate action. This movement provokes attacks of opportunity as normal.
