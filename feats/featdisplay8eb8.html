Dilettante - Feats Dilettante Source Seekers of Secrets pg. 17 What some would call dabbling you call a wide range of interests. Your breadth of knowledge offers unexpected insights in many situations. Prerequisites: 2 ranks each in 5 different Knowledge skills. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on Knowledge checks if you have 1-5 ranks in that skill. This bonus does not stack with Skill Focus. You can make untrained Knowledge checks with DCs up to 15. Normal: You may only make untrained Knowledge checks for common knowledge (DC 10 or lower).
