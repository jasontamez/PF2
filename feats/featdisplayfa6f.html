Dodge - Feats Dodge (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 122 Your training and reflexes allow you to react swiftly to avoid an opponents' attacks. Prerequisites: Dex 13. Benefit: You gain a +1 dodge bonus to your AC. A condition that makes you lose your Dex bonus to AC also makes you lose the benefits of this feat. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 118 When you move your speed or farther, you can spend a number of stamina points up to double your Dexterity bonus. If you do, until the start of your next turn, you gain an increase to the dodge bonus granted by the Dodge feat equal to half the number of stamina points you spent. Mythic Dodge Source Mythic Adventures pg. 63 When focused, you become nearly impossible to strike. Prerequisites: Dodge. Benefit: The bonus to AC from Dodge increases by 1. As an immediate action, you can expend one use of mythic power to grant yourself an additional +10 dodge bonus to AC against one attack.
