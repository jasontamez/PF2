Spiritual Guardian - Feats Spiritual Guardian Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 157 Your spiritual guardians are more powerful than those of most others. Prerequisites: Ability to cast spiritual weapon or spiritual ally, spirit magic class feature. Benefit: Whenever you cast spiritual weapon, spiritual ally, or a similar spell that grants you a spiritual guardian, that guardian uses your shaman level instead of your base attack bonus to determine its base attack bonus, potentially granting it multiple attacks. Additionally, it gains a +2 bonus on caster level checks to overcome spell resistance as well as on damage rolls.
