Friendly Shroud - Feats Friendly Shroud Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 13 You can help conceal your allies. Prerequisites: Stealth 1 rank. Benefit: Whenever you are adjacent to an ally, and neither you nor that ally move for at least 1 round, and both you and that ally attempt Stealth checks, that ally can use your Stealth check result if it is better than his own.
