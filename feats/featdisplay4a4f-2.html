Legionnaire's Inspiration - Feats Legionnaire's Inspiration Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 76, Andoran, Birthplace of Freedom pg. 29 You inspire your comrades to stand against impossible odds. Prerequisites: Affiliated with the Golden Legion. Benefit: As a standard action, you can select one creature you can see. All allies within 30 feet who are able to see and hear you gain a +1 morale bonus to their Armor Class against that creature for a number of rounds equal to your Charisma bonus (minimum 1 round). You can do this once per day, plus one additional time for every 3 character levels you have.
