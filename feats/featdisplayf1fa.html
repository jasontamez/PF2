Kraken Throttle - Feats Kraken Throttle (Combat) Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 13 You tighten your grip around your target's throat. Prerequisites: Wis 13, Improved Grapple, Improved Unarmed Strike, Kraken Style, base attack bonus +5 or monk level 5th. Benefit: The damage that you deal with the Kraken Style feat on a successful grapple combat maneuver check increases to an amount equal to your Wisdom bonus + 2. While using this style, you can choke your opponent when you successfully maintain a grapple instead of choosing to damage, move, pin, or tie up your opponent. This suffocates your opponent (Pathfinder RPG Core Rulebook 445). The grappled opponent can take a breath during any round in which you do not maintain the grapple.
