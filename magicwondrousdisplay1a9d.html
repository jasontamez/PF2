Bracers of Falcon's Aim - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bracers of Falcon's Aim Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 272 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot wrist; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Each of these crimson leather bracers is emblazoned with the image of a soaring golden falcon scanning the ground for easy prey. Once per day, on command, the wearer gains the benefits of aspect of the falcon for 1 minute. The wearer must wear these bracers continuously for 24 hours before activating this ability. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, aspect of the falcon (Advanced Player's Guide); Cost 2,000 gp
