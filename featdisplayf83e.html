Greater Feint - Feats Greater Feint (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 125 You are skilled at making foes overreact to your attacks. Prerequisites: Combat Expertise, Improved Feint, base attack bonus +6, Int 13. Benefit: Whenever you use feint to cause an opponent to lose his Dexterity bonus, he loses that bonus until the beginning of your next turn, in addition to losing his Dexterity bonus against your next attack. Normal: A creature you feint loses its Dexterity bonus against your next attack. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 121 You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. If you have at least 1 point in your stamina pool, you can feint as a swift action, but the feint causes the opponent to become flat-footed only for your next attack rather than for all attacks until the beginning of your next turn.
