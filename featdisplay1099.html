Eroding Strikes - Feats Eroding Strikes (Combat, Combination) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 27 With careful, repeated blows, you weaken your foe's defenses. Prerequisites: Weapon Focus with bludgeoning weapon. Benefit: When you deal damage with a melee attack with a bludgeoning weapon to an opponent you have already damaged this round, you can also reduce the value of that target's damage reduction by 1 until the beginning of your next turn. This reduction stacks with itself.
