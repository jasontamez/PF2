Rostland Edge - Weapons - Magic Equipment Rostland Edge Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 26, Inner Sea Combat pg. 56 Aura moderate evocation and transmutation CL 10th Slot none; Price 21,820 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description Crafted to defend against enemies seeking to damage it rather than its wielder, a Rostland edge is a +2 countering impervious Aldori dueling sword. If the weapon is ever destroyed, it explodes with tremendous force, dealing 10d6 points of fire damage to every creature within a 20-foot-radius spread (Reflex DC 14 half). Construction Requirements Combat Reflexes, Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cat's grace, fabricate, fireball, make whole; Price 11,070 gp
