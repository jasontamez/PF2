Dark Affinity - Feats Dark Affinity Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 30 You have observed the dark stalkers who roam the Hao Jin Tapestry and adopted some of their tricks. Prerequisites: Character level 3rd, must have visited the Hao Jin Tapestry. Benefit: You can cast darkness once per day as a spell-like ability using your character level as your caster level. At 7th level, you can also cast deeper darkness once per day as a spell-like ability using your character level as your caster level.
