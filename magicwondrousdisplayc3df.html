High Elven Bracers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment High Elven Bracers Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 273 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 8th Slot wrist; Price 30,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These finely crafted leather bracers have plates of silvered steel covering the back, and silver buckles at the wrist and forearm. The bracers enable the wearer to use any traditional elven weapon (longbows, composite longbows, longswords, rapiers, shortbows, composite shortbows, and any weapon with the word "elven" in its name) as if he were proficient in its use. If he uses such a weapon and is already proficient with it, he gains a +1 competence bonus on attack and damage rolls. Both bracers must be worn for the magic to be effective. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Craft Magic Arms and Armor, creator must be an elf; Cost 15,000 gp
