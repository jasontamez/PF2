Asura Sight - Feats Asura Sight (Combat) Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 8 Taking inspiration from the many faces of asuras, you have learned to split your focus to avoid being caught off guard. Prerequisites: Asura Style, base attack bonus +9 or monk level 9th. Benefit: When using Asura Style, you gain improved uncanny dodge as a rogue of your character level.
