Expanded Fiendish Resistance - Feats Expanded Fiendish Resistance Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 174 You gain extra fiendish resistances. Prerequisites: Tiefling. Benefit: Pick one of the following energy types that you do not already have resistance to: acid, cold, electricity, or fire. You gain resistance 5 to that energy type. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. Each time you do, pick another energy type you do not have resistance to. You gain resistance 5 to that energy type.
