Gauntlets of Fumbling - Cursed Items - Magic Equipment Gauntlets of Fumbling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 346, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 540 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot hands; Weight 2 lbs. Description These gauntlets perform according to their appearance until the wearer finds herself under attack or in a lifeand- death situation. At that time, the curse is activated. The wearer becomes fumble-fingered, with a 50% chance each round of dropping anything held in either hand. The gauntlets also lower the wearer's Dexterity score by 2 points. Once the curse is activated, the gloves can be removed only by means of a remove curse spell, a wish, or a miracle.
