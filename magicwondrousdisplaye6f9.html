Arrowmaster's Bracers Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 270 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot wrist; Price 13,900 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This simple pair of leather shooting bracers are designed to protect the wearer's wrist from being snapped by bowstrings. They appear similar to a set of bracers of archery, but a wearer notices the difference as soon as she dons them. The wearer of these bracers gains damage reduction 5/ magic against ranged weapons, as well as a +1 deflection bonus against ranged attacks. In addition, once per day, as a swift action, the wearer can grant herself a +20 insight bonus on her next ranged attack roll before the end of her next turn. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Craft Magic Arms and Armor, protection from arrows, true strike; Cost 6,950 gp
