Critical Hits Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 110 When you threaten a critical hit, roll to confirm at your full bonus and apply the effects of the critical hit to any one of your hits. If your original attack roll scored multiple hits and the critical confirmation roll also falls within your weapon's critical threat range, you score two critical hits and can apply them to any two hits.
