Uplifting Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Uplifting Boots Source Black Markets pg. 23 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot feet; Price 11,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description These worn, leather traveling boots respond to the wearer's holy power. The wearer can sacrifice one use of channel energy, lay on hands, or fervor as a standard action to instead walk on air (as per the air walk spell) for the next minute. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item; air walk; creator must have the channel energy, lay on hands, or fervor class ability; Cost 5,500 gp
