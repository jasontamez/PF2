Altar of Gorum - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Gorum Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 247 Aura moderate abjuration and transmutation [chaotic] CL 10th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 750 lbs. Description This altar is an iron platform and frame designed to support a heavy suit of spiked armor and weapons, typically mounted in the shape of a massively imposing, 8-foot-tall warrior. Praying at the altar grants a +4 morale bonus on saves against fear effects, and any non-masterwork iron or steel weapon the worshiper wields is treated as if it were masterwork. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, magic circle against law, masterwork transformation, remove fear, creator must worship Gorum; Cost 4,000 gp
