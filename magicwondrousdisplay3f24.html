Sable Company Elite Saddle - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Sable Company Elite Saddle Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 62 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 14,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description Several alterations along the edges of this well-maintained leather saddle allow for the movement of a mount's wings. A Sable Company elite saddle grants a mount wearing it a +5 competence bonus on Fly checks, and grants the rider a +5 competence bonus on Ride checks. If the rider is unsaddled during flight, he is immediately subject to a feather fall effect. The mount additionally gains the benefits of any teamwork feats the rider possesses. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fly, mount, creator must have 5 ranks in Ride; Cost 7,000 gp
