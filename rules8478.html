Activate Magic Items Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 64 This use of Spellcraft functions as the Use Magic Device core skill, but uses Intelligence instead of Charisma. Action: None. Try Again: Yes, but if you ever roll a natural 1 while attempting to activate an item and you fail, you can't try to activate that item again for 24 hours.
