Miscellaneous - Equipment Riding kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 47 Price 16 gp (common), 36 gp (exotic mount); Weight 54 lbs. (common), 59 lbs. (exotic mount) Description This kit includes a bit and bridle, a saddle, a saddle blanket, saddlebags, and 2 days' worth of feed for a mount. The weight can be lightened 10 pounds by discarding the feed.
