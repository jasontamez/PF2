Staff of Internal Assault Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 31 Aura moderate varies CL 8th Slot none; Price 20,800 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description Crumbling bark continuously flakes from this solid, aged oak staff without diminishing its size. It allows the use of the following spells:
  • Calcific touch (2 charges)
  • Corrosive touch (1 charge)
  • Touch injection (2 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, calcific touch, corrosive touch, touch injection; Cost 10,400 gp
