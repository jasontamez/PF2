Staff of Bolstering - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Bolstering Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 195, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 296 Aura moderate transmutation CL 8th Slot none; Price 41,600 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description The top of this rosewood shaft narrows and then expands again, bound by a number of metal rings. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Bear's endurance (1 charge)
  • Bull's strength (1 charge)
  • Cat's grace (1 charge)
  • Greater magic weapon (1 charge)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, bear's endurance, bull's strength, cat's grace, greater magic weapon; Cost 20,800 gp
