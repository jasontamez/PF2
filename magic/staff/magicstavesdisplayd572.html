Staff of Hunting Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 31 Aura strong varies CL 13th Slot none; Price 51,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This length of aged ivory is carved with numerous hunting scenes which seem to animate and tell a story when the staff is rotated. It allows the use of the following spells:
  • Acute senses (1 charge)
  • Bard's escape (4 charges)
  • Locate creature (2 charges)
  • Unwilling shield (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, acute senses, bard's escape, locate creature, unwilling shield; Cost 25,500 gp
