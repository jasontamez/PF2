Staff of Transmutation - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Transmutation Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 202, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 495 Aura strong transmutation CL 13th Slot none; Price 82,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This staff is generally carved from or decorated with petrified wood or fossilized bone that has been etched with tiny but complex runes. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Alter self (1 charge)
  • Blink (1 charge)
  • Expeditious retreat (1 charge)
  • Baleful polymorph (2 charges)
  • Polymorph (2 charges)
  • Disintegrate (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, alter self, baleful polymorph, blink, disintegrate, expeditious retreat, polymorph; Cost 41,000 gp
