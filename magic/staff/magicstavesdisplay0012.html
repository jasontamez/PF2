Staff of Illumination - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Illumination Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 197, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 494 Aura strong evocation CL 15th Slot none; Price 51,500 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This staff is usually sheathed in a layer of silver and decorated with gold and amber sunbursts. A staff of illumination allows use of the following spells:
  • Dancing lights (1 charge)
  • Flare (1 charge)
  • Daylight (2 charges)
  • Sunburst (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, dancing lights, daylight, flare, sunburst; Cost 25,750 gp
