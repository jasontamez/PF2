Staff of Aspects - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Aspects Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 194 Aura moderate transmutation CL 9th Slot none; Price 37,600 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This stout staff is made of ash. Four faces of animals-a bear, a falcon, a stag, and a wolf-are carved at its top. The aspect staff functions as a +1/+1 quarterstaff and allows the use of the following spells:
  • Aspect of the bear (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
  • Aspect of the falcon (1 charge; Advanced Player's Guide)
  • Aspect of the stag (2 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
  • Aspect of the wolf (3 charges; Advanced Player's Guide)
Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Craft Staff, aspect of the bear, aspect of the falcon, aspect of the stag, aspect of the wolf; Cost 19,100 gp
