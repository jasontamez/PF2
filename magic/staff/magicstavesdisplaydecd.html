Staff of Obstacles - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Obstacles Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 199, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 297 Aura moderate varies CL 9th Slot none; Price 52,850 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description Dark and solid, this worn staff appears to be carved of rough stone, yet feels and weighs like light wood. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Arcane lock (1 charge)
  • Grease (1 charge)
  • Wind wall (1 charge)
  • Wall of fire (2 charges)
  • Wall of ice (2 charges)
  • Wall of stone (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, arcane lock, grease, wall of fire, wall of ice, wall of stone, wind wall; Cost 27,050 gp
