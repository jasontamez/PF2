Staff of Size Alteration - Staves - Magic Equipment Staff of Size Alteration Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 200, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 495 Aura moderate transmutation CL 8th Slot none; Price 26,150 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This staff of dark wood is stouter and sturdier than most magical staves, with a gnarled and twisted knot of wood at the top end. It allows use of the following spells:
  • Enlarge person (1 charge)
  • Reduce person (1 charge)
  • Shrink item (2 charges)
  • Mass enlarge person (3 charges)
  • Mass reduce person (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, enlarge person, mass enlarge person, mass reduce person, reduce person, shrink item; Cost 13,075 gp
