Defending - Weapons - Magic Equipment Defending Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 139, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 469 Aura moderate abjuration CL 8th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A defending weapon allows the wielder to transfer some or all of the weapon's enhancement bonus to his AC as a bonus that stacks with all others. As a free action, the wielder chooses how to allocate the weapon's enhancement bonus at the start of his turn before using the weapon, and the bonus to AC lasts until his next turn. This ability can only be placed on melee weapons. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shield or shield of faith; Price —
