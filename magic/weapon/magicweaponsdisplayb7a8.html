Disarming Blade - Weapons - Magic Equipment Disarming Blade Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 153 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Price 17,820 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This +1 dueling rapier ends duels quickly and decisively by depriving the wielder's opponent of her weapon. Following a successful disarm attempt with the weapon, the wielder can move the disarmed weapon 5 feet in any direction as a swift action. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cat's grace, mage hand; Price 9,070 gp
