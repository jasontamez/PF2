Cutlass of Waves - Weapons - Magic Equipment Cutlass of Waves Source Villain Codex pg. 189 Aura moderate transmutation CL 6th Slot none; Price 9,615 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This +1 cutlass is constantly soaked with brine. The wielder's attacks with a cutlass of waves don't take penalties from being underwater, as if the cutlass were a piercing weapon. While a creature holds the cutlass in its hand, a low-cresting wave of water carries the cutlass's holder along the deck of a ship or through the water, otherwise as per slipstream. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, slipstream; Price 4,650 gp
