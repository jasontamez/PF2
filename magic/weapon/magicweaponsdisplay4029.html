Blade of Three Fancies - Weapons - Magic Equipment Blade of Three Fancies Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 254 Aura faint illusion CL 3rd Slot none; Price 5,248 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description Three colorful silk ribbons tied at the base of this elegant, ornately chased +1 glaive flutter gracefully with every movement and never seem to get dirty. The glaive grants a +4 sacred bonus on one Perform skill (chosen when the weapon is created). The wielder retains this bonus so long as the weapon remains within 10 feet, which allows two-handed instruments to be played. Additionally, once per day the wielder may use color spray. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, color spray, eagle's splendor; Price 2,778 gp
