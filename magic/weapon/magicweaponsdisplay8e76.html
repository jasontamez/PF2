Glamered - Weapons - Magic Equipment Glamered Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 142 Aura moderate illusion CL 10th Slot weapon quality; Price +4,000 gp; Weight — Description A glamered weapon can be commanded to change its shape and appearance to assume the form of another object of similar size. The weapon retains all its properties (including weight) when so disguised but does not radiate magic. Only true seeing or similar magic reveals the true nature of a glamered weapon while it is disguised. After a glamered weapon is used to attack, this special ability is suppressed for 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, disguise self, magic aura; Price +2,000 gp
