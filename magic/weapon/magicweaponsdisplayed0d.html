Mighty Cleaving - Weapons - Magic Equipment Mighty Cleaving Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 145, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 469 Aura moderate evocation CL 8th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. A mighty cleaving weapon allows a wielder using the Cleave feat to make one additional attack if the first attack hits, as long as the next foe is adjacent to the first and also within reach. This additional attack cannot be against the first foe. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, divine power; Price —
