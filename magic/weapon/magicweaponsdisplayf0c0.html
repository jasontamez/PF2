Pistol of the Infinite Sky - Weapons - Magic Equipment Pistol of the Infinite Sky Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 158 Aura strong conjuration CL 15th Slot none; Price 73,300 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description An infinity symbol is engraved on both sides of this +5 pistol's mother of pearl grip, and the barrel is adorned with gold depicting the moon, planets, and stars against a night sky of the pistol's cold black steel. This pistol never needs to be reloaded. After one shot is fired, a bullet and powder magically appear in the chamber. This ammunition never suffers a misfire. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, reloading hands; Price 37,300 gp
