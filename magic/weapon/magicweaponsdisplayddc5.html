Gauntlets of Rending - Weapons - Magic Equipment Gauntlets of Rending Source Monster Codex pg. 225 Aura faint evocation CL 5th Slot hands; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description These Large gauntlets are bulky, and the palms and fingers are studded with wickedly serrated spikes. The wearer gains a +2 bonus on damage rolls with its claw attacks; the wearer adds this bonus damage once for each claw attack used in a rend. The crude construction and thick metal used in the gauntlets make it difficult for the wearer to perform fine manipulation. Consequently, the wearer takes a -4 penalty on all Dexterity-based checks involving her hands. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, magic weapon; Price 4,000 gp
