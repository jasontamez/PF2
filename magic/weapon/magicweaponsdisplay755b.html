Swift Obsidian Greataxe - Weapons - Magic Equipment Swift Obsidian Greataxe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 161, Advanced Race Guide pg. 58 Aura moderate transmutation CL 10th Slot none; Price 11,320 gp; Weight 12 lbs. Description The blade of this +1 keen greataxe is carved from jet-black obsidian. When the wielder charges an enemy, he receives a +10 foot enhancement bonus to his speed that round. Once per day, he can command the axe to grant all allies within 30 feet a +10 foot enhancement bonus to base speed for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, expeditious retreat, keen edge; Price 5,802 gp
