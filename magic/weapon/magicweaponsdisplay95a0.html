Biting Bracelet - Weapons - Magic Equipment Biting Bracelet Source Spymaster's Handbook pg. 30 Aura moderate illusion CL 10th Slot none; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This +1 heavy wrist launcher appears to be a lavish, gold bracelet. This functions like the glamered weapon special ability (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Equipment 142), except a biting bracelet is able to only assume this one specific form, and can revert to its innocuous appearance 1 round after being used as a weapon.
