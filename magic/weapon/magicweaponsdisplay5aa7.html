Conflagration Pike - Weapons - Magic Equipment Conflagration Pike Source Pathfinder: Hollow Mountain #1 pg. 27 Aura faint evocation CL 3rd Slot none; Price 3,110 gp; Weight 12 lbs. Description This +1 ranseur is warm to the touch. Once per day as an immediate action after successfully attacking a target with the ranseur, the wielder can command it to burst into flames, dealing an extra 2d6 points of fire damage to the target and 1d6 points of fire damage to the wielder. The fire goes out immediately afterward. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, burning hands; Price 1,710 gp
