Frostblood Axe - Weapons - Magic Equipment Frostblood Axe Source Monster Codex pg. 69 Aura moderate necromancy [evil] CL 10th Slot none; Price 40,310 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description This +1 frost wounding battleaxe sacrifices the souls of those it kills to the foul gods of the frost giants. Whenever it kills a creature with at least 1 Hit Die (including creatures killed by its bleed damage), as an immediate action the wielder can activate either divine favor on himself or bless on himself and his allies. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bleed, bless, chill metal, death knell, divine favor; Price 20,310 gp
