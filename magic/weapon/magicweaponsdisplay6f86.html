Chaos Hammer - Weapons - Magic Equipment Chaos Hammer Source Mythic Adventures pg. 144 Aura moderate evocation [chaotic] CL 9th Slot none; Price 38,312 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This oversized hammer crackles with chaotic energy. Once per day as a standard action, the wielder of this +1 anarchic warhammer can strike the ground to create the effects of the chaos hammer spell (Will DC 16, CL 9th) that bursts out from the warhammer's wielder. A mythic creature wielding this weapon can expend one use of mythic power to activate this ability without using up its daily use. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, align weapon, chaos hammer, creator must be chaotic; Price 19,312 gp
