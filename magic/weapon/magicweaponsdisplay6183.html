Frozen Crossbow - Weapons - Magic Equipment Frozen Crossbow Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 22 Aura moderate conjuration CL 8th Slot none; Price 25,700 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Description When this +2 frost repeating heavy crossbow is empty and underwater, pulling the reloading lever as a free action creates and loads a bolt of solid ice. The icicle bolt and its wielder ignore all penalties for using a ranged weapon underwater. The icicle bolt deals an amount of cold damage equal to that of a normal bolt and melts 1 round after leaving the crossbow. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, freedom of movement, icicle dagger; Price 13,200 gp
