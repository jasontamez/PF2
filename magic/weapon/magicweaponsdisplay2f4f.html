Jurist - Weapons - Magic Equipment Jurist Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 144, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 288 Aura faint transmutation CL 4th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description When an inquisitor wielder uses her judgment class ability while wielding a jurist weapon, it grants her an enhancement bonus on her Perception checks and to CMD. The bonus is +1 on the first round of her judgment, and increases by +1 each round, to a maximum of +3 on the third and following rounds.
