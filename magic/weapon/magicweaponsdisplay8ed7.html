Heartswood Spear - Weapons - Magic Equipment Heartswood Spear Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 156 Aura strong conjuration CL 14th Slot none; Price 50,302 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description This humble weapon appears to be little more than a crudely sharpened stake or makeshift spear, but in truth it is a far more formidable weapon. In addition to being a +2 undead bane spear, a heartswood spear deals bludgeoning damage when it is swung as a staff. When employed in such a manner, it also possesses the disruption property. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, heal, summon monster; Price 25,302 gp
