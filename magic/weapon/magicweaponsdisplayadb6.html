Adaptive - Weapons - Magic Equipment Adaptive Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 134 Aura faint transmutation CL 1st Slot weapon quality; Price +1,000 gp; Weight — Description This ability can only be placed on composite bows. An adaptive bow responds to the strength of its wielder, acting as a bow with a strength rating equal to its wielder's Strength bonus. The wielder can fire it with a lesser Strength bonus (and cause less damage) if desired. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, warp wood; Price +500 gp
