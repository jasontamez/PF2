Spirit Blade - Weapons - Magic Equipment Spirit Blade Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 161, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 291 Aura moderate abjuration CL 10th Slot none; Price 48,502 gp; Weight 1 lb.
