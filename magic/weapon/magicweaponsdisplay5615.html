Menacing - Weapons - Magic Equipment Menacing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 145, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 288 Aura moderate illusion CL 10th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons. A menacing weapon helps allies deal with flanked foes. When the wielder is adjacent to a creature that is being flanked by an ally, the flanking bonus on attack rolls for all flanking allies increases by +2. This ability works even if the wielder is not one of the characters flanking the creature. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, phantasmal killer; Price —
