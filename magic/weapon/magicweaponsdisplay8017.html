Mokele-Mbembe Tail Whip - Weapons - Magic Equipment Mokele-Mbembe Tail Whip Source Mystery Monsters Revisited pg. 25 Aura moderate evocation CL 9th Slot none; Price 32,301 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This item is crafted from tightly braided rawhide from a water buffalo skin, and is usually awarded to a prominent member of a tribe. The mokele-mbembe tail whip functions as a +2 whip, but deals an additional 1d6 points of sonic damage on a successful hit. In addition, anyone hit by a critical hit with the whip must succeed at a DC 21 Fortitude save or be stunned for 1 round. A successful critical hit with the whip does not multiply the weapon's sonic damage. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shout; Price 16,301 gp
