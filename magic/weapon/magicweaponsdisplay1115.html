Shatterstone Hammer - Weapons - Magic Equipment Shatterstone Hammer Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 29 Aura strong transmutation CL 7th Slot none; Price 26,712 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description The head of this +1 warhammer is made from magically treated stone that is as hard as metal. A shatterstone hammer deals double damage against unattended earthen and stone objects, and gains the benefit of being a bane weapon when used against creatures of the earth subtype and constructs made of clay or stone. Once per day on a successful attack made with the hammer, the wielder can cause the target to be slowed (as the slow spell). A DC 14 Will save negates this effect. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shatter, slow; Price 13,512 gp
