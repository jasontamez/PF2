Lacerating Rapier - Weapons - Magic Equipment Lacerating Rapier Source Magical Marketplace pg. 27 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Price 10,302 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description The blade of this +1 rapier is imbued with the ability to change form slightly when used to sneak attack foes, allowing the wielder to hit multiple targets. Three times per day when the wielder makes a sneak attack against a foe that is adjacent to another creature, she may choose to attack both opponents with a single strike as the blade unwinds, lengthens, and bends to meet their flesh. The wielder uses the same attack roll result to hit both creatures. If successful, the attack deals weapon damage to any creature it hits, though only the first creature takes sneak attack damage. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, greater magic weapon, mirror strike; Price 5,302 gp
