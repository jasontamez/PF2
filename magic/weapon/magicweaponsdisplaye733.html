Dagger of Venom - Weapons - Magic Equipment Dagger of Venom Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 153, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 471 Aura faint necromancy CL 5th Slot none; Price 8,302 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This black +1 dagger has a serrated edge. It allows the wielder to use a poison effect (as the spell, save DC 14) upon a creature struck by the blade once per day. The wielder can decide to use the power after he has struck. Doing so is a free action, but the poison effect must be invoked in the same round that the dagger strikes. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, poison; Price 4,302 gp
