Dispelling - Weapons - Magic Equipment Dispelling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 140 Aura strong abjuration CL 10th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A dispelling weapon functions like a spell storing weapon, but it may only store dispel magic; however, the caster level check to dispel gains an additional bonus equal to the weapon's enhancement bonus. This bonus also applies to a magus's dispelling strike arcana or a barbarian's spell sunder or sunder enchantment combat maneuver check.
