Hearthhammer - Weapons - Magic Equipment Hearthhammer Source Tombs of Golarion pg. 29 Aura moderate (Nethys: school unlisted in source) CL 9th Slot none; Price 30,000 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description The stone handle of this +3 adamantine warhammer is carved in the form of a stately dwarven woman. The wielder of Hearthhammer gains a +5 competence bonus on Craft (armor, jewelry, stonemasonry, traps, and weapon) checks. If the wielder is a humanoid with the dwarf subtype, this bonus increases to +10. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, fabricate; Price 16,506 gp
