Rapier of Battlefield Movement - Weapons - Magic Equipment Rapier of Battlefield Movement Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 216 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot none; Price 30,320 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This +2 rapier has elegant lines and a stylish basket hilt. The wielder of this weapon gains a +5 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks made to move through an enemy's threatened area or through its space. As a swift action after hitting with a melee attack using this weapon, the wielder can spend 1 panache point to move 10 feet without provoking attacks of opportunity. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, freedom of movement, creator must have 10 ranks in Craft (weapons); Price 15,350 gp
