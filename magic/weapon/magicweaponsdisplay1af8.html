The Brazen Claw - Weapons - Magic Equipment The Brazen Claw Source Dark Markets - A Guide to Katapesh pg. 17 Aura moderate transmutation CL 9th Slot weapon; Price 18,315 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This jagged piece of curved brass with a razor-sharp edge functions as a +2 vicious scimitar. If a gnoll wields the brazen claw, he takes no damage from the sword's vicious ability. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, enervation, magic weapon, creator must be a gnoll; Price 9,158 gp, 733 XP
