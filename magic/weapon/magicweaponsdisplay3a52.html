Miserable - Weapons - Magic Equipment Miserable Source Agents of Evil pg. 26 Aura moderate conjuration CL 8th Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A miserable weapon cuts through the energy resistance of certain foes. Miserable weapons must be crafted with a designated creature type (just like bane weapons), and each time they deal damage to their designated foes, their magic halves the creatures' natural energy resistances for 1 round.
