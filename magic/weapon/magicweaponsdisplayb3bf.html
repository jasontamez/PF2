Talmandor Blade - Weapons - Magic Equipment Talmandor Blade Source Lost Treasures pg. 56 Aura moderate abjuration, conjuration, and evocation CL 7th Slot none; Price 58,615 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This +2 holy mithral longsword continually sheds light as the spell. While wielded, the Talmandor Blade grants the wielder low-light vision (or darkvision 60 feet, if the wielder already has low-light vision) and a +4 bonus on Perception checks. Additionally, the sword grants a +4 sacred bonus on saving throws against paralysis and petrification spells and effects. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, darkvision, holy smite, stone to flesh, creator must be good; Price 29,465 gp
