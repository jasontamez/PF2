Dwarfbond Hammer - Weapons - Magic Equipment Dwarfbond Hammer Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 154 Aura moderate evocation CL 7th Slot none; Price 25,312 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description In the hands of a dwarf, this +1 warhammer functions as a +2 returning warhammer. It can be hurled with a 20-foot range increment. When hurled, a dwarfbond hammer deals an extra 1d8 points of damage against creatures of the giant subtype. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, creator must be a dwarf of at least 7th level; Price 12,812 gp
