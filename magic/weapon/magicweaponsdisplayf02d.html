Penetrating - Weapons - Magic Equipment Penetrating Source Magical Marketplace pg. 23 Aura moderate evocation CL 9th Slot none; Price +2 bonus; Weight — Description This weapon special ability can only be placed on ammunition. A creature struck by a piece of penetrating ammunition must succeed at a DC 17 Fortitude save or the ammunition proceeds to travel into another creature in line. The attacker must be able to trace a line starting at her space and passing through both targets to make this additional attack.
