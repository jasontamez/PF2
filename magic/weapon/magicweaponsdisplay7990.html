Heretical Source Quests and Campaigns pg. 30 Aura moderate evocation CL 9th Slot none; Price +2 bonus; Weight — Description A heretical weapon functions as a fervent weapon, but when wielded against a member of the religion to which it is attuned, the weapon's enhancement bonus is +2 better than its actual bonus. It also deals an extra 2d6 points of damage against followers of the designated religion. This extra damage is not multiplied on a critical hit.
