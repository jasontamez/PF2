Undercutting Axe - Weapons - Magic Equipment Undercutting Axe Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 163, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 291 Aura moderate necromancy CL 9th Slot none; Price 23,310 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description This +1 giant-bane battleaxe feels unusually heavy, as if it were made from something far heavier than mere wood and steel. Once per day its wearer can, as a swift action after a successful attack roll, invoke a maximized ray of enfeeblement on the creature struck (11 point Strength penalty, 7 rounds, Fortitude DC 16 half). Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Maximize Spell, ray of enfeeblement, summon monster I; Price 11,810 gp
