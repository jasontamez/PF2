Launcher of Distrarction - Weapons - Magic Equipment Launcher of Distrarction Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 240 Aura moderate illusion CL 6th Slot none; Price 10,550 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description Twice per day, as a swift action when the wielder of this +1 heavy wrist launcher makes an attack with the weapon, he can have the launcher make no sound, and can instead create a false sound of a crossbow being fired from another location within 40 feet of him. In addition, for 6 rounds, any auras upon the wielder that would normally be revealed by divination spells, such as detect spells, are redirected to the same location as the crossbow sound. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, ghost sound, misdirection; Price 5,550 gp
