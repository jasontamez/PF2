Red Crusader's Quill - Weapons - Magic Equipment Red Crusader's Quill Source Pathfinder #141: Last Watch pg. 32 Aura moderate necromancy and transmutation CL 10th Slot none; Price 25,320 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description An artful rapier with a lotus petal guard, this magnificent weapon dedicated to Arazni's former incarnation is useful to warriors and artists alike. The tip of this narrow +3 rapier can be used as a mundane writing instrument, producing any color of ink the wielder desires. Once per day as a free action when the wielder confirms a critical hit against a creature with the Red Crusader's quill, she can inscribe an arcane mark on the creature. Once per month, the wielder can instead inscribe a mark of justice; this mark ends immediately if the wielder inscribes another mark of justice with the Red Crusader's quill. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, arcane mark, mark of justice, creator must worship Arazni; Price 12,820 gp
