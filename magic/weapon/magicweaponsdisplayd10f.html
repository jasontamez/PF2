Swashbuckler's Rapier - Weapons - Magic Equipment Swashbuckler's Rapier Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 216 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot none; Price 7,320 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This +1 rapier has the nondescript, clean lines of a practice foil. The wielder can spend 1 panache point as a swift action to give the rapier the bane special ability for 1 round, and can pick any subtype of humanoids as the designated foe. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, magic weapon, creator must have 5 ranks in Craft (weapons); Price 3,820 gp
