Azlanti Orrery - Artifacts - Magic Equipment Azlanti Orrery Source Pathfinder #122: Into the Shattered Continent pg. 52 Aura moderate enchantment and transmutation CL 15th Slot none; Price —; Weight 1,750 lbs. Description This immense brass, bronze, and crystal orrery models Golarion's solar system as it was in the Age of Legend. If consulted for 10 minutes or more, the orrery grants a +5 circumstance bonus on Knowledge (geography) checks to navigate or to answer questions about outer space. Once per month, it can be used to contact alien minds on far-flung worlds-this works identically to the spell contact other plane (use the demigod line to determine the effects of the spell or ability score decreases). Destruction Although it is a minor artifact, the Azlanti orrery can be destroyed by regular damage. It has hardness 20 and 300 hit points.
