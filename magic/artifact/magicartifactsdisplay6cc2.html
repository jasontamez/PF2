Usher of the Black Rain - Artifacts - Magic Equipment Usher of the Black Rain Source Bestiary 6 pg. 161 Aura overwhelming all CL 29th Slot none; Price —; Weight 12 lbs. Description Apollyon's weapon is the Usher of the Black Rain. He can cause this weapon to shift from a scythe to a composite longbow (Str +16) as a move action, but in both forms the Usher is a +5 unholy wounding weapon. As a longbow, it creates ammunition automatically as it is fired. Against foes who are currently suffering from a disease, the Usher of the Black Rain deals an additional 6 points of profane damage with each hit. Destruction The Usher of the Black Rain burns to ash if Apollyon's cloak (which was stitched from the flesh of the angels whose souls empower his bow) is brought to Nirvana, washed clean there, and then buried while intoning each dead angel's long-forgotten name.
