Man Mountain Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Man Mountain Armor Source The Harrow Handbook pg. 31 Aura strong transmutation CL 15th Slot armor; Price 32,350 gp; Weight 75 lbs. Description Three times per day, as an immediate action when struck by a melee attack while he is touching the ground, the wearer of this +1 titanic stoneplate can cause the earth around him to shake, turning the area into difficult terrain. The radius of this effect is equal to 1d4 x 5 feet, and any creature in the affected area must succeed at a DC 15 Reflex save or fall prone. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, earthquake; Price 17,150 gp
