Gunner's Buckler - Armor - Magic Equipment Gunner's Buckler Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 31 Aura faint abjuration CL 3rd Slot shield; Price 10,155 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description The top edge of this +1 mithral buckler features a notched gun rest. A character wearing a gunner's buckler who uses a firearm while threatened by an opponent in melee does not provoke attacks of opportunity, but takes a -4 penalty on all ranged attack rolls in any round in which he makes a ranged attack while threatened in melee. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cat's grace, shield; Price 5,155 gp
