Invincible Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Invincible Armor Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 127 Aura strong abjuration CL 13th Slot armor; Price 137,650 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description Invincible armor shifts between three different appearances, but always weighs the same and protects as +2 impervious moderate fortification full plate. As an immediate action, the wearer can change the armor into heavy plate that blocks slashing attacks (DR 10/bludgeoning or piercing), thickly layered chainmail that blocks piercing attacks (DR 10/ bludgeoning or slashing), or overlapping metal scales that block bludgeoning attacks (DR 10/piercing or slashing). In addition, invincible armor provides the wearer with 5 points of resistance to acid, cold, electricity, fire, and sonic, and is itself unharmed by damage from any of these energy types. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, fabricate, make whole, resist energy, and limited wish or miracle; Price 69,650 gp
