Jarring - Armor - Magic Equipment Jarring Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 210 Aura moderate evocation CL 6th Slot none; Price +2 bonus; Weight — Description A shield or set of armor with this ability causes unpleasant resonant vibrations in weapons that strike its wearer. As an immediate action up to three times per day, when the wearer is hit by a melee attack made with a weapon, he can activate the armor or shield to send jarring vibrations back through the weapon. The attacker must succeed at a DC 16 Fortitude saving throw or become dazed for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shatter; Price +2 bonus
