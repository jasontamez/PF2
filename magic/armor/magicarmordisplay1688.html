Spiritwalk Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Spiritwalk Armor Source Mythic Adventures pg. 142 Aura strong illusion and transmutation CL 11th Slot armor; Price 25,160 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +2 improved shadow leather armor is attractive, yet eerie. It grants its wearer the ability to use vanish as a quickened spell-like ability five times per day. If a mythic wearer uses the vanish ability, he can expend one use of mythic power to also make himself incorporeal for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Mythic Crafter, vanish, ethereal jaunt; Price 12,660 gp
