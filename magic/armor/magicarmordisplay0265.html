Tempest Shield - Armor - Magic Equipment Tempest Shield Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 133 Aura moderate evocation CL 6th Slot shield; Price 15,170 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description The face of this +1 bashing heavy steel shield swirls with the image of swirling clouds, driving rain, or falling snow. When used to make a shield bash attack, the shield's wielder can make a bull rush maneuver as a free action against her target without provoking an attack of opportunity from the creature being bull rushed. Once per day on command, the wielder can cause the tempest shield to emit a s tream of high-powered air as the gust of wind spell. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength, gust of wind; Price 8,170 gp
