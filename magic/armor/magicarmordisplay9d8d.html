Phoenix Armor Source Champions of Purity pg. 31 Aura moderate evocation and transmutation CL 9th Slot armor; Price 36,130 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description This brilliant golden +2 fire resistant full plate is decorated with an ornate feather motif curling across its surface and seems to radiate warmth. Once per day, the wearer can invoke the name of the phoenix that blessed the armor and bring the might of that phoenix against the wearer's foe. Activating the armor causes it to ignite, wreathing the wearer in red-gold flames as if under the effects of fire shield. In addition, great golden wings unfold out of the back of the armor, allowing the wearer to fly as per the spell. Both of these effects last for 9 rounds. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, fire shield, fly; Price 18,890 gp
