Mind Buttressing - Armor - Magic Equipment Mind Buttressing Source Magical Marketplace pg. 7 Aura faint abjuration CL 12th Slot armor; Price +2 bonus; Weight — Description Mind buttressing armor grants the wearer a +2 resistance bonus on Will saves and renders her immune to possession and mental control (including charm and compulsion effects like command and charm person). If it's donned by a creature already under the influence of such an effect, the creature immediately receives another saving throw (if one was allowed to begin with) against the spell or effect. If the check is successful, the effects are suppressed until the creature removes the armor, after which they resume. This ability can be applied only to medium or heavy armor. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, protection from chaos, evil, good, or law; Price +2 bonus
