Lupine Rageskin - Armor - Magic Equipment Lupine Rageskin Source Horror Adventures pg. 214 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor; Price 12,160 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 leather armor consists of wolf skins sewn together with sinew. When the wearer rages, he automatically turns into a Medium wolf, as if using change shape (beast shape I). The armor melds with his new form as normal, but the armor's enhancement bonus changes into a +1 enhancement bonus to her natural armor. When the rage ends, the wearer reverts to his own form. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, beast shape I; Price 6,160 gp
