Stalwart Breastplate - Armor - Magic Equipment Stalwart Breastplate Source Mythic Adventures pg. 143 Aura strong abjuration CL 13th Slot armor; Price 22,350 gp; Weight 40 lbs. Description The burden of this weighty +1 light fortification breastplate gives it an armor check penalty of -5. As an immediate action, the wearer can negate the effects of a successful combat maneuver made to bull rush, overrun, or trip her. As a swift action, the wearer can expend one use of mythic power to gain immunity to ability damage, ability drain, critical hits, and sneak attacks. This effect lasts for 1 round per mythic tier the wearer possesses. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Mythic Crafter, iron body, either limited wish or miracle; Price 11,350 gp
