Enchanted Eelskin - Armor - Magic Equipment Enchanted Eelskin Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 126 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor; Price 11,160 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 slick leather armor is made of shining, slippery eelskin that allows the wearer to move with deadly grace and sudden swiftness, especially underwater. When immersed in water, enchanted eelskin gains the shadow property and grants the wearer a swim speed of 30 feet and the ability to breathe water. In addition, during a surprise round the wearer can act as if she had a full round of actions, rather than a single standard action, as long as those actions do not require leaving the water. If she does leave the water, her surprise round turn immediately ends. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, grease, invisibility, touch of the sea (Advanced Player's Guide); Price 5,660 gp
