Bashing - Armor - Magic Equipment Bashing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 115, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 463 Aura moderate transmutation CL 8th Slot shield quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A shield with this special ability is designed to perform a shield bash. A bashing shield deals damage as if it were a weapon of two size categories larger (a Medium light shield thus deals 1d6 points of damage and a Medium heavy shield deals 1d8 points of damage). The shield acts as a +1 weapon when used to bash. Only light and heavy shields can have this ability. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength; Price +1 bonus
