Shield of the Mazeborn - Armor - Magic Equipment Shield of the Mazeborn Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 23 Aura faint divination and enchantment CL 5th Slot shield; Price 18,170 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 bashing heavy steel shield resembles a bull's head with a complex, maze-like rune inscribed on the creature's forehead. In addition to the shield's bashing ability, the shield allows the wielder to use know direction at will and find the path once per week. As long as the shield is equipped, it also grants the wearer immunity to the maze spell. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength, find the path, know direction; Price 9,170 gp
