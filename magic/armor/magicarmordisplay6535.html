Emissary's Breastplate - Armor - Magic Equipment Emissary's Breastplate Source Heroes of the Streets pg. 15 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor; Price 11,350 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Description When not being worn, this simple steel +1 breastplate appears to be typical magic armor. When worn by a cavalier with the banner class feature, an emissary's breastplate automatically displays the cavalier's heraldry and counts as his banner. As long as the cavalier is not in disguise, he is considered to be displaying his banner while wearing the armor. Additionally, the wearer can expend a use of his challenge ability to speak on behalf of himself, his lord, or other agent who has given him the authority to do so. This allows him to use tongues (as per the spell) and issue a single command (as per the spell). Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, arcane mark, command, tongues, creator must have 5 ranks in Knowledge (nobility); Price 5,850 gp
