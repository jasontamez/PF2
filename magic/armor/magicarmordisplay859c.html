Energy Resistance - Armor - Magic Equipment Energy Resistance Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 117, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 463 Aura faint abjuration CL 3rd Slot armor/shield quality; Price +18,000 gp; Weight — Description A suit of armor or a shield with this special ability protects against one type of energy (acid, cold, electricity, fire, or sonic) and is designed with patterns depicting the element it protects against. The armor absorbs the first 10 points of energy damage per attack that the wearer would normally take (similar to the resist energy spell).
