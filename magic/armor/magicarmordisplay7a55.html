Milanite Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Milanite Armor Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 252 Aura faint conjuration CL 3rd Slot armor; Price 2,780 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 leather armor usually looks like heavy protective work clothing, such as the garb of a professional blacksmith, butcher, or woodcutter. Once per day when an adjacent ally falls unconscious, the wearer can use cure light wounds on that ally as a swift action; if the wearer falls unconscious and this ability has not already been expended for the day, it automatically triggers on the wearer. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cure light wounds; Price 1,470 gp
