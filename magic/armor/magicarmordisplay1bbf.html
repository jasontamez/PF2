Avalanche Shield - Armor - Magic Equipment Avalanche Shield Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 130 Aura strong evocation CL 12th Slot shield; Price 19,170 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description The front of this +1 bashing heavy steel shield looks as though it is crafted from hundreds of bits of rubble and larger stones. When used to make a shield bash attack, stones fall from shield and cling to the target of the shield bash. The target must succeed at a DC 14 Fortitude saving throw or be entangled for 1d4 rounds. Once per day on command, the target's wielder can choose to petrify the target of a shield bash instead. The target must succeed at a DC 16 Fortitude saving throw or become petrified for 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength, shape stone, flesh to stone; Price 9,670 gp
