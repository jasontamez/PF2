Daystar Half-Plate - Armor - Magic Equipment Daystar Half-Plate Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 125, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 284 Aura strong evocation CL 15th Slot armor; Price 81,250 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description This glowing +1 half-plate is decorated with various images of the sun and depictions of nature. At will, the wearer can command the armor to shine with a daylight spell for 10 minutes. Once per day, the wearer can command it to shine as per the sunburst spell. The sunburst is centered on the wearer and the wearer is immune to its effects. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, daylight, sunburst; Price 41,000 gp
