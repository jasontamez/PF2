Grinding - Armor - Magic Equipment Grinding Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 119 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor/shield quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A shield or suit of armor with this special ability is covered with jagged burrs and razor-sharp serrations that saw and grind whenever the wearer is grappled or entangled. Any creature succeeding at a grapple combat maneuver check against the wearer takes damage equal to the armor or shield's enhancement bonus (with each successful check) as the barbs and blades bite into it. This special ability counts as armor spikes for the purpose of making attacks on the wearer's turn. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, keen edge; Price +1 bonus
