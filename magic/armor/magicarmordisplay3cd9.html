Storval's Fang - Armor - Magic Equipment Storval's Fang Source Disciple's Doctrine pg. 30 Aura moderate transmutation CL 6th Slot none; Price 38,312 gp; Weight 6 lbs. Description This metal +1 klar is shaped like the head of a storm roc, whose sharp feathers serve as the klar's spikes, and it hums and crackles like a distant stormy sky. When used to make a shield bash attack, a Storval's fang deals an additional 1d6 points of sonic damage. In addition, the wielder gains electricity resistance 5. When the wielder confirms a critical hit with a Storval's fang, it unleashes a thunderclap, deafening the target of the critical hit for 1 minute unless that target succeeds at a DC 16 Fortitude saving throw. If the wielder of a Storval's fang also wields a Storval's thunder and uses the Thunder and Fang feat, each weapon deals an additional 1d6 points of electricity damage, the DCs to negate their critical hit effects increase by 2, and a creature that fails such a saving throw is also pushed back 10 feet and knocked prone. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, lightning bolt, resist energy, sound burst, creator must be of Shoanti descent; Price 19,312 gp
