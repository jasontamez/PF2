Collapsible Tower - Armor - Magic Equipment Collapsible Tower Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 131 Aura moderate transmutation CL 6th Slot shield; Price 8,170 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description Three times per day, this +2 heavy steel shield can be commanded to transform into a +2 tower shield. It can stay in tower shield form for 1 minute or until it is commanded to take its normal form again (a swift action). A collapsible tower's weight does not change when it takes tower shield form. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, shrink item; Price 4,170 gp
