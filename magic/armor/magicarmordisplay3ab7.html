Murderer's Blackcloth - Armor - Magic Equipment Murderer's Blackcloth Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 128, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 285 Aura moderate necromancy CL 9th Slot armor; Price 12,405 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This silk +1 shadow padded armor is as dark as night and stitched with thread the color of dried blood. When worn by a rogue with the bleed talent, it increases the bleed damage of the rogue's sneak attacks by +1. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bleed, invisibility, silence; Price 6,280 gp
