Plate Armor of the Deep - Armor - Magic Equipment Plate Armor of the Deep Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 128, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 464 Aura moderate abjuration CL 11th Slot armor; Price 24,650 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description This +1 full plate is decorated with a wave and fish motif and often has tiny fins and scales worked into the design. Although the armor remains as heavy and bulky as normal full plate, the wearer of plate armor of the deep is treated as unarmored for purposes of Swim checks. The wearer can breathe underwater and can converse with any waterbreathing creature with a language. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, freedom of movement, tongues, water breathing; Price 13,150 gp
