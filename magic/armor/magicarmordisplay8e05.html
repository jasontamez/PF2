Undead Controlling - Armor - Magic Equipment Undead Controlling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 123, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 463 Aura strong necromancy CL 13th Slot armor/shield quality; Price +49,000 gp; Weight — Description Undead controlling armor and shields often have skeletal or other grisly decorations or flourishes to their decor. They let the user control up to 26 Hit Dice of undead per day, as the control undead spell. At dawn each day, the wearer loses control of any undead still under his sway. Armor or a shield with this ability appears to be made of bone; this feature is entirely decorative and has no other effect on the armor. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, control undead; Price +24,500 gp
