Lens of the Dark Tapestry - Armor - Magic Equipment Lens of the Dark Tapestry Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 31 Aura moderate evocation CL 7th Slot shield; Price 29,170 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Description The matte surface of this +1 voidglass heavy shield is cold to the touch. The shield's face contains a disorienting field of flickering stars. On command, the wearer of this shield can take 1 point of Wisdom drain to banish a single creature within 30 feet into the Dark Tapestry. The target of this ability must succeed at a DC 16 Will save or be teleported into the airless void of space. The target can attempt a new saving throw as a full-round action each round to return. While banished in this way, the target takes 3d6 points of cold damage per round and must hold its breath or begin to suffocate. If the Wisdom damage is negated or does not occur, the ability does not function. If the Wisdom drain is healed, any banished creature returns, and the shield becomes permanently nonmagical. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, teleport; Price 15,670 gp
