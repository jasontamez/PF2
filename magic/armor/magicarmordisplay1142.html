Celestial Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Celestial Armor Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 125, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 464 Aura faint transmutation [good] CL 5th Slot armor; Price 22,400 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Description This +3 chainmail is so fine and light that it can be worn under normal clothing without betraying its presence. It has a maximum Dexterity bonus of +8, an armor check penalty of -2, and an arcane spell failure chance of 15%. It is considered light armor and allows the wearer to use fly on command (as the spell) once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, fly, creator must be good; Price 11,350 gp
