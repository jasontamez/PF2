Brawling - Armor - Magic Equipment Brawling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 116 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor quality; Price +3 bonus; Weight — Description The wearer of brawling armor gains a +2 bonus on unarmed attack and damage rolls, including combat maneuver checks made to grapple. Her unarmed strikes count as magic weapons for the purpose of bypassing damage reduction. These bonuses do not apply to natural weapons. This special ability does not prevent the wearer's unarmed strikes from provoking attacks of opportunity or make the wearer's unarmed strikes count as armed attacks. The brawling ability can be applied only to light armor. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength; Price +3 bonus
