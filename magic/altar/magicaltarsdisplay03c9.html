Altar of Erastil - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Erastil Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 247 Aura moderate abjuration, conjuration, and divination [good, lawful] CL 10th Slot none; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1,000 lbs. Description This altar is a large stone hearth that is normally kept blazing and features a prominent image of Erastil wielding his sacred bow, using the holy weapon either in defense of a home or to hunt game for food. Praying at the altar grants a +2 circumstance bonus on Handle Animal and Heal checks, or one of the following magic weapon special abilities (worshiper's choice) to one magic weapon carried or wielded by the worshiper: animal-bane, conserving, huntsman, or merciful. This effect is suppressed if the weapon leaves the worshiper's grasp, but resumes if the weapon is returned to the worshiper. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, abundant ammunition, cure light wounds, detect animals or plants, magic circle against chaos, magic circle against evil, summon monster I, creator must worship Erastil; Cost 5,000 gp
