Altar of Cayden Cailean - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Cayden Cailean Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 246 Aura moderate abjuration [chaotic, good] CL 10th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 150 lbs. Description This altar is usually a heavy wooden box, often incorporated into the bar of a tavern or another social resting place for adventurers or revelers, and contains large stores of alcohol and the god's holy text. Praying at the altar grants a +2 resistance bonus on saving throws against fear and poison. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, magic circle against evil, magic circle against law, neutralize poison, remove fear, creator must worship Cayden Cailean; Cost 4,000 gp
