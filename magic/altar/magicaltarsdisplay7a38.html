Altar of Iomedae - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Iomedae Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 248 Aura moderate abjuration and transmutation [lawful, good] CL 10th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 1,000 lbs. Description This altar is a marble slab engraved with images of the Acts of Iomedae (see page 81), with a longsword bearing a continual flame spell mounted atop it. Praying at the altar grants a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls and saves against fear effects. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bless, magic circle against chaos, magic circle against evil, creator must worship Iomedae; Cost 4,000 gp
