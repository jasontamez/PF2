Altar of Asmodeus - Altars - Magic Equipment Altar of Asmodeus Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 246 Aura moderate abjuration and enchantment [evil, lawful] CL 10th Slot none; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 750 lbs. Description This altar is an elaborate nine-tiered iron frame built over a shallow nine-tiered pit and features many trappings of prisons and slavery. Praying at the altar grants the ability to cast hold person (DC 13) as a spell-like ability once in the next 24 hours. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, hold person, magic circle against chaos, magic circle against good, creator must worship Asmodeus; Cost 8,000 gp
