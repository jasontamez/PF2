Plague Mask - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Plague Mask Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 247 Aura faint necromancy; CL 5th Slot head; Price 7,500 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This stylized bird mask consists of a pair of goggles attached to a ceramic beak that covers the nose and mouth. The goggles are not magic and can be replaced with other goggles or eye slot items. It grants the wearer a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws against disease. Once per day on command, the user can remove disease (as the spell) on a single creature touched. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, remove disease; Cost 3,750 gp
