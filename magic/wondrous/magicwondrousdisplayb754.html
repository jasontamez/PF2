Boots of Striding and Springing - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of Striding and Springing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 228, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 503 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 5,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These boots increase the wearer's base land speed by 10 feet. In addition to this striding ability (considered an enhancement bonus), these boots allow the wearer to make great leaps. She can jump with a +5 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, longstrider, creator must have 5 ranks in the Acrobatics skill; Cost 2,750 gp
