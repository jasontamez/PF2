Cloak of Displacement (Minor) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 262, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 507 Aura faint illusion; CL 3rd Slot shoulders; Price 24,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This item appears to be a normal cloak, with a pattern very similar to a major cloak of displacement nocturnal designs, but when worn its magical properties distort and warp light waves. This displacement works similar to the blur spell, granting a 20% miss chance on attacks against the wearer. It functions continually. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, blur; Cost 12,000 gp
