Hand of Glory - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Hand of Glory Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 259, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 516 Aura faint varied; CL 5th Slot neck; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This mummified human hand hangs around a character's neck by a leather cord (taking up space as a magic necklace would). If a magic ring is placed on one of the fingers of the hand, the wearer benefits from the ring as if wearing it herself, and it does not count against her two ring limit. The hand can wear only one ring at a time. Even without a ring, the hand itself allows its wearer to use daylight and see invisibility each once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, animate dead, daylight, see invisibility; Cost 4,000 gp
