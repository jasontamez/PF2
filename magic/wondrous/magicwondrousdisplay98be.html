Bag of Tricks (Tan) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bag of Tricks Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 62 Aura faint conjuration; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 3,400 gp (Gray), 4,500 gp (Aquamarine), 8,500 gp (Rust), 16,000 gp (Tan); Weight 1 lb. Description This small sack appears empty. Anyone reaching into the bag feels a small, fuzzy ball. If the ball is removed and tossed up to 20 feet away, it turns into an animal. The animal serves the character who drew it from the bag for 10 minutes (or until slain or ordered back into the bag), at which point it disappears. It can follow any of the commands described in the Handle Animal skill. Each of the three kinds of bags of tricks produces a different set of animals.
