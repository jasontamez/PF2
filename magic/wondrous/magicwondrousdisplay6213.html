Drums of Haste Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 292 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 45,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This set of kettle drums is carried on a shoulder strap and played one-handed using a single two-sided mallet. By making a DC 20 Perform (percussion) check, the bearer can grant the effects of a haste spell on up to 5 creatures. The drums can affect up to 25 creatures per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, haste; Cost 22,500 gp
