Spectral Shroud - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Spectral Shroud Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 222 Aura moderate necromancy; CL 5th Slot chest; Price 26,000 gp; Weight — Description This thin, bleached cloth covers the wearer's entire torso. Some also cover the wearer's face, but don't interfere with the wearer's vision. The morbid burial shroud grants the wearer some affinity with the spectral dead. The wearer can discern invisible or ethereal creatures as though using see invisibility. Once per day, the wearer can become incorporeal for 10 rounds and gain a fly speed equal to half his base speed with perfect maneuverability. The wearer can't attack while incorporeal, but can dismiss the effect as a move action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, hide from undead, see invisibility; Cost 13,000 gp
