Campfire Bead - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Campfire Bead Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 286, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 302 Aura faint evocation; CL 1st Slot none; Price 720 gp; Weight — Description This tiny glass bead transforms itself into a small (2-foot-tall) pile of burning logs whenever its command word is spoken. The fire burns for 8 hours or until extinguished, at which point it turns back into a bead. The owner of the item must wait twice as long as the fire burned before he can order the bead to become a campfire again. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, produce flame; Cost 360 gp
