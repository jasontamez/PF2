Spectacles of Understanding - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Spectacles of Understanding Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 227 Aura faint divination; CL 2nd Slot eyes; Price 3,000 gp; Weight — Description When worn, these innocent-looking spectacles convert any written language to one known by the wearer, as the comprehend languages spell. The glasses are also good at detecting falsified documents, granting their wearer a +5 bonus to Linguistics checks to identify forgeries and the ability make such checks untrained. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, comprehend languages; Cost 1,500 gp
