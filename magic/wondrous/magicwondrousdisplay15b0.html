Incense of Dulled Senses - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Incense of Dulled Senses Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 30 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 10th Slot none; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description This gray incense smells of cold granite. When burned, the smoke from the incense spreads to fill a space equal to ten 10-foot cubes. This smoke remains stationary unless moved by wind. If inhaled by a creature with blindsense, scent, or tremorsense, the creature must attempt a DC 20 Fortitude save. If it fails the save, it does not benefit from its blindsense, tremorsense, or scent abilities for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes the smoke from the incense dissipates and loses all magical properties. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, blindness/deafness; Cost 750 gp
