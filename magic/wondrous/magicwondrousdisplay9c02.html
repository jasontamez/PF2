Gloves of the Keen Evaluator - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gloves of the Keen Evaluator Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 18 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot hands; Price 1,200 gp; Weight — Description While worn, these white latex gloves provide an impressive grip for studying intricate objects. The wearer gains a +4 competence bonus on Appraise checks to determine the value of an item. While involved in negotiations regarding the studied item, the wearer can use the result of her Appraise check as the result of Diplomacy checks to haggle over the price of the item. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, eagle's splendor, investigative mind; Cost 600 gp
