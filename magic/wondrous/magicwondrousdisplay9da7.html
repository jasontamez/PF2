Pipes of Sounding - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pipes of Sounding Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 315, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 526 Aura faint illusion; CL 2nd Slot none; Price 1,800 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description When played by a character who has the Perform (wind instruments) skill, these shiny metallic pan pipes create a variety of sounds that can imitate creatures, environmental sounds, or most other sounds. The figment sounds are the equivalent of ghost sound. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, ghost sound; Cost 900 gp
