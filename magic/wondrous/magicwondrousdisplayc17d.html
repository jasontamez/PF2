Sniper Goggles (Greater) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Sniper Goggles (Greater) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 227 Aura moderate divination; CL 8th Slot eyes; Price 50,000 gp; Weight — Description These goggles feature stubby periscopelike lenses that expand and retract depending on the needs of the wearer.
