Golden Eagle Epaulets - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Golden Eagle Epaulets Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 77, Andoran, Spirit of Liberty pg. 26 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot shoulders; Price 4,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These decorative golden braids allow the wearer to cast bless and sanctuary each once per day. They also grant a +2 competence bonus on Diplomacy checks and checks for one other skill, depending on what branch of the Eagle Knights the epaulets are attuned to (Perception for the Steel Falcons, Ride for the Golden Legion, Swim for the Gray Corsairs, or Stealth for the Twilight Talons). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bless, guidance, sanctuary; Cost 2,250 gp
