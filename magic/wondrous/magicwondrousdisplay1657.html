Caparison of Resistance (+4 bonus) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Caparison of Resistance Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 16 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot shoulders; Price 2,700 gp (+1 bonus), 10,800 gp (+2 bonus), 24,300 gp (+3 bonus), 43,200 gp (+4 bonus), 67,500 gp (+5 bonus); Weight 4 lbs. Description This garment is intended for a horse or similar quadruped, but it can change size and shape to fit any four-legged creature ranging from Small to Huge in size. When worn, a caparison of resistance offers the wearer magic protection in the form of a +1 to +5 resistance bonus on all saving throws (Fortitude, Reflex, and Will). This bonus on saving throws also applies to a single rider who is mounted on the creature (in the case of multiple riders, the resistance bonus is not shared at all and applies only to the mount). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, resistance, creator's caster level must be at least three times the cloak's bonus; Cost 1,350 gp (+1 bonus), 5,400 gp (+2 bonus), 12,150 gp (+3 bonus), 21,600 gp (+4 bonus), 33,750 gp (+5 bonus)
