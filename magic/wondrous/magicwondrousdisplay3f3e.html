Fervent Searcher's Gloves - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Fervent Searcher's Gloves Source Merchant's Manifest pg. 15 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 7,400 gp; Weight — Description These dark blue, silken gloves are fingerless. Twice per day as a move action, the wearer can touch the ground or any other solid surface and say the command word to visibly reveal all footprints within 20 feet. When the gloves reveal footprints in this way, the wearer gains a +5 bonus on Survival checks to follow those tracks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, residual tracking; Cost 3,700 gp
