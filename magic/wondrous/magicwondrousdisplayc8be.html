Serpent Belt - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Serpent Belt Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 213 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot belt; Price 9,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This belt's wearer gains a +4 bonus on saves against poisons and a +2 bonus on Escape Artist checks. In addition, once per day on command, the belt can be animated as a venomous snake or constrictor snake (Bestiary 255) that obeys the animator's spoken commands for up to an hour. If the animated snake is slain or moves 100 feet away from the animator, the snake reverts into belt form. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, beast form I, neutralize poison, summon nature's ally II; Cost 4,500 gp
