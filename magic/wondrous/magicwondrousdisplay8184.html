Eyes of the Eagle - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Eyes of the Eagle Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 225, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 512 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot eyes; Price 2,500 gp; Weight — Description These lenses are made of special crystal and fit over the eyes of the wearer. They grant a +5 competence bonus on Perception checks. Both lenses must be worn to gain the benefit. Wearing only one causes a character to become dizzy and stunned for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, clairaudience/clairvoyance; Cost 1,250 gp
