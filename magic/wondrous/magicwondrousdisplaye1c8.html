Steward's Iron Key - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Steward's Iron Key Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 270 Aura faint abjuration and transmutation; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 3,000 gp; Weight — Description This simple iron key looks like something a castellan or majordomo would carry on her key ring. When held in hand, the bearer can use open/close at will. Once per day the bearer can speak a command word to use either arcane lock or knock. Though Alseta's church may use more elaborate keys for important ceremonies, the goddess's humble nature means most magical keys crafted by her followers are nondescript items such as this one. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, arcane lock, knock, open/close; Cost 1,500 gp
