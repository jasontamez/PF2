Bracers of the Glib Entertainer Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 272 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot wrist; Price 7,900 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These decorative bracers are stippled with vibrant colors and have various tiny bells sewn onto their surface. While wearing them, the wearer gains a +5 competence bonus on Perform checks. Once per day on command, the user can gain the benefit of the glibness spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, glibness; Cost 3,950 gp
