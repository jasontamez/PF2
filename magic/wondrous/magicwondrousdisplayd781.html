Quickfingers Gloves - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Quickfingers Gloves Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 67 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 2,500 gp; Weight — Description These dark-colored, skin-tight gloves are typically made of either supple calfskin or silk. A wearer of these gloves with at least 1 rank in Sleight of Hand can perform such a check as a move action by taking a -10 penalty on the check rather than the normal -20. Both gloves must be worn for the magic to be effective. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cat's grace, haste; Cost 1,250 gp
