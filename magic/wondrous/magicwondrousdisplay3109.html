Dhoti of Style Mastery - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Dhoti of Style Mastery Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 107 Aura faint necromancy; CL 3rd Slot body; Price 5,400 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description When the wearer enters a stance from a style feat while wearing a dhoti of style mastery, he gains 1d8+3 temporary hit points plus an additional 1d8 temporary hit points for each feat he has that lists that style feat as a prerequisite (maximum 3d8+3 temporary hit points). These temporary hit points last as long as the wearer maintains the stance during that combat, but vanish when the combat concludes. The wearer cannot gain temporary hit points in this way more than once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, any style feat, false life; Cost 2,700 gp
