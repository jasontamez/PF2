Headband of Ponderous Recollection - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Headband of Ponderous Recollection Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 251 Aura moderate divination and transmutation; CL 8th Slot headband; Price 5,100 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This black leather headband has two small amber gemstones set so they rest on the wearer's forehead. The headband grants the wearer a +2 enhancement bonus to Intelligence. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the headband is worn. It also grants skill ranks as a headband of vast intelligence, but always grants ranks in a Knowledge skill. As a swift action three times per day, the wearer can call upon the headband to reveal the abilities and weaknesses of a creature within line of sight. The headband fills the wearer's mind with information as though the wearer had made a Knowledge check with a natural roll of a 5, adding her bonuses to the check as normal. At the start of her next turn, the wearer receives additional information as though she had made the check with a natural roll of 10. At the start of her turn after that, the wearer receives a final piece of information, treating her natural roll as 15. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, know the enemy (Ultimate Magic); Cost 2,550 gp
