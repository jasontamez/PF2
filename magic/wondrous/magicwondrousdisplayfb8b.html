Ghungroos of Entrancement - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Ghungroos of Entrancement Source Villain Codex pg. 225 Aura faint enchantment; CL 3rd Slot feet; Price 3,750 gp; Weight — Description Each of these musical anklets bears rows of tiny bells that accentuate the rhythmic elements of a trained dancer's performances and enhance an accompanying musical performance, fascinating her audiences. When using a distraction bardic performance, the wearer gains a +5 competence bonus on Perform (dance) checks; when using a fascinate bardic performance, the wearer gains a +1 competence bonus to the saving throw DC. In addition, the wearer can use Perform (dance) with a countersong bardic performance, and gains a +2 competence bonus on the Perform (dance) check. The wearer takes a -2 penalty on Stealth checks while wearing the ghungroos. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, hypnotism, creator must have bardic performance and 5 ranks in Perform (dance); Cost 1,875 gp
