Bookmark of Deception - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bookmark of Deception Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 284 Aura faint illusion; CL 5th Slot none; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description This bookmark alters the book that holds it so that it appears to be another book entirely, even upon a thorough reading. The apparent book is determined when the bookmark is crafted, and is usually an especially boring or commonplace book such as a legal or religious text. The bookmark also protects itself and the book with nondetection. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, nondetection, secret page; Cost 750 gp
