Vest of Escape - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vest of Escape Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 223, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 532 Aura faint conjuration and transmutation; CL 4th Slot chest; Price 5,200 gp; Weight — Description This simple silk vest looks nondescript, but is actually studded with numerous secret pockets sewn into its lining. Hidden inside these are magic lockpicks that provide a +4 competence bonus on Disable Device checks. The vest also grants its wearer a +6 competence bonus on Escape Artist checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, knock, grease; Cost 2,600 gp
