Laurel Wreath - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Laurel Wreath Source Mythic Adventures pg. 153 Aura faint enchantment; CL 5th Slot head; Price 19,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This crown of laurel leaves inspires its wearer to achieve mighty deeds. She gains a +4 morale bonus on saving throws against mind-affecting effects. If she succeeds at a saving throw against a harmful mind-affecting effect, the surge of confidence gives her a +2 morale bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, and weapon damage rolls for 1 minute. If she fails such a saving throw, she is instead shaken for 1 minute, though this condition doesn't stack with other fear effects to make her frightened or panicked. A mythic creature wearing the wreath gains 2 temporary hit points per tier at the start of each day. These temporary hit points are restored whenever the wearer rests 8 hours or otherwise gains the equivalent of doing so. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, false hope, good hope; Cost 9,500 gp
