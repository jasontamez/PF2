Discriminating Cube - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Discriminating Cube Source Classic Treasures Revisited pg. 13 Aura moderate evocation; CL 10th Slot none; Price 40,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This cube of force only creates one type of screen, which excludes living matter. Each of the five active faces sets the screen to exclude a different type of living matter (using the same categories as the bane weapon special ability). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, summon monster I, wall of force; Cost 20,000 gp
