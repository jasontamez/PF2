Fangwood Clasp - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Fangwood Clasp Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 28 Aura faint illusion; CL 5th Slot neck; Price 14,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This forest green clasp is engraved with a stylized version of the tree and blade of Nirmathas. If the wearer has the woodland stride ability, she gains the ranger's camouflage ability in forest terrain. If the wearer has an animal companion that also benefits from her woodland stride ability, the companion gains the camouflage ability as well. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, disguise self, invisibility; Cost 7,000 gp
