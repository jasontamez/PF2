Vest of the Deep - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vest of the Deep Source Pathfinder #99: Dance of the Damned pg. 16 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 7th Slot chest; Price 2,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description A vest of the deep creates a constant shell of magical energy that protects the wearer from extreme environmental pressure, such as that inflicted by water at a depth of over 100 feet. It does not provide any protection from constriction or other forms of direct crushing damage, nor does it grant water breathing. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, life bubble; Cost 1,000 gp
