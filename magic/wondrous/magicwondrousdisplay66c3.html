Mind Sentinel Medallion - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mind Sentinel Medallion Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 259 Aura faint abjuration; CL 1st Slot neck; Price 3,500 gp; Weight — Description The surface of this silver medallion is covered in angular geometric shapes that refract light randomly. The medallion grants a continuous +2 resistance bonus on saves versus mind-affecting spells, spell-like abilities, and supernatural abilities. If the wearer fails a save to avoid becoming dominated or confused, the amulet immediately grants the wearer a second saving throw to resist the effect. If this second save is successful, the medallion's power fades away entirely and it becomes a simple piece of silver jewelry. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, protection from evil; Cost 1,750 gp
