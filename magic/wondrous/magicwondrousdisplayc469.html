Beast Wrestler's Gauntlets - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Beast Wrestler's Gauntlets Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 28 Aura faint conjuration; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 7,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These gauntlets grant a +1 enhancement bonus on grapple checks. Each pair of gauntlets is created to fight a specific type or subtype of creature from the list of creature types in the bane weapon special ability. While the wearer is grappling a creature of the designated type, the gauntlets' enhancement bonus increases by 2 and the wearer deals an additional 2d6 points of nonlethal damage when dealing damage as part of a successful grapple check. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, summon monster I; Cost 3,750 gp
