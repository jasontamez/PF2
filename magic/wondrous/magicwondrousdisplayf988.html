Slippers of the Primordial Wilds - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Slippers of the Primordial Wilds Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 14 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 9th Slot feet; Price 24,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description These fine, supple slippers are made from giant cowslip petals. The wearer cannot be tracked or otherwise detected by scent. When standing with one foot in one terrain and the other in another terrain (such as at a cave mouth, a shore, or a forest's edge), a gathlain wearer can speak a command word to move to either the Material Plane or the First World, as per plane shift, twice per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, plane shift; Cost 12,000 gp
