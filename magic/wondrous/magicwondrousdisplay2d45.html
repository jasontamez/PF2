Burglar's Bracers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Burglar's Bracers Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 273 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot wrist; Price 1,050 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description On command, these plain leather bracers can transform into a set of masterwork thieves' tools. They can transform back to their bracer form with another such command. Once per day, while using these thieves' tools or wearing these bracers, the user can take 10 on a single Disable Device check, even when in immediate danger or distracted. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, shrink item; Cost 550 gp
