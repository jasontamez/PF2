Ushanka of the Northlands - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Ushanka of the Northlands Source Pathfinder #68: The Shackled Hut pg. 61 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot head; Price 4,500 gp; Weight — Description This large, furry hat is sewn from the thick pelt of an animal native to the frozen north, and has earflaps that can be pulled down or folded up over the crown of the hat. When worn, an ushanka of the northlands grants its wearer a +2 resistance bonus on saving throws against cold effects and a +5 competence bonus on Survival checks. When the earflaps are pulled down, the wearer gains a +2 bonus on saving throws against sonic effects, but also takes a -2 penalty on Perception checks made to hear. Pulling the earflaps down is a move action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, resistance, creator must have 5 ranks in Survival; Cost 2,250 gp
