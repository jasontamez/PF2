Pauldrons of the Bull - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pauldrons of the Bull Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 267 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot shoulders; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description These steel pauldrons are engraved with images of charging bulls. When their wearer makes a bull rush combat maneuver, she can make the check twice and take the better result. Furthermore, they grant a +2 enhancement bonus to CMD against bull rushes. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bull's strength; Cost 5,000 gp
