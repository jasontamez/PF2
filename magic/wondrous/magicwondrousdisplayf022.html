Cloak of the Duskwalker Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 264 Aura faint illusion; CL 3rd Slot shoulders; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This dark, velvety cloak billows with a dramatic flourish in its wearer's wake. The cloak grants its wearer low-light vision. Once per day, on command, the wearer can surround herself with darkness, as the darkness spell. While this darkness is active, the cloak also grants the wearer darkvision 60 feet as well as low-light vision. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, darkness, darkvision; Cost 5,000 gp
