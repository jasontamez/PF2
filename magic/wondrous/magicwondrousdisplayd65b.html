Zoic of the Primeval - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Zoic of the Primeval Source Lost Treasures pg. 63 Aura strong enchantment (compulsion); CL 17th Slot neck; Price 50,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This collection of twigs and dinosaur teeth can be strung through with a length of sinew to create a necklace that offers limited protection against dangerous animals. The bearer of the Zoic of the Primeval gains a +1 insight bonus to AC against all non-humanoids. Against animals and outsiders, this AC bonus increases to +2. Additionally, once per day the wearer can use the Zoic of the Primeval to dominate any creature of the animal or outsider type as by dominate monster (Will DC 23 negates). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, dominate monster; Cost 25,000 gp
