False Face - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment False Face Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 229 Aura none (see text); CL 3rd Slot head; Price 1,600 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This simple, hand-painted party mask emanates no magical aura. Its true usefulness is revealed only when it's worn. When a person dons the mask, she instantly assumes the appearance of single specific Small or Medium humanoid creature. Each mask has a single "face" that can't be changed once the item is created. This effect otherwise works as disguise self. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, disguise self, magic aura; Cost 800 gp
