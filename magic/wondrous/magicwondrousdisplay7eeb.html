Rhombocrystal - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Rhombocrystal Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 268, Gods and Magic pg. 59 Aura faint evocation; CL 1st Slot none; Price 2,250 gp; Weight — Description This jewel-like lens slowly changes colors over time, with no discernible pattern. It may appear as a ruby one morning and a sapphire that night, only to become opaline for the entire next day. Most owners set their rhombocrystal in a metal festoon on a chain and carry it as a pendant, monocle, or loupe. A rhombocrystal can be used as a magnifying glass, and grants its bearer a +5 competence bonus on Appraise checks involving gems and crystals. If Nivi Rhombodazzle is the bearer's patron, the bearer gains a +1 luck bonus on Reflex saving throws. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, divine favor, creator must have 5 ranks in Appraise; Cost 1,125 gp
