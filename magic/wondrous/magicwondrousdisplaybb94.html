Ambrosia - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Ambrosia Source Mythic Adventures pg. 146 Aura strong conjuration; CL 18th Slot none; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This divine food comes in many forms, from a light and airy honeyed cake to a deep, rich chocolate. A mythic creature that eats this food is restored and replenished. Eating this food takes 1 minute, and when the meal is done, the consumer regains all her uses of mythic power for the day. If a nonmythic creature consumes ambrosia, that creature is subject to neutralize poison, remove curse, and remove disease, each at caster level 10th. A mythic creature can choose to either gain this effect instead of regaining mythic power. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, greater heroism, heal; Cost 5,000 gp
