Manteau of the Mouse - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Manteau of the Mouse Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 29 Aura strong transmutation; CL 10th Slot chest; Price 12,500 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This soft fur vest comes in a variety of shades of brown, black, and white. Any creature larger than the wearer takes a -1, -2, -4, or -8 penalty on Perception checks to notice the wearer, depending on whether it is one, two, three, or four or more size categories larger than the wearer, respectively. In addition, the wearer can use beast shape III once per day to transform into a mouse. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, beast shape III; Cost 6,250 gp
