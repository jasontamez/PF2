Helm of Teleportation - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Helm of Teleportation Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 244, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 518 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 9th Slot head; Price 73,500 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description A character wearing this helm may teleport three times per day, instantly transporting himself and objects he might be carrying on his person to a designated destination, exactly as if he had cast the spell of the same name. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, teleport; Cost 36,750 gp
