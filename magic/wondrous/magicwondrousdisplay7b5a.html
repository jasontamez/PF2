Quick Action Slippers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Quick Action Slippers Source Magical Marketplace pg. 25 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot feet; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The wearer of a pair of quick action slippers may stand up from prone as a free action. Such movement still provokes attacks of opportunity as normal. If the wearer has the stand up rogue talent, the act of standing up from prone no longer provokes attacks of opportunity. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, haste, creator must have the stand up rogue talent; Cost 5,000 gp
