Gloves of Pulverizing Disruption - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gloves of Pulverizing Disruption Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 26 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot hands; Price 9,400 gp; Weight — Description Three times per day, the wearer can use these thick leather gloves to make a melee touch attack against an undead creature, a construct, or a nonmagical object and deal 3d4 points of damage to it. When the wearer deals damage in this way, the gloves absorb part of the matter that makes up the target. This creates dust that is stored within the gloves for 24 hours. The next time the wearer attempts a dirty trick combat maneuver, the dust flies from the gloves, providing a +2 bonus on that attempt. If the wearer does not attempt a dirty trick combat maneuver before 24 hours elapses, the dust disappears. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, corrosive touch; Cost 4,700 gp
