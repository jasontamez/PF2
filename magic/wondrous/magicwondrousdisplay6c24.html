Arachnid Goggles - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Arachnid Goggles Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 224 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 9th Slot eyes; Price 15,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This leather band is studded with dozens of faceted crystals of varying size, two of which fit over the eyes. When wearing these goggles, the wearer uses all the crystals as eyes, gaining all-around vision (Bestiary 2). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, countless eyes (Ultimate Magic); Cost 7,500 gp
