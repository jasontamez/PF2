Kikuya's Sensu - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Kikuya's Sensu Source Pathfinder #52: Forest of Spirits pg. 61 Aura moderate evocation; CL 11th Slot none; Price 30,840 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This folding fan made from cypress wood and lacquered paper is said to have belonged to Kikuya, a legendary Minkai oiran, or courtesan. When held in the hand, Kikuya's sensu grants a +2 competence bonus on the bearer's Charisma-based checks. While held, the fan provides the somatic component for spells cast by the bearer. Once per day on command, the bearer can use one face of the fan to cast calm emotions. Also once per day, the fan's other face can create a forceful hand. In addition, the bearer can whisper behind Kikuya's sensu and send the words up to 3 miles away, as the whispering wind spell, once per day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, calm emotions, cat's grace, forceful hand, whispering wind; Cost 15,420 gp
