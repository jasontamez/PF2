Wind Fan - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Wind Fan Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 325, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 532 Aura faint evocation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 5,500 gp; Weight — Description A wind fan appears to be nothing more than a wood and papyrus or cloth instrument with which to create a cooling breeze. By uttering the command word, its possessor causes the fan to duplicate a gust of wind spell. The fan can be used once per day with no risk. If it is used more frequently, there is a 20% cumulative chance per usage during that day that the device tears into useless, nonmagical tatters. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, gust of wind; Cost 2,750 gp
