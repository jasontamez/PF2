Poet's Cloak - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Poet's Cloak Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 233 Aura moderate enchantment; CL 7th Slot shoulders; Price 9,750 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Even after cleaning and mending, this traveler's cloak appears worn and weathered. For wearers with the bardic performance class feature, it grants the use of raging song as a 4th-level skald by spending rounds of bardic performance. For wearers with the raging song class feature, it grants the use of inspire courage and inspire competence as a 4th-level bard by spending rounds of raging song. In addition, each poet's cloak allows its wearer to grant one specific rage power when using raging song in place of his own rage powers (if any). The specific rage power varies by cloak. It must be usable by a 4th-level skald, and can't have any prerequisites other than level. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, good hope, rage, creator must have the bardic performance or raging song class feature; Cost 4,875 gp
