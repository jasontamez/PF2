Halo of Inner Calm - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Halo of Inner Calm Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 242, Advanced Race Guide pg. 173 Aura strong abjuration; CL 15th Slot head; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This silvery ring looks like a halo. When worn by a tiefling it hovers just above his head, though it still uses the head magic item slot. It helps to calm the baser emotions and the dark whispers that plague tieflings, granting the wearer a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws against all spells with the emotion descriptor. When worn by a tiefling of a good alignment, it provides spell resistance 13 against spells with the evil descriptor and a +2 sacred bonus on saving throws. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, holy aura, creator must be a tiefling of good alignment; Cost 8,000 gp
