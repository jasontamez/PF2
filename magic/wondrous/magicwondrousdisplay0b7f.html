Hand of the Mage Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 259, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 516 Aura faint transmutation; CL 2nd Slot neck; Price 900 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This mummified elf hand hangs by a golden chain around a character's neck (taking up space as a magic necklace would). It allows the wearer to utilize the spell mage hand at will. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, mage hand; Cost 450 gp
