Longarm Bracers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Longarm Bracers Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 274 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot wrist; Price 7,200 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These bracers are constructed from woven strands of bamboo. Three times per day, as a swift action, the wearer can stretch her arms beyond their limits, increasing her reach by 5 feet until the start of her next turn. Her weapon attack rolls take a -4 penalty, while unarmed strikes and natural weapons using the arms take no penalty. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, alter self; Cost 3,600 gp
