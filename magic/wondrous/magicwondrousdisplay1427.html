Minotaur Belt - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Minotaur Belt Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 212 Aura faint transmutation; CL 4th Slot belt; Price 11,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This belt grants its wearer a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength, and the ability to ignore difficult terrain while charging or making an overrun, bull rush, or trample attempt. Treat the enhancement bonus to Strength as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Items, bull's strength, feather step (Advanced Player's Guide); Cost 5,500 gp
