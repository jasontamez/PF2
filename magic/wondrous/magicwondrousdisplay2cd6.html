Vest of the Cockroach - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vest of the Cockroach Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 223, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 310 Aura faint necromancy; CL 3rd Slot chest; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This padded and slightly bulky vest, made from red-brown cloth, causes its wearer to feel vaguely uncomfortable whenever she is lying prone. If the wearer is a rogue, she gains the resiliency rogue talent. If she already has this talent, activating the talent grants her 2 hit points per rogue level instead of 1. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, false life; Cost 8,000 gp
