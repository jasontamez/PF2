Bane Baldric - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bane Baldric Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 220 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 10th Slot chest; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This ornate sash of embroidered velvet stretches across the chest from shoulder to waist. If the wearer is an inquisitor, she is treated as five levels higher when using her bane and greater bane abilities. If the wearer is not an inquisitor, she gains the bane ability of a 5th-level inquisitor, but must first attune a light or one-handed melee weapon to the baldric by hanging it from the cloth for 24 hours, and can only use the bane ability with the attuned weapon. Attuning a new weapon to the baldric ends the attunement for the previous weapon. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, summon monster I, creator must be an inquisitor; Cost 5,000 gp
