Tremor Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Tremor Boots Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 233 Aura faint divination; CL 5th Slot feet; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These brown leather boots have a reinforced toe of steel with grooves along the soles, crafted to help the wearer pick up vibrations from the surrounding environment. These boots grant the wearer tremorsense 5 feet as long as he is standing on solid ground. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, see invisibility; Cost 5,000 gp
