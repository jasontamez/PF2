Reaper's Mask - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Reaper's Mask Source Pathfinder #102: Breaking the Bones of Hell pg. 13, Pathfinder #2: The Skinsaw Murders pg. 47, Rise of the Runelords Anniversary Edition pg. 422 Aura moderate enchantment [evil]; CL 7th Slot head; Price 12,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The wearer of a reaper's mask gains a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks made against creatures that aren't immune to fear and a +1 profane bonus on damage with slashing weapons made against living creatures. The wearer can cast confusion twice per day (DC 16). When a reaper's mask is donned, the wearer takes 1 point of Charisma damage-most Skinsaw cultists don their masks well in advance of a mission so they have time to recover. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, confusion, deathwatch; Cost 6,000 gp
