Trapspringer's Gloves - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Trapspringer's Gloves Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 239, Pathfinder Society Field Guide pg. 54 Aura faint divination; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These studded leather gloves look similar to workman's gloves, though they are reinforced with numerous metal strips that the user can extend or retract as she wills, turning the tip of each finger into a customizable tool useful in examining and disarming traps. While worn, these gloves grant a +5 competence bonus on all Disable Device checks made to disarm traps. In addition, the gloves grant the user a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws made against traps. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, find traps; Cost 2,000 gp
