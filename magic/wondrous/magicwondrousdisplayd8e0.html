Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Dark Green Rhomboid (Normal) Ioun Stone Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 28 Aura strong divination; CL 12th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight — Description This ioun stone is forest green with flecks of yellow. At all times, it allows you to immediately notice any poison within 5 feet of you. This effect is otherwise identical to the detect poison spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, detect poison, creator must be 12th level; Cost 4,000 gp
