Anchoring Belt - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Anchoring Belt Source Mythic Adventures pg. 147 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th Slot belt; Price 60,000 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This belt looks like nothing more than iron chains connecting large pieces of slate. It grants its wearer a +5 inherent bonus to combat maneuver defense against bull rush, drag, grapple, overrun, reposition, and trip combat maneuvers and a +5 inherent bonus on saving throws against any effect that attempts to move the wearer with physical force or energy. Furthermore, if the wearer is a mythic creature and targeted by an effect that would teleport her, she can choose whether or not she wishes to be teleported. As a standard action, the wearer can expend one use of mythic power to create a 30-foot-radius aura that affects all enemy creatures within it as though they were subject to a dimensional anchor spell. This aura lasts for a number of rounds equal to the wearer's mythic tier. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, bull's strength, dimensional anchor; Cost 30,000 gp
