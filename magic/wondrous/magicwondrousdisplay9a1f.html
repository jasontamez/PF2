Vambraces of Defense - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vambraces of Defense Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 275 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot wrist; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description These polished steel vambraces grant a +1 deflection bonus to AC. Once per day, as an immediate action, if the wearer has at least one hand free, when he would normally be hit with an attack from a ranged weapon, he may deflect it with the bracers, taking no damage from it, as if he had the Deflect Arrows feat. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, shield; Cost 3,000 gp
