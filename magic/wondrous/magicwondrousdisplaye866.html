Corset of the Vishkanya - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Corset of the Vishkanya Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 214, Advanced Race Guide pg. 209 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot body; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This black corset fits neatly beneath armor. As a free action, the wearer can compress herself to fit through tight spaces as though affected by a squeeze spell (Advanced Race Guide) for up to 10 rounds per day. While using the corset's magic, she gains a +5 bonus on Escape Artist checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, grease, squeeze; Cost 1,500 gp
