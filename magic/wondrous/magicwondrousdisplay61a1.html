Headband of Seduction - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Headband of Seduction Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 251 Aura faint enchantment and transmutation; CL 5th Slot headband; Price 40,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This plain white band makes the wearer seem more attractive. The wearer gains a +2 competence bonus on Charisma-based checks with other members of his creature type. If the wearer is of the humanoid type, he gains a +5 competence bonus on creatures of his subtype instead. Once per day, the wearer can attempt to affect a creature as the spell unnatural lust (Ultimate Magic) but with a duration of 10 minutes. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, unnatural lust; Cost 20,000 gp
