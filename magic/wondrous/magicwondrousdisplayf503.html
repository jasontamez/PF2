Diabolical Masquerade Mask - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Diabolical Masquerade Mask Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 263 Aura faint conjuration, enchantment, and illusion; CL 1st Slot head; Price 2,900 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This masquerade mask resembles a handsome horned devil with red lenses in place of eyes. It functions as a hat of disguise. The wearer can use charm person and infernal healing each once per day. When infernal healing is active, the mask becomes visible whether or not the wearer is using its disguise abilities (although all other elements of the disguise remain). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, charm person, disguise self, infernal healing; Cost 1,450 gp
