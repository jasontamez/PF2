Shoes of the Firewalker - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Shoes of the Firewalker Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 233 Aura moderate evocation; CL 6th Slot feet; Price 21,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These low-cut shoes of polished red leather feature golden eyelets and stitching in a fiery pattern. They grant the wearer fire resistance 10 and allow the wearer to walk atop lava and magma as if subject to the water walk spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, resist energy, water walk; Cost 10,500 gp
