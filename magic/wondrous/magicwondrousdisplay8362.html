Rootsense Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Rootsense Boots Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 251 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot feet; Price 9,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Twigs sprout from these tall brown boots. On command, while the wearer is on the ground, ghostly roots grow from the boots' soles into the ground, granting their wearer tremorsense 30 feet for as long the wearer remains on the ground in the same space. While the roots are active, the boots provide two additional benefits. When the wearer is the target of a bull rush or trip attempt, living roots momentarily sprout from the boots into the earth, granting the wearer a +4 bonus to CMD to resist the attempt. The wearer also benefits from the flexibility of a living willow branch, gaining a +4 circumstance bonus on combat maneuver or Escape Artist checks to escape a grapple or slip bonds. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, entangle; Cost 4,500 gp
