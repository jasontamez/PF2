Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Deep Brown Sphere (Normal) Ioun Stone Source Dungeoneer's Handbook pg. 28 Aura strong divination; CL 12th Slot none; Price 10,000 gp; Weight — Description This deep brown ioun stone is streaked with amber and looks much like a tiger's eye gem save for its perfectly spherical shape. While using this stone, you know at all times exactly how many feet you are under or above sea level. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, discern location, creator must be 12th level; Cost 5,000 gp
