Cloak of Good Fortune - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Cloak of Good Fortune Source Disciple's Doctrine pg. 28 Aura moderate evocation; CL 9th Slot shoulders; Price 12,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This elegant cloak has simple prayers to kami stitched into its lining. The wearer gains a +1 luck bonus on attack rolls, and once per day, the wearer can spend 10 minutes reciting prayers from the cloak's lining to pose a single question to a nearby spirit, as per commune. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, commune, divine favor; Cost 6,250 gp
