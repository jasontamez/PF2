Candle of Truth Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 286, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 505 Aura faint enchantment; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 2,500 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This white tallow candle, when burned, calls into place a zone of truth spell (Will DC 13 negates) in a 5-foot radius centered on the candle. The zone lasts for 1 hour, while the candle burns. If the candle is snuffed before that time, the effect is ended and the candle ruined. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, zone of truth; Cost 1,250 gp
