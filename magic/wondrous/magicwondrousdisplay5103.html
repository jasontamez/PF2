Fog-Cutting Lenses - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Fog-Cutting Lenses Source Monster Codex pg. 69, Pathfinder #4: Fortress of the Stone Giants pg. 50, Rise of the Runelords Anniversary Edition pg. 421 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot face; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These goggles are made of polished rock crystal, with frames of polished brass and a simple leather strap and buckle sized for a giant's head (but easily adjustable for smaller wearers). The goggles allow the wearer to see through magical and normal fogs, mists, and similar obscurement. They do not confer darkvision or low-light vision. Further, the goggles distort and skew vision strangely, imposing a -4 penalty on Perception checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, darkvision, fog cloud; Cost 4,000 gp
