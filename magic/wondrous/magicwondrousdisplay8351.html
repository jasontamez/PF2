Gloves of Unfolding Wind - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gloves of Unfolding Wind Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 107 Aura faint evocation; CL 3rd Slot hands; Price 10,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These white fingerless gloves are decorated with a spiral on the back of the hand. For the purposes of resolving the effects of high winds, the wearer is treated as one size larger and his thrown weapons are treated as siege weapons. In addition, the wearer can spend 1 ki point to create a gust of wind. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, gust of wind; Cost 5,000 gp
