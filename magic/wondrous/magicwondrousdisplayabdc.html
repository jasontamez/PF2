Belt of Incredible Dexterity (+4) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Belt of Incredible Dexterity Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 209, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 502 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 8th Slot belt; Price 4,000 gp (+2), 16,000 gp (+4), 36,000 gp (+6); Weight 1 lb. Description This belt has a large silver buckle, usually depicting the image of a tiger. The belt grants the wearer an enhancement bonus to Dexterity of +2, +4, or +6. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cat's grace; Cost 2,000 gp (+2), 8,000 gp (+4), 18,000 gp (+6)
