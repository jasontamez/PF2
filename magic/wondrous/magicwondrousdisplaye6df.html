Bracers of Steadiness - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bracers of Steadiness Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 272 Aura faint abjuration; CL 1st Slot wrist; Price 2,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description These tortoise-shell bracers fit so snuggly against their wearer's skin that they almost seem part of him. The wearer of these bracers becomes exceptionally calm and composed if she takes her time to accomplish particular tasks. Whenever she takes 20 on a skill check for a skill that primarily depends on her arms and hands, she gains a +5 competence bonus on the check. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, aid; Cost 1,000 gp
