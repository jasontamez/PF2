Shrinking Berries - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Shrinking Berries Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 25 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 300 gp; Weight — Description These berries cause animals that eat them to transform into smaller and far less dangerous versions of themselves, as per the spell pup shape (Fortitude DC 14 negates), except other creatures do not need to attempt Will saves to attack the transformed animal and the effect lasts for 24 hours. These berries can be concealed in meat or other typical foods of an animal's diet. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, pup shape; Cost 150 gp
