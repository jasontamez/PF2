Ganji Doll (Greater) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Ganji Doll (Greater) Source Occult Adventures pg. 258 Aura moderate necromancy; CL 12th Slot none; Price 36,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description A greater ganji doll looks similar to and functions as the normal version, except that it can be used 5 times per day, imposes a -4 penalty on the target's saving throws against its effects, and has the following altered or additional special abilities in addition to all the abilities of a ganji doll. Damage: Damaging the doll causes the target to take 6d6 points of damage (Fortitude DC 16 half). This damage ignores all resistances and immunities. Locate Creature: The bearer of a ganji doll can use it to locate its bound target as locate creature. Suggestion: The user whispers in the doll's ear, creating the effect of a suggestion spell. The target must attempt a DC 16 Will save to resist the effect. Unlike with the spell, the target doesn't need to comprehend the user's language. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bestow curse, locate creature, suggestion, and a piece of the body of the humanoid to be affected; Cost 18,000 gp
