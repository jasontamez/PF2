Anaconda's Coils Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 208 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 8th Slot belt; Price 18,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This snakeskin belt's buckle is shaped like a serpent's head. The wearer gains a +2 enhancement bonus to Strength and a +2 competence bonus on grapple combat maneuver checks. Treat the enhancement bonus to Strength as temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. In addition, the belt grants the wearer the constrict ability (Bestiary 298) for 1d6 points of damage plus the wearer's Strength modifier. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, beast shape I, bull's strength; Cost 9,250 gp
