Boots of Teleportation - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of Teleportation Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 228, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 504 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 9th Slot feet; Price 49,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description When scrutinized closely, parts of these light gray boots appear to fade in and out of existence. Any character wearing this footwear may teleport three times per day, exactly as if she had cast the teleport spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, teleport; Cost 24,500 gp
