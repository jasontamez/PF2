Pipes of Dissolution - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pipes of Dissolution Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 315 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot none; Price 12,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description Carvings of winter scenes decorate this set of pan pipes. Activating the pipes requires a DC 15 Perform (wind instruments) check. Creatures within 30 feet gain a +2 competence bonus on caster level checks to dispel magical effects and overcome spell resistance. Maintaining the performance is challenging, requiring a move action and a new Perform check each round, with the DC increasing by +1 for each round the instrument is played. The magic ends when the player voluntarily stops or fails the Perform check, after which the pipes cannot be used again for 1 hour. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, dispel magic; Cost 6,000 gp
