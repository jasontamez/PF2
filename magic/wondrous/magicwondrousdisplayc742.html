Earth Child Faceguard - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Earth Child Faceguard Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 28 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot head; Price 10,000 gp; Weight — Description Decorated with the emblems of the Earth Child cadre, an earth child faceguard is designed to refine the wearer's focus when fighting giants. The wearer gains the benefits of Earth Child Style (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Combat 99), regardless of whether she meets the prerequisites. If the wearer does not possess the defensive training racial trait, the dodge bonus to AC against giants is reduced to +2. If the wearer already possesses the Earth Child Style feat, she can enter the earth child stance as a free action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Earth Child Style, know the enemy; Cost 5,000 gp
