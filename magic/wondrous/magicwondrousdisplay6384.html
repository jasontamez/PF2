Headband of Intuition Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 250 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot headband; Price 7,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This headband gives its wearer the gift of foresight, effectively allowing her to determine whether or not a particular course of action will grant weal and/or woe. Three times per day the wearer of this headband can, as a standard action, gain information about a specified course of action as if using the augury spell, except that the wearer always gets a meaningful reply. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, augury; Cost 3,500 gp
