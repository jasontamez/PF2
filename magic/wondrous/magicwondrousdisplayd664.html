Tunic of Deadly Might Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 223 Aura faint evocation; CL 3rd Slot chest; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This tunic is crafted from golden thread, and has silver-hemmed sleeves. The wearer gains a +2 bonus to Combat Maneuver Defense against disarm and sunder combat maneuvers. If the wearer is a samurai, twice per day as a swift action he can apply the effects of the thundering weapon special ability (Core Rulebook 472) to a successful critical hit against the target of his challenge. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, blindness/deafness, mage armor; Cost 3,000 gp
