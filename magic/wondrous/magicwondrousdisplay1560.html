Cloak of Elvenkind - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Cloak of Elvenkind Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 263, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 507 Aura faint illusion; CL 3rd Slot shoulders; Price 2,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These cloaks of famed elven design are enhanced to reflect the wearer's surroundings, aiding him in blending in with any terrain. When this plain gray cloak is worn with the hood drawn up around the head, the wearer gains a +5 competence bonus on Stealth checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, invisibility, creator must be an elf; Cost 1,250 gp
