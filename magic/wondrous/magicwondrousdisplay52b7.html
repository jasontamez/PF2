Dryad Sandals - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Dryad Sandals Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 230 Aura moderate conjuration and transmutation; CL 9th Slot feet; Price 24,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These sandals are woven from grass, lengths of pliant vine, living leaves, and supple bark. In forest terrain, the wearer leaves no tracks or scent and gains a +2 competence bonus on Stealth checks. The wearer can move through natural undergrowth without impediment or harm as a druid using woodland stride (Core Rulebook 51), and once per day she may step inside a tree and remain there for up to 9 hours, or teleport to a similar tree within range, as tree stride but allowing only a single teleport. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, pass without trace, tree stride, creator must be a druid; Cost 12,000 gp
