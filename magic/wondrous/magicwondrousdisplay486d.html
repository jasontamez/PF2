Elixir of Elemental Protection - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Elixir of Elemental Protection Source Dragonslayer's Handbook pg. 30 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th Slot none; Price 1,800 gp; Weight — Description Scintillating colors swirl through this liquid. The elixir grants the drinker 100 points of protection from the first type of elemental damage he takes after drinking it. Unused points of protection dissipate 1 hour after the liquid is consumed.
