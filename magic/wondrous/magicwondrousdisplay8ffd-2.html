Bag of Everlasting Dung - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bag of Everlasting Dung Source Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 26 Aura faint conjuration (creation); CL 5th Slot none; Price 500 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This leather bag exudes a pungent scent when opened. Each day it provides enough dried dung to fuel a small fire for 8 hours or fertilize a 20-foot-square patch of land. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, create food and water; Cost 250 gp, 20 XP
