Caltrop Bead (Cold Iron) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Caltrop Bead Source Demon Hunter's Handbook pg. 28 Aura faint conjuration; CL 7th Slot none; Price 40 gp (Iron), 80 gp (Cold Iron); Weight 2 lbs. Description The wielder may throw this small, dense iron sphere as a ranged touch attack (with a 10-foot range increment) or use it as a sling bullet. Upon striking a solid object, a caltrop bead explodes into caltrops in a 10-foot-radius burst. A creature directly hit by a caltrop bead takes 2d4 points of piercing damage, and adjacent creatures take 1d4 points of damage as the bead suddenly bursts. Caltrops created by a caltrop bead remain for 1 hour before disappearing. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, minor creation; Cost 20 gp (iron), 40 gp (cold iron)
