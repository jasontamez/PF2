Gem of Seeing - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gem of Seeing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 300, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 514 Aura moderate divination; CL 10th Slot none; Price 75,000 gp; Weight — Description This finely cut and polished stone is indistinguishable from an ordinary jewel in appearance. When it is gazed through, a gem of seeing enables the user to see as though she were affected by the true seeing spell. A gem of seeing can be used for as many as 30 minutes a day, in increments of 5 minutes. These increments do not need to be consecutive. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, true seeing; Cost 37,500 gp
