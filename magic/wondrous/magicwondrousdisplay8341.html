Mitre of the Hierophant - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mitre of the Hierophant Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 247 Aura moderate divination; CL 9th Slot head; Price 18,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This ostentatious hat functions only for a wearer who can cast divine spells. The wearer gains a +5 competence bonus on Diplomacy and Knowledge (religion) checks. Once per day on command, the wearer can ask a single question of her patron deity, as if by using commune. Once per week on command, the wearer can touch a creature, atoning it for past misdeeds as if using an atonement spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, atonement, commune, guidance; Cost 9,000 gp
