Philanderous Compact - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Philanderous Compact Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 27 Aura faint illusion; CL 1st Slot none; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description This small, polished leather case opens to reveal a mirror, face powder, rouge, and a puff. Using the puff and uttering a command word allows you to change your appearance once per day as per disguise self. Additionally, you get a +2 competence bonus to Gather Information checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, disguise self, creator must have at least 5 ranks in Gather Information; Cost 750 gp, 60 XP
