Carpet of Comfort - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Carpet of Comfort Source People of the Sands pg. 29 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot none; Price 90,000 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This specialized carpet of flying always measures 10 feet by 10 feet. When it's in flight, its passengers are shielded from wind and extreme temperatures as if inside a tiny hut (though without creating an opaque, immobile sphere). In addition, once per day while the carpet of comfort is on the ground, a command word transforms the carpet into an opulent silk tent that, other than its appearance, is equivalent to a secure shelter. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, overland flight, secure shelter, tiny hut; Cost 45,000 gp
