Eyes of Keen Sight - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Eyes of Keen Sight Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 225 Aura faint transmutation; CL 1st Slot eyes; Price 6,000 gp; Weight — Description These clear crystals fit over the wearer's eyes. They grant the wearer a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks, and the wearer gains low-light vision. If the wearer has low-light vision already, these lenses double the distance the wearer can see with this vision. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, keen senses (Advanced Player's Guide); Cost 3,000 gp
