Vambraces of the Tactician - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Vambraces of the Tactician Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 275 Aura moderate enchantment; CL 7th Slot wrist; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description These vambraces change their appearance depending on who wears them. If the wearer is a cavalier, these vambraces display the symbol of his order. When unworn or in the hands of a non-cavalier, they appear to be an ordinary pair of steel vambraces. A cavalier wearing these vambraces is considered two class levels higher for purposes of determining the effects of his challenge class feature. Furthermore, once per day as a swift action, a cavalier with the tactician class feature can increase the area in which he grants teamwork feats. Before the end of his next turn, when the cavalier grants his allies a teamwork feat, he can grant it to all allies within 60 feet, though they must still be able to see and hear him. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, heroism; Cost 4,000 gp
