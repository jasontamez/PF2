Burglar Boots (Major) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Burglar Boots (Major) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 230 Aura moderate divination; CL 10th Slot feet; Price 46,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description These boots look and act as the minor burglar boots, but grant a +10 competence bonus on Perception checks and to AC and on Reflex saves. Also, once per day on command, these boots grant the wearer the ability to use find traps as the spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, find traps, creator must have at least 12 ranks in Perception; Cost 23,000 gp
