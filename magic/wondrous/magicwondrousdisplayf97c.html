Elixir of Agility - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Elixir of Agility Source Potions and Poisons pg. 20 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 450 gp; Weight — Description This square flask contains an effervescent green liquid that causes the imbiber to become extremely nimble, albeit a bit jittery, for the next 30 minutes. During this time, the imbiber's base speed increases by 5 feet and she gains a +2 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks and initiative checks.
