Pelt of Primal Power - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pelt of Primal Power Source People of the North pg. 29 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot shoulders; Price 26,000 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description A prized item among Ulfen berserkers and vikings, a pelt of primal power is a large bearskin worn as a cloak, with the bear's head sitting atop the wearer's head. When donned, a pelt of primal power grants its wearer a +4 armor bonus to AC. In addition, once per day the wearer can transform into a grizzly bear (as beast shape II) for up to 10 minutes. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, beast shape II, mage armor; Cost 13,000 gp
