Efreeti Horns - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Efreeti Horns Source Adventurer's Guide pg. 107 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 8th Slot headband; Price 24,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The wearer of efreeti horns gains a +4 enhancement bonus to Wisdom. In addition, the wearer can spend 1 ki point as a full-round action to gain the effects of enlarge person (self only) or reduce person (self only) for 1 minute. These effects apply to the wearer regardless of her actual creature type. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, enlarge person, owl's wisdom, reduce person; Cost 12,000 gp
