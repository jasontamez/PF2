Everburning Lantern - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Everburning Lantern Source Mythic Adventures pg. 151 Aura moderate evocation; CL 6th Slot none; Price 3,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This small, brass hooded lantern gives off light as a daylight spell. If the bearer expends one use of mythic power, the lantern gives off light as a mythic daylight spell for 1 hour. While giving off light as mythic daylight, the lantern automatically dispels all non-mythic darkness spells or effects that come within its light. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, mythic daylight; Cost 1,500 gp
