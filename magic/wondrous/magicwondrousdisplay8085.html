Fan of Flirting - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Fan of Flirting Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 246 Aura faint enchantment; CL 1st Slot none; Price 1,700 gp; Weight — Description This fan, a favorite tool of courtiers and courtesans, is usually decorated with a delicate design of flowers or geometric patterns. Once per day, the wielder can use the fan to attempt to completely infatuate a humanoid target, causing it to stare deeply into the wielder's eyes and ignore everything else around it. The wielder utters a command word and holds the fan in front of his face, flourishing it and peering over its top to make eye contact with the target. The target must succeed at a DC 11 Will save or fall under the combined effects of the hypnotism and lock gaze spells. The effects last for 5 rounds or until the wielder dismisses it by closing the fan. Afterward, the target is charmed by the fan's user for 1 hour (as charm person). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, charm person, hypnotism, lock gaze; Cost 850 gp
