Grim Lantern - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Grim Lantern Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 301 Aura faint evocation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 5,800 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This macabre lantern is composed of a humanoid skull dangling from a short length of chain. A small fragment of soulstuff is trapped inside, burning slowly in a heatless flame. Its flickering light shines from the skull's eye sockets, casting light as a bullseye lantern. On command, the lantern can consume its soulstuff in a sudden blast of heat, as burning hands. Doing so extinguishes the lantern. Whenever a living creature with at least 1 HD is slain within 30 feet of an empty grim lantern, the lantern absorbs a fragment of that creature's soul, restoring its glow. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, burning hands, creator cannot be good; Cost 2,900 gp
