Seafoam Shawl - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Seafoam Shawl Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 268, Advanced Race Guide pg. 195 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot shoulders; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This shawl of delicate lace can be activated once per day to grant the benefits of the fins to feet spell (Advanced Race Guide), transforming the wearer's lower fins or appendages into feet whenever he is on dry land-unless dismissed, this effect lasts for 5 hours. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fins to feet (Advanced Race Guide); Cost 3,000 gp
