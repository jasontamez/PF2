Amulet of Wind Defiance - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Amulet of Wind Defiance Source Pathfinder #112: The Whisper Out of Time pg. 73 Aura moderate transmutation [air]; CL 9th Slot neck; Price 10,000 gp; Weight — Description This circular stone amulet is engraved with a fine, spiraling line that winds inward toward the center, creating the impression of a powerful vortex. The amulet surrounds the wearer with a reactive field that counters the negative effects of natural or magically controlled winds. The wearer treats all such effects as though she is under the infuence of freedom of movement. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, control winds; Cost 5,000 gp
