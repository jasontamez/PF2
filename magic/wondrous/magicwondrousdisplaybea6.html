Branding Iron of Tracking - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Branding Iron of Tracking Source Villain Codex pg. 21 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 9,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This 2-foot-long metal rod has a unique sigil on the end. When the sigil is heated in a fire, it can be used to brand a creature or object. Anytime after the branding, a creature can hold the branding iron of tracking and know the direction to the nearest brand, as if using locate object for branded objects or locate creature for branded creatures. The branding iron of tracking only tracks the brand itself, so cutting or scraping off the brand can fool the item. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, brand, locate creature, locate object; Cost 4,500 gp
