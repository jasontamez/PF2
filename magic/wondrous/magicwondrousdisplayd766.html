Wings of Flying - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Wings of Flying Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 269, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 532 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot shoulders; Price 54,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description A pair of these wings might appear to be nothing more than a plain cloak of old, black cloth, or they could be as elegant as a long cape of blue feathers. When the wearer speaks the command word, the cloak turns into a pair of bat or bird wings that empower her to fly with a speed of 60 feet (average maneuverability), also granting a +5 competence bonus on Fly skill checks. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fly; Cost 27,000 gp
