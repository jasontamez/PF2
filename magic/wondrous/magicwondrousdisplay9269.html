Blind Helm - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Blind Helm Source Mythic Adventures pg. 147 Aura strong divination; CL 13th Slot head; Price 85,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This plain steel helm completely covers the face, lacking even eyeholes or a slit to peer out of. Its wearer is blinded by the helm, but gains blindsense with a range of 60 feet. As a swift action, the wearer can expend one use of mythic power to gain blindsight with a range of 60 feet for 1 minute, or instead expend two uses of mythic power to gain blindsight with a range of 120 feet for 1 minute. The wearer can expend three uses of mythic power to gain the effects of truesight for 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Mythic Crafter, echolocation, true seeing; Cost 42,500 gp
