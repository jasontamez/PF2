Helm of Telepathy - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Helm of Telepathy Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 244, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 518 Aura faint divination and enchantment; CL 5th Slot head; Price 27,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This pale metal or ivory helm covers much of the head when worn. The wearer can use detect thoughts at will. Furthermore, he can send a telepathic message to anyone whose surface thoughts he is reading (allowing twoway communication). Once per day, the wearer of the helm can implant a suggestion (as the spell, Will DC 14 negates) along with his telepathic message. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, detect thoughts, suggestion; Cost 13,500 gp
