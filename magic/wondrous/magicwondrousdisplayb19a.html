Featherlight Compass - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Featherlight Compass Source Planar Adventures pg. 53 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description This compass's components are so fine that it is practically weightless. When held in the hand in an area of subjective directional gravity, the bearer automatically succeeds at Wisdom checks to change direction and can increase or decrease his falling speed by 30 feet during the first round after he changes his subjective gravitational direction. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, know direction; Cost 750 gp
