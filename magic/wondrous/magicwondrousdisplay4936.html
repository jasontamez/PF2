Void Pennant - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Void Pennant Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 324 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 10th Slot none; Price 14,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This long tapering pennant resembles a stylized serpentine figure wearing a shimmering crown. The figure's claws function as clasps, attaching it to a lance, polearm, frame, or staff. The pennant has no effect when not mounted properly, or when activated by a lawfully aligned creature. Planting the pennant in the ground generates an area of twisted space around the flag out to a range of 30 feet. Any creature that teleports into or out of this area is nauseated for 1 round (a DC 18 Fortitude save reduces this penalty to sickened). Any creature may end the effect by uprooting the pennant as a standard action. The pennant functions for 10 rounds per day. These rounds do not need to be consecutive. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, dimensional anchor; Cost 7,000 gp
