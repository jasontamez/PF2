Flute of Persuasion - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Flute of Persuasion Source Pathfinder: Origins #6: Ezren pg. 35 Aura moderate conjuration; CL 4th Slot none; Price 14,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This slender flute inscribed with diabolical symbols grants additional control over charmed creatures. When played by a person who succeeds at a DC 13 Perform (wind instruments) check, the flute allows the player to issue a telepathic command to a single creature within 30 feet he has previously charmed, per the suggestion spell. If the player issues a suggestion that would put the charmed creature in harm's way, the creature receives a DC 13 Will save to resist. A successful save prevents the victim from following the order that round, but the creature remains charmed in any case. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, suggestion; Cost 7,000 gp
