Ring Of The Cacodaemon - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Ring Of The Cacodaemon Source Book of the Damned - Volume 3: Horsemen of the Apocalypse pg. 41 Aura strong necromancy; CL 8th Slot ring; Price 15,000 gp; Weight — Description Sculpted into the intricate form of a golden cacodaemon, this ring's wide-open mouth is designed to hold a soul gem created by a cacodaemon. The ring allows its wearer to tap into the residual memories of the mangled soul it encapsulates. Once per day, the wearer may interrogate the soul in the gem, similar to using speak with dead, except the soul answers telepathically. Once per day, the bearer can project the soul's last tormented moments before it was consumed and bound, creating a cone of terror equivalent to a fear spell (DC 16). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fear, speak with dead; Cost 7,500 gp.
