Druid's Vestment - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Druid's Vestment Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 215, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 532 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot body; Price 3,750 gp; Weight — Description This light garment is worn over normal clothing or armor. Most such vestments are green, embroidered with elaborate plant or animal motifs. When this item is worn by a character with the wild shape ability, the character can use that ability one additional time each day. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, polymorph or wild shape ability; Cost 1,375 gp
