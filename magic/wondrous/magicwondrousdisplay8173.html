Gauntlet of Rust Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 235, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 514 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 7th Slot hands; Price 11,500 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This single metal gauntlet looks rusted and pitted but is actually quite powerful. Once per day, it can affect an object as with the rusting grasp spell. It also completely protects the wearer and her gear from rust (magical or otherwise), including the attack of a rust monster. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, rusting grasp; Cost 5,750 gp
