Restorative Ointment - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Restorative Ointment Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 317, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 526 Aura faint conjuration; CL 5th Slot none; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description A jar of this unguent is 3 inches in diameter and 1 inch deep, and contains 5 applications. Placed upon a poisoned wound or swallowed, the ointment detoxifies any poison (as neutralize poison with a +5 bonus on the check). Applied to a diseased area, it removes the disease (as remove disease with a +5 bonus on the check). Rubbed on a wound, the ointment cures 1d8+5 points of damage (as cure light wounds). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cure light wounds, neutralize poison, remove disease; Cost 2,000 gp
