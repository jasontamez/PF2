Swallowtail Bracers - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Swallowtail Bracers Source Pathfinder #75: Demon's Heresy pg. 63 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th Slot wrists; Price 27,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These delicate sliver bracers are decorated with blue butterflies. While worn, swallowtail bracers grant the wearer a +1 luck bonus on all saving throws, ability checks, and skill checks. If the wearer worships Desna, the bracers grant an additional boon: Once per day, by speaking a command word and the name and/or title of the intended recipient, the wearer may cast dream. Such a wearer is also immune to nightmare, and when the wearer is targeted by that spell, the effects reflect back upon the caster. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, divine favor, dream, creator must worship Desna; Cost 13,500 gp
