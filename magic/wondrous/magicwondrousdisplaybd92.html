Suggestive Tea - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Suggestive Tea Source Pathfinder #67: The Snows of Summer pg. 61 Aura faint enchantment (compulsion); CL 5th Slot none; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description A single dose of these dried tea leaves is enough to brew a cup of savory tea for one creature, who then becomes susceptible to suggestion for 1 hour after drinking the tea. During this time, anyone can suggest a course of activity to the drinker, who carries out the order to the best of his ability, as suggestion (Will DC 14 negates). In addition, the character drinking the tea has no recollection of the time spent carrying out the compulsion. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, modify memory, suggestion; Cost 750 gp
