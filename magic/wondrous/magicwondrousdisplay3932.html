Scale of the Mariner - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Scale of the Mariner Source Treasure of Chimera Cove pg. 25 Aura faint abjuration and transmutation; CL 5th Slot amulet; Price 8,000 gp; Weight — Description These simple silver brooches are often found pinned to the capes of captains of the Andoren merchant marine.
