Bastion Boots - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bastion Boots Source The Harrow Handbook pg. 30 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 10th Slot feet; Price 10,500 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description These metal boots are engraved with the imagery of The Keep, and evoke the unwavering determination represented by that card. The boots can be used as an immovable rod, even when worn. If the boots are moved, the wearer moves with the boots. Even while not activated, bastion boots grant the wearer a +4 bonus to her CMD against combat maneuvers to move her. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, levitate; Cost 5,250 gp
