Headband of Mental Superiority (+4) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Headband of Mental Superiority Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 251, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 517 Aura strong transmutation; CL 16th Slot headband; Price 16,000 gp (+2), 64,000 gp (+4), 144,000 gp (+6); Weight 1 lb. Description This ornate headband is decorated with numerous clusters of small gemstones. The headband grants the wearer an enhancement bonus to all mental ability scores (Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma) of +2, +4, or +6. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the headband is worn. The headband also grants skill ranks as a headband of vast intelligence. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, eagle's splendor, fox's cunning, owl's wisdom; Cost 8,000 gp (+2), 32,000 gp (+4), 77,000 gp (+6)
