Brooch of Shielding - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Brooch of Shielding Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 257, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 505 Aura faint abjuration; CL 1st Slot neck; Price 1,500 gp; Weight — Description This appears to be a piece of silver or gold jewelry used to fasten a cloak or cape. In addition to this mundane task, it can absorb magic missiles of the sort generated by the spell or spell-like ability. A brooch can absorb up to 101 points of damage from magic missiles before it melts and becomes useless. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, shield; Cost 750 gp
