Icon of Aspects - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Icon of Aspects Source Tears at Bitter Manor pg. 54 Aura moderate evocation; CL 7th Slot none; Price 5,500 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description When a cleric with an icon of aspects prepares spells, she may replace the granted powers of one of her domains with those of a different domain she doesn't have but that is associated with her deity. This lasts until the next time she prepares spells. The cleric must meet the level requirements for both abilities. For example, a 6th-level cleric with the Good domain who swaps for the Healing domain can't use healer's blessing even though she meets the level requirement, because the holy lance power of the Good domain requires her to be 8th level. The icon affects only granted powers, not domain spells, bonus feats, or other benefits. The icon can affect only one person at a time. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, imbue with spell ability, creator must be a divine spellcaster; Cost 2,750 gp
