Halflight Charm - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Halflight Charm Source Pathfinder #63: The Asylum Stone pg. 58, City of Strangers pg. 50 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 2,500 gp; Weight — Description This amulet is a small crystal at the end of a simple leather thong. It glows brightly from within, shedding light equivalent to that of a torch. Once per day, the amulet's bearer can clutch the object and call for help. Doing so immediately contacts the three closest Duskwardens and gives them intimate knowledge of the pendant's location, as per the locate object spell, out to a distance of a mile. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, light, locate object; Cost 1,250 gp
