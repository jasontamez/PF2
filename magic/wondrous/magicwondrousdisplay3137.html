Gloves of Dueling - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gloves of Dueling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 236, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 305 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot hands; Price 15,000 gp; Weight — Description These supple gloves grant the wearer a +4 bonus to her CMD against disarm attacks, attempts to sunder her wielded weapons, and effects that cause her to lose her grip on her weapons (such as grease). The wearer doesn't drop held weapons when panicked or stunned. If the wearer has the weapon training class feature and is using an appropriate weapon, her weapon training bonus increases by +2. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, greater magic weapon; Cost 7,500 gp
