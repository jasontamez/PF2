Palelight Torch - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Palelight Torch Source Blood of Shadows pg. 21 Aura faint evocation; CL 2nd Slot none; Price 250 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This torch is made from a dull, gray wood that bears intricate carvings. It burns like a normal torch, but only gives off dim light in a 40-foot radius. In areas of normal light, the torch reduces the light level to dim. The torch has no effect in areas of bright light. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, light; Cost 125 gp
