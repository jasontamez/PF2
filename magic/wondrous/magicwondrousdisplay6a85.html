Gravewatch Pendant - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gravewatch Pendant Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 258 Aura faint abjuration; CL 3rd Slot neck; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description Favored by the grave-wardens of sites known to be infested with the spirits of the dead, this pendant is an iron ring strung on a necklace made from polished and discolored bones, nails, and teeth. A faint blue light limns the wearer, protecting him from attack. Undead creatures are repulsed by this light and take a -2 penalty on all attack rolls against the wearer of a gravewatch pendant. Incorporeal undead creatures are further repulsed and instead take a -4 penalty on all attack rolls against the wearer. Undead creatures can sense this light easily, however, and receive a +4 circumstance bonus on Perception checks to detect a creature wearing this pendant. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, protection from evil; Cost 4,000 gp
