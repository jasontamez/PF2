Dance of Seven Veils - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Dance of Seven Veils Source Pathfinder #135: Runeplague pg. 67 Aura moderate illusion and transmutation; CL 9th Slot head or neck; Price 27,000 gp; Weight — Description This scarf is crafted from fine, almost transparent silk of a deep red hue. When worn about the head as a veil or the neck as a scarf, the dance of seven veils disguises the wearer in a different shape, as per disguise self. The wearer can cast levitate at will, gains a +4 competence bonus on Bluff and Diplomacy checks, and gains a +2 enhancement bonus to Intelligence and Charisma. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, disguise self, eagle's splendor, fox's cunning, levitate, feather fall; Cost 13,500 gp
