Scabbard of Stanching - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Scabbard of Stanching Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 318, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 309 Aura faint conjuration; CL 5th Slot none; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This fine, red leather sheath is decorated with gold filigree. It fits any bladed slashing weapon. While worn, a scabbard of stanching protects against bleed damage. Anytime the wearer suffers a bleed effect, the scabbard automatically stanches it. It has no effect on bleed damage from effects that require a DC 16 or higher caster level or Heal check. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cure light wounds; Cost 2,500 gp
