Belt of Physical Might (+6) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Belt of Physical Might Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 210, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 502 Aura strong transmutation; CL 12th Slot belt; Price 10,000 gp (+2), 40,000 gp (+4), 90,000 gp (+6); Weight 1 lb. Description This belt grants the wearer an enhancement bonus to two physical ability scores (Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution) of +2, +4, or +6. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. These bonuses are chosen when the belt is created and cannot be changed. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bear's endurance, bull's strength, and/or cat's grace; Cost 5,000 gp (+2), 20,000 gp (+4), 45,000 gp (+6)
