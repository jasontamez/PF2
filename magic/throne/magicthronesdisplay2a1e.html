Throne of Pleasing Mien - Thrones - Magic Equipment Throne of Pleasing Mien Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 31 Aura moderate abjuration and enchantment CL 10th Slot none; Price 8,000 gp; Weight 350 lbs. Description This beautiful throne is delicately crafted from pale stone carved with images of dancing nymphs and frolicking satyrs. A throne of pleasing mien imparts its continuous heroism effect to anyone seated upon it. A creature that bows or otherwise offers fealty to anyone sitting upon the throne (regardless of whether the person is a rightful ruler or not) gains a +2 circumstance bonus on Charisma-based skill checks and a +1 morale bonus on Will saving throws for 24 hours. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, eagle's splendor, heroism, resistance; Cost 4,000 gp
