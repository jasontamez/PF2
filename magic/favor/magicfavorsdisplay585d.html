Token of Gallantry - Favors - Magic Equipment Token of Gallantry Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 13 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot none; Price 2,400 gp; Weight — Description This favor is a lace handkerchief or scrap of silken clothing that is tied around the pommel or haft of a melee weapon. For 24 hours, that weapon becomes good-aligned for the purpose of bypassing damage reduction. As long as the favor is active, the token does not become soiled or bloody. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, align weapon, status; Cost 1,200 gp
