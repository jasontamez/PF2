Ring of Serene Contortions - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Serene Contortions Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 259 Aura faint abjuration CL 5th Slot ring; Price 1,200 gp; Weight — Description This simple copper ring bears the image of a blue enamel hand.
