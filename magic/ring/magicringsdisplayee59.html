Ring of Heroes - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Heroes Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 325 Aura moderate (no school) CL 7th Slot ring; Price 8,000 gp; Weight — Description Sculpted in the shape of two noble looking bronze lions charging toward a centrally placed garnet, this ring grants the wearer the Luck of Heroes feat. The ring must be worn for 24 hours before it takes effect. The wearer can also call upon the ring's power to gain a hero point that must be used immediately, although this causes the ring to lose its powers permanently. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, Luck of Heroes; Cost 4,000 gp
