Ring of Torag - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Torag Source Hollow's Last Hope pg. 16 Aura faint abjuration CL 3rd Slot ring; Price 1,200 gp; Weight — Description This simple golden ring has a large red gemstone set into it that sparkles with an inner fire. The wearer of the ring gains fire resistance 10 against the first fire attack that hits him that day. This protection renews itself every morning at dawn. In addition, the wearer receives a +1 resistance bonus on saves made against fire spells and effects. The ring must be worn for 24 hours to have any effect. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, resist energy; Cost 600 gp, 48 XP
