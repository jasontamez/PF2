Ring of Ancestral Blood Magic - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Ancestral Blood Magic Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 216 Aura moderate transmutation CL 10th Slot ring; Price 4,000 gp; Weight — Description While the wearer is bloodraging, he can cast a bonus spell gained from his bloodrager bloodline without expending a spell slot. He can do this three times per day, and must expend a number of rounds of bloodrage equal to the level of the spell to be cast. The wearer must meet all the other requirements for spellcasting. Construction Requirements Endurance, Extend Spell, Forge Ring, creator must have the bloodrage class feature; Cost 2,000 gp
