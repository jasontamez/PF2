Ring of Natural Attunement (Drake) - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Natural Attunement (Drake) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 217 Aura moderate conjuration CL 7th Slot ring; Price 12,880 gp; Weight — Description This coiling band is made of jade carved into the shape of a sea drake. If the wearer can cast summon nature's ally spells, the wearer adds forest drake and shadow drake to the 4th-level list of monsters he can summon with those spells, adds flame drake to the 5th-level list, adds frost drake to the 6th-level list, and adds desert drake to the 9th-level list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon a shadow drake, as if by the summon nature's ally IV spell. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, greater magic fang, summon nature's ally IV; Cost 6,440 gp
