Ring of the Grasping Grave - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of the Grasping Grave Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 176 Aura faint necromancy CL 1st Slot ring; Price 2,000 gp; Weight — Description The wearer of this ring can make a melee touch attack against an undead creature no more than one size category larger than himself, cutting off that creature from undying energy. The wearer never suffers any ill effects that might normally occur from coming into physical contact with an undead creature touched in this way. On a successful hit, the undead cannot be healed or aided by the effects of negative energy for 1d4 rounds. During this duration, positive energy affects the undead creature normally. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, sculpt corpse (Advanced Player's Guide); Cost 1,000 gp
