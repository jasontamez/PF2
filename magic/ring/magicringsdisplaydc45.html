Ring of Powerful Blood Magic - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Powerful Blood Magic Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 218 Aura moderate evocation CL 9th Slot ring; Price 3,000 gp; Weight — Description When the wearer of this ring is bloodraging and casts a spell, as a swift action he can increase the save DC of the spell by 1. The wearer can do this a maximum of three times per day. This has no effect on spells that do not require saving throws. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, Spell Focus, creator must have the bloodrage class feature; Cost 1,500 gp
