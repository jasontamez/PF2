Red Hound Ring - Rings - Magic Equipment Red Hound Ring Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 259 Aura faint abjuration, conjuration, and transmutation CL 5th Slot ring; Price 4,700 gp; Weight — Description This white gold ring is engraved with the images of a mastiff and a thundercloud. It functions as a ring of protection +1. In addition, once per day, the wearer may use summon monster I to summon a red mastiff (use statistics for a celestial dog). Additionally, once per day the wearer may use beast shape I to transform into a Small red mastiff. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, beast shape I, shield of faith, summon monster I, creator must be at least 3rd level; Cost 2,350 gp
