Ring of Natural Attunement (Kami) - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Natural Attunement (Kami) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 217 Aura faint conjuration CL 5th Slot ring; Price 7,800 gp; Weight — Description This ring carved from a single piece of cherry wood bears an inscription written in Tien. If the wearer can cast summon nature's ally spells, the wearer adds shikigami to the 3rd-level list of monsters he can summon with those spells, adds kodama to the 5th-level list, adds zuishin to the 7th-level list, and adds toshigami to the 9th-level list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon a shikigami, as if by the summon nature's ally III spell. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, commune with nature, summon nature's ally III; Cost 3,900 gp
