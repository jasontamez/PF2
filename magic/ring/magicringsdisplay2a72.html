Ring of Climbing - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Climbing Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 168, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot ring; Price 2,500 gp; Weight — Description This magic ring has the deceptively simple appearance of a plain leather cord that wraps securely around a finger.
