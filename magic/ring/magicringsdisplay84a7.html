Nethysian Ring of Minor Spell Storing - Rings - Magic Equipment Nethysian Ring of Minor Spell Storing Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 259 Aura moderate abjuration and transmutation [chaotic, evil, good, lawful] CL 5th Slot ring; Price 20,000 gp; Weight — Description This ring is made of several metals hammered together into a mottled pattern, with Nethys's holy symbol stamped on one end. It functions as a ring of minor spell storing, except domain spells from Nethys's domains (see page 100) count as 1 spell level lower (minimum 1st level) for the purpose of storing them in the ring. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, imbue with spell ability; Cost 10,000 gp
