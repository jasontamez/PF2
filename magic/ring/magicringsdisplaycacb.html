Ring of Summoning Affinity (Inevitable) - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Summoning Affinity (Inevitable) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 219 Aura faint conjuration [law] CL 5th Slot ring; Price 8,100 gp; Weight — Description This ring resembles a gear or cog. If the wearer can cast summon monster spells, the wearer adds arbiter to the 3rd-level list of monsters she can summon with those spells, adds zelekhut to the 7th-level list, adds kolyarut to the 8th-level list, and adds marut to the 9th-level list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon an arbiter, as if by the summon monster III spell. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, planar ally or planar binding, creator must be lawful neutral; Cost 4,050 gp
