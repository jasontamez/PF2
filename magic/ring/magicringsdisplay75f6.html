Ring of Summoning Affinity (Agathion) - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Summoning Affinity (Agathion) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 218 Aura faint conjuration [good] CL 5th Slot ring; Price 7,700 gp; Weight — Description This ring is forged from electrum and inlaid with runes. If the wearer can cast summon monster spells, the wearer adds silvanshee to the 3rd-level list of monsters she can summon with those spells, adds vulpinal to the 5th-level list, adds avoral to the 7th-level list, and adds leonal to the 8th-level list. Also, once per day on command the wearer can use this ring to summon a silvanshee, as if by the summon monster III spell. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, planar ally or planar binding, creator must be neutral good; Cost 3,850 gp
