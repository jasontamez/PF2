Plants - Magic Equipment Gravebane Petals Source Wilderness Origins pg. 16 Aura faint necromancy CL 3rd Slot none; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 50 lbs. Description When packed tightly within the mouth of a corpse or undead creature, the deep red petals of the gravebane flower prevent any decay, as if the corpse were under the effects of gentle repose, and they completely mask any stench with a sickly sweet fragrance. These effects persist for 1 month. A single bush grows one new flower each day, and it takes 1 month to accumulate enough gravebane petals to preserve a single corpse. The plant has a 52-week growing period. Construction Requirements Cultivate Magic Plant, rage, cultivator must have 3 ranks in Knowledge (nature); Cost 8,000 gp
