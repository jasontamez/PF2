Rod of Metamagic, Concussive (normal) - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Metamagic, Concussive Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 188 Aura strong (no school) CL 17th Slot none; Price 9,000 gp (lesser), 32,500 gp (normal), 73,000 gp (greater); Weight 5 lbs. Description Up to three times per day the wielder can cast a spell with the sonic descriptor that causes those who take damage from that spell to take a -2 penalty on attack rolls, saving throws, skill checks, and ability checks for a number of rounds equal to the actual spell level of the spell cast as though using the Concussive Spell feat (Ultimate Magic). Construction Requirements Craft Rod, Concussive Spell; Cost 4,500 gp (lesser), 16,250 gp (normal), 36,500 gp (greater)
