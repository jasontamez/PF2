Rod of Metamagic, Selective (lesser) - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Metamagic, Selective Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 190, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 296 Aura strong (no school) CL 17th Slot none; Price 3,000 gp (lesser), 11,000 gp (normal), 24,500 gp (greater); Weight 5 lbs. Description The wielder can cast up to three spells per day as though using the Selective Spell feat (Advanced Player's Guide). For each spell, he can select up to four creatures of his choice within the area that are unaffected. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, Selective Spell; Cost 1,500 gp (lesser), 5,500 gp (normal), 12,250 gp (greater)
