Rod of the Wayang - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of the Wayang Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 185 Aura moderate evocation CL 8th Slot none; Price 12,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This rod is lacquered jet black, covered with bright white runes, and capped with crown of jagged smoky quartz. When the rod's wielder casts a spell of the shadow subschool, she increases the DC of that spell's saving throw by 1. Furthermore, once per day on command, wielder can treat positive and negative energy effects as if she were an undead creature, taking damage from positive energy and healing damage from negative energy. This ability lasts for 1 minute unless dismissed as a free action. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, shadow conjuration greater, shadow evocation greater; Cost 6,000 gp
