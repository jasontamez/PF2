Rod of Metamagic, Reach (normal) - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Metamagic, Reach Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 190, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 295 Aura strong (no school) CL 17th Slot none; Price 3,000 gp (lesser), 11,000 gp (normal), 24,500 gp (greater); Weight 5 lbs. Description The wielder can cast up to three spells per day with a onestep increase in their range category (such as from close to medium, or medium to long) as though using the Reach Spell feat (Advanced Player's Guide). Construction Requirements Craft Rod, Reach Spell; Cost 1,500 gp (lesser), 5,500 gp (normal), 12,250 gp (greater)
