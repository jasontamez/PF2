Scepter of the Arclords - Rods - Magic Equipment Scepter of the Arclords Source Lost Treasures pg. 52 Aura strong universal CL 20th Slot none; Price 200,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description The Scepter of the Arclords is a powerful device capable of harvesting all the ambient magic from a large area and focusing it into a single powerful effect. Once per day the wielder can command the Scepter of the Arclords to duplicate any spell of 3rd level or lower. This automatically succeeds, but there is a 10% chance the wielder also gains a random minor spellblight (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 94). Additionally, once per week the wielder can command the item to create any magic effect he desires. Roll d% to determine the effect of this command.
