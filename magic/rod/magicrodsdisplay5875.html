Rod of Metal and Mineral Detection - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Metal and Mineral Detection Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 182, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 485 Aura moderate divination CL 9th Slot none; Price 10,500 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This rod is valued by treasure hunters and miners alike, for it pulses and hums in the wielder's hand in the proximity of metal. As the wearer aims the rod, the pulsations grow more noticeable as it points to the largest mass of metal within 30 feet. However, the wielder can also concentrate on a specific metal or mineral. If the specific mineral is within 30 feet, the rod points to any places it is located, and the rod wielder knows the approximate quantity as well. If more than one deposit of the specified metal or mineral is within range, the rod points to the largest cache first. Each operation requires a full-round action. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, locate object; Cost 5,250 gp
