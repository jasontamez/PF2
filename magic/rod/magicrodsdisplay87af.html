Chastising Baton - Rods - Magic Equipment Chastising Baton Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 30 Aura moderate necromancy CL 7th Slot none; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This short metal rod is etched with the outlines of various weapons and is usually owned by strong-armed rulers who keep a tight rein on their nation's military. A chastising baton adds 1 to the saving throw DC of any compulsion spell cast by the wielder and adds the pain descriptor to the spell. Creatures that succeed at a Will save against a compulsion spell cast by the wielder are racked with pain, taking 1d6 points of nonlethal damage and becoming sickened for 1 round. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, pain strike; Cost 2,500 gp
