Rod of the Alicorn - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of the Alicorn Source Inner Sea Intrigue pg. 55 Aura moderate conjuration CL 9th Slot none; Price 25,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This twisted wooden rod resembles the horn of a unicorn. Three times per day, the user can touch the rod to a target as a standard action and heal it with cure light wounds. Once per day, the user can heal a creature in this way with cure moderate wounds. Once per day, the user can also cast neutralize poison on a target in this way. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, cure light wounds, cure moderate wounds, neutralize poison, creator must be good-aligned; Cost 12,500 gp
