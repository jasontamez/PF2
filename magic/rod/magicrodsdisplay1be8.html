Rod of Beguiling - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Beguiling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 180 Aura faint abjuration CL 5th Slot none; Price 18,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This eye-catching rod is bedecked with glittering spangles. The wielder gains a +5 competence bonus on Bluff and Sleight of Hand checks, as well as on opposed Charisma checks to give commands to charmed creatures. A rod of beguiling allows a bard's fascinate, suggestion, and mass suggestion bardic performance to take effect as if she were 5 levels higher, and grants a +5 bonus on a bard's Perform checks when using distraction. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, hypnosis; Cost 9,000 gp
