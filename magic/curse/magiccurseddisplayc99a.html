Unstable Musket Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 351 Aura moderate transmutation CL 6th Slot none; Weight 9 lbs. Description This item seems to be a normal +1 musket but has a misfire chance of 1-5. No effect or ability can reduce this misfire chance. This musket must be used as its owner's primary ranged weapon and can only be discarded after the owner is subject to a remove curse spell or similar effect.
