Pauldrons of the Jackass Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 348 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot shoulders; Weight 3 lbs. Description These steel pauldrons seem to be emblazoned with stampeding horses until they are worn, and then the horses change shape to that of dumfounded donkeys. The wearer of these pauldrons takes a -4 penalty to Dexterity and reduces all speeds by 10 feet. A successful remove curse spell is required before this item can be removed.
