-2 Cursed Sword Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 342, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 543 Aura strong evocation CL 15th Slot none; Weight 4 lbs. Description This longsword performs well against targets in practice, but when used in combat its wielder takes a -2 penalty on attack rolls. All damage dealt is also reduced by 2 points, but never below a minimum of 1 point of damage on any successful hit. The sword always forces that character to employ it rather than another weapon.
