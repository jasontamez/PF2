Net of Snaring - Cursed Items - Magic Equipment Net of Snaring Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 348, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 541 Aura moderate evocation CL 8th Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Description This net provides a +3 bonus on attack rolls but can only be used underwater. Underwater, it can be commanded to shoot forth up to 30 feet to trap a creature.
