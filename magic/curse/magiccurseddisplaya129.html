Scattershot Bracers Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 351 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot wrists; Weight 1 lb. Description These wristbands appear to be bracers of archery until they are worn. The wearer takes a -2 penalty on all ranged attack rolls. A successful remove curse spell is required before these bracers can be removed.
