Incense of Obsession Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 347, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 541 Aura moderate enchantment CL 6th Slot none; Weight — Description These blocks of incense appear to be incense of meditation. If meditation is conducted while the incense of obsession are burning, the user becomes totally confident that her spell ability is superior due to the magic incense. She uses her spells at every opportunity, even when not needed or useless. The user remains obsessed with her abilities and spells until all have been used or cast, or until 24 hours have elapsed.
