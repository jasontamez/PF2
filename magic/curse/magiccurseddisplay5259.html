Liar's Well - Cursed Items - Magic Equipment Liar's Well Source Classic Treasures Revisited pg. 61 Aura strong conjuration CL 17th Slot none; Price 84,250 gp; Weight — Description When opened, this cursed well of many worlds has a 50% chance of displaying a false image of its destination. For example, the well may appear to open onto the plane of Axis while in reality opening onto a layer of the Abyss, though the difference is not always to the user's detriment. Any creatures emerging from the other side are not subject to the illusion. The false image is defeated by a true seeing spell. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, gate, mirage arcana; Cost 42,125 gp
