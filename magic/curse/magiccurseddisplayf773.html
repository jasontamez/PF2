Ring of Lifebleed Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 349 Aura moderate necromancy CL 10th Slot ring; Weight — Description Before being worn, this ring appears to be a ring of regeneration. Once it is worn in combat, however, the true nature of the ring becomes apparent. Each time the wearer is hit with a melee or ranged weapon attack, he takes an additional 1d4 points of damage. This ring cannot be removed until the wearer dies or by way of a remove curse, wish, or miracle spell. Construction Requirements ring of regeneration; Cost —
