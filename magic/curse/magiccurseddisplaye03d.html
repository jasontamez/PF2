Ring of Clumsiness Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 349, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 542 Aura strong transmutation CL 15th Slot ring; Weight — Description This ring operates exactly like a ring of feather falling. However, it also makes the wearer clumsy. She takes a -4 penalty to Dexterity and has a 20% chance of spell failure when trying to cast any arcane spell that has a somatic component. (This chance of spell failure stacks with other arcane spell failure chances.) Construction Requirements ring of feather falling; Cost —
