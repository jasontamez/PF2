Ring of Spell Devouring Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 349 Aura strong abjuration CL 13th Slot ring; Weight — Description This delicate ring appears to be a ring of spell storing or a ring of spell turning, but instead of aiding casting or counterspelling, the ring hinders such efforts. Each time the wearer casts an arcane spell, he suffers a 25% spell failure chance. Furthermore, the wearer of the ring takes a -4 penalty on Spellcraft checks to identify a spell when attempting to counterspell. The ring can only be removed by a remove curse spell or a similar effect. Construction Requirements ring of spell storing (any) or a ring of spell turning; Cost —
