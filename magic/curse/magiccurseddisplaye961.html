Armor of Arrow Attraction Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 342, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 538 Aura strong abjuration CL 16th Slot armor; Weight 50 lbs. Description Magical analysis indicates that this armor is a normal suit of +3 full plate. The armor works normally with regard to melee attacks but actually attracts ranged weapons. The wearer takes a -15 penalty to AC against ranged weapons. The true nature of the armor does not reveal itself until the character is fired upon in earnest.
