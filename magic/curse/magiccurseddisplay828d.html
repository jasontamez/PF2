Unwieldy Glaive Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 351 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Weight 10 lbs. Description This weapon appears and acts as a +2 glaive, but cannot be used to make attacks of opportunity, and hinders its wielder whenever such an attack is attempted. When its wielder attempts an attack of opportunity, the glaive flails around madly, causing its wielder to become unbalanced. The wielder takes a -2 penalty to AC and on attack rolls until the end of its next turn. Construction Requirements +2 glaive; Cost —
