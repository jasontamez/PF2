Unlucky Figurine Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 351 Aura moderate enchantment CL 11th Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Description This figurine appears to be a figurine of wondrous power, but instead of having the ability to animate and serve its owner, it curses that owner with bad luck. While in possession of this figurine, the owner takes a -2 penalty to AC and CMD, and a -2 penalty on attack rolls, skill checks, ability checks, and saving throws.
