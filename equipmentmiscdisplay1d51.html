Portable bridge - Miscellaneous - Equipment Portable bridge Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57 Price 200 gp; Weight 60 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description When staked down with eight pitons (four on each side of the gap), this rope and slat bridge can support up to 1,500 pounds and span a 30-foot gap. It rolls up into a parcel that can be carried slung over the back or by a pack animal. Convincing an animal to cross a portable bridge requires the come or heel trick.
