Enhanced Coven - Feats Enhanced Coven Source Blood of the Coven pg. 13 By deepening your connection with your coven members, you have made your coven capable of casting more spells each day. Prerequisites: Changeling, coven hex or Coven-Touched. Benefit: Your changeling coven can cast three more spells each day from its list of spell-like abilities. If multiple members of your coven take this feat, the benefits stack.
