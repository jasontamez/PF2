Flanking Foil - Feats Flanking Foil (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 102 Fighting multiple foes is easy for you. Benefit: Whenever you hit an adjacent opponent with a melee attack, until the start of your next turn, that opponent does not gain any flanking bonus on attack rolls while it is flanking you and cannot deal sneak attack damage to you. It can still provide a flank for its allies. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119 When using this feat, you can spend 5 stamina points. If you do, your target cannot flank with its allies against you until the start of your next turn.
