Sleeve holster - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sleeve holster Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 205 Price 100 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Tools Description When worn inside voluminous sleeves, this leather holster allows you to draw a concealed hand crossbow or coat pistol as a move action. The weapon sits on sliders and is drawn directly into the hand. Unlike a wrist sheath, a sleeve holster is bulky enough that it is obvious on close inspection, though under a loose enough garment it might not provoke a reactive Perception check. An individual sleeve holster fits for either a hand crossbow or a coat pistol, but not both.
