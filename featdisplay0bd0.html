Discerning Eye - Feats Discerning Eye Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 46 You are not easily fooled by illusions and forgeries. Prerequisites: Elf or half-elf, keen senses racial trait. Benefit: You receive a +2 racial bonus on saving throws against illusion spells and effects and a +2 bonus on Linguistic checks to detect forgeries. You can use the Linguistic skill to detect forgeries untrained.
