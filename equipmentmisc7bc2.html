Miscellaneous - Equipment Tinctures Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Tinctures are alchemical remedies consisting of reagents dissolved in an alcoholic solution. These mixtures produce potent effects beyond those of an ordinary alchemical concoction, but the increased benefits are not without cost. In addition to requiring more expensive components, tinctures tend to cause harmful effects upon being imbibed. The following are a number of common tinctures. Drawing and drinking a tincture follows the same rules as drawing and drinking a potion. A character trained in Craft (alchemy) can create tinctures. These items weigh one-quarter this amount when made for Small characters. Containers for Small characters also carry one-quarter the normal amount. These items weigh approximately three-quarters this amount when made for Small characters. Containers for Small characters also carry one-quarter the normal amount.
