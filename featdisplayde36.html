Reckless Rage - Feats Reckless Rage Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 155 Your boundless rage grants your blows extra strength, though at the expense of accuracy. Prerequisites: Power Attack; rage or raging song class feature. Benefit: When you use Power Attack while raging or while using raging song, you take an additional -1 penalty on melee attack rolls and combat maneuver checks, and you gain an additional +2 bonus on melee damage rolls. Modify this damage bonus appropriately based on the type of weapon you are using, as normal for Power Attack.
