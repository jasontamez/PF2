Blade Lash - Spells Blade Lash Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 175 School transmutation; Level bloodrager 1, magus 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target your melee weapon Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description Your weapon elongates and becomes whiplike. As part of casting this spell, you can use this weapon to attempt a trip combat maneuver against one creature within 20 feet, and you gain a +10 bonus on your roll, after which the weapon returns to its previous form.
