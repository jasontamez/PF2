Web Bolt - Spells Web Bolt Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 107 School conjuration (creation); Level arcanist 1, bloodrager 1, magus 1, sorcerer 1, witch 1, wizard 1 (drow) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect fist-sized blob of webbing Duration 1 min./level Saving Throw Reflex negates, see text; Spell Resistance no Description You launch a ball of webbing at a target, which must make a save or be affected as if by a web spell occupying only the creature's space. If the creature saves or breaks free of the webbing, the remaining webs dissolve and the square is not considered difficult terrain. The spell has no effect if the target is not on or adjacent to a solid surface that can support the webbing.
