Kitsune Tricks - Feats Kitsune Tricks (Combat) Source Dirty Tactics Toolbox pg. 15 Your quick wit and even quicker maneuvers leave your opponents reeling. Prerequisites: Int 13; Combat Expertise; Improved Dirty Trick; Kitsune Style; base attack bonus +3 or monk level 3rd. Benefit: While you are using the Kitsune Style feat, you can apply two different conditions with a single dirty trick combat maneuver check. Removing both conditions imparted in this way requires only one action.
