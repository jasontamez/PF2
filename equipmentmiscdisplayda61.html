Clear ear - Miscellaneous - Equipment Clear ear Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 15 gp; Weight — Category Black Market Description This green gel is poured into the user's ear and takes effect 2 hours later, enhancing senses and memory but increasing irritability. For 6 hours, the user gains a +2 alchemical bonus on Perception and Knowledge checks and a -2 penalty on all Charisma-based checks.
