Pure Legion Assault - Feats Pure Legion Assault (Combat) Source Spymaster's Handbook pg. 23 The Pure Legion trained you to apply your knowledge of religions to predicting and defeating divine servitors. Prerequisites: Bluff 1 rank, Knowledge (religion) 1 rank, Pure Legion Recruit trait (Pathfinder Player Companion: People of the Sands 21) . Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on saving throws against divine spells and effects and abilities from outsiders. If you must attempt a saving throw against a divine spell, you gain a +1 bonus on attack rolls against the creature that cast the spell
