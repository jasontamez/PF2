Plant Puppet - Wild Talents - Kineticist Plant Puppet Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 59, Occult Origins pg. 9 Element wood; Type utility (Sp); Level 5; Burn 0 You can control plants, causing them to attack your foes. This functions as the aether puppet wild talent, except it controls plants instead of objects. At 12th level, you can grant hardness 5 to Large and larger animated plants.
