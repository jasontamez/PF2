Designating (lesser) - Weapons - Magic Equipment Designating (lesser) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 140 Aura moderate enchantment CL 7th Slot weapon quality; Price +2 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can only be placed on ranged weapons or ammunition. Each time a ranged weapon or ammunition with this ability hits a creature, its magic designates the target. Allies gain a +2 morale bonus on melee attack and melee damage rolls against the designated target for 1 round.
