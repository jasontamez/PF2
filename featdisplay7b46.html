Expressionless - Feats Expressionless Source Heroes of Golarion pg. 6 Other races have difficulty reading your wooden features. Prerequisites: Wyrwood. Benefit: Your lack of emotion make it difficult for non-wyrwoods to relate to you. When dealing with any race other than your own, you gain a +2 bonus on Bluff checks. In addition, creatures of other races take a -2 penalty on Diplomacy and Sense Motive checks attempted against you.
