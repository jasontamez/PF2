Wizard's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wizard's kit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 58 Price 21 gp; Weight 21 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit includes a backpack, a bedroll, a belt pouch, a flint and steel, ink, an inkpen, an iron pot, a mess kit, soap, a spell component pouch, torches (10), trail rations (5 days), and a waterskin. The kit does not contain a spellbook because a wizard begins play with a spellbook and does not need to purchase one.
