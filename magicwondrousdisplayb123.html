Belt of Physical Perfection (+4) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Belt of Physical Perfection Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 210, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 502 Aura strong transmutation; CL 16th Slot belt; Price 16,000 gp (+2), 64,000 gp (+4), 144,000 gp (+6); Weight 1 lb. Description This belt has a large platinum buckle and grants the wearer an enhancement bonus to all physical ability scores (Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution) of +2, +4, or +6. Treat this as a temporary ability bonus for the first 24 hours the belt is worn. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bear's endurance, bull's strength, cat's grace; Cost 8,000 gp (+2), 32,000 gp (+4), 77,000 gp (+6)
