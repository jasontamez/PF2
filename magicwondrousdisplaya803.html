Combat Sandals - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Combat Sandals Source Heroes of the Streets pg. 14 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 9th Slot feet; Price 18,000 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description Worked brown leather straps run up the backs and around the soles of this sturdy set of footwear. The wearer of a pair of combat sandals can charge through allies' squares without difficulty, and allies adjacent to the wearer don't provide cover to targets the wearer attacks with reach or ranged weapons. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, expeditious retreat, freedom of movement, true strike; Cost 9,000 gp
