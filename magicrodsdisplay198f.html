Rod of Grasping Hexes - Rods - Magic Equipment Rod of Grasping Hexes Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 220 Aura strong (no school) CL 17th Slot none; Price 11,000 gp; Weight 5 lbs. Description This rod is crafted from a gnarled branch covered in sharp thorns. Three times per day when a wielder of this rod uses a hex (but not an advanced hex or grand hex), she can use this rod's power to double the range of the hex, so long as the hex has a range measured in feet. Construction Requirements Craft Rod, Reach Spell, creator must possess the hex class feature; Cost 5,500 gp
