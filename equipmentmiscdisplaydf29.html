Spell component pouch - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spell component pouch Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 5 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Tools Description A spellcaster with a spell component pouch is assumed to have all the material components and focuses needed for spellcasting, except for components that have a specific cost, divine focuses, and focuses that wouldn't fit in a pouch. Most spell component pouches are waterproof and can be strung onto a belt or bandolier.
