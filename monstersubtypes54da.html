Monster Subtypes Azata Source Bestiary 6 pg. 302, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 311, Bestiary 2 pg. 306, Bestiary 4 pg. 304, Bestiary 5 pg. 304 Azatas are a race of celestials, or good outsiders, native to chaotic good-aligned Outer Planes. An azata has the following traits (unless otherwise noted in a creature's entry).
  • Darkvision 60 feet and low-light vision.
  • Immunity to electricity and petrification.
  • Resistance to cold 10 and fire 10.
  • Truespeech (Su): All azatas can speak with any creature that has a language, as though using a tongues spell (caster level 14th). This ability is always active.
