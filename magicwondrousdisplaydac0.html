Mithral Rose - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mithral Rose Source Mythic Adventures pg. 154 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot none; Price 9,000 gp; Weight — Description This seemingly delicate silver rose is rigid and nearly unbreakable. When wearing the rose openly on his chest, the wearer treats all of his natural attacks, unarmed attacks, and weapon attacks as if they were made using silver weapons. The wearer can expend one use of mythic power when making a melee attack against an enemy that possesses DR/silver to negate that DR for a number of rounds equal to the wearer's mythic tier. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Mythic Crafter, magic weapon; Cost 4,500 gp
