Shrewd Liason - Feats Shrewd Liason Source Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 97 Your dealings with both pirates and the Red Mantis have taught you to be exceedingly careful. Prerequisites: Mediogalti affinity. Benefit: Opponents do not gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls for flanking you, although they can still sneak attack you. You also gain a +2 bonus on Sense Motive checks made to resist a foe's feinting in combat Bluff checks.
