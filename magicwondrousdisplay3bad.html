Orb of the Waybringer - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Orb of the Waybringer Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 267, Gods and Magic pg. 58 Aura faint evocation; CL 3rd Slot neck; Price 3,900 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This giant pearl is carved with entwined flying dragons and strung on a fine silver chain; the pearl changes size according to the size of the wearer so that its diameter is no greater than the wearer's thumb. Once per day, the wearer can breathe a cone of searing flame that functions as burning hands, except it deals electricity damage instead of fire. If the dragon god Apsu is the wearer's patron, once per day the wearer can use eagle's splendor. On command, the orb glows with a light spell or darkens again. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, eagle's splendor, light, shocking grasp; Cost 1,950 gp
