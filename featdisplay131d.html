Saoc Brethren Scholar - Feats Saoc Brethren Scholar Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 8 You have learned to draw additional power from celestial phenomena, harnessing their influence for longer than their natural duration. Prerequisites: Saoc Brethren Initiate, Knowledge (arcana) 5 ranks, Knowledge (nature) 5 ranks, Knowledge (planes) 5 ranks. Benefit: You increase the duration of effects caused by astrological events. The duration of effects that last less than a day is increased by 50%, while those that last 24 hours or longer last 1 additional day.
