Whisper of Ancients - Phrenic Amplifications - Psychic Whisper of Ancients (Ex) (Heroes of Golarion pg. 13): The psychic can send her consciousness into the Astral Plane for a glimpse of synchronous information. While casting a linked divination spell, the psychic can spend 1 point from her phrenic pool to gain a +2 bonus on any associated skill check. She can spend 2 points from her phrenic pool to instead gain a +4 bonus.
