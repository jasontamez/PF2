Armored coat - Armor - Equipment Armored coat Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 179 Statistics Cost 50 gp Weight 20 lbs. Armor Bonus +4; Max Dex Bonus +3; Armor Check Penalty -2 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 20%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description This sturdy leather coat is reinforced with metal plates sewn into the lining. An armored coat is more cumbersome than light armor but less effective than most medium armors. The advantage of it is that a person can don it or remove it as a move action (there is no "don hastily" option for an armored coat). If worn over other armor, use the better AC bonus and worse value in all other categories; an armored coat has no effect if worn with heavy armor. The only magic effects that apply are those of armor, clothing, or items worn on top.
