Spindle of Perfect Knowledge Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 333, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 314 Aura strong transmutation CL 20th Slot none; Price —; Weight — Description The spindle of perfect knowledge is large, bright, and flawless marquise-cut diamond ioun stone the size of a halfling's fist. The gemstone would be a treasure in itself if it lacked magic, but it is also a powerful ioun stone. The spindle of perfect knowledge functions like a headband of mental superiority +4 and grants 5 ranks in two random Knowledge skills. The wearer can communicate and understand languages as if using comprehend languages, tongues, and telepathy with a range of 100 feet. Destruction The spindle of perfect knowledge is destroyed if worn by a mindless creature for an entire year.
