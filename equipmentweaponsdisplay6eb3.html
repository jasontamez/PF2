Ripsaw glaive, gnome - Weapons - Equipment Ripsaw glaive, gnome Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 9, Gnomes of Golarion pg. 22 Statistics Cost 30 gp Weight 12 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special reach, see text Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Polearms Description The blade of this glaive is serrated and mounted on an axle, around which a heavy cord is wrapped. When the cord is pulled as a move action, the blade spins rapidly for a number of rounds equal to your Strength bonus. While the blade is spinning, you gain a +2 bonus on damage rolls with the weapon. When the blade is not spinning, treat this weapon as a glaive.
