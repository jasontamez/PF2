Disorienting Maneuver - Feats Disorienting Maneuver Source Ultimate Combat pg. 96 Your erratic movements disorient your opponent. Prerequisites: Dodge, Acrobatics 5 ranks. Benefit: If you successfully use Acrobatics to tumble through an opponent's space, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on attack rolls against that opponent until the start of your next turn. If you choose to make a trip attempt against that opponent, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus on your combat maneuver check. This bonus on trip also lasts until the start of your next turn.
