Trapped Beverage Source Kobolds of Golarion pg. 31 Aura faint conjuration; CL 3rd Slot none; Price 300 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This brightly labeled bottle or waterskin could appear to contain any number of different beverages, from absinthe to wine. However, it's actually a magical trap, and when the bottle is opened, a spider swarm (Pathfinder RPG Bestiary 258) pours out. The swarm stays in the area for 3 rounds, then disappears. Detecting that the bottle is trapped requires a successful DC 25 Perception check made by a character with the trapfinding ability, and disabling it requires a successful DC 25 Disable Device check. After being triggered, the trapped beverage remains a mundane bottle or wineskin. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, summon swarm; Cost 150 gp
