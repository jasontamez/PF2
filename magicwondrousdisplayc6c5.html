Gauntlets of the Skilled Maneuver - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Gauntlets of the Skilled Maneuver Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 235 Aura faint transmutation; CL 3rd Slot hands; Price 4,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The wearer of these gauntlets gains a +2 bonus on one type of combat maneuver check (such as bull rush, disarm, or steal) chosen by the creator when the item is created. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, creator must have the appropriate Improved combat maneuver feat; Cost 2,000 gp
