Styx sap - Poisons - Equipment Styx sap Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 145 Price 2,600 gp; Weight — Type contact; Save Fortitude DC 18 Onset 1d4 minutes; Frequency 1/10 minutes for 60 minutes Effect 1d4 Wis damage, and the creature loses its memories from the past 10 minutes, as though they had been erased by the spell modify memory Cure 1 save Description This milky-white sap can be harvested from a rare jungle tree known as a "weeping maiden" in the local tongue. The tree's branches end in long, vine-like tendrils that hang low to the ground and bear large, bitter-tasting red fruit. While the fruit is safe to eat, the sap of the tree carries powerful neurotoxins that cause short-term memory loss in those that consume it. In some cultures, styx sap is occasionally taken deliberately in order to allow one to forget unpleasant or tragic events.
