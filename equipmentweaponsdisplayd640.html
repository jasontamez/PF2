Dorn-dergar, dwarven - Weapons - Equipment Dorn-dergar, dwarven Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 8, Dwarves of Golarion pg. 21 Statistics Cost 50 gp Weight 15 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type B; Special reach, see text Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Flails Description This 10-foot-long chain is weighted at the end by a ball of solid iron the size of a large fist. By adjusting the slack of the chain, the weapon can be used either with or without reach. Changing between using it as a normal weapon and a reach weapon is a move action.
