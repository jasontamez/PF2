Rainbow scarab shell - Poisons - Equipment Rainbow scarab shell Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 18 Price 150 gp; Weight — Type injury; Save Fortitude DC 14 Onset 1 round; Frequency 1/round for 4 rounds Effect 1d4 Strength damage and suffocation (see above) Cure 1 save Description This iridescent toxin is made from the crushed shells of a rare type of Osirian beetle. A creature reduced to 0 Strength by the poison asphyxiates and immediately begins suffocating. Creatures that do not breathe are immune to this suffocation, but not the Strength damage.
