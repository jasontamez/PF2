Lamellar (iron) - Armor - Equipment Lamellar (iron) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, Ultimate Combat pg. 128 Statistics Cost 200 gp Weight 50 lbs. Armor Bonus +7; Max Dex Bonus +0; Armor Check Penalty -7 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 40%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description Lamellar is a type of armor in which small plates of various types of materials are strung together in parallel rows using fine cord. Lamellar plates can be constructed from lacquered leather, horn, or even stone, though suits of iron and steel are the most common. Lamellar armor can be crafted into various shapes, including partial pieces such as breastplates, greaves, or even entire coats. The properties of specific suits and pieces of lamellar armor are determined by their material.
