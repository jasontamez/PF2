Spells Overland Flight Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 317 School transmutation; Level alchemist 5, arcanist 5, investigator 5, magus 5, occultist 5, psychic 5, shaman 5, sorcerer 5, spiritualist 5, summoner 4, summoner (unchained) 5, witch 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 hour/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description This spell functions like a fly spell, except you can fly at a speed of 40 feet (30 feet if wearing medium or heavy armor, or if carrying a medium or heavy load) with a bonus on Fly skill checks equal to half your caster level. When using this spell for long-distance movement, you can hustle without taking nonlethal damage (a forced march still requires Constitution checks). This means you can cover 64 miles in an 8-hour period of flight (or 48 miles at a speed of 30 feet).
