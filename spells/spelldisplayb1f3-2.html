Echolocation - Spells Echolocation Source Ultimate Magic pg. 218 School transmutation [sonic]; Level alchemist 4, arcanist 5, bard 4, druid 4, hunter 4, investigator 4, occultist 4, psychic 5, skald 4, sorcerer 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V Effect Range personal Target you Duration 10 minutes/level Description You can perceive the world by creating high-pitched noises and listening to their echoes. This gives you blindsight to a range of 40 feet. The echo-producing noises are too high-pitched to be heard by most creatures, and can only be detected by dragons, other creatures with this ability (such as bats), and creatures with hearing-based blindsense or blindsight. You cannot use this ability if you are deaf, and cannot detect anything in an area of silence.
