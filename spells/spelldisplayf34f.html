Spellcurse - Spells Spellcurse Source Magic Tactics Toolbox pg. 6 School necromancy [curse]; Level antipaladin 2, cleric 3, inquisitor 2, occultist 4, oracle 3, psychic 3, shaman 4, witch 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a fragment of a destroyed magical item) Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target 1 creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will half; Spell Resistance yes Description You disrupt any spell energy affecting your target, causing that energy to crackle with power and harm the target. The target takes 1d6 points of damage for each spell with a duration of 1 round or greater currently affecting it. The spells themselves are not dispelled or modified.
