Unwitting Ally - Spells Unwitting Ally Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 253 School enchantment (charm) [mind-affecting]; Level bard 0, mesmerist 0, psychic 1, skald 0 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature Duration 1 round Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You befuddle the target's mind. The target has difficulty telling friend from foe for a short period of time. The subject is considered your ally and not your enemies' ally while determining flanking. The subject takes no other hostile action against your enemies due to this spell's effect.
