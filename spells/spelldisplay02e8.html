Reviving Finale - Spells Reviving Finale Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 239 School conjuration (healing); Level bard 3, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S Effect Range 20 ft. Area a 20-ft.-radius burst centered on you Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will half (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You must have a bardic performance in effect to cast this spell. With a flourish, you immediately end your bardic performance, and allies within the area of the spell regain 2d6 hit points. This spell has no effect on undead creatures.
