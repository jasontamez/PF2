Spells Fabricate Bullets Source Ultimate Combat pg. 229 School transmutation; Level arcanist 1, redmantisassassin 1, sorcerer 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 round Components V, S, M (1 pound of lead or other soft metal worth 2 gp) Effect Range touch Target 1 pound of soft metal Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You convert 1 pound of lead into bullets. When you cast this spell, you decide whether you create normal sling bullets, firearm bullets, or firearm pellets. The spell creates two sling bullets, 30 firearm bullets, or 10 uses of pellets.
