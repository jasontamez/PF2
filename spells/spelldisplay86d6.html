Sign of the Dawnflower - Spells Sign of the Dawnflower Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 25 School transmutation; Level cleric 0, druid 0, hunter 0, oracle 0, paladin 1, ranger 1, warpriest 0 (Sarenrae) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S, F (your holy symbol, or a depiction of either Sarenrae's holy symbol or the goddess herself) Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target one creature/level Duration 1 hour/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description As message, except the only information you can transmit is that you are a worshiper of Sarenrae. Once notified, the target can reply that he understands your sign. The spell transmits meaning, not sound, and transcends language barriers.
