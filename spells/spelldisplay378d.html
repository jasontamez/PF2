Purified Calling - Spells Purified Calling Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 237 School conjuration (healing); Level summoner 4, summoner (unchained) 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a stick of incense) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute Description Your eidolon is restored to full health when you summon it. Upon casting this spell, you must immediately begin the ritual to summon your eidolon. Upon completion of the ritual, your eidolon appears at full hit points and without any damage or penalties to its ability scores, regardless of its previous condition. In addition, any temporary negative conditions affecting your eidolon are immediately removed. Permanent conditions and ability drain are not affected by this spell.
