Fair is Foul - Spells Fair is Foul Source Blood of the Coven pg. 21 School transmutation [curse]; Level antipaladin 2, arcanist 4, cleric 3, oracle 3, shaman 3, sorcerer 4, spiritualist 3, warpriest 3, witch 2, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a newt eye) Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration permanent (D) Saving Throw Fort negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You curse the target with a disfiguring or painful mutation. The target gains one random deformity from the Arcane Malignancies table (see page 10). The curse bestowed by this spell cannot be dispelled but can be removed with a break enchantment, limited wish, miracle, remove curse, or wish spell.
