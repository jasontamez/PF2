Chains of Fire - Spells Chains of Fire Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 131 School evocation [fire]; Level arcanist 6, magus 6, sorcerer 6, wizard 6 (ifrit) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, F (a drop of oil and a small piece of flint) Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Targets one primary target, plus one secondary target/level (each of which must be within 30 ft. of the primary target) Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Reflex half; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions like chain lightning, except as noted above, and the spell deals fire damage instead of electricity damage.
