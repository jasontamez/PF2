Resist Starvation - Spells Resist Starvation Source Villain Codex pg. 21 School transmutation; Level antipaladin 1, cleric 1, oracle 1, shaman 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (crumb of food) Effect Range touch Target one living creature touched Duration 1 day/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description If the target doesn't eat on the day this spell is cast on it, the DC to avoid taking nonlethal damage from starvation on the following day doesn't increase by 1. This spell fails if the target hasn't already attempted at least one Constitution check to avoid starvation.
