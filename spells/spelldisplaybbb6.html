Hunter's Howl - Spells Hunter's Howl Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 228 School necromancy [emotion, fear, mind-affecting]; Level hunter 1, ranger 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 20 ft. Area 20-ft.-radius burst Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance none Description With a single primal howl, you strike fear in your opponents. Until the end of the spell's duration, you treat those affected by it as if they were your favored enemy, gaining a +2 bonus on weapon attack and damage rolls against them, and a +2 bonus on Bluff, Knowledge, Perception, Sense Motive, and Survival checks against them. If an affected creature is already one of your favored enemies, it is shaken instead. This is a mind-affecting fear effect.
