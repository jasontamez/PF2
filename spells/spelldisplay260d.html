Distracting Cacophony - Spells Distracting Cacophony Source Ultimate Magic pg. 217 School evocation [sonic]; Level arcanist 3, bard 2, psychic 3, skald 2, sorcerer 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Area 20-ft. spread Duration 1 round/level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description The air fills with loud and discordant noise, making concentration difficult. Casting a spell in this area of cacophony requires a concentration check (DC 15 + the level of the spell being cast). Any other concentration checks in the area have their DCs increased by 5. The DC of Perception checks involving hearing is likewise increased by 5.
