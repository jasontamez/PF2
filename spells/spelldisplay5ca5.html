Slick Walls - Spells Slick Walls Source Monster Hunter's Handbook pg. 31 School conjuration (creation); Level arcanist 2, bard 2, magus 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, summoner 2, summoner (unchained) 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (drop of oil) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Area 30-ft.-radius emanation centered on a point in space Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You coat all walls and ceilings within the spell's area with a slick substance. The DC of all Climb checks for the affected surfaces increases by twice your caster level (maximum +20). Creatures cannot take 10 on Climb checks while scaling the affected surfaces, even if they have a climb speed or other ability that would normally allow taking 10. This spell has no effect on magical walls or surfaces, such as the sides of a hole created with create pit.
