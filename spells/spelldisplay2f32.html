Horn of Pursuit - Spells Horn of Pursuit Source Ultimate Magic pg. 223 School evocation [sonic]; Level bard 1, hunter 1, inquisitor 1, paladin 1, ranger 1, skald 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S Effect Range personal Effect 3 peals of a horn Duration 1 round Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You create the sound of a large hunting horn, blowing up to three notes as loud as a roaring dragon. These notes can be heard up to 2 miles away in typical outdoor conditions. You can make these sounds at any point during the spell's duration as a free action, and each sounding can be short or long, allowing you to send very simple coded messages.
