Persuasive Goad - Spells Persuasive Goad Source Ultimate Magic pg. 231 School necromancy [pain]; Level inquisitor 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration instantaneous and 1 minute Saving Throw Fortitude negates; Spell Resistance yes Description Your eyes flash and the target takes 1d6 points of nonlethal damage.
