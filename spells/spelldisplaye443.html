Free Spirit - Spells Free Spirit Source Inner Sea Temples pg. 23 School abjuration; Level bard 3, cleric 3, druid 3, hunter 3, medium 3, oracle 3, shaman 3, skald 3, spiritualist 3, warpriest 3, witch 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level Description This spell allows you to channel the essence of Cayden Cailean's philosophy, giving you the benefits of a freedom of movement spell and granting you a +4 bonus on saving throws against fear effects. The spell also makes you drunk. For the duration of the spell, you take a -2 penalty on attack rolls, ability checks, skill checks, and concentration checks. If you somehow remove or avoid these penalties, the spell's duration ends.
