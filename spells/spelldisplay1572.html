Ray of Frost - Spells Ray of Frost Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 330 School evocation [cold]; Level arcanist 0, magus 0, sorcerer 0, wizard 0 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect ray Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description A ray of freezing air and ice projects from your pointing finger. You must succeed on a ranged touch attack with the ray to deal damage to a target. The ray deals 1d3 points of cold damage.
