Seismic Fissure - Spells Seismic Fissure Source Wilderness Origins pg. 27 School transmutation [earth]; Level druid 7, shaman 7 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Effect 10-ft.-by-80-ft. fissure in the earth, 40 ft. deep Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Reflex negates; Spell Resistance no Description With a thrust of tectonic energy, you split the earth. Each creature standing in the area where the fissure opens must succeed at a Reflex saving throw to avoid falling into the rift. Those standing adjacent to the fissure when it is created must succeed at a Reflex save or be knocked prone by tremors. This spell is effective only when cast on an area of dirt, earth, sand, or stone.
