Spells Hide from Animals Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 296 School abjuration; Level druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1, shaman 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S, DF Effect Range touch Targets one creature touched/level Duration 10 min./level (D) Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes Description Animals cannot sense the warded creatures. Even extraordinary or supernatural sensory capabilities, such as blindsense, blindsight, scent, and tremorsense, cannot detect or locate warded creatures. Animals simply act as though the warded creatures are not there. If a warded character touches an animal or attacks any creature, even with a spell, the spell ends for all recipients.
