Bestow Grace - Spells Bestow Grace Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 205 School abjuration; Level paladin 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range touch Target one good creature touched Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw Will (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description With this spell you can bestow your divine grace on another good creature for a short amount of time, infusing that creature with a portion of your holy virtue. When you touch the subject, you grant that creature a sacred bonus to its saving throws equal to its Charisma bonus (if any) on all saving throws.
