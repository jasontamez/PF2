Sealed Life, Greater - Spells Sealed Life, Greater Source Occult Adventures pg. 185 School abjuration; Level spiritualist 6 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions as sealed life, except it additionally provides the benefits of death ward and renders its targets immune to effects that extract or transfer its soul, such as possession and trap the soul. This spell prevents a soul from returning to its body if it has already departed, until the spell's duration expires.
