Enemy Insight - Spells Enemy Insight Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 181 School divination; Level hunter 2, ranger 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Targets you plus one willing creature/3 levels, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You forge a mental bond between yourself and the other targets, each of which must have an Intelligence score of 3 or higher. Choose one of your favored enemy types (such as goblinoids or magical beasts). You grant the targets half your favored enemy bonus against that type of creature. If any target moves out of range, the spell ends for it.
