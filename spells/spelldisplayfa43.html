Detect Aberration - Spells Detect Aberration Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 215 School divination; Level druid 1, hunter 1, psychic 1, ranger 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Area cone-shaped emanation Duration concentration, up to 10 minutes/level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like detect animals or plants, except it detects creatures of the aberration type.
