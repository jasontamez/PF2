Revelation - Spells Revelation Source Seekers of Secrets pg. 17 School divination; Level arcanist 3, bard 2, cleric 3, oracle 3, skald 2, sorcerer 3, warpriest 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 round Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 round/level; see text Description You gain a brief understanding of the workings of a single puzzle, device, or trap, gaining an insight bonus equal to your caster level (maximum +10) on checks to disable, manipulate or solve the object in question. Your insight is ineffable; you are unable to communicate your understanding to others. At the GM's option, this spell may instead give you a clue or other piece of useful information. In this case you may communicate the clue to others and retain knowledge of it after the spell's duration has expired.
