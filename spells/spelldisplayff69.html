Stage Fright - Spells Stage Fright Source Arcane Anthology pg. 27 School enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, fear, mindaffecting]; Level antipaladin 3, arcanist 3, bard 3, inquisitor 3, mesmerist 3, psychic 3, skald 3, sorcerer 3, witch 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target 1 creature/level, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You fill your targets with the sudden fear of failure. A creature that fails its saving throw takes a -4 penalty on ability checks, skill checks, and any checks that require concentration (such as casting a spell in difficult circumstances or operating a complex device).
