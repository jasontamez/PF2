Spells Thoughtsense Source Occult Adventures pg. 190 School divination [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 5, medium 4, mesmerist 4, psychic 4, sorcerer 5, spiritualist 4, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, M (a bit of dried brain tissue) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level Description You automatically detect and locate conscious creatures within 60 feet, as if you possessed the blindsight ability. Nondetection, mind blank, and similar effects can block this effect. Thoughtsense can distinguish between sentient (Intelligence 3 or greater) and non-sentient (Intelligence 1-2) creatures, but otherwise provides no information about the creatures it detects.
