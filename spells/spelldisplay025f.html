Mythic Break Source Mythic Adventures pg. 87 You can target up to 1 Medium or smaller object per caster level. Augmented (3rd): If you expend two uses of mythic power, target objects gain the broken condition on a successful save and are destroyed on a failed save. This doesn't affect mythic objects or objects held or carried by mythic creatures. Targets all Medium or smaller objects in a 30-ft. burst centered on you Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes (object) Description You release a burst of destructive energy.
