Weapons Against Evil - Spells Weapons Against Evil Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 245, Faiths of Purity pg. 29 School transmutation; Level cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, paladin 1, warpriest 1 (Iomedae) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Targets one weapon/level, no two of which can be more than 20 ft. apart Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Fortitude negates (harmless, object); Spell Resistance yes (harmless, object) Description Each weapon this spell affects each shines with pale light that dimly illuminates a 5-foot square. These weapons also ignore the DR of evil creatures that have DR 5 or lower, as long as the damage reduction is not DR/epic.
