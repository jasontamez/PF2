Summon Barghest II - Spells Summon Barghest I Source Pathfinder Comics #10 pg. 26 School conjuration (summoning) [evil]; Level cleric 4, oracle 4, warpriest 4 Casting Casting Time 1 round Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect one summoned barghest Duration 1 round/level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell summons a barghest from the Abyssal realm of Basalfeyst to serve the caster, functioning similarly to summon monster save that it may only be used to summon a single barghest. Although the summoned barghest does not have the feed ability, if you are at least caster level 9th the summoned barghest appears with 2 growth points (and with it the appropriate bonuses to its attacks, CMB, saves, skill checks, hit points, and caster level).
