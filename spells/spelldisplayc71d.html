Ventriloquism - Spells Ventriloquism Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 365 School illusion (figment); Level arcanist 1, bard 1, medium 1, mesmerist 1, psychic 1, redmantisassassin 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, spiritualist 1, summoner 1, summoner (unchained) 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, F (parchment rolled into cone) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect intelligible sound, usually speech Duration 1 min./level (D) Saving Throw Will disbelief (if interacted with); Spell Resistance no Description You can make your voice (or any sound that you can normally make vocally) seem to issue from someplace else. You can speak in any language you know. With respect to such voices and sounds, anyone who hears the sound and rolls a successful save recognizes it as illusory (but still hears it).
