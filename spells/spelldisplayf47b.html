Thundering Drums - Spells Thundering Drums Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 249 School evocation [sonic]; Level bard 3, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 15 ft. Area cone-shaped burst Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude partial; Spell Resistance yes Description You strike the ground in front of you, filling the area in front of you with the thunder of pounding drums. Any creature in the area takes 1d8 points of sonic damage per caster level (maximum 5d8) and is knocked prone. A successful Fortitude save halves the damage and negates being knocked prone.
