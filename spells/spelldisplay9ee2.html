Repair Undead, Mass - Spells Repair Undead, Mass Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 191 School necromancy; Level arcanist 5, sorcerer 5, spiritualist 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Targets one undead creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will half (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description This spell functions as repair undead, except as noted above, and it cures 1d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +20) on each selected creature.
