Spells Undetectable Alignment Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 363 School abjuration; Level alchemist 2, antipaladin 2, bard 1, cleric 2, inquisitor 2, investigator 2, mesmerist 1, oracle 2, paladin 2, psychic 2, skald 1, warpriest 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature or object Duration 24 hours Saving Throw Will negates (object); Spell Resistance yes (object) Description An undetectable alignment spell conceals the alignment of an object or a creature from all forms of divination.
