Vitriolic Mist - Spells Vitriolic Mist Source Ultimate Magic pg. 248 School evocation [acid]; Level alchemist 4, arcanist 4, bloodrager 4, investigator 4, sorcerer 4, summoner 4, summoner (unchained) 4, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a piece of lemon rind) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 round/level (D) Description This functions as fire shield, except it wreathes you in yellow or green acidic mist instead of hot or cold flames. The spell deals acid damage to attackers and protects you against acid damage. This spell does not shed light.
