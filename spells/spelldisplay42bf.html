Respectful Quiet - Spells Respectful Quiet Source Inner Sea Races pg. 222 School illusion (glamer); Level bard 3, cleric 5, inquisitor 3, oracle 5, skald 3, warpriest 5 (Chelaxian) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Targets any number of creatures in a 30-ft.-radius burst Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description All targets are rendered silent, as per silence. The silence affects the entirety of each creature but does not extend beyond. However, the silence is one-way'sounds from outside the creatures' spaces can still enter. Quieted creatures gain a +10 circumstance bonus on Stealth checks.
