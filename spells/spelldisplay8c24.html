Sense Spirit Magic - Spells Sense Spirit Magic Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 191 School divination; Level shaman 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 24 hours Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You gain greater sensitivity to magic associated with your primary spirit and wandering spirits (any spells on the spirit magic spell lists for these spirits, as well as spell-like abilities that duplicate the effects of those spells). You gain a +10 circumstance bonus on Spellcraft checks to identify these spells if they are cast within the area, and a +2 circumstance bonus on saving throws to resist the effects of such spells. If any of these spells require an attack roll, you are never considered flat-footed against such attacks.
