Rally Point - Spells Rally Point Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 237 School enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, good, mind-affecting]; Level paladin 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 5 ft. Area one 5-ft. square Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You create a spot that has the power to briefly hearten any good creature who comes into contact with it. A good creature who enters this square (even if simply as part of its normal move) gains a +2 morale bonus on attacks, saving throws, and 2 temporary hit points per caster level for 1 round. Nongood creatures gain no benefit from this spell. A creature cannot benefit more than once from the same casting of this spell.
