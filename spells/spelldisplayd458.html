Litany of Thunder - Spells Litany of Thunder Source Ultimate Combat pg. 235 School evocation [language-dependent, sonic]; Level antipaladin 4, inquisitor 5, paladin 4 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 round Saving Throw Fortitude negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You call down a thunderous boom upon your enemy. The target becomes deafened until the condition is removed, and is confused for 1 round. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.
