Spells Litany of Escape Source Ultimate Combat pg. 234 School conjuration (teleportation) [language-dependent]; Level antipaladin 3, inquisitor 4, paladin 3 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one willing creature that is grappled Duration instantaneous Saving Throw no; Spell Resistance yes Description With a powerful prayer, you call upon the servants of your god to whisk a friend out of a grapple. The target loses the grappled and pinned conditions and is teleported 10 feet. If there is no available space for the target to teleport to, the spell fails.
