Speak with Animals - Spells Speak with Animals Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 346 School divination; Level bard 3, druid 1, hunter 1, psychic 2, ranger 1, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 min./level Description You can ask questions of and receive answers from animals, but the spell doesn't make them any more friendly than normal. Wary and cunning animals are likely to be terse and evasive, while the more stupid ones make inane comments. If an animal is friendly toward you, it may do some favor or service for you.
