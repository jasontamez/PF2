Spells Longstrider Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 305 School transmutation; Level druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a pinch of dirt) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 hour/level (D) Description This spell gives you a foot enhancement bonus to your base speed. It has no effect on other modes of movement, such as burrow, climb, fly, or swim. Longstrider, Greater Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 186 School transmutation; Level druid 3, hunter 3, ranger 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a pinch of dirt) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 hour/level (D) Description This functions as longstrider, except it gives you a foot enhancement bonus to your base speed and a foot enhancement bonus to your other modes of movement (burrow, climb, fly, swim, and so on). It does not affect movement modes you do not actually have-for example, if you do not have a swim speed, this spell does not grant you a swim speed.
