Shield of Wings - Spells Shield of Wings Source Paths of the Righteous pg. 9 School transmutation [fire]; Level cleric 3, inquisitor 3, oracle 3, paladin 3, ranger 3, warpriest 3 (Ragathiel) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level (see below) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description You gain five burning wings that grant a fly speed equal to the number of wings ?? 10 (average maneuverability) for an initial fly speed of 50 feet. The first time in a round where you would take fire damage, a wing vanishes (reducing the fly speed granted) and you gain fire resistance 30 for the remainder of the round. When you are down to one wing (and a fly speed of 10 feet), your maneuverability drops to poor. When you lose your final wing, you become immune to fire damage for 1 additional minute, after which the effects of this spell end.
