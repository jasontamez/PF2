Bloatbomb - Spells Bloatbomb Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 175 School necromancy [acid, death]; Level antipaladin 4, arcanist 4, cleric 4, oracle 4, sorcerer 4, warpriest 4, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target living creature touched Duration instantaneous and 1 minute/level (see text) Saving Throw Fortitude negates or Reflex half (see text); Spell Resistance yes Description The target creature dies if it fails a Fortitude saving throw and its Hit Dice are no more than half your caster level. If it dies, its corpse rapidly putrefies. The next creature to touch the corpse within 1 minute per level of the target's death causes the corpse to explode in a 10-foot-radius burst, dealing 3d6 points of acid damage (Reflex half).
