Blood Rage - Spells Blood Rage Source Orcs of Golarion pg. 28 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level adept 3, arcanist 3, bard 2, cleric 3, oracle 3, skald 2, sorcerer 3, warpriest 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a drop of blood from each creature to be affected) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one willing living creature per 2 levels, no two of which may be more than 30 ft. apart Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless Description The targets of this spell become angrier as they fight, the pain of their wounds fueling their strength. An affected creature gains a cumulative +2 morale bonus to Strength and a cumulative -1 penalty to AC for every 5 points of damage it takes (maximum +10 Strength, -5 AC) for the duration of the spell. These bonuses last until the spell expires or the target falls unconscious.
