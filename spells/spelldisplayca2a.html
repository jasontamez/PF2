Detect Good - Spells Detect Good Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 267 School divination; Level adept 1, cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, shaman 1, spiritualist 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 60 ft. Area cone-shaped emanation Duration concentration, up to 10 min./level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like detect evil, except that it detects the auras of good creatures, clerics or paladins of good deities, good spells, and good magic items, and you are vulnerable to an overwhelming good aura if you are evil.
