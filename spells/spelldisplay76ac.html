Antiplant Shell - Spells Antiplant Shell Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 243 School abjuration; Level druid 4, hunter 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 10 ft. Area 10-ft.-radius emanantion, centered on you Duration 1 min./level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description The antiplant shell spell creates an invisible, mobile barrier that keeps all creatures within the shell protected from attacks by plant creatures or animated plants. As with many abjuration spells, forcing the barrier against creatures that the spell keeps at bay strains and collapses the field.
