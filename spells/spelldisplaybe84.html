Cackling Skull - Spells Cackling Skull Source Ultimate Magic pg. 210 School illusion (glamer); Level witch 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one skull Duration permanent until discharged Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions as magic mouth, except rather than creating an illusory mouth on any surface, it affects a skull. In addition, when the skull's magic is triggered, it releases wild and unnerving cackles before speaking its message. All creatures that can hear the cackles must save or become shaken for 1d4 rounds.
