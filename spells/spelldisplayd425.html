Burdened Thoughts - Spells Burdened Thoughts Source Giant Hunter's Handbook pg. 26 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 3, druid 2, hunter 2, shaman 2, sorcerer 3, witch 2, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a lead bearing wrapped in clay) Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target one creature Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You fill a creature's mind with the realization of its earthly weight and responsibilities. Disheartened and wearied by these thoughts, the creature gains heavy encumbrance and is unable to fly (if already flying, it must land as soon as possible). If the creature is three or more size categories larger than you, it's also staggered.
