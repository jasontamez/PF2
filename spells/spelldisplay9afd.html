Spells Prying Eyes, Greater Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 326 School divination; Level arcanist 8, psychic 8, sorcerer 8, witch 8, wizard 8 Casting Casting Time 1 minute Components V, S, M (a handful of crystal marbles) Effect Range 1 mile Effect 10 or more levitating eyes Duration 1 hour/level; see text (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like prying eyes, except that the eyes can see all things as they actually are, just as if they had true seeing with a range of 120 feet. Thus, they can navigate darkened areas at normal speed. Also, a greater prying eye's maximum Perception modifier is +25 instead of +15.
