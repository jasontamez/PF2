Miasmal Dread - Spells Miasmal Dread Source Inner Sea Races pg. 221 School enchantment (compulsion) [fear, mind-affecting]; Level antipaladin 2, bard 2, bloodrager 2, inquisitor 3, skald 2 (Half-Orc) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 30 feet Area cone-shaped burst Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description A cloud of sickly green mist pours from your mouth and coalesces around creatures in the cone. Attempt a single Intimidate check to demoralize as part of casting the spell, comparing the result against the DC to Intimidate each foe within the area. You demoralize all foes whose DC you meet or exceed.
