Billowing Skirt - Spells Billowing Skirt Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 21 School transmutation; Level arcanist 2, bard 2, inquisitor 2, occultist 2, shaman 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, F (a skirt, kilt, or gown) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level Description A skirt, kilt, or gown you are wearing enables you to control your falls through the air. A creature wearing the enchanted item of clothing falls slowly as if affected by feather fall and can attempt a DC 15 Fly skill check as a move action to hover in place for 1 round.
