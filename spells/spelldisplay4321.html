Jester's Jaunt - Spells Jester's Jaunt Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 230 School conjuration (teleportation); Level bard 3, mesmerist 3, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target one living creature Duration instantiations Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You teleport the target to a space you can see within 30 feet of the target. The destination must be on solid ground, and the teleportation cannot end in a space that is by nature hazardous to the creature you are teleporting.
