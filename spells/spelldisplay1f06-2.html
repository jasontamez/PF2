Purging Finale - Spells Purging Finale Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 237 School conjuration (healing); Level bard 3, skald 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You must have a bardic performance in effect to cast this spell. With a flourish, you immediately end your bardic performance, removing one of the following conditions on a creature within range affected by your bardic performance: cowering, dazzled, exhausted, paralyzed, shaken, or stunned.
