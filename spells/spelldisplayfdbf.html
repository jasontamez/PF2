Mantle of the Magic Warriors - Spells Mantle of the Magic Warriors Source Inner Sea Races pg. 220 School transmutation [good]; Level arcanist 5, druid 5, hunter 5, shaman 5, sorcerer 5, wizard 5 (Mwangi) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target one good-aligned creature/3 levels Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You weave golden masks shaped like animals around the targets' faces, imbuing each target with an animal spirit that matches her strength of character.
