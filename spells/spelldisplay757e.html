False Face - Spells False Face Source Heroes of the High Court pg. 25 School transmutation (polymorph); Level alchemist 1, arcanist 1, bard 1, inquisitor 1, investigator 1, mesmerist 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, witch 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a folded-paper mask) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 hour/level Description This spell alters details of your appearance so that you are no longer recognizable as yourself. Your new appearance is within the norms for your race, ethnicity, and gender. You lack control over the details of your appearance; however, when casting false face, you can choose an appearance you previously used, allowing you to maintain specific identities.
