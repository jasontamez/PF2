Spells Hallucinatory Terrain Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 293 School illusion (glamer); Level arcanist 4, bard 4, mesmerist 4, psychic 4, redmantisassassin 4, skald 4, sorcerer 4, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 10 minutes Components V, S, M (a stone, a twig, and a green leaf) Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Area one 30-ft. cube/level (S) Duration 2 hours/level (D) Saving Throw Will disbelief (if interacted with); Spell Resistance no Description You make natural terrain look, sound, and smell like some other sort of natural terrain. Structures, equipment, and creatures within the area are not hidden or changed in appearance.
