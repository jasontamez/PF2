Celestial Companion - Spells Celestial Companion Source Plane-Hopper's Handbook pg. 12 School transmutation; Level arcanist 2, druid 2, hunter 2, paladin 1, ranger 2, shaman 2, sorcerer 2, summoner 1, summoner (unchained) 1, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF/M Effect Range touch Target animal companion, eidolon, familiar, or mount Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You bestow divine protection on your companion. The target gains spell resistance equal to 5 plus half your caster level against spells and spell-like effects produced by evil creatures. The target also gains a +1 sacred bonus on saving throws and DR 5/evil or silver. Creatures under the effects of this spell often take on a celestial appearance, such as golden feathers or a radiant aura, but they are not truly celestial creatures.
