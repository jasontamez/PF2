Spells Sleet Storm Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 344 School conjuration (creation) [cold]; Level arcanist 3, bloodrager 3, druid 3, hunter 3, magus 3, shaman 3, sorcerer 3, witch 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M/DF (dust and water) Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Area cylinder (40-ft. radius, 20 ft. high) Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description Driving sleet blocks all sight (even darkvision) within it and causes the ground in the area to be icy. A creature can walk within or through the area of sleet at half normal speed with a DC 10 Acrobatics check. Failure means it can't move in that round, while failure by 5 or more means it falls (see the Acrobatics skill for details). The sleet extinguishes torches and small fires.
