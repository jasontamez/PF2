Insanity - Spells Insanity Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 301 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 7, mesmerist 6, psychic 7, sorcerer 7, witch 7, wizard 7 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target one living creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description The affected creature suffers from a continuous confusion effect, as the spell.
