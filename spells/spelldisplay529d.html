Telekinetic Maneuver - Spells Telekinetic Maneuver Source Occult Adventures pg. 188 School transmutation; Level arcanist 4, magus 4, psychic 3, sorcerer 4, spiritualist 4, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration concentration (up to 1 round/level) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions as telekinesis, but it can be used only to perform a bull rush, disarm, drag, grapple (including pin), reposition, steal, or trip combat maneuver.
