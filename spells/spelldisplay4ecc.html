Frigid Touch - Spells Frigid Touch Source Ultimate Magic pg. 221 School evocation [cold]; Level arcanist 2, bloodrager 2, druid 2, hunter 2, magus 2, sorcerer 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell causes your hand to glow with a pale blue radiance. Your melee touch attack deals 4d6 points of cold damage and causes the target to be staggered for 1 round. If the attack is a critical hit, the target is staggered for 1 minute instead.
