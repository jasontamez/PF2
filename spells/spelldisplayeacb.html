Sound Burst - Spells Sound Burst Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 346 School evocation [sonic]; Level bard 2, cleric 2, occultist 2, oracle 2, skald 2, warpriest 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, F/DF (a musical instrument) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Area 10-ft.-radius spread Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude partial; Spell Resistance yes Description You blast an area with a tremendous cacophony. Every creature in the area takes 1d8 points of sonic damage and must succeed on a Fortitude save to avoid being stunned for 1 round. Creatures that cannot hear are not stunned but are still damaged.
