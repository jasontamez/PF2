Instant Enemy - Spells Instant Enemy Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 229 School enchantment; Level hunter 3, ranger 3 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature that is not your favored enemy. Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description With this spell you designate the target as your favored enemy for the remainder of its duration. Select one of your favored enemy types. For the duration of the spell, you treat the target as if it were that type of favored enemy for all purposes.
