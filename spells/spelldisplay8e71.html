Forced Repentance - Spells Forced Repentance Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 224 School enchantment (compulsion) [emotion, mind-affecting]; Level inquisitor 4, paladin 4 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one evil creature without the evil subtype Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You force an evil creature that does not have the evil subtype to momentarily reflect on its past actions and be overcome by grief and conscience. The target immediately drops prone and begins to loudly confess all of its sins and transgressions to the caster for the duration of the spell. The spell immediately ends if you move out of line of sight or if the target is attacked.
