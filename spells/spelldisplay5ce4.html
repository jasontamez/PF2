Spells Scorching Ray Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 337 School evocation [fire]; Level adept 2, arcanist 2, bloodrager 2, magus 2, sorcerer 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect one or more rays Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description You blast your enemies with a searing beam of fire. You may fire one ray, plus one additional ray for every four levels beyond 3rd (to a maximum of three rays at 11th level). Each ray requires a ranged touch attack to hit and deals 4d6 points of fire damage. The rays may be fired at the same or different targets, but all rays must be aimed at targets within 30 feet of each other and fired simultaneously. Mythic Scorching Ray Source Mythic Adventures pg. 107 Each ray's damage increases to 6d6 points of fire damage. The first ray bypasses fire resistance and immunity.
