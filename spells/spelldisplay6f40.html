Aspect of the Falcon - Spells Aspect of the Falcon Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 203 School transmutation (polymorph); Level druid 1, hunter 1, ranger 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level Description You take on an aspect of a falcon. Your eyes become wide and raptor-like, and you grow feathers on the sides of your head. You gain a +3 competence bonus on Perception checks, a +1 competence bonus on ranged attacks, and the critical multiplier for your bows and crossbows becomes 19-20/??3. This effect does not stack with any other effect that expands the threat range of a weapon, such as the Improved Critical feat or a keen weapon.
