Qualm - Spells Qualm Source Ultimate Combat pg. 241 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 2, bard 2, inquisitor 2, medium 2, mesmerist 2, psychic 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 minute/level or until discharged Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description The target is suddenly beset with unexplainable doubts about the effectiveness of its actions and the righteousness of its cause. The creature takes a -10 penalty on its ability checks, skill checks, and concentration checks, until the duration ends, or until it spends its entire turn doing absolutely nothing (it spends a full-round action gaining focus). Spending an entire turn doing nothing discharges the spell.
