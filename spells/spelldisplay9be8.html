Infuse Self - Spells Infuse Self Source Planar Adventures pg. 41 School transmutation (polymorph); Level alchemist 3, arcanist 3, bard 3, bloodrager 3, investigator 3, magus 3, skald 3, sorcerer 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 10 minutes/level (D) Description When you cast this spell, you assume the form of a native outsider from the following list: aasimar, aphorite, duskwalker, fetchling, ganzi, ifrit, oread, shabti, suli, sylph, tiefling, or undine. You gain a +2 size bonus to one physical ability score of your choice. If the race has resistance to one or more energy types, you gain energy resistance 5 to your choice of one of those types. If the race gains a racial bonus on saving throws or skills, you gain those bonuses. If the race has darkvision or low-light vision, you gain those qualities.
