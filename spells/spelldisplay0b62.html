Chord of Shards - Spells Chord of Shards Source Ultimate Magic pg. 211 School evocation; Level bard 1, skald 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 15 ft. Area cone-shaped burst Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Reflex negates; Spell Resistance no Description During your bardic performance, you can strike a chord whose notes transform into a shower of razor sharp, crystalline shards. The shards deal 2d6 points of piercing damage to all creatures caught in the area of effect. Mythic Chord of Shards Source Mythic Adventures pg. 88 The damage dealt increases to 2d8 points of piercing damage and bypasses all damage reduction. Any creature damaged by the shards takes 1d8 points of sonic damage.
