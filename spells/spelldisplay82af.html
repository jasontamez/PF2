Shield Speech, Greater - Spells Shield Speech Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 25 School abjuration; Level arcanist 1, bard 1, cleric 1, druid 1, hunter 1, oracle 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, warpriest 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range 10 ft. Target you and one other creature Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You can securely communicate with one creature within range. So long as your target remains within 10 feet of you, you and it can speak freely to each other without fear of being overheard. Communications that involve shield speech cannot be spied on, such as with a divination spell, and observers of the conversation can tell you are communicating but cannot read lips or hear the conversation unless they are the target of the spell. If you or the target speak to any other creatures, they can hear you normally (only communication between you and the target is protected). The spell does not shield writing, sign language, or any form of communication other than speaking.
