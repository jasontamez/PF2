Spells Freedom Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 287 School abjuration; Level arcanist 9, psychic 9, sorcerer 9, wizard 9 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) or see text Target one creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes Description The subject is freed from spells and effects that restrict movement, including binding, entangle, grappling, imprisonment, maze, paralysis, petrification, pinning, sleep, slow, stunning, temporal stasis, and web. To free a creature from imprisonment or maze, you must know its name and background, and you must cast this spell at the spot where it was entombed or banished into the maze.
