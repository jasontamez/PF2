Spells Share Memory Source Ultimate Magic pg. 238 School divination; Level arcanist 2, bard 2, medium 2, mesmerist 2, occultist 2, psychic 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target you and one creature touched Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You momentarily link your mind with the target and share a single memory of no longer than 1 minute. You can show the target one of your memories, show the target one of its own memories, or view one of the target's memories.
