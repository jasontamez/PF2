Seducer's Eyes - Spells Seducer's Eyes Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 240, Faiths of Balance pg. 28 School enchantment (charm) [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 2, bard 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 (Calistria) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range personal Target you Duration 10 minutes/level (D) Description You gain a bonus on Charisma-based skill checks equal to 1 + 1 for every 4 caster levels you possess (to a maximum of +5), but only when interacting with those who might conceivably find you sexually attractive. You do not gain this bonus against those you or your allies are attacking or threatening.
