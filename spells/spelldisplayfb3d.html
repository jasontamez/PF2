Hex Ward - Spells Hex Ward Source Ultimate Magic pg. 223 School abjuration; Level inquisitor 1, occultist 1, shaman 1, witch 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration 1 hour/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You give the target a +4 resistance bonus on saving throws against witch hexes. Mythic Hex Ward Source Mythic Adventures pg. 98 The target gains immunity to one witch hex (but not a major hex or grand hex) of your choice. Augmented (3rd): If you expend two uses of mythic power, instead of granting immunity to one witch hex, you can grant immunity to two hexes or one major hex. If you're at least 6th tier and expend three uses of mythic power, you instead grant immunity to three hexes, two major hexes, or one grand hex.
