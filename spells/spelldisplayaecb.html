With the Wind - Spells With the Wind Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 237 School abjuration [air]; Level arcanist 2, bloodrager 2, druid 2, hunter 2, magus 2, occultist 2, ranger 2, sorcerer 2, spiritualist 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 minute/level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You create eddies in the air around the subject that protect it from being checked or blown away. The target can't be moved by winds of less than windstorm force unless it wishes to be.
