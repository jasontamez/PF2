Aura of Distraction - Spells Aura of Distraction Source Psychic Anthology pg. 10 School enchantment [mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 2, psychic 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range Area 20-ft.-radius emanation centered on you Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You emit an aura of subsonic noise and mental imagery that makes concentrating difficult. Any creature within the aura that fails its save takes a -5 penalty on concentration checks. A creature must attempt the Will save upon entering the area of the aura, and the penalty ends as soon as the creature leaves the aura. A creature that succeeds at its save is immune to the effects of the aura for this casting of the spell, even if it leaves and reenters the aura, but must attempt another Will save each additional time this spell is cast. You are immune to your own aura of distraction.
