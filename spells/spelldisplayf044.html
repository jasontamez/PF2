Antilife Shell - Spells Antilife Shell Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 242 School abjuration; Level cleric 6, druid 6, hunter 6, oracle 6, psychic 6, shaman 6, warpriest 6 Casting Casting Time 1 round Components V, S, DF Effect Range 10 ft. Area 10-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you Duration 1 min./level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description You bring into being a mobile, hemispherical energy field that prevents the entrance of most types of living creatures. The effect hedges out animals, aberrations, dragons, fey, giants, humanoids, magical beasts, monstrous humanoids, oozes, plants, and vermin, but not constructs, elementals, outsiders, or undead. This spell may be used only defensively, not aggressively. Forcing an abjuration barrier against creatures that the spell keeps at bay collapses the barrier.
