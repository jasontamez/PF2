Rubberskin - Spells Rubberskin Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 31 School transmutation; Level alchemist 4, arcanist 5, investigator 4, magus 4, sorcerer 5, witch 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a piece of rubber or drop of tar) Effect Range personal Duration 10 minutes/level Description Your skin becomes more elastic, distributing crushing damage efficiently. For the duration of the spell, all bludgeoning and falling damage you take is converted into nonlethal damage. This spell has no effect if you're immune to nonlethal damage.
