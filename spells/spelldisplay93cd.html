Spells Detect Law Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 267 School divination; Level adept 1, cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, shaman 1, spiritualist 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 60 ft. Area cone-shaped emanation Duration concentration, up to 10 min./level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like detect evil, except that it detects the auras of lawful creatures, clerics of lawful deities, lawful spells, and lawful magic items, and you are vulnerable to an overwhelming lawful aura if you are chaotic.
