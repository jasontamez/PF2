Spells Dispel Chaos Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 271 School abjuration [lawful]; Level cleric 5, inquisitor 5, oracle 5, paladin 4, shaman 5, warpriest 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range touch Target or Targets you and a touched evil creature from another plane, or you and an enchantment or evil spell on a touched creature or object Duration 1 round/level or until discharged, whichever comes first Saving Throw see text; Spell Resistance see text Description This spell functions like dispel evil, except that you are surrounded by constant, blue lawful energy, and the spell affects chaotic creatures and spells rather than evil ones.
