Abjuring Step - Spells Abjuring Step Source Ultimate Combat pg. 221 School abjuration [force]; Level alchemist 1, arcanist 1, bard 1, investigator 1, magus 1, occultist 1, psychic 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a rabbit's foot) Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 round/level or until discharged Description This spell creates a subtle and complicated force effect that is most effective when used by a still or slow-moving spellcaster or formula user. While you are subject to this spell, you can take two 5-foot steps each round and still cast spells and use spell-like abilities without provoke attacks of opportunity. This spell automatically discharges as soon as you make an attack, cast a harmful spell against another creature, or move more than 5 feet with a single move action. If the action that discharges the spell also provokes attacks of opportunity, that action still provokes attacks of opportunity as normal.
