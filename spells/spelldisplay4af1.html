Fool's Forbiddance - Spells Fool's Forbiddance Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 224 School abjuration [mind-affecting]; Level bard 6, psychic 8, skald 6 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a ring of keys) Effect Range 10 ft. Area 10-ft.-radius spherical emanation centered on you Duration concentration Saving Throw Will partial; Spell Resistance yes Description Through your antics and performance, you create an area of warding that adversely affects all enemies that dare enter it. When an enemy creature enters the area it must make an immediate Will saving throw. If it fails, the creature is confused as long as it is in the area and for 1 round after it leaves. If it succeeds on the saving throw, the creature is staggered as long as it is in the area and for 1 round after it leaves.
