Spells Wail of the Banshee Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 365 School necromancy [death, sonic]; Level arcanist 9, psychic 9, shaman 9, sorcerer 9, witch 9, wizard 9 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature/level within a 40-ft.-radius spread Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude negates; Spell Resistance yes Description When you cast this spell, you emit a terrible, soul-chilling scream that possibly kills creatures that hear it (except for yourself). The spell affects up to one creature per caster level, inflicting 10 points of damage per caster level. Creatures closest to the point of origin are affected first.
