Prediction of Failure - Spells Prediction of Failure Source Ultimate Magic pg. 232 School divination [curse, fear, mind-affecting]; Level arcanist 8, occultist 6, psychic 8, sorcerer 8, witch 8, wizard 8 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration permanent or 1 round/level (see text) Saving Throw Will partial; Spell Resistance yes Description You wrack the target's body and mind with the anguish and suffering of every bitter failure it will ever experience, rendering it permanently shaken and sickened. A successful Will save reduces the duration to 1 round per level. If the target is a spellcaster, failing this saving throw means it also gains a random minor spellblight (see Ultimate Magic).
