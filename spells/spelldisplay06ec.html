Fester, Mass - Spells Fester Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 221 School necromancy; Level inquisitor 3, witch 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (rotted meat) Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature Duration 1 round/level or 1 round; see text Saving Throw Fortitude partial; Spell Resistance yes Description Necrotic energy permeates the target, blocking healing abilities. The subject gains spell resistance equal to 12 + your caster level against effects that restore hit points or grant temporary hit points. In addition, any healing provided by effects that ignore spell resistance (such as fast healing, regeneration, and some spells) are halved. If the target succeeds on a Fortitude saving throw, fester lasts only a single round.
