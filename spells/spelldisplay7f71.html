Toilsome Chant - Spells Toilsome Chant Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 19 School enchantment (compulsion)[mind-affecting]; Level bard 1, skald 1 (dwarf) Casting Casting Time see text Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature Duration see text Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description You can cast this spell as part of the action to begin an inspire competence bardic performance. The benefit of inspire competence persists for as long as is necessary to complete the target's next skill check using the chosen skill (up to a maximum of 1 hour per caster level), even if you cease your bardic performance.
