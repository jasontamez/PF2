Sadomasochism - Spells Sadomasochism Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 239 School necromancy [pain]; Level antipaladin 3, arcanist 4, bloodrager 4, cleric 3, inquisitor 3, magus 4, oracle 3, sorcerer 4, warpriest 3, witch 4, wizard 4 (Zon-Kuthon) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 1 minute/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates, see text; Spell Resistance yes Description While subject to this spell, any time you are dealt damage, your attacker must roll damage for the attack twice and take the higher roll, but the attacker must also succeed at a Will saving throw or become demoralized (Core Rulebook 99) for 1 round. Each time you deal damage to a creature demoralized by this spell, you roll damage twice and take the higher result.
