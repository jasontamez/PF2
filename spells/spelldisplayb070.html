Investigative Mind - Spells Investigative Mind Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 185 School enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; Level alchemist 2, arcanist 2, bard 2, investigator 2, medium 2, mesmerist 2, occultist 2, psychic 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range personal Target you Duration 10 minutes/level or until discharged Description Your mind is able to correlate information effectively. When attempting an Appraise, Knowledge, Linguistics, or Spellcraft check, you can roll twice and take the higher result. If you have an ability that allows you to roll twice, you cannot use that ability and this benefit on the same roll. The spell is discharged once you have used its benefit a number of times equal to your caster level.
