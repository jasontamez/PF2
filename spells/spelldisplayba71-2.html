Undine's Curse - Spells Undine's Curse Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 179 School necromancy [curse, evil]; Level arcanist 1, sorcerer 1, witch 1, wizard 1 (undine) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 hour/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description The target loses its body's natural ability to breathe automatically. As long as it remains conscious and is able to take physical actions, it keeps breathing and is able to function normally. If it is ever unconscious (including sleeping) or unable to take physical actions, it stops breathing and stops breathing, must hold its breath, and might begin to suffocate. Creatures that do not have to breathe are immune to this spell.
