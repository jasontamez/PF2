Technomancy - Spells Technomancy Source Technology Guide pg. 11 School divination; Level arcanist 1, bard 1, skald 1, sorcerer 1, wizard 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (oil stirred with a coil of wires) Effect Range 60 ft. Target cone-shaped emanation Duration 3 rounds/level Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions as detect magic, except it detects the presence of technological objects instead of magical objects. The spell grants you the Technologist feat for the purposes of attempting Knowledge (engineering) checks to identify the properties of technological items in your possession. If you already possess this feat, you gain a +10 bonus on Knowledge (engineering) checks to identify item properties.
