Curse of Magic Negation - Spells Curse of Magic Negation Source Ultimate Magic pg. 215 School abjuration [curse]; Level arcanist 4, cleric 5, inquisitor 4, mesmerist 3, occultist 4, oracle 5, psychic 4, sorcerer 4, warpriest 5, witch 4, wizard 4 Casting Casting Time 1 round Components V, S, M (powdered lead and platinum worth 250 gp) Effect Range medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level) Target one creature Duration 10 minute/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance no Description You disrupt the target creature's ability to draw upon magical energies. For the duration of the spell, it gains the negated spellblight (see page 97). Dispel checks to remove the spellblight take a -5 penalty due to this spell's interference with magical energies.
