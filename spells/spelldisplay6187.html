Spells Fox's Cunning Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 286 School transmutation; Level alchemist 2, arcanist 2, bard 2, investigator 2, medium 2, psychic 2, redmantisassassin 2, skald 2, sorcerer 2, summoner 2, summoner (unchained) 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M/DF (hairs or dung from a fox) Effect Range touch Target you Duration 1 min./level Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes Description The target becomes smarter. The spell grants a +4 enhancement bonus to Intelligence, adding the usual benefits to Intelligence-based skill checks and other uses of the Intelligence modifier. Wizards (and other spellcasters who rely on Intelligence) affected by this spell do not gain any additional bonus spells for the increased Intelligence, but the save DCs for spells they cast while under this spell's effect do increase. This spell doesn't grant extra skill ranks.
