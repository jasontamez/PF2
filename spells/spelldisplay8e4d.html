Old Salt's Curse - Spells Old Salt's Curse Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 69 School necromancy [curse]; Level druid 5, hunter 5, mesmerist 4, witch 5 (human) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a flask of seawater) Effect Range touch Target one creature Duration permanent Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description You inflict a curse of the roiling sea upon the target, making it permanently sickened. Anytime the target is on or in the water more than a mile from shore, it also becomes staggered with seasickness. This curse cannot be dispelled, though remove curse or break enchantment can negate it.
