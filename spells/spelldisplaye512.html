Spells Detect Chaos Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 266 School divination; Level adept 1, cleric 1, inquisitor 1, oracle 1, shaman 1, spiritualist 1, warpriest 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range 60 ft. Area cone-shaped emanation Duration concentration, up to 10 min./level (D) Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like detect evil, except that it detects the auras of chaotic creatures, clerics of chaotic deities, chaotic spells, and chaotic magic items, and you are vulnerable to an overwhelming chaotic aura if you are lawful.
