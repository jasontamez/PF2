Spells Blight Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 250 School necromancy; Level arcanist 5, druid 4, hunter 4, shaman 5, sorcerer 5, witch 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, DF Effect Range touch Target plant touched Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude half; see text; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell withers a single plant of any size. An affected plant creature takes 1d6 points of damage per level (maximum 15d6) and may attempt a Fortitude saving throw for half damage. A plant that isn't a creature doesn't receive a save and immediately withers and dies. This spell has no effect on the soil or surrounding plant life.
