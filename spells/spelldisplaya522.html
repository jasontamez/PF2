Stricken Heart - Spells Stricken Heart Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 194 School necromancy [death]; Level arcanist 2, inquisitor 2, mesmerist 2, psychic 2, shaman 3, sorcerer 2, spiritualist 2, witch 2, wizard 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration instantaneous Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell covers your hand with a writhing black aura. As part of casting the spell, you can make a melee touch attack that deals 2d6 points of negative energy damage and causes the target to be staggered for 1 round. If the attack is a critical hit, the target is staggered for 1 minute instead. Creatures immune to precision damage are immune to the staggered effect.
