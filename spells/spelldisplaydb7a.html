Spirit Call - Spells Spirit Call Source Advanced Class Origins pg. 31 School enchantment; Level druid 1, hunter 1, shaman 1 Casting Casting Time 1 full round Components V, S, DF Effect Range 100 ft. Effect 100-ft.-radius spread centered on you Duration 1 minute/level Description You call out to nature spirits associated with your spirit (not your wandering spirit) if you are a shaman, or your domain if you are a druid, beckoning them forth to pay attention to your current location. For the duration of the spell, all spells from your spirit magic list or domain list that are cast within the affected area have their caster level increased by 1, and all spellcasters casting such spells receive a +4 insight bonus on their concentration checks for those spells only.
