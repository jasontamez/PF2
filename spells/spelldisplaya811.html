Agonizing Rebuke - Spells Agonizing Rebuke Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 126 School illusion (phantasm) [emotion, mind-affecting, pain]; Level antipaladin 2, cleric 3, inquisitor 3, mesmerist 3, oracle 3, psychic 3, warpriest 3, witch 3 (hobgoblin) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one living creature Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes. Description With a word and a gesture, you instill such apprehension about attacking you in your target that doing so causes it mental distress and pain. Each time the target makes an attack against you, targets you with a harmful spell, or otherwise takes and action that would harm you, it takes 2d6 points of nonlethal damage.
