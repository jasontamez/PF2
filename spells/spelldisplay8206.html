Spells Mirage Arcana Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 314 School illusion (glamer); Level arcanist 5, bard 5, mesmerist 5, psychic 5, skald 5, sorcerer 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Area one 20-ft. cube/level (S) Duration concentration +1 hour/level (D) Saving Throw Will disbelief (if interacted with); Spell Resistance no Description This spell functions like hallucinatory terrain, except that it enables you to make any area appear to be something other than it is. The illusion includes audible, visual, tactile, and olfactory elements. Unlike hallucinatory terrain, the spell can alter the appearance of structures (or add them where none are present). Still, it can't disguise, conceal, or add creatures (though creatures within the area might hide themselves within the illusion just as they can hide themselves within a real location).
