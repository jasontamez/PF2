Soothing Word - Spells Soothing Word Source GameMastery Condition Cards pg. 0 School conjuration (healing); Level cleric 2, druid 2, hunter 2, inquisitor 2, oracle 2, paladin 2, ranger 2, warpriest 2, witch 2 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target 1 creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless) Description The target of this spell causes several conditions that he suffers from to be lessened in severity by one step. If he is nauseated, he is instead sickened. If he is stunned, he is instead dazed. If he is exhausted, he is fatigued. If he is frightened, he is shaken. If he is paralyzed, he is staggered. The duration of the effect is unchanged; the effect is simply reduced to the lesser version. This spell has no effect on conditions not listed above.
