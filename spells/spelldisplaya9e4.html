Spells Horrid Wilting Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 298 School necromancy; Level arcanist 8, shaman 8, sorcerer 8, witch 8, wizard 8 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M/DF (a bit of sponge) Effect Range long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level) Targets living creatures, no two of which can be more than 60 ft. apart Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude half; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell evaporates moisture from the body of each subject living creature, causing flesh to wither and crack and crumble to dust. This deals 1d6 points of damage per caster level (maximum 20d6). This spell is especially devastating to water elementals and plant creatures, which instead take 1d8 points of damage per caster level (maximum 20d8).
