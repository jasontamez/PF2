Nauseating Dart - Spells Nauseating Dart Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 189 School conjuration (creation) [poison]; Level druid 1, hunter 1, witch 1 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Effect one poisonous stinger Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude partial (see text); Spell Resistance no Description You spit a poisonous stinger around the size of a quill at a creature within range, as though you had fired a dart from a blowgun. You must succeed at a ranged attack to hit your target. The stinger deals 1d2 points of piercing damage and poisons the target, causing it to become nauseated for 1 round unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save.
