Death from Below - Spells Death from Below Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 39 School abjuration; Level arcanist 2, bard 3, bloodrager 2, skald 3, sorcerer 2, wizard 2 (gnome) Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range touch Target creature touched Duration 1 round/level Description You grant the target a dodge bonus to its Armor Class against attacks from larger creatures. The bonus is equal to +1 for every size category the attacker is larger than the target of the spell, to a maximum of +1 per 3 caster levels. If the spell's target is a gnome, the maximum bonus is equal to +1 per 2 caster levels.
