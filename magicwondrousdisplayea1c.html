Bone Razor - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Bone Razor Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 283 Aura faint necromancy; CL 5th Slot none; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This jagged bone knife seems too thin and frail for combat but easily cuts flesh. If used to perform a coup de grace on a helpless living creature and the creature dies from the attack, the creature's flesh begins to peel off and its bones animate as a skeleton under the command of the bearer of the razor. The creature's flesh is not destroyed, but decays at a steady rate. The bearer of the razor can spend 1 minute reattaching the flesh to the animate skeleton, which ends the necromantic magic and results in a normal corpse (though a corpse of a person killed or mutilated by knife wounds). If the bearer uses the razor to flense and animate another creature's bones, the previous animate skeleton is immediately destroyed. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, animate dead; Cost 2,500 gp
