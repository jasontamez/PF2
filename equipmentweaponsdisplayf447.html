Double-barreled shotgun - Weapons - Equipment Double-barreled shotgun Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 43, Ultimate Combat pg. 140 Statistics Cost 7,000 gp Weight 15 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type B and P; Special scatter Misfire 1-2; Capacity 2 Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Description This twin-barreled shotgun can be shot one barrel at a time, or both together as one attack. A double shot that fires bullets is inaccurate, and takes a -4 penalty on both attacks. A double shot that fires bullets targets only a single creature and increases the damage of each barrel to 2d6 points (Small) or 2d8 points (Medium) for a total of 4d6 or 4d8 points of damage. A doublebarreled shotgun uses metal cartridges (loaded with either a bullet or pellets) as ammunition. This is an advanced firearm.
