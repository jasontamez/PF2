Gnome Weapon Focus - Feats Gnome Weapon Focus (Combat) Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 37 Your extensive training with traditional gnome weapons gives you an advantage. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, gnome, proficient with all martial weapons. Benefit: You gain a +1 bonus on attack rolls with gnome weapons (weapons with "gnome" in the title). Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 120 When you make a successful attack with a gnome weapon against an opponent that is at least one size category larger than you, you can spend up to 5 stamina points. If you do, you gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points you spent.
