Damned Soldier - Feats Damned Soldier Source Book of the Damned pg. 178 Your favored boons reflect your martial predilections. Prerequisites: Fiendish Obedience. Benefit: When you gain boons through the Fiendish Obedience feat, you can choose a sentinel boon or an exalted boon, but once you select this boon, it can't be changed. You gain a +1 profane bonus on weapon damage rolls with your god's favored weapon.
