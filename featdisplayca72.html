Psychovore Strike - Feats Psychovore Strike (Combat) Source Pathfinder: Spiral of Bones #3 pg. 29 You can land blows that confuse your enemies. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Psychovore Style, Sense Motive 9 ranks. Benefit: When you strike the creature you are gaining a dodge bonus to AC against from Psychovore Style with your unarmed strike, you can force that creature to attempt a Will saving throw (DC = 10 + 1/2 your character level + your Wisdom modifier), in addition to dealing damage normally. A creature who fails its save is confused for 1d6 rounds. You must declare that you are using this feat before you make your attack roll (thus, a failed attack roll ruins the attempt). You can use this ability a number of times per day equal to 1 + your Wisdom bonus (minimum once per day), and no more than once per round. This is a mind-affecting effect, and a creature confused in this way never attacks you.
