Carriage - Miscellaneous - Equipment Carriage Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 100 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 3 cp Category Transport, Land Description This four-wheeled vehicle can transport as many as four people within an enclosed cab, plus two drivers. In general, two horses (or other beasts of burden) draw it. A carriage comes with the harness needed to pull it.
