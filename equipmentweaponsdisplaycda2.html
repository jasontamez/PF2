Featherweight dart - Weapons - Equipment Featherweight dart Source Ultimate Intrigue pg. 232 Statistics Cost 1 gp (10) Weight — Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description These extremely small and lightweight darts function like blowgun darts, except they are used to deliver poisons surreptitiously instead of dealing damage. When you hit with a dart, the poison is injected into the target while the dart breaks off and falls away, leaving the wound hard to spot. Any amount of damage reduction, hardness, or similar protection prevents the featherweight dart from delivering its injected poison. Anyone examining the target must succeed at a DC 20 Perception check to find the puncture wound, while finding the dart itself at the location where it fell requires a successful DC 15 Perception check. This ammunition can be used in both blowguns and wrist launchers.
