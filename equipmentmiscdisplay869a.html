Squire's outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Squire's outfit Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 20 Price 5 gp; Weight 8 lbs. Category Clothing Description This outfit includes heavy woolen pants, a tunic, leather boots, a belt, sturdy gloves, a cap, and a heavy tabard bearing the colors or sigil of a noble house or organization. Many organizations provide new squires this outfit for free, with the expectation they wear it during their duties.
