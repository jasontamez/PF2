Galley Slave - Feats Galley Slave Source Taldor, Echoes of Glory pg. 28 Your experience as a galley slave has given you some unique insight into the world of sailing and piracy. Prerequisites: Profession (sailor) 2 ranks, current or former galley slave. Benefit: You get a +2 circumstance bonus on Profession (sailor) and Use Rope checks made while aboard a ship with oars and rowers. When fighting on a ship, your first hit each battle gets a +2 damage bonus.
