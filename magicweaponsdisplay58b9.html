Ghost Touch - Weapons - Magic Equipment Ghost Touch Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 142, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 469 Aura moderate conjuration CL 9th Slot weapon quality; Price +1 bonus; Weight — Description A ghost touch weapon deals damage normally against incorporeal creatures, regardless of its bonus. An incorporeal creature's 50% reduction in damage from corporeal sources does not apply to attacks made against it with ghost touch weapons. The weapon can be picked up and moved by an incorporeal creature at any time. A manifesting ghost can wield the weapon against corporeal foes. Essentially, a ghost touch weapon counts as both corporeal or incorporeal. This special ability can only be placed on melee weapons and ammunition.
