Ranger Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 89 A character who chooses ranger as his secondary class gains the following secondary class features. Track: At 3rd level, he gains the track class feature, treating his character level as his effective ranger level. Favored Enemy: At 7th level, he gains the 1st favored enemy class feature. Favored Terrain: At 11th level, he gains the 1st favored terrain class feature. Expert Tracker: At 15th level, he gains the woodland stride and swift tracker class features. Quarry: At 19th level, he gains the quarry class feature.
