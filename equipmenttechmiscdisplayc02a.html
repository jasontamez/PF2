Flashlight - Miscellaneous - Technology Flashlight Source Technology Guide pg. 45 Price 30 gp Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge/8 hours Description When activated, a flashlight creates a beam of normal light in a 60-foot cone. It also increases the light level in the area beyond this initial cone by one step, out to a 120-foot cone. It does not increase the light level in normal light or bright light. A flashlight has no effect in areas of magical darkness. Construction Craft DC 16; Cost 15 gp Craft Technological Item, production lab
