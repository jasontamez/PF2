Miscellaneous - Equipment Hourglass Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 181, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 25 gp (1 hour), 20 gp (1 minute), 10 gp (6 seconds); Weight 1 lb. (1 hour), 1/2 lb. (1 minute), — (6 seconds) Description The standard hourglass takes 1 hour to empty the sand from the upper chamber; smaller ones that mark time more precisely are also readily available, in 1-minute and 6-second sizes. Hourglasses tracking more than an hour are rare.
