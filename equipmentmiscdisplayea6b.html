Desert kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Desert kit Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 9 Price 30 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Category Kits Description This kit contains tools necessary for staying cool and hydrated in hot, dry terrain. It includes a 10-foot-by-10-foot piece of canvas, 3 doses of blain bane (see above), a hot weather outfit, a signal whistle, a scarf, and four waterskins.
