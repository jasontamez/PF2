Wading boots - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wading boots Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 245 Price 50 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Clothing Description These waterproof boots are felt lined and have a thin layer of metal around the soles. The top of each boot ends just before the knee, with a cord for tying the top shut over clothing or exposed skin. These boots assist in prolonged hikes through deep mud or shallow pools, granting the wearer a +1 circumstance bonus on Profession (fisher) checks while fishing in shallow water.
