Shark Style - Feats Shark Style (Combat, Style) Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 59 You fight like a shark, shredding your foes. Prerequisites: Improved Unarmed Strike, Swim 3 ranks, base attack bonus +3 or monk level 3rd. Benefit: You can deal piercing or bludgeoning damage with your unarmed strikes. Your piercing unarmed strikes and bite attacks deal an additional 1d6 points of bleed damage.
