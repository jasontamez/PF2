Special Attacks pounce, rend (2 claws, 1d8+18), trample (2d8, DC 30) Statistics Str 35, Dex 18, Con 24, Int 2, Wis 11, Cha 5 Base Atk +12; CMB +26; CMD 40 Feats Acrobatic, Cleave, Diehard, Endurance, Improved Critical (claw), Iron Will, Power Attack, Run Skills Acrobatics +11, Climb +25, Perception +7, Stealth +3, Swim +19 Ecology Environment cold hills or mountains Organization solitary or pair Treasure none Description Warcats of Rull are massive creatures with the shapes and habits of great cats but the armored skin of armadillos. Weighing 4,000 pounds and stretching 25 feet from nose to the base of its tail, a warcat of Rull is a dangerous predator with a large territory. Wild warcats of Rull hunt alone or with a single mate, and have been known to take down prey ranging from megafauna to giants and even young dragons. The Ice Tooth orc clan catches warcats of Rull as cubs and trains them to serve the clan.
