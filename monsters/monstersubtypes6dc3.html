Monster Subtypes Chaotic Source Bestiary 6 pg. 303, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 311, Bestiary 2 pg. 306, Bestiary 3 pg. 304, Bestiary 4 pg. 304, Bestiary 5 pg. 304 This subtype is usually applied to outsiders native to the chaotic-aligned Outer Planes. Most creatures that have this subtype also have chaotic alignments; however, if their alignments change, they still retain the subtype. Any effect that depends on alignment affects a creature with this subtype as if the creature had a chaotic alignment, no matter what its alignment actually is. The creature also suffers effects according to its actual alignment. A creature with the chaotic subtype overcomes damage reduction as if its natural weapons and any weapons it wields were chaotic-aligned (see Damage Reduction).
