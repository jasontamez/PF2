Melee heavy pick +9 (1d6+4/x4), short sword +9 (1d6+6/19-20) Special Attacks phantom trench, phantom wall Statistics Str 18, Dex 15, Con -, Int 13, Wis 10, Cha 11 Base Atk +5; CMB +9; CMD 21 Feats Combat Expertise, Iron Will, Lightning Reflexes, Two-Weapon Fighting Skills Climb +11, Disable Device +6, Knowledge (engineering) +8, Perception +10, Stealth +9 Languages Common, Jistka Ecology Environment any ruins (Cheliax or Rahadoum) Organization solitary, squad (2-10), or company (12-24 forsaken arbalesters, defenders, and foot soldiers) Treasure standard (hide armor, heavy pick, short sword) Special Abilities Phantom Trench (Su) Three times per day as a full-round action, a forsaken defender can create an extradimensional trench, as create pit (Reflex DC 14 avoids), except the pit must be in a contiguous shape made of up to four 5-foot squares. Phantom Wall (Su) Three times per day as a standard action, a forsaken defender can create a wall from loose rocks and bricks, as wall of stone, except the maximum area of the wall is 20 feet and the wall is always 10 feet high and 1 inch thick. The wall persists for 4 rounds. Description Long ago, these defense specialists planned and supervised the construction of field fortifications for the Jistka Imperium.
