Fiery (CR +0 or +1) Monster Summoner's Handbook pg. 19 Acquired/Inherited Template Acquired Simple Template Yes Usable with Summons Yes - Requires the feat Versatile Summon Monster or Versatile Summon Nature's Ally Fiery creatures are native denizens of the Elemental Planes of Fire, and act as conduits to the burning energies of their home plane. This template can be applied only to a non-outsider that has none of the following subtypes: air, cold, earth, fire, or water. Creatures with a swim speed can't be fiery creatures. A fiery creature's CR increases by 1 only if the base creature has 5 or more HD. Rebuild Rules: Type gains the fire subtype; Senses gains darkvision 60 ft.; Defensive Abilities gains DR as noted on the table below; Attacks gains bonus fire damage as noted on the table below on attacks with natural weapons and metal weapons.
