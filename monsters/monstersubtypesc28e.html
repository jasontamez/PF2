Monster Subtypes Water Source Bestiary 6 pg. 308, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 313, Bestiary 2 pg. 309, Bestiary 3 pg. 308, Bestiary 4 pg. 308, Bestiary 5 pg. 310 This subtype is usually used for outsiders with a connection to the Elemental Plane of Water. Creatures with the water subtype always have swim speeds and can move in water without making Swim checks. A water creature can breathe underwater and can usually breathe air as well. Water creatures treat the Swim skill as a class skill.
