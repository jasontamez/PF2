Monster Families Mortic Source Pathfinder #139: The Dead Road pg. 86 Mortics are humanoids afflicted with the physiology and appetites of undead. All mortics are pulled between two worlds: as living humanoids, they crave community and friendship, but their necromantic nature forces them to indulge in inhuman hungers and commit violence against the living. As a result, most mortics lurk at the edge of civilization or isolate themselves with others of their own kind. Although living creatures, mortics can suspend their living processes temporarily, becoming more like undead. Members Angheuvore (CR 2), Etioling (CR 10), Gurgist (CR 6), Jitterbone (CR 4), Lifeleecher (CR 8), Relictner (CR 12)
