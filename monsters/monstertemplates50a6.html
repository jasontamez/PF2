Accursed (CR +1) Horror Adventures pg. 248 Acquired/Inherited Template Acquired Simple Template Yes Usable with Summons No An accursed creature suffers under a powerful curse and can spread a spiteful curse of its own to others. Choose a thematic curse that you can convey to the players, but don't change the creature's statistics. You can use the sample curses as inspiration. Quick Rules: Two claw attacks that deal 1d4 points of damage (for Medium creatures); natural attacks cause bestow curse (DC = 10 + 1/2 creature's HD + creature's Cha modifier, CL = creature's HD) on a successful hit (this can inflict any effect of bestow curse or spread the creature's personal curse). Rebuild Rules: Melee two claw attacks that each deal 1d4 points of damage (for Medium creatures); Special Attacks natural attacks cause bestow curse (DC = 10 + 1/2 creature's HD + creature's Cha modifier, CL = creature's HD) on a successful hit (this can inflict any effect of bestow curse or spread the creature's personal curse); Special Qualities the creature is under a particular curse.
