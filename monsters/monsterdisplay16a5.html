Special Attacks constrict (4d6+19) Statistics Str 36, Dex 14, Con 23, Int 1, Wis 13, Cha 2 Base Atk +9; CMB +26 (+30 grapple); CMD 39 (can't be tripped) Feats Dodge, Improved Critical (bite), Improved Initiative, Power Attack, Skill Focus (Perception), Weapon Focus (bite) Skills Climb +21, Perception +22, Swim +21 Ecology Environment warm swamps Organization solitary or pair Treasure none Description Of the multitude of slithering predators that infest jungles, forests, and swamps, few inspire such terror as the giant anaconda.
