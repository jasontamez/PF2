Description The quetzalcoatlus is perhaps the largest of the pterosaurs, a category of flying reptile common in regions where dinosaurs dwell. Known to some as the "swamp stalker" and others by the misnomer "dragonling," it is truly an awe-inspiring sight in the air, with an impressive 40-foot wingspan.
