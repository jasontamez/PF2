Special Attacks ambush pit, constrict (2d6+11), poison, pounce Statistics Str 33, Dex 13, Con 20, Int -, Wis 12, Cha 2 Base Atk +11; CMB +26 (+30 grapple); CMD 37 (49 vs. trip) Skills Climb +15, Perception +5, Stealth -3 (+1 in deserts); Racial Modifiers +4 Climb, +4 Perception, +8 Stealth (+12 in deserts) Ecology Environment warm deserts Organization solitary Treasure none Special Abilities Ambush Pit (Ex) A giant emperor scorpion can spend 1 minute digging itself into the soil or other ground debris. When dug in, the scorpion has improved cover granting it a +10 bonus on Stealth checks. Poison (Ex) Sting-injury; save Fort DC 22; frequency 1/round for 6 rounds; effect 1d4 Str and 1d4 Dex; cure 1 save. The save DC is Constitution-based.
