Wings of Protection - Monsters Wings of Protection The iron automaton flying overhead is little more than a great pair of reptilian wings, at least fifty feet across. Its body resembles a long, thin rope, coiling and corkscrewing, with a featureless blunt head and tapered tail. This weightlessness provides the wings of protection a +4 bonus on opposed grappling checks when a grappled opponent attempts to escape a grapple. Description Wings of protection (singular and plural) were created to guard the skies above Dragonfall from any intruder. A wings of protection coils itself around its victims to crush the life from them.
