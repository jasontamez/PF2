Monster Subtypes Air Source Bestiary 6 pg. 302, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 310, Bestiary 2 pg. 306, Bestiary 3 pg. 303, Bestiary 4 pg. 304, Bestiary 5 pg. 303 This subtype is usually used for outsiders that have a connection to the Elemental Plane of Air. Air creatures always have fly speeds and usually have perfect maneuverability. Air creatures treat Fly as a class skill.
