Statistics Str 16, Dex 13, Con 15, Int 1, Wis 13, Cha 2 Base Atk +2; CMB +6; CMD 17 Feats Improved Bull Rush, Power Attack Skills Perception +6, Swim +15 Ecology Environment warm oceans Organization solitary, pair, or flight (3-12) Treasure none Description Often found buried in mud, sand, or sea grass, this normally passive creature raises its tail above its body like a scorpion when cornered or injured. Its stinger is a barbed spine with two grooves allowing the toxin to enter the wound. While often found in bays and estuaries, some breeds of stingray can make their home in rivers far upstream from salt water. Stingrays, like their larger manta ray cousins, use a form of electrolocation to find prey in the silt-laden waters.
