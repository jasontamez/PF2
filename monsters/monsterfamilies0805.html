Monster Families Bore Worm Source Pathfinder #117: Assault on Longshadow pg. 82 Bore worms are a vile breed of subterranean burrowing worm, attacking prey both in their Darklands home and, more rarely, on the planet's surface. Three major variants of the worms exist, each representing a different stage in the creature's long life cycle-the swarm, monarch, and empress stages. Members Bore Worm Monarch (CR 7), Bore Worm Swarm (CR 5), Empress Bore Worm (CR 10)
