Special Attacks thumb spikes Statistics Str 27, Dex 14, Con 19, Int 2, Wis 12, Cha 7 Base Atk +6; CMB +16; CMD 28 (32 vs. trip) Feats Power Attack, Run, Skill Focus (Perception), Skill Focus (Stealth), Weapon Focus (claw) Skills Perception +12, Stealth +4 Ecology Environment warm forests or swamps Organization solitary, pair, or pack (3-6) Treasure none Special Abilities Thumb Spikes (Ex) An iguanodon's thumb spikes can inflict grievous wounds-they deal triple damage on a successful critical hit. Description Iguanodons are large, herbivorous dinosaurs that inhabit swamps and forests where they can take advantage of abundant vegetation. The iguanodon is capable of moving on two feet or on four, quickly switching from one stance to the other, depending on whether it needs to move through dense foliage or to reach delectable morsels hanging up in the canopy. Although iguanodons are herbivores, they are notoriously quick to anger. Their thumb spikes make their claws particularly devastating weapons-a well-placed blow from one of these claws can turn a hungry predator into a cowering beast with one swift strike.
