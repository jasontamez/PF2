Monster Subtypes Demon Source Bestiary 6 pg. 303, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 311, Bestiary 2 pg. 307, Bestiary 3 pg. 305, Bestiary 4 pg. 305, Bestiary 5 pg. 305 Demons are chaotic evil outsiders that call the Abyss their home. Demons have a particular suite of traits (unless otherwise noted in a creature's entry), as summarized here.
  • Immunity to electricity and poison.
  • Resistance to acid 10, cold 10, and fire 10.
  • A demon's natural weapons, as well as any weapon it wields, are treated as chaotic and evil for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction.
  • Except where otherwise noted, demons speak Abyssal, Celestial, and Draconic.
  • Telepathy.
  • Summon (Sp): Demons share the ability to summon others of their kind, typically another of their type or a small number of less powerful demons.
