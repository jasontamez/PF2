Monster Subtypes Earth Source Bestiary 6 pg. 303, Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 311, Bestiary 2 pg. 307, Bestiary 3 pg. 305, Bestiary 4 pg. 305, Bestiary 5 pg. 305 This subtype is usually used for outsiders with a connection to the Elemental Plane of Earth. Earth creatures usually have burrow speeds, and most earth creatures can burrow through solid rock. Earth creatures with a burrow speed have tremorsense.
