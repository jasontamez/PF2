Cornugon Stun - Feats Cornugon Stun (Combat) Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 26 You are able to make stunning attacks with special monk weapons. Prerequisites: Stunning Fist. Benefit: You may use Stunning Fist when making melee attacks with special monk weapons as well as when making unarmed attacks. Normal: You can only use Stunning Fist when making an attack with an unarmed strike.
