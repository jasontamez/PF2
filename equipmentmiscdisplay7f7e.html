Dung grenade - Miscellaneous - Equipment Dung grenade Source Antihero's Handbook pg. 28 Price 100 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Weapons Description The casing of this modified fuse grenade is partially filled with animal dung or guano. You can throw a dung grenade as a splash weapon with a range increment of 10 feet. Any creature directly hit by a dung grenade or in its splash radius takes 1d6 points of fire damage and is covered with the grenade's malodorous contents, becoming sickened for 1d4 rounds. A target can attempt a DC 15 Reflex saving throw to halve the damage and the duration of the sickened condition. Crafting a dung grenade requires a successful DC 25 Craft (alchemy) check. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
