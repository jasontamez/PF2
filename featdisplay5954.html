Press to the Wall - Feats Press to the Wall (Combat) Source Melee Tactics Toolbox pg. 7 You gain an advantage when your opponent can't easily retreat. Prerequisites: Step Up, base attack bonus +3. Benefit: If you are the only character threatening an opponent, you can treat solid, immovable objects that occupy a square (such as columns, tree trunks, and walls) and are adjacent to that opponent as allies threatening the opponent when determining whether you flank the foe.
