Wrist sheath (spring loaded) - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wrist sheath (spring loaded) Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 5 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Adventuring Gear Description This item works like a standard wrist sheath, but releasing an item from it is a swift action. Preparing the sheath for this use requires cranking the sheath's tiny gears and springs into place (a full-round action that provokes an attack of opportunity).
