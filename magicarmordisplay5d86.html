Icon of Order - Armor - Magic Equipment Icon of Order Source Divine Anthology pg. 6 Aura moderate abjuration CL 9th Slot shield; Price 26,170 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description An icon of order is a +2 heavy steel shield that typically displays the sword-and-sunburst holy symbol of Iomedae on a white field. The wielder of an icon of order gains a +2 sacred bonus on saves against spells and effects with the chaotic descriptor. Once per day, the wielder can cast protection from chaos as a swift action. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, Quicken Spell, protection from chaos; Price 13,170 gp
