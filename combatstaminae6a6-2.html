Two-Weapon Fighting - Combat Stamina - Feats Two-Weapon Fighting Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 135 Related Feat Two-Weapon Fighting You can spend any number of stamina points to reduce the penalty from Two-Weapon Fighting on attacks made with a weapon in your primary hand by 1 for every 2 stamina points you spent. This benefit lasts until the start of your next turn. You can't reduce the penalty below 0.
