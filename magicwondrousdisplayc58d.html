Remote Activation Cube - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Remote Activation Cube Source Classic Treasures Revisited pg. 14 Aura moderate evocation; CL 10th Slot none; Price 70,000 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Description This cube can be activated or deactivated from a distance via use of the spell mage hand or the hand of the apprentice ability.
