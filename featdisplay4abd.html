Blighted Critical - Feats Blighted Critical (Critical) Source Ultimate Magic pg. 143 With a critical hit from a spell or spell-like ability, you give the target a minor spellblight. Prerequisites: Caster level 5th. Benefit: Whenever you confirm a critical hit with a touch spell, ranged touch spell, or spell-like ability against an opponent, the victim gains a random minor spellblight (see page 95). Special: You can apply the effects of only one critical feat to a given critical hit unless you possess Critical Mastery.
