Fleet - Feats Fleet Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 124 You are faster than most. Benefit: While you are wearing light or no armor, your base speed increases by 5 feet. You lose the benefits of this feat if you carry a medium or heavy load. Special: You can take this feat multiple times. The effects stack. Mythic Fleet Source Mythic Adventures pg. 65 You're fantastically light on your feet. Prerequisites: Fleet. Benefit: Your base land speed increases by 5 feet, regardless of what armor you wear or whether you're encumbered. This bonus stacks with the bonus gained from Fleet. Special: You can take this feat multiple times, but no more than the number of times you've taken non-mythic Fleet. The effects stack.
