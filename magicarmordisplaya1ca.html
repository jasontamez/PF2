Trained Barding - Armor - Magic Equipment Trained Barding Source Armor Master's Handbook pg. 27 Aura moderate transmutation CL 9th Slot armor; Price 8,350 gp; Weight 30 lbs. Description This suit of Large +2 scale mail barding is equipped with leather straps and loops suitable for attaching harnesses and leashes. It can fit any Large animal or magical beast, though not creatures of any other type. When worn by an animal, this armor grants the animal two additional tricks or one general purpose (as defined by the Handle Animal skill), determined at the time the armor is created. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, awaken; Price 4,350 gp
