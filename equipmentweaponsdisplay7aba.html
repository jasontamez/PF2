Boomerang - Weapons - Equipment Boomerang Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 33, Adventurer's Armory pg. 2, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176, Ultimate Equipment pg. 21 Statistics Cost 3 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range 30 ft.; Type B; Special see text Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Thrown Description The boomerang is primarily a hunting tool, but it is not uncommon for these versatile thrown weapons to be carried into battle. A boomerang is made of wood and is flat, with a long haft, a pronounced forward curve, and a sharp backward bend at the head. A hunting boomerang does not fly in a returning path.
