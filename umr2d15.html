Universal Monster Rules Block Attacks (Ex) [Mythic] Source Bestiary 5 pg. 290, Mythic Adventures pg. 226 Once per round, when the creature is hit by a melee or ranged attack, it can attempt a melee attack using its highest attack bonus. If this result exceeds the result from the attack against it, the creature is unaffected by the attack (as if the attack had missed). Format: block attacks Location: Defensive Abilities.
