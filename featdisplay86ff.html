Extra Summons - Feats Extra Summons Source Ultimate Magic pg. 150 You can summon monsters more often each day. Prerequisites: Ability to cast summon monster as a spell-like ability, summoner 1st. Benefit: You gain 1 additional use of your summon monster spell-like ability per day. Special: You can take this feat multiple times, but it can only be taken once for every five summoner levels you possess.
