Woundweal - Poisons - Equipment Woundweal Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 19 Price 100 gp; Weight — Type injury; Save Fortitude DC 18 Onset 1 round; Frequency 1/day Effect impaired healing (see description) Cure 2 saves Description This gritty black paste is a poison that interferes with an afflicted creature's ability to recover from injuries. All Heal checks applied to the creature suffer a -10 penalty. In addition, anyone using magical healing on the target must make a DC 25 caster level check to succeed.
