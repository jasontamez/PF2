Belt of Tumbling - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Belt of Tumbling Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 211 Aura faint transmutation; CL 1st Slot belt; Price 800 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This thin and flexible cotton cord is meant to be wrapped several times around wearer's waist. The belt's wearer gains a +4 competence bonus on Acrobatics checks made to move through a threatened square or through an enemy's space. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, cat's grace; Cost 400 gp
