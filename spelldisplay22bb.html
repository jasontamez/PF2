Sealed Sending - Spells Sealed Sending Source Inner Sea Intrigue pg. 61 School conjuration (creation) [language-dependent]; Level arcanist 5, sorcerer 5, wizard 5 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range unlimited Effect one nonmagical parchment with a written message Duration 1 hour/level or until discharged Saving Throw none (harmless); Spell Resistance no (harmless) Description You create a scroll bearing a message in any language you can speak, with a maximum length of 50 words. The scroll magically appears anywhere you have viewed at least once on the same plane of existence you are on now. When the spell ends or when the message has been read completely, the scroll disintegrates, leaving no trace. This spell cannot be detected, traced, or blocked by any spell of a lower level.
