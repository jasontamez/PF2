Coat of Shells (Gillmen, Merfolk) - Armor - Magic Equipment Coat of Shells (Gillmen, Merfolk) Source Inner Sea Races pg. 224 Aura moderate transmutation CL 7th Slot armor; Price 15,350 gp; Weight 20 lbs. Description The following suit of armor is generally available only to members of the indicated race or ethnicity. Overlapping scallop shells form this suit of +2 scale mail. While the wearer is swimming in water, the coat of shells restricts her movement speed as light armor instead of as medium armor. Whether worn in or out of water, a coat of shells requires medium armor proficiency. When out of the water, a coat of shells keeps its wearer's skin wet and cool. This provides the wearer resist fire 5 and the benefits of the endure elements spell against hot temperatures only, and it doubles the amount of time a wearer with the water dependent racial trait or water dependency can spend out of water without harm. None of these benefits apply in water. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, create water, endure elements, freedom of movement, creator must be either a gillman or a merfolk; Price 7,850 gp
