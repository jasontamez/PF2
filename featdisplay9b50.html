Improved Elemental Counterspell - Feats Improved Elemental Counterspell Source Elemental Master's Handbook pg. 23 You can charge your kinetic blast with disruptive elemental energy that tears apart elemental spells. Prerequisites: Two basic blast wild talents from two different elements, expanded element class feature, kinetic blast class feature. Benefit: You can use a readied kinetic blast to counterspell any spell of equal or lower level, provided that the target spell has any of the following descriptors: air, cold, darkness, earth, electricity, fire, force, light, or water. Normal: A readied kinetic blast can be used to counterspell spells that share its descriptor.
