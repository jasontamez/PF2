Skyseeker Thrash - Feats Skyseeker Thrash (Combat) Source Blood of the Ancients pg. 28 Your fighting style is enhanced by sheer fortitude in the face of intimidating foes. Prerequisites: Con 13, Skyseeker Style, Step Up, base attack bonus +4. Benefit: While using Skyseeker Style, you gain a cumulative +1 bonus on melee damage rolls for every size category your opponent is larger than you.
