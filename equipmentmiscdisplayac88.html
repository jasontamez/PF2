Quick-step concoction - Miscellaneous - Equipment Quick-step concoction Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 27 Price 75 gp; Weight — Category Concoctions Description This minty fluid quickens your feet and improves your balance, granting a +2 alchemical bonus on Acrobatics checks and to your CMD against trip attempts. However, you become easily distracted and take a -2 penalty on Perception checks.
