Duelist of the Shrouded Lake - Combat Stamina - Feats Duelist of the Shrouded Lake Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24 Related Feat Duelist of the Shrouded Lake By spending 5 stamina points when you attempt a combat maneuver check with an Aldori dueling sword, you gain a +4 bonus to your AC against attacks made by that target until the beginning of your next turn.
