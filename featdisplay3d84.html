Stylized Spell Mastery - Feats Stylized Spell Mastery Source Inner Sea Intrigue pg. 16 You have altered your understanding of a select number of spells Prerequisites: Spell Mastery, Stylized Spell, Bluff 5 ranks, Spellcraft 5 ranks. Benefit: You do not modify the spell slot used when applying the Stylized Spell metamagic feat to any spell you have mastered with Spell Mastery. Normal: A stylized spell uses up a spell slot 1 higher than the spell's actual level.
