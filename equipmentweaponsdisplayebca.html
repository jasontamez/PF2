Tiger fork - Weapons - Equipment Tiger fork Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 8 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type P; Special brace, monk Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Monk; Polearms; Spears Description This long, tridentlike weapon consists of a three-pronged metal fork set upon an 8-foot-long shaft. It is wielded much like a staff, with the wielder grasping the shaft from the center and jabbing with its forked end. A tiger fork can be set to receive a charge.
