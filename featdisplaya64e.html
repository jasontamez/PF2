Shapeshifter Savage - Feats Shapeshifter Savage (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 19 You have enough control over your transformations that you can lash out at an opponent while assuming your alternate form. Prerequisites: Shapeshifter Style, Shapeshifter Twist, base attack bonus +8, change shape ability, shapechanger subtype. Benefit: While you are using Shapeshifter Style, if you use your change shape or wild shape ability as a standard action, you can also make a single melee attack with one of your natural weapons at your highest base attack bonus against one foe within your reach. Special: A character with the wild shape class feature counts as having the change shape ability and shapechanger subtype for the purpose of meeting this feat's prerequisites.
