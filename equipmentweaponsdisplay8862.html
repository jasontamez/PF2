Throwing shield - Weapons - Equipment Throwing shield Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 33, Adventurer's Armory pg. 5, Ultimate Combat pg. 145, Ultimate Equipment pg. 21 Statistics Cost +50 gp Weight — Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range 20 ft.; Type B; Special performance, trip Category Ranged; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Thrown Description This shield is designed for throwing and has specially designed straps that allow you to unclasp and throw it as a free action. Tower shields cannot be throwing shields. Neither a shield's enhancement bonus to AC nor its shield spikes apply on your attack or damage rolls. A throwing shield can't be disarmed.
