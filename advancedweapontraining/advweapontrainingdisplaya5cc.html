Dazzling Intimidation - Advanced Weapon Training - Fighter Dazzling Intimidation (Ex) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 18 The fighter applies his weapon training bonus to Intimidate checks and can attempt an Intimidate check to demoralize an opponent as a move action instead of a standard action. If he has the Dazzling Display feat, he can use it as a standard action instead of a full-round action.
