Ring of Sustenance - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Sustenance Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 175, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura faint conjuration CL 5th Slot ring; Price 2,500 gp; Weight — Description This ring continually provides its wearer with life-sustaining nourishment. The ring also refreshes the body and mind; its wearer only needs to sleep 2 hours per day to gain the benefit of 8 hours of sleep. This allows a spellcaster that requires rest to prepare spells to do so after only 2 hours, but does not allow a spellcaster to prepare spells more than once per day. The ring must be worn for a full week before it begins to work. If it is removed, the owner must wear it for another week to reattune it to himself. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, create food and water; Cost 1,250 gp
