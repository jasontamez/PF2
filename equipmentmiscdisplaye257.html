Underwater goggles - Miscellaneous - Equipment Underwater goggles Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 57 Price 5 gp; Weight — Category Adventuring Gear Description These inexpensive, watertight goggles are essential protection for land-dwellers' eyes, particularly in salt water or water high in particulates. Any land-dweller wearing goggles can use visual Perception like an aquatic native (see page 46).
