Toiler's Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Toiler's Armor Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 253 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot armor; Price 3,920 gp; Weight 40 lbs. Description This +1 chainmail looks like something a talentless dwarven apprentice would make-ugly, asymmetrical, and finished quickly rather than with care. The wearer gains a luck bonus or penalty equal to 1d6-3 on Craft checks to make armor or weapons while wearing it. This bonus or penalty is determined the first time the wearer attempts such a Craft check to determine a day's progress. If the wearer removes the armor, this bonus or penalty persists for an additional 24 hours. Once per day, the wearer can use magic weapon. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, crafter's curse, crafter's fortune, magic weapon; Price 2,110 gp
