Improved Cleaving Finish - Feats Improved Cleaving Finish (Combat) Source Ultimate Combat pg. 105 You can cut down many opponents in a single strike. Prerequisites: Str 13, Cleave, Cleaving Finish, Great Cleave, Power Attack, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: You can use Cleaving Finish any number of times per round. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 Once per round when you confirm a critical hit, you can spend 5 stamina points to treat that attack as if you reduced the target to 0 or fewer hit points for the purpose of using the Cleaving Finish feat.
