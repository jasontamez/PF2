Voice of the Wind - Wild Talents - Kineticist Voice of the Wind Source Occult Adventures pg. 29 Element air; Type utility (Sp); Level 1; Burn 0 Saving Throw none; SR yes When you whisper, you can have the wind carry your voice to the ears of anyone you can see within 120 feet, as if they were one of the targets of a message spell you cast. Alternatively, you can send your voice to a distant location as if casting whispering wind.
