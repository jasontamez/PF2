Mantle of the Crusader Host - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Mantle of the Crusader Host Source Inner Sea Combat pg. 62 Aura strong abjuration; CL 20th Slot chest; Price 95,000 gp; Weight — Description This holy shroud is inscribed with hundreds of sacred passages in homage to the gods of good.
