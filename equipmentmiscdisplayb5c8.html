Donkey or mule - Miscellaneous - Equipment Donkey or mule Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 81 Price 8 gp; Weight 600-800 lbs. Category Mounts/Pets Description Donkeys and mules are stolid in the face of danger, hardy, surefooted, and capable of carrying heavy loads over vast distances. Unlike a horse, a donkey or mule is willing (though not eager) to enter dungeons and other strange or threatening places. Donkeys and mules have the same statistics as ponies (see the Bestiary).
