Aberrant Tumor - Feats Aberrant Tumor Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 136 To the surprise of others, that strange growth on you is actually your spellcasting companion. Prerequisites: Aberrant bloodline. Benefit: You gain a tumor familiar, as the tumor familiar alchemist discovery (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 17), with an effective alchemist level equal to the level of the class that grants your aberrant bloodline for determining the tumor familiar's abilities. If multiple classes grant you the aberrant bloodline, those class levels stack for determining your effective alchemist level.
