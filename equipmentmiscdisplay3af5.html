Sextant - Miscellaneous - Equipment Sextant Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 185, Pirates of the Inner Sea pg. 21 Price 500 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description A sextant is used to determine your latitude. Using a sextant with an astrolabe gives you a +4 circumstance bonus on Survival checks made to prevent getting lost.
