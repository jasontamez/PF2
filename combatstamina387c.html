Canny Tumble - Combat Stamina - Feats Canny Tumble Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 115 Related Feat Canny Tumble Just before you make an attack roll with the +2 circumstance bonus granted by this feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points. When you do, if your attack hits, you gain a bonus on the damage roll equal to double the number of stamina points spent.
