Poisoner's Jacket (Greater) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 221 Aura strong conjuration and necromancy; CL 15th Slot chest; Price 58,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This jacket has the abilities of a lesser poisoner's jacket, and once per day the wearer can create a vial that reproduces the effect of any poison in the Core Rulebook that costs 4,000 gp or less. The jacket's poison always looks like a murky lime fluid. This poison becomes inert after 1 hour. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, major creation, poison; Cost 29,000 gp
