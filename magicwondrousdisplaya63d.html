Grappler's Mask - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Grappler's Mask Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 242, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 306 Aura faint transmutation; CL 1st Slot head; Price 5,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This mask is fashioned from dark leather decorated with geometrical patterns that emphasize the wearer's fearsomeness. It covers the wearer's face, but leaves his mouth and eyes uncovered. The wearer may attempt to bull rush and grapple without provoking attacks of opportunity. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, bull's strength, cat's grace, expeditious retreat; Cost 2,500 gp
