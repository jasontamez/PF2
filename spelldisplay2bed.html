Litany of Sloth - Spells Litany of Sloth Source Ultimate Combat pg. 235 School enchantment (compulsion) [language-dependent, mind-affecting]; Level antipaladin 1, inquisitor 1, paladin 1 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V, S, DF Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature Duration 1 round Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description With a litany against the wages of sloth, you slow the target's defenses. The target cannot make attacks of opportunity or cast spells defensively. While subject to this spell, the target cannot be the target of another spell that has the word "litany" in the title.
