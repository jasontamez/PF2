Shadow Piercings - Magic Equipment Head Piercings Source Champions of Corruption pg. 29 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot head; Price 3,750 gp (Minor), 6,000 gp (Major), 33,600 gp (Greater); Weight — Description Whether they are crowns of pins, pinned-back lips, or simple nose rings, these piercings accentuate the owner's terrifying presence and inner resolve.
