Area 20-ft.-radius emanation, centered on you Duration 1 round/level (D) Saving Throw Will negates; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions as death knell aura, except a dying creature in the area cannot stabilize on its own and automatically takes 1 point of bleed damage on its turn each round.
