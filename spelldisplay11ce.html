Burst of Speed - Spells Burst of Speed Source Ultimate Combat pg. 225 School transmutation; Level alchemist 3, antipaladin 3, bloodrager 3, hunter 3, inquisitor 3, investigator 3, magus 3, medium 3, paladin 3, psychic 3, ranger 3 Casting Casting Time 1 swift action Components V Effect Range personal Target you Duration see text Description Until the end of your turn, you gain a foot bonus to speed (or foot bonus if you are wearing Medium or Heavy armor), your movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity, and you can move through the space of creatures that are larger than you are, but you cannot end your movement this round in a space occupied by a creature.
