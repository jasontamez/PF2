Designating (greater) - Weapons - Magic Equipment Designating (greater) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 140 Aura moderate enchantment CL 12th Slot weapon quality; Price +4 bonus; Weight — Description This special ability can only be placed on ranged weapons or ammunition. Each time a ranged weapon or ammunition with this ability hits a creature, its magic designates the target. Allies gain a +4 morale bonus on melee attack rolls and a +6 morale bonus on melee damage rolls against the designated target for 1 round.
