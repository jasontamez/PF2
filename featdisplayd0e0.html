Stick-Fighting Maneuver - Feats Stick-Fighting Maneuver (Combat) Source Martial Arts Handbook pg. 12 Your attacks with sticks have become blindingly fast, and you can throw in a combat maneuver between your attacks. Prerequisites: Stick-Fighting Counter, Stick-Fighting Style, base attack bonus +6. Benefit: When making a full attack using only clubs, a quarterstaff, dan bongs, saps, or tonfas, you can perform a combat maneuver as an extra attack using your full base attack bonus.
