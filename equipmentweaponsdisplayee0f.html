Blunt arrows (20) - Weapons - Equipment Blunt arrows (20) Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 22, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 176 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight 3 lbs. Category Ammunition; Proficiency Ammunition Description These arrows have rounded wooden tips that deal bludgeoning damage rather than piercing. An archer can use a blunt arrow to deal nonlethal damage (at the normal -4 attack penalty for using a lethal weapon to deal nonlethal damage).
