Fey-Guarded - Feats Fey-Guarded Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 111 You have trained your mind to resist fey magic-both that cast by fey creatures and that cast by other denizens of the First World. Prerequisites: Knowledge (nature) or Knowledge (planes) 3 ranks. Benefit: You gain a +2 morale bonus on saving throws against enchantment and illusion spells and spell-like abilities cast by creatures of the fey type.
