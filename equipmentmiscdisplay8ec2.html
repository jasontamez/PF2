Wing sheaths - Miscellaneous - Equipment Wing sheaths Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 13 Price 10 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description Wing sheaths are protective coverings created from thick fabric or canvas. The sheaths are designed to tightly cover a pair of wings to keep them hidden beneath a cloak or loose-fitting clothing. Noticing hidden wings bound within wing sheaths requires a successful DC 20 Perception check. Donning or removing wing sheaths requires 1 minute, or a full-round action with assistance. Wing sheaths can be burst with a successful DC 22 Strength check.
