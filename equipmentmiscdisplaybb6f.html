Oil of the masters - Miscellaneous - Equipment Oil of the masters Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 207 Price 50 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Alchemical Tools Description This gold-tinted oil smells of wood shavings. When you apply it to the strings of a stringed instrument or the body of a woodwind, it improves the quality of the instrument's sound. For 1 hour, anyone playing the instrument gains a +2 alchemical bonus on the appropriate Perform check. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
