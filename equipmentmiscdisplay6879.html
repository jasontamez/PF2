Spellbook - Miscellaneous - Equipment Spellbook Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 77, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 158 Price 15 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Category Tools Description A spellbook has 100 pages of parchment, and each spell takes up one page per spell level (one page each for 0-level spells). A newly purchased spellbook contains no spells. A wizard character begins play with a spellbook containing the spells he knows (Core Rulebook 79).
