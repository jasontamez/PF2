Ring of Three Wishes - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Three Wishes Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 176, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura strong universal CL 20th Slot ring; Price 120,000 gp; Weight — Description This ring is set with three rubies. Each ruby stores a wish spell, activated by the ring. When a wish is used, that ruby disappears. For a randomly generated ring, roll 1d3 to determine the remaining number of rubies. When all the wishes are used, the ring becomes a nonmagical item. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, miracle or wish; Cost 97,500 gp
