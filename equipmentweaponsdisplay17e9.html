Hooked lance - Weapons - Equipment Hooked lance Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 19, Ultimate Combat pg. 131 Statistics Cost 3 gp Weight 10 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical x4; Range —; Type P; Special reach, trip Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Polearms Description This slender, 10-foot-long polearm has an added hook protruding from the head that is used to snag the clothing and armor of opponents. A hooked lance, unlike a lance, can't be wielded in one hand while mounted and doesn't deal extra damage if used while mounted.
