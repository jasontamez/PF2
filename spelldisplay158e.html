Spells Cure Serious Wounds, Mass Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 263 School conjuration (healing); Level cleric 7, druid 8, oracle 7, shaman 7, witch 8 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S Effect Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels) Target one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Will half (harmless) or Will half; see text; Spell Resistance yes (harmless) or yes; see text Description This spell functions like mass cure light wounds, except that it cures 3d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +35).
