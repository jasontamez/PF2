Phase Arrow - Weapons - Magic Equipment Phase Arrow Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 24 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Price 916 gp ; Weight 1/20 lb. Description When this translucent +2 arrow is fired, it becomes ethereal until it enters the target's space. A phase arrow ignores cover of all kinds, including total cover, and can be fired at a target even if the wielder doesn't have line of effect. However, if the wielder cannot see or otherwise pinpoint the target, she risks attacking the wrong space, and even if she attacks the right space, the target still has total concealment against the attack (granting a 50% miss chance). Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, blink; Price 461 gp
