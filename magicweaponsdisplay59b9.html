Tigress's Strand - Weapons - Magic Equipment Tigress's Strand Source Lost Treasures pg. 57 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot none; Price 18,305 gp; Weight 3 lbs. Description This unusual whip is made of spider silk hardened to the consistency of cartilage and studded with bear claws. Tigress's Strand acts as +1 dueling scorpion whip. Tigress's Strand also provides a +4 bonus on saving throws against the effects of web and similar spells as well as against web special attacks (such as from spiders or aranea). Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, cat's grace, web; Price 9,305 gp
