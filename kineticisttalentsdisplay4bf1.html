Slick - Wild Talents - Kineticist Slick Source Occult Adventures pg. 27 Element water; Type utility (Sp); Level 1; Burn 0 Saving Throw Reflex negates; SR no As a standard action, you call forth slippery water or ice, creating any of the effects of the grease spell for 1 round. You can accept 1 point of burn at the start of your next turn to extend the duration to 1 minute per kineticist level you possess.
