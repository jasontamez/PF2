Prophet's Pectoral Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 222 Aura faint divination; CL 3rd Slot chest; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description The sacred stones, graven star-patterns, and wisdom-writings of the ancients on this chestplate increase the wearer's chance of success with augury, divination, and contact other plane. The wearer adds 1d6 to the d% roll each time she uses such a spell. When casting commune or commune with nature, the wearer may ask one additional question or gain one additional piece of information. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, divination, guidance; Cost 3,000 gp
