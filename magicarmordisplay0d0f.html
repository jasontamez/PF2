Volcanic Shield - Armor - Magic Equipment Volcanic Shield Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 133 Aura faint evocation CL 5th Slot shield; Price 14,170 gp; Weight 10 lbs. Description This +1 bashing heavy steel shield burns with an internal fire upon its boss, gaining the flaming weapon special ability when used to make shield bash attacks. On command, the wielder can make the shield belch forth smoke as a smokestick, but this smoke does not impair the shield user's vision. Once per day, the wielder may cause the volcanic shield to erupt in a cascade of brimstone as the flaming hands spell. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bull's strength, burning hands, pyrotechnics; Price 7,170 gp
