Minkaian ceremonial tea - Miscellaneous - Equipment Minkaian ceremonial tea Source Adventurer's Armory 2 pg. 28 Price 900 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description This tea is extremely rare and expensive, requiring several years to grow and prepare. A geisha bard (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Magic 27) who uses this tea as part of her tea ceremony to inspire courage increases the bonuses granted by 1. A lotus geisha bard (Pathfinder Player Companion: Dragon Empires Primer 22) who drinks this tea increases the bonus granted by her enrapturing performance ability by 1; this effect lasts for 24 hours.
