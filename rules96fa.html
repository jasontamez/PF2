Adamantine Source Ultimate Combat pg. 199 Armor pieces made of adamantine grant their wearers damage reduction of 1/- if they are light armor, 2/- if they are medium armor, and 3/- if they are heavy armor. Adamantine armor pieces are always masterwork in quality. Armor pieces normally made of steel that are made of adamantine have one-third more hit points than normal.
