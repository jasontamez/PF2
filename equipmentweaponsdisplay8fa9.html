Kama - Weapons - Equipment Kama Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 143 Statistics Cost 2 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d4 (small), 1d6 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type S; Special monk, trip Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Blades, Light; Monk Description Similar to a sickle-and in some regions still used to reap grain-a kama is a short, curved blade attached to a simple handle, usually made of wood. It is sometimes also referred to as a kai, and is frequently used in pairs by martial artists.
