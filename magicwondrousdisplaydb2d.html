Pact Parchment - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Pact Parchment Source Cheliax, Empire of Devils pg. 21 Aura moderate enchantment; CL 11th Price 3,500 gp; Weight — Description Written and signed in blood, a pact parchment ensures the utter and undeniable fulfillment of the actions stated within. Upon a pact parchment, you can inscribe a contract, defining a certain service or course of action by one or more creatures. If all listed creatures willingly (without magical influence, threats, and so on) sign this pact in blood, they are bound to fulfill this agreement. Henceforth everyone breaking the pact suffers a -4 penalty on attack rolls, saves, ability checks, and skill checks, until they either satisfy the pact's conditions (if still possible) or the parchment is destroyed. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, geas/quest; Cost 1,750 gp
