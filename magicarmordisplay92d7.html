Kimlé Coat - Armor - Magic Equipment Kimlé Coat Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 252 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot armor; Price 2,300 gp; Weight 15 lbs. Description This +1 leather armor is made of kimlé (see page 73) and resembles a snug light coat with a tattered lower fringe in the shape of trailing seaweed. The wearer of a kimlé coat gains a +5 bonus on Swim checks. Once per day, the wearer can breathe water for 1 minute. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, water breathing, creator must have 5 ranks in the Swim skill; Price 1,230 gp
