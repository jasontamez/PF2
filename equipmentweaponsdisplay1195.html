Nine-section whip - Weapons - Equipment Nine-section whip Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 20, Ultimate Combat pg. 133 Statistics Cost 8 gp Weight 3 lbs. Damage 1d6 (small), 1d8 (medium); Critical 19-20/x2; Range —; Type B; Special blocking, distracting, monk, trip Category One-Handed; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Flails; Monk Description The nine-section whip consists of steel bars linked together. The final bar is a 6-inch weighted spike. This weapon can be wielded as a single-handed weapon, a two-handed weapon, or in pairs. Nine-section whips often have colored cloth flags attached to the ends to make them easier to control.
