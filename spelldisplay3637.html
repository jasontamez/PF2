Sabotage Construct - Spells Sabotage Construct Source People of the River pg. 27 School transmutation; Level arcanist 6, bard 5, inquisitor 5, skald 5, sorcerer 6, witch 6, wizard 6 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components S, V Effect Range touch Targets all constructs in a 15-ft.-radius burst Duration 1 round/level Saving Throw Will partial; Spell Resistance yes Description This spell functions as confusion, except that it affects only constructs. When you are rolling for a confused construct's behavior, a result of 26-50 indicates the construct twitches spasmodically. On a successful Will save, the construct is confused for only 1 round.
