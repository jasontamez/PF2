Entertainer's outfit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Entertainer's outfit Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 89, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 3 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Clothing Description This set of flashy-perhaps even gaudy- clothes is meant for entertaining. Entertainer's outfits come in a wide range of styles, from scandalous to flattering to absurd. While the outfit looks whimsical, its practical design does nothing to inhibit your ability to tumble, dance, walk a tightrope, or run (if the audience turns ugly).
