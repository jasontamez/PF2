Harrow deck - Miscellaneous - Equipment Harrow deck Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 57, Curse of the Crimson Throne Player's Guide pg. 12, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 213, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Inner Sea World Guide pg. 293 Price 100 gp; Weight — Category Entertainment Description This is a traditional fortune-telling deck of cards used by soothsayers and seers. Some harrow decks are elaborately illustrated, but most are parchment or paper cards with hand-painted images. Harrow decks are often handed down through generations and treated with utmost care by their users as a result.
