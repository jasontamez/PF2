Battle poi - Weapons - Equipment Battle poi Source Adventurer's Armory pg. 2, Legacy of Fire Player's Guide pg. 22 Statistics Cost 5 gp Weight 2 lbs. Damage 1d3 fire (small), 1d4 fire (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type fire; Special — Category Light; Proficiency Exotic Weapon Groups Flails Description This pair of arm-length chains has handles at one end and heavy fuel-soaked torch heads at the other. The weight of the poi is insufficient to deal physical damage, but the burning fuel deals fire damage. If you are proficient in battle poi, you are treated as if you have the Two-Weapon Fighting feat for the purposes of making poi attacks. Poi can be extinguished by spending a full round action smothering them in sand or submerging them in water.
