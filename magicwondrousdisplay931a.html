Hyperboreal Robe - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Hyperboreal Robe Source Pathfinder #68: The Shackled Hut pg. 60 Aura moderate abjuration and necromancy; CL 6th Slot body; Price 7,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This beautiful robe is fashioned from fine blue silk embroidered with silver thread, and trimmed with owl feathers and tiny shards of crystal. The robe grants its wearer a +2 resistance bonus on all saving throws. In addition, the flesh of the robe's wearer becomes searing cold to the touch. Anyone who strikes the wearer with an unarmed strike, a touch attack, or a natural weapon takes 1d6 points of cold damage. The wearer can activate or deactivate this ability as a free action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, chill touch, resistance; Cost 3,500 gp
