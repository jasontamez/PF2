Amulet of Hidden Strength Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 254 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot neck; Price 9,000 gp; Weight — Description This glass teardrop hangs on a platinum chain and can be used once per day to grant a surge of power. This surge can be used in one of two ways. As a swift action, the wearer of the amulet can make one melee or ranged attack at his highest attack bonus. Alternatively, if the wearer of the amulet has a ki pool, he can instead focus on the power of the amulet as a full-round action and regain two ki points. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, creator must have a ki pool; Cost 4,500 gp
