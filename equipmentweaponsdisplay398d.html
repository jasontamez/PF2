Shang gou - Weapons - Equipment Shang gou Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, Ultimate Combat pg. 134 Statistics Cost 6 gp Weight 1 lb. Damage 1d3 (small), 1d4 (medium); Critical x2; Range —; Type S; Special disarm or trip, monk Category Light; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Monk Description Forged in pairs, these solid metal blades end in curved hooks, while the hand guards are hammered into outward-facing spiked crescents. Both the crescent and the tip of the hilt are sharpened for combat. They can be wielded as single weapons or with two-weapon fighting styles. These weapons gain the disarm special feature when they are used individually. A pair of shang gou can also be formed into a single one-handed weapon to gain the trip special feature.
