Spell Mastery - Feats Spell Mastery Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 134 You have mastered a small handful of spells, and can prepare these spells without referencing your spellbooks at all. Prerequisites: 1st-level wizard Benefit: Each time you take this feat, choose a number of spells that you already know equal to your Intelligence modifier. From that point on, you can prepare these spells without referring to a spellbook. Normal: Without this feat, you must use a spellbook to prepare all your spells, except read magic. Mythic Spell Mastery Source Mythic Adventures pg. 74 You're so familiar with certain spells you can prepare them with the greatest of ease. Prerequisites: Spell Mastery. Benefit: You can prepare spells you have selected with Spell Mastery as a full-round action. You must spend the appropriate amount of time preparing other spells you have not mastered. As a full-round action, you can expend one use of mythic power to prepare all your spells that you have selected with Spell Mastery.
