Gravelly tonic - Miscellaneous - Equipment Gravelly tonic Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 207 Price 50 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description This tonic is sludgy, and its scent resembles the tang of iron shavings. Drinking gravelly tonic causes your voice to deepen and become more grating for 1 hour, granting you a +5 alchemical bonus on Intimidate checks. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
