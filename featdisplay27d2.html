Favored Judgment - Feats Favored Judgment Source Ultimate Magic pg. 150 Your judgment against a particular type of creature is particularly harsh. Prerequisites: Wis 13, judgment class feature. Benefit: Select a favored race from the ranger's favored enemies chart. Any sacred or profane bonus you gain from a judgment is 1 higher for attacks you make against or take from creatures that match the selected favored enemy. Special: You can gain this feat multiple times. Its effects do not stack. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a different favored enemy.
