Bloodgorge - Miscellaneous - Equipment Bloodgorge Source Alchemy Manual pg. 6 Price 40 gp; Weight 1/2 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description When you imbibe this syrupy black serum, your blood thins dramatically and gushes forth from even minor wounds. Anytime you take bleed damage, any creatures adjacent to you must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC 10 + the amount of bleed damage taken) or be nauseated for 1 round. A dose of bloodgorge lasts for 1 hour. Alchemical Recipe Recipe (5 cold iron + 10 dew of lunary)/filtration; Craft 25 Time 10 minutes; Tools filter; Type alchemical remedy
