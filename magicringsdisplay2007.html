Ring of Animal Friendship - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Animal Friendship Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 166, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura faint enchantment CL 3rd Slot ring; Price 10,800 gp; Weight — Description A ring of animal friendship always bears some sort of animal-like design in its craftsmanship. On command, this ring affects an animal as if the wearer had cast charm animal, causing the animal to regard the wearer as a trusted friend. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, charm animal; Cost 5,400 gp
