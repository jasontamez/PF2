Monster Templates Monster Templates Giant (CR +1) Pathfinder RPG Bestiary pg. 294 Acquired/Inherited Template Acquired Simple Template Yes Usable with Summons No Creatures with the giant template are larger and stronger than their normal-sized kin. This template cannot be applied to creatures that are Colossal. Quick Rules: +2 to all rolls based on Str or Con, +2 hp/ HD, -1 penalty on all rolls based on Dex. Rebuild Rules: Size increase by one category; AC increase natural armor by +3; Attacks increase dice rolled by 1 step; Ability Scores +4 size bonus to Str and Con, -2 Dex.
