Heaven's Step - Feats Heaven's Step (Combat) Source Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 73 Like the daring Heaven's Step Offensive championed by Grand Prince Gennaris III of Taldor, you confidently take the fight to your enemy and strike an unexpected blow. Prerequisites: Dex 15, Following Step, Step Up, Step Up and Strike, Two-Weapon Fighting, base attack bonus +10. Benefit: When you make an attack using Step Up and Strike, you can also make an extra attack with your offhand weapon using your highest base attack bonus. Both attacks take the normal penalties for two-weapon fighting.
