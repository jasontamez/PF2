Narrow Frame - Feats Narrow Frame Source Animal Archive pg. 19 Your excellent coordination allows you to maneuver better in close quarters. Prerequisites: Escape Artist 1 rank, animal or magical beast. Benefit: You do not take penalties on your attack rolls or to your AC for squeezing through a narrow space that is at least half as wide as your normal space, though each move into or through a narrow space still counts as if it were 2 squares. Normal: You take a -4 penalty on attack rolls and a -4 penalty to AC while squeezing.
