Arboreal Armor - Armor - Magic Equipment Arboreal Armor Source Heroes of the Wild pg. 28 Aura moderate transmutation CL 9th Slot armor; Price 18,670 gp; Weight 25 lbs. Description The oaken plates of this +1 wild wooden armor are rough with untreated bark. The wearer can activate the armor with a command word, causing her flesh to become treelike in appearance and resilience. This grants the wearer a +4 enhancement bonus to her existing natural armor bonus and removes her need to breathe, but reduces her speed by half and imposes a -2 penalty on Reflex saves. This effect remains until dismissed as a free action. The wearer can use this ability for up to 10 rounds per day, though the rounds need not be consecutive. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, baleful polymorph, barkskin; Price 9,420 gp
