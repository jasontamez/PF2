Filter mask - Miscellaneous - Equipment Filter mask Source Heroes of the Darklands pg. 10 Price 5 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Category Adventuring Gear Description A filter mask is a rubber-sealed leather mask with a filtering respiration apparatus. Ordinary filter masks have cloth filters laced with floral perfumes, while masterwork filter masks use alchemical charcoal filters. When worn, a filter mask provides a +2 bonus on Fortitude saves against inhaled poisons or diseases and other inhaled particles such as smoke or spores.
