Cackling Hag's Blouse - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Cackling Hag's Blouse Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 220 Aura faint enchantment; CL 3rd Slot chest; Price 6,000 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Description This loose-fitting blouse is adorned with grotesque fetishes and trophies, granting the wearer a +2 competence bonus on Intimidate checks. If the wearer is a witch, she gains the cackle hex. If the wearer already has the cackle hex, twice per day she can use her cackle ability as a swift action instead of a move action. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, hideous laughter or the cackle hex, creator must have 5 ranks in Intimidate; Cost 3,000 gp
