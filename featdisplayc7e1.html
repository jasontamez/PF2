Brewmaster - Feats Brewmaster Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 17 You can concoct potent brews. Prerequisites: Craft (alchemy) 1 rank, Profession (brewer) 1 rank, dwarf. Benefit: You gain a +2 bonus on Craft (alchemy) and Profession (brewer) checks, and you add +1 to the DC of any ingested poison you create.
