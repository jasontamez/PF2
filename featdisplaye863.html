Faerie's Strike - Feats Faerie's Strike (Combat) Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 147 Ephemeral wisps of nature spirits dance about those you strike. Prerequisites: Nature Magic or the ability to cast druid or ranger spells; Vital Strike, Knowledge (nature) 5 ranks. Benefit: When you use Vital Strike (or Improved Vital Strike or Greater Vital Strike), the creature you hit with the attack must succeed at a Will save (DC = 10 + 1/2 your character level + your Wisdom modifier) or be illuminated as if by faerie fire for 1 minute. You can use this benefit a number of times per day equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 119 When using this feat, you can spend up to 5 stamina points to increase the DC of the effect by an amount equal to the number of stamina points you spent.
