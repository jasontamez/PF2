Heavy wooden - Armor - Equipment Heavy wooden Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 7 gp Weight 10 lbs. Armor Bonus +2; Max Dex Bonus —; Armor Check Penalty -2 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 15%; Speed —/— Description A heavy wooden shield is essentially the same as a heavy steel shield, except that it responds differently to some spells and effects (such as rusting grasp). A druid can use a heavy wooden shield, but not a heavy steel shield.
