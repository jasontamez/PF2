Expanded Resistance - Feats Expanded Resistance Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 37 You have expanded your natural resistance to magic to encompass different kinds of magic. Prerequisites: Gnome, illusion resistance racial trait. Benefit: Select one school of magic other than illusion. You gain a +2 racial bonus on saving throws against spells or effects from the selected school. Special: You may select this feat more than once. Its effects do not stack. Each time you select it, it applies to a different school of magic.
