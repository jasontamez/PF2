Halfling jugglesticks - Miscellaneous - Equipment Halfling jugglesticks Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 66 Price 25 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Entertainment Description This group of four brightly colored sticks is adorned with colorful streamers that can be juggled and manipulated to create displays and patterns. Halflings use them to even greater effect, gaining a +2 circumstance bonus on Perform (comedy) checks.
