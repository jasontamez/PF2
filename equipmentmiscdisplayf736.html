Miscellaneous - Equipment Coffin Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 56, Adventurer's Armory pg. 19, Pathfinder Campaign Setting pg. 213 Price 10 gp (common), 100 gp (ornate); Weight 30 lbs. (common), 50 lbs. (ornate) Description A plain coffin is made of simple wood and has a loose, flat lid that can be nailed onto it. Ornate coffins are favored by aristocratic families for displaying their dead, and include upholstered cloth liners and a hinged lid.
