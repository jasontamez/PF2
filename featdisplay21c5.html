Simple Weapon Proficiency - Feats Simple Weapon Proficiency (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 133 You are trained in the use of basic weapons. Benefit: You make attack rolls with simple weapons without penalty. Normal: When using a weapon with which you are not proficient, you take a -4 penalty on attack rolls. Special: All characters except for druids, monks, and wizards are automatically proficient with all simple weapons. They need not select this feat.
