Blade of the Purge - Feats Blade of the Purge Source Pathfinder #132: The Six-Legend Soul pg. 72 Emulating the fervor of Grand Prince Stavian I, you can identify a religion you perceive to be opposed to your own and empower your weapons to strike deadly blows against its adherents. Prerequisites: Bane class feature. Benefit: When you activate your bane class feature, you can select a deity or religion other than your own. Your bane effect applies only to followers of that deity or religion.
