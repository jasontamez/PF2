Lightning Reflexes - Feats Lightning Reflexes Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 130 You have faster reflexes than normal. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on all Reflex saving throws. Mythic Lightning Reflexes Source Mythic Adventures pg. 68 Your reflexes are superhuman. Prerequisites: Lightning Reflexes. Benefit: Whenever you roll a Reflex saving throw against a spell, spell-like ability, or supernatural ability from a non-mythic source, roll twice and take the higher result.
