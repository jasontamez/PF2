Full plate - Armor - Equipment Full plate Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 9, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 151 Statistics Cost 1,500 gp Weight 50 lbs. Armor Bonus +9; Max Dex Bonus +1; Armor Check Penalty -6 Arcane Spell Failure Chance 35%; Speed 20 ft./15 ft. Description This metal suit comprises multiple pieces of interconnected and overlaying metal plates, incorporating the benefits of numerous types of lesser armor. A complete suit of full plate (or platemail, as it is often called) includes gauntlets, heavy leather boots, a visored helmet, and a thick layer of padding that is worn underneath the armor. Each suit of full plate must be individually fitted to its owner by a master armorsmith, although a captured suit can be resized to fit a new owner at a cost of 200 to 800 (2d4  100) gold pieces.
