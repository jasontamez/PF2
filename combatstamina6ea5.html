Improved Impaling Critical - Combat Stamina - Feats Improved Impaling Critical Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 123 Related Feat Improved Impaling Critical When using this feat to impale a foe, you can spend 5 stamina points to immobilize the foe in its current space (instead of halving its movement) for as long as you maintain your grip and the foe is impaled. If you let go of the weapon, the foe can move, but its movement is still halved.
