Laser sight - Miscellaneous - Technology Laser sight Source Technology Guide pg. 48 Price 8,000 gp Slot none; Weight 1 lb. Capacity 10; Usage 1 charge/hour Description This small, cylindrical device can be attached to the barrel of any firearm. When activated, it emits a low-power laser beam that paints a small red dot on the target. This effect grants the user a +1 circumstance bonus on all attack rolls made with that weapon. Further, a laser sight reduces the penalty for firing into melee by 2. Construction Craft DC 24; Cost 4,000 gp Craft Technological Item, production lab
