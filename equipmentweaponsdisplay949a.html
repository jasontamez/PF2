Glaive-guisarme - Weapons - Equipment Glaive-guisarme Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 18, Adventurer's Armory pg. 3, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 178 Statistics Cost 12 gp Weight 10 lbs. Damage 1d8 (small), 1d10 (medium); Critical x3; Range —; Type S; Special brace, reach, see text Category Two-Handed; Proficiency Martial Weapon Groups Polearms Description This polearm combines the blade of a glaive with a wicked hook. A mounted opponent hit by a glaive-guisarme takes a -2 penalty on her Ride check to stay mounted.
