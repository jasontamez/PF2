Celerity - Wild Talents - Kineticist Celerity Source Occult Adventures pg. 24 Element air; Type utility (Sp); Level 3; Burn 0 You can galvanize the flow of electricity within your allies to enhance their speed. This acts as haste except it lasts for 1 round. You can accept 1 point of burn to increase the duration to 1 round per kineticist level you possess.
