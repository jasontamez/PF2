Monstrification Staff - Staves - Magic Equipment Monstrification Staff Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 221 Aura faint transmutation (polymorph) CL 8th Slot none; Price 12,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description The figures on this scrimshawed ivory staff slowly morph from one monstrous figure into another, and often grow to take up more space on the staff's surface. An alchemist or other character capable of creating extracts can pour an extract of enlarge person over this staff to restore 1 charge to the staff or pour an extract of monstrous physique I over it to restore 3 charges.
  • Enlarge person (1 charge)
  • Monstrous physique I (3 charges)
Construction Requirements Craft Staff, enlarge person, monstrous physique I, creator must be an alchemist; Cost 6,150 gp
