Curses Perilous Demesne, Greater Source Horror Adventures pg. 145 Type regional curse; Save Will DC 19 Effect As per major perilous demesne, except up to six hazards, each of CR 10 or less, imperil creatures in the area. The hazards are arranged so that if any are encountered together, the encounter's effective CR from hazards must be no more than 11 (for instance, two CR 9 hazards could be encountered together). Cure Remove curse removes a greater perilous demesne only if followed by a casting of hallow at the effect's center.
