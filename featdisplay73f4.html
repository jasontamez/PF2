Spiritual Balance - Feats Spiritual Balance Source Occult Adventures pg. 140 You focus your ki in order to quell the influence of outside spirits on you. Prerequisites: Ki pool class feature, still mind class feature. Benefit: The saving throw bonus from your still mind class feature applies against possession effects, and as an immediate action you can spend 1 point from your ki pool to double the saving throw bonus provided by still mind. Normal: Your still mind class feature applies only against enchantment spells and effects.
