Greater Spell Penetration - Feats Greater Spell Penetration Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 125 Your spells break through spell resistance much more easily than most. Prerequisites: Spell Penetration. Benefit: You get a +2 bonus on caster level checks (1d20 + caster level) made to overcome a creature's spell resistance. This bonus stacks with the one from Spell Penetration.
