Tea of transference - Miscellaneous - Equipment Tea of transference Source Alchemy Manual pg. 25 Price 40 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Remedies Description This foul-tasting green tea allows monks to focus their ki on improving abilities they've gained through other training. By drinking a cup of tea of transference, you can expend 1 point from your ki pool to regain 1 point from your arcane pool, 2 rounds of barbarian rage, 2 rounds of bardic performance, one use of channel energy, 1 grit point, one use of smite evil, one expended extract slot at least 1 level lower than the highest-level extract you can create, or one expended spell slot at least 2 levels lower than the highest-level spell you can cast. You must have the ki pool class feature and the class feature you want to regain uses of in order to benefit from tea of transference. Alchemical Recipe Recipe (5 gold + 5 realgar + 15 spirit of wine)/sublimation; Craft 20 Time 1 day; Tools retort; Type alchemical remedy
