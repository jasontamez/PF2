Greater Trip - Feats Greater Trip (Combat) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 126 You can make free attacks on foes that you knock down. Prerequisites: Combat Expertise, Improved Trip, base attack bonus +6, Int 13. Benefit: You receive a +2 bonus on checks made to trip a foe. This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by Improved Trip. Whenever you successfully trip an opponent, that opponent provokes attacks of opportunity. Normal: Creatures do not provoke attacks of opportunity from being tripped. Combat Trick (from the Combat Stamina feat) Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 122 You can select this feat even if you don't meet the ability score prerequisite (Intelligence 13). You gain the benefits of this feat only as long as you have at least 1 stamina point in your stamina pool. You can spend 2 stamina points after you successfully trip an opponent to deal 1d6 points of falling damage to the target.
