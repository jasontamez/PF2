Winged Boots Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 233, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 504 Aura moderate transmutation; CL 8th Slot feet; Price 16,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These boots appear to be ordinary footgear. On command, they sprout wings at the heel and let the wearer fly, without having to maintain concentration, as if affected by a fly spell (including a +4 bonus on Fly skill checks). The wearer can fly three times per day for up to 5 minutes per flight. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, fly; Cost 8,000 gp
