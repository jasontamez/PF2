Aldori Dueling Disciple - Combat Stamina - Feats Aldori Dueling Disciple Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 24 Related Feat Aldori Dueling Disciple During any combat that is not a duel, you can spend 1 stamina point at the start of your turn to treat the combat as a duel until the start of your next turn for the purpose of gaining the benefits of the Aldori Dueling Discipline feat.
