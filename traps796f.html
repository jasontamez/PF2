Bear Trap Source Advanced Player's Guide pg. 184 Type mechanical; Perception DC 15; Disable Device DC 20 Effects Trigger location; Reset manual Effect Atk +10 melee (2d6+3); sharp jaws spring shut around the creature's ankle and halve the creature's base speed (or hold the creature immobile if the trap is attached to a solid object); the creature can escape with a DC 20 Disable Device check, DC 22 Escape Artist check, or DC 26 Strength check
