Iron Band of Iron Mind - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Iron Band of Iron Mind Source Pathfinder #91: Battle of Bloodmarch Hills pg. 73 Aura moderate abjuration; CL 9th Slot headband; Price 15,000 gp; Weight 4 lbs. Description This headband grants a +4 bonus on saving throws against all enchantment spells. When worn by a creature with the giant subtype, it makes the wearer immune to enchantments that target only humanoid creatures (such as charm person or hold person). Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, break enchantment, creator must have the giant subtype; Cost 7,500 gp
