Pressure pill - Miscellaneous - Equipment Pressure pill Source Aquatic Adventures pg. 56 Price 75 gp; Weight — Category Alchemical Tools Description When you swallow this marble-shaped alchemical pill as a full-round action, it instantly adjusts your body to the surrounding water pressure, though you take 3 points of nonlethal damage for every 100 feet of adjustment necessary (see Pressure on page 48).
