Swordplay Upset - Feats Swordplay Upset (Combat) Source Weapon Master's Handbook pg. 17 You catch opponents off guard with your attacks. Prerequisites: Combat Expertise, Improved Feint, Swordplay Style, Weapon Focus with the chosen weapon, base attack bonus +5. Benefit: While using Swordplay Style, as an immediate action, you can attempt a feint against an opponent that makes a melee attack against you and misses.
