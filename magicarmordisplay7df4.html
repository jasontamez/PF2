Tireless Tracking Hide - Armor - Magic Equipment Tireless Tracking Hide Source Advanced Class Guide pg. 212 Aura faint transmutation CL 5th Slot armor; Price 11,165 gp; Weight 25 lbs. Description This +1 hide armor is always made from the pelt of a migratory herd animal. Anyone wearing this armor can maintain a hustle for a full 8 hours without growing fatigued, doubling her normal daily overland movement rate. After that, the wearer begins to take nonlethal damage each hour, per the hustle rules (Core Rulebook 171). This armor does not increase tactical movement during combat. The benefits of this armor aren't replenished until the character has had a full night's rest. Construction Requirements Craft Magic Arms and Armor, bear's endurance; Price 5,665 gp
