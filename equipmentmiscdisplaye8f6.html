Mirage mixture - Miscellaneous - Equipment Mirage mixture Source Potions and Poisons pg. 22 Price 300 gp; Weight — Category Tincture Description This fizzy drink causes a tingling in the optic nerves. For 1 hour after drinking this tincture, you gain a +2 alchemical bonus on Perception checks and Will saving throws to disbelieve illusions. You take a -2 penalty on Will saves against other mind-affecting effects for the same duration. Construction Craft (Alchemy) DC 25
