Boots of the Cat - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Boots of the Cat Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 229 Aura faint transmutation; CL 1st Slot feet; Price 1,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description These high-soled blue boots provide a great deal of comfort and arch support while also making the wearer appear a little bit taller than normal. The boot's wearer always takes the minimum possible damage from falls (as if the GM had rolled a 1 on each die of damage incurred by the fall) and at the end of a fall always lands on his feet. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, feather fall; Cost 500 gp
