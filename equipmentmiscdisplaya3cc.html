Frostbite ointment - Miscellaneous - Equipment Frostbite ointment Source Pathfinder #51: The Hungry Storm pg. 67 Price 50 gp; Weight 1 lb. Category Alchemical Remedies Description One hour after being applied, this alchemical salve cures any creature suffering from frostbite, though any nonlethal damage from cold or exposure remains until the victim recovers. A creature cannot benefit from frostbite ointment more than once in 24 hours.
