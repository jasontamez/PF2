Verdant Spell - Feats Verdant Spell (Metamagic) Source Ultimate Wilderness pg. 119, Heroes of the Wild pg. 26 Your magical connection to nature allows you to entice, fool, and misdirect plants and fungi as though they were people. Prerequisites: Spell Focus (enchantment), Knowledge (nature) 6 ranks. Benefit: A verdant spell affects plant creatures (even mindless plant creatures) as if they weren't immune to mind-affecting effects, but it has no effect on other types of creatures. A verdant spell uses up a spell slot 2 levels higher than the spell's actual level. This feat works only on mind-affecting spells. Normal: Creatures of the plant type are immune to mind-affecting effects.
