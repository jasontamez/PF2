Throat Slicer - Feats Throat Slicer (Combat) Source Heroes of the Streets pg. 29 Sometimes the only way to deal with those who see your illicit activities is to get rid of them. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1. Benefit: When using a 1-handed, light, or natural weapon, you can deliver a coup de grace to an unconscious, bound, or pinned target (though not other kinds of helpless targets) as a standard action.
