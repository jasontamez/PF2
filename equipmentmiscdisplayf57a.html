Longship - Miscellaneous - Equipment Longship Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 87, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 159 Price 10,000 gp; Price of Passage (per mile) 5 cp Category Transport, Sea Description This 75-foot-long ship with 40 oars requires a total crew of 50. It has a single mast and a square sail, and it can carry 50 tons of cargo or 120 soldiers. A longship can make sea voyages. It moves about 3 miles per hour when being rowed or under sail.
