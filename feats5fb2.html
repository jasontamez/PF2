Feats Targeting Feats Description Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 8 Several of the following feats have the new targeting type. If you are using the optional called shots rules (Pathfinder RPG Ultimate Combat 193), attacks made with targeting feats count as called shots for the purposes of abilities or effects that modify called shots, such as the Improved Called Shot feat from Ultimate Combat.
