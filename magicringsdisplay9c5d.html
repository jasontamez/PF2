Ring of Maniacal Devices - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Maniacal Devices Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 172, Advanced Player's Guide pg. 292 Aura faint transmutation CL 3rd Slot ring; Price 5,000 gp; Weight — Description This elaborately decorated ring is crafted with the design of a fierce dragon wrapped greedily around a shining gemstone. It grants the wearer a +5 competence bonus on all Craft (traps) and Disable Device checks. Even if he has no ranks in these skills, the wearer may make Craft (traps) and Disable Device checks as if he were trained in them. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, fox's cunning, creator must have 5 ranks in the relevant skill; Cost 2,500 gp
