Imbue With Addiction - Spells Imbue With Addiction Source Black Markets pg. 19, Magical Marketplace pg. 24 School transmutation; Level alchemist 2, arcanist 3, investigator 2, sahirafiyun 3, sorcerer 3, witch 3, wizard 3 Casting Casting Time 1 standard action Components V, S, M (a dose of an addictive drug or substance) Effect Range touch Target one living creature Duration instantaneous Saving Throw Fortitude negates; Spell Resistance yes Description The target immediately becomes addicted to the drug used as the material component during the casting of the spell. If the target was ever addicted to the drug at any point in the past, it takes a -4 penalty on its saving throw.
