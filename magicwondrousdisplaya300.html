Shepsi-ak's Funerary Mask - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Shepsi-ak's Funerary Mask Source Undead Unleashed pg. 43 Aura moderate varied; CL 9th Slot head; Price 9,000 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description An undead wearing this golden mask is treated as both an undead and a creature of the type it was before becoming undead (including any associated subtypes) for the purposes of determining its eligibility as a target of illusion and transmutation spells (such as alter self or disguise self). A corpse wearing the mask gains the effects of gentle repose and sanctify corpse. A living creature wearing the mask gains no effects. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, polymorph, sanctify corpse; Cost 4,500 gp
