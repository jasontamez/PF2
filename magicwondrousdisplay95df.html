Cloak of Resistance (+2) - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Cloak of Resistance Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 264, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 507 Aura faint abjuration; CL 5th Slot shoulders; Price 1,000 gp (+1), 4,000 gp (+2), 9,000 gp (+3), 16,000 gp (+4), 25,000 gp (+5); Weight 1 lb. Description Flecks of silver or steel are often sown amid the fabric of these magical cloaks. This garment offers magical protection in the form of a +1 to +5 resistance bonus on all saving throws (Fortitude, Reflex, and Will).
