Celestial lamp - Miscellaneous - Equipment Celestial lamp Source Advanced Race Guide pg. 87 Price 300 gp; Weight 2 lbs. Category Tools Description This polished lantern contains a continual flame and sheds light as a common lamp. If its font of consecrated crystal and metalwork is filled with holy water, the lamp's light is sanctified for 24 hours, adding a +1 bonus to the save DC of channeled positive energy or energy channeled to harm evil outsiders within a 30-foot radius.
