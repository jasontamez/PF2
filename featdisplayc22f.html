Strong Comeback - Feats Strong Comeback Source Ultimate Combat pg. 121 You learn quickly from past mistakes. Benefit: Whenever you are allowed to reroll an ability check, a skill check, or a saving throw, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus on the reroll. Mythic Strong Comeback Source Mythic Adventures pg. 75 You never make the same mistake twice. Prerequisites: Strong Comeback. Benefit: Whenever you're allowed to reroll an ability check, a skill check, or a saving throw, roll two dice and take the higher result, before adding the bonus from Strong Comeback.
