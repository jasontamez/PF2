Ring of Force Shield - Rings - Magic Equipment Ring of Force Shield Source Ultimate Equipment pg. 171, PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 479 Aura moderate evocation CL 9th Slot ring; Price 8,500 gp; Weight — Description This ring generates a shield-sized (and shield-shaped) wall of force that stays with the ring and can be wielded by the wearer as if it were a heavy shield (+2 AC). This special creation has no armor check penalty or arcane spell failure chance since it is weightless and encumbrance-free. It can be activated and deactivated at will as a free action. Construction Requirements Forge Ring, wall of force; Cost 4,250 gp
