Miscellaneous - Equipment Ear trumpet Source Ranged Tactics Toolbox pg. 20 Price 25 gp (common), 100 gp (masterwork); Weight 2 lbs. (common), 2 lbs. (masterwork) Description This foot-long brass funnel amplifies sounds entering through the broad end for anyone listening at the narrow end. The listener gains a +2 circumstance bonus on sound-based Perception checks but must use one hand to hold the trumpet to the ear in order to gain the benefit. A masterwork ear trumpet's exquisite acoustic properties grant the listener a +4 circumstance bonus instead.
