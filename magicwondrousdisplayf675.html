Portrait of Health - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Portrait of Health Source Gallery of Evil pg. 32 Aura moderate conjuration (healing); CL 11th Price 35,650 gp; Weight — Description This beautiful watercolor painting depicts a clear mountain stream flanked by moss-covered stones and dew-drenched ferns. Mountains rise up in the background, casting rippled shadows across the water's surface. The silver frame is etched with symbols of healing. By touching the painting while uttering one of three seperate command words, you may cast the following spells: 3/day - maximized cure light wounds (13 points); 1/day - maximized cure moderate wounds (26 points); 1/week - heal. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, Maximize Spell, cure light wounds, cure moderate wounds, heal; Cost 17,825 gp, 1,426 XP
