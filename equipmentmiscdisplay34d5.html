Zombie slayer's kit - Miscellaneous - Equipment Zombie slayer's kit Source Undead Slayer's Handbook pg. 15 Price 170 gp; Weight 31 lbs. Category Kits Description This large satchel contains the tools needed to put down potential zombie uprisings. The kit includes a shovel, two flasks of oil, a bullseye lantern, a flask of alchemist's fire, two flasks of holy water, 50 feet of hemp rope, a crowbar, a hammer and 10 nails, two vials of antiplague, and a wooden holy symbol.
