Pinpoint Targeting - Combat Stamina - Feats Pinpoint Targeting Source Pathfinder Unchained pg. 129 Related Feat Pinpoint Targeting When you use this feat, you can spend stamina points to retain the ability to move. If you spend 2 stamina points, you can take a 5-foot step after you make the attack. If you instead spend 5 stamina points, you can move up to half your speed as a move action.
