Strength in Defeat - Feats Strength in Defeat Source Heroes from the Fringe pg. 20 Half-drow tap into inner reserves, fighting even more fiercely when losing. Prerequisites: Base attack bonus +1, darkborn half-elf. Benefit: When reduced to half of your maximum hit points or fewer, you gain a +1 morale bonus on attack and damage rolls. This bonus increases to +2 at 8th level and to +3 at 15th level.
