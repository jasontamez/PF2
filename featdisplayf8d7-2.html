Empower Spell - Feats Empower Spell (Metamagic) Source PRPG Core Rulebook pg. 122 You can increase the power of your spells, causing them to deal more damage. Benefit: All variable, numeric effects of an empowered spell are increased by half including bonuses to those dice rolls. Saving throws and opposed rolls are not affected, nor are spells without random variables. An empowered spell uses up a spell slot two levels higher than the spell's actual level.
