First General of the East - Feats First General of the East (Origin) Source Pathfinder #139: The Dead Road pg. 73 Your creator was a soldier and person of war, and your memories are filled with the taste of blood. Prerequisites: Character level 5th, shabti. Benefit: Once per day, you can cast greater magic weapon as a spell-like ability. You gain a second daily use at 9th level, and a third at 15th level. Only you can benefit from this spell-like ability; anyone else who wields the enchanted weapon gains no benefit from the spell. The caster level for this ability is equal to your character level.
