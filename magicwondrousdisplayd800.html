Belt of the Snake King - Wondrous Items - Magic Equipment Belt of the Snake King Source Inner Sea Gods pg. 260, Pathfinder #42: Sanctum of the Serpent God pg. 28, Gods and Magic pg. 54 Aura faint transmutation; CL 5th Slot belt; Price 2,600 gp; Weight 1 lb. Description This belt writhes like a living snake, and even hisses and blinks its red eyes. It clasps by holding its tail in its mouth. The wearer gains a +1 enhancement bonus to natural armor. If the serpentfolk god Ydersius is the wearer's patron, once per day, the wearer can use magic fang on one of his own natural weapons. Construction Requirements Craft Wondrous Item, barkskin, magic fang, summon monster I; Cost 1,300 gp
